Earning Experience Points

Advanced Strategies

To earn more skills, you must level up. To do that, you need to earn as many Experience Points as possible. This section will list stages and skills that you can take advantage of to earn Experience Points quickly.

[Stages you can use to earn Experience Points quickly]
The Total Score number on each mission’s results screen (the combination of the Action Bonus, Enemy Bonus, Ring Bonus and Technical Bonus) is directly linked to your Experience Points, so the missions below are those that allow you to earn higher scores in relatively short times.
* All scores assume no damage (Technical Bonus × 2)
