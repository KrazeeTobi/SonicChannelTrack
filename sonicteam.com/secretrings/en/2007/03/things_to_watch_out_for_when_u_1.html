Things to watch out for when using Speed Break and Time Break

Advanced Strategies

Vulnerability when activating Speed Break
When activating a Speed Break, Sonic is vulnerable for the first 0.5 seconds. Try to make sure Sonic isn’t open to enemy attacks during that time.


Speed Break Overrides
If you activate a Speed Break when Sonic is touching a Dash Panel, the Speed Break is cancelled, and the Dash Panel acceleration takes priority. You can use combinations like performing a Speed Break right after the Dash Panel acceleration ends, or first using a Speed Break into a Dash Panel.


Controls for Canceling a Time Break
When using a Time Break, Sonic must be on the ground in order to cancel it. You cannot cancel when Sonic is in midair, so you can use Jump Cancel to reduce the amount of Soul Gauge energy used.
