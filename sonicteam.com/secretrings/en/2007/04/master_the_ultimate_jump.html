Master the Ultimate Jump

Advanced Tactics

Perhaps you’ve noticed Sonic occasionally do an amazing jump when he hits an obstacle during gameplay.
In Sonic and the Secret Rings, Sonic’s actions are governed by physics calculations, so depending on Sonic’s speed and the angle at which he hits an obstacle, he can perform jumps beyond his usual abilities.
The controls are very difficult if you try to do this on purpose, and it’s nearly impossible to perform consistently.
The trick is to jump so you glance off the corner of an obstacle. If you can pull this off, you can skip nasty traps and save time. The timing of the jump is affected by the shape of the obstacle, but it’s a useful trick for advanced players.
Once you master this jump, you can use it to blaze your own trails through the missions.
