With 30+ events spread across different venues and disciplines, you’re bound to find the sport that’s perfect for you! Whether you’re playing Joy-Con in hand or opting for button controls, you can show off your skills in Tokyo 2020 events against others in either local or online multiplayer.

New for Tokyo 2020!

Conquer the climbing wall, come out on top in the kumite, ride some gnarly waves and shred the skatepark with four new events for the Olympic Games Tokyo 2020!

Sport Climbing

Climb as high as you can within the time limit. Look before you leap!

Karate - Kumite

Land hits on your opponent to earn points. The first to 10 points wins! Hajime!

Surfing - Shortboard

Ride the waves and pull off as many tricks as you can within the time limit!

Skateboarding - Park

Aim for a high score by performing as many tricks as you can before time runs out!


Field of dreams

Dream Events make their exciting return in Mario & Sonic at the Olympic Games Tokyo 2020! These exciting activities take existing Olympic sports and turn up the dial by adding iconic elements from the Super Mario and Sonic series.

Dream Shooting

Either as four separate players or in two teams of two, roam around the arena and shoot at targets to get the highest score. Aim for that giant target to gain a ton of points!

Dream Racing

Compete against seven other racers in this action-packed downhill race. Perform tricks to gain a speed boost on landing and gather items like Rings, Red Shells and Bananas dotted round the track to help you reach the finish first!

Dream Karate

Knock your opponents down to fill up the arena with panels of your color. Pick up items like POW Blocks and Chaos Emeralds to turn the tide of battle, or activate your Super Power Move to knock down anyone close to you!
