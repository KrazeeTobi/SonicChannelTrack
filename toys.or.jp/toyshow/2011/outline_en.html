Name International Tokyo Toy Show 2011
Period June 16 (Thu) to 19 (Sun), 2011
[Buyer's Days] June 16 (Thu) 9:30am to 6:00pm | 17 (Fri) 9:30am to 6:00pm
[Public Days] June 18 (Sat) 9:00am to 5:00pm | 19 (Sun) 9:00am to 4:00pm
Venue Tokyo Big Sight West Exhibition Hall No. 1-4
3-11-1 Ariake, Koto-ku, Tokyo
Organizer The Japan Toy Association (JTA)
Co-Organizer Tokyo Metropolitan Government
Supported by Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry
Admission Free on all days
[Buyer's Days] Visitors with business purpose only *
(registration upon admission is required)
[Public Days] General public
*show held on June 16 and 17 is a trade show solely for business discussions, and people who are not engaged in business (including students) will not be admitted to enter.

Anyone engaged in business may gain admittance to the International Tokyo Toy Show by filling out an invitation form and submitting it to the reception counter.

In order to avoid a long queue, the Japan Toy Association will prepare a registration counter reserved for foreign buyers only. You will need to submit an invitation form (which can be downloaded below), together with two business cards of yours.


Confirmation of Admission (* This is not a required procedure.)

If you wish to ensure for admission in advance, in order to remove your unnecessary concerns whether you will sure to be admitted, the Japan Toy Association will pre-confirm your admission. To contact the association, please click here.

The Japan Toy Association will send you an e-mail stating your confirmation number. Please print out the e-mail and bring it to the show. Note: This pre-confirmation is solely for the purpose of removing your concerns about admission, so please follow the regular admission procedure of filling out the invitation form and submitting it to the reception counter.
