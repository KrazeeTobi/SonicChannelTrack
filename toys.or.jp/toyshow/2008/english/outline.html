* The show held on June 19 and 20 is a trade show solely for business discussions, and people who are not engaged in business (including students) will not be admitted to enter.

In order to avoid a long queue, the Japan Toy Association will prepare a registration counter reserved for foreign buyers only. You will need to submit an invitation form (which can be downloaded below), together with two business cards of yours.

If you wish to ensure for admission in advance, in order to remove your unnecessary concerns whether you will sure to be admitted, the Japan Toy Association will pre-confirm your admission. To contact the association, please click here.

The Japan Toy Association will send you an e-mail stating your confirmation number. Please print out the e-mail and bring it to the show.
Note: This pre-confirmation is solely for the purpose of removing your concerns about admission, so please follow the regular admission procedure of filling out the invitation form and submitting it to the reception counter.
