Press/Media Registration


Welcome to TGS 2000 Autumn Press/ Media Registration. We invite qualified journalists from print, broadcast and online media to register for a complimentary media badge that provides:

Exhibit floor access
Use of TGS 2000 Autumn Press/ Media Center facilities


We will send you an application form on the pre-registration starting, if you send us a printed copy of your online publication /your magazine including the cover page and a few pages of the text. In addition, we need your information:

1,Company name 4,Address
2,Magazine/Program title 5,TEL/FAX Number
3,Contact person 6, E-mail address

Once we have received your registration form with credentials via fax, you will receive a confirmation sheet via fax in approximately two weeks confirming or denying your registration.
