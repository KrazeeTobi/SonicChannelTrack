TOKYO GAME SHOW 2000 AUTUMN Topics


　"The Entertainment of Your life"

The theme of the "TOKYO GMAE SHOW 2000 AUTUMN" is "The Entertainment of Your Life." This theme means that video games are entertainment in everyday life and tools making our life enjoyable.

Poster and Ticket Design express all family members enjoying a video game together.

The poster and ticket design express the every day life of usual Japanese family or possibly Japanese sweet old home. The design of all family members enjoying a video game expresses the attraction of video games enjoyed by all generations. "Ferret," the popular character of Tokyo Game Show, appears on the design as the family member. The design also expresses the familiarity of the video games by featuring "Ferret." The words "MINNA GAME GA SUKINANDA - Everyone loves video games!" are put into the design as a catch line.

"Venture Booth" and "Network Entertainment Corner" are newly established.

"Venture Booth" is an exclusive space for trying and enjoying the video games. At the booth, attendants can try greater number of the video games.

"Network Entertainment Corner" is a space for network contents including online games and network games.

You will be quick to experience the online network world growing rapidly with communication infrastructure improvement.

"Game Show Internet DE IKIMASHOU"
 - Enjoy virtual Tokyo Game Show on the CESA's website.

"CESA GAME.COM," the general game portal site managed by CESA, is featuring Tokyo Games Shoe 2000 Autumn on special pages from September 12 through to October 12. The catch line is "Game Show Internet DE IKIMASHOU"

On September 18, a special project for Tokyo Game Show 2000 Autumn started on the site. The main plans are as follows:

  • Online sales of video games, special goods and etc.
  • Making present of trial video games and promotional merchandise
  • Free wallpaper for PC and "i MODE"
  • Exhibitors' website cruising game that keyword should be found

Report of the venue, News flash of the exhibited titles, Costume Contest Report and Booth events Report are available with moving images on the CESA's website during the session.

(Cooperation: Foreign TV Japan http://www.ftv.co.jp)

The latest information about Tokyo Game Show 2000 Autumn including events schedule is available on "i MODE" during the session. (http://www.cesagame.com/i)

Kids' Corner Outline

Kids' Corner, which is filled with a number of children every time, is set up at the Exhibition Hall 8 as usual. "Try & Touch Zone," where children can enjoy video games, and "Sales Zone," where special goods for children are available, are newly established. Special attractions and stage shows for little children including face painting, balloon arts and juggling by circus clown are held. With the cooperation of EPSON HANBAI Co., Ltd., "Photography Corner," where taking picture with popular game characters is available, is set up.
