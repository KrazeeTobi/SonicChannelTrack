Daily Report : September 19 (General Admission Day)

"Creator Talk Battle - seek the secrets of the next-generation hardware" was held at the event stage. Three famous creators talked about the new hardware, and answered the questions asked by the audience.

10 exhibitors at the Kids Corner drew 19,366 visitors. Children enjoyed new games and shows on the stage.

A number of games with elaborate graphics flocked to the PC Corner. Visitors enjoyed flight-simulator, action, and gun-shooting making the best use of PC characteristic features.

The Sales Corner had a great vogue as ever. A great throng of people visited this corner to buy premium products.
