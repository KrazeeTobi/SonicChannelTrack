CESA Developers' Conference Tokyo 1999

"CESA Developers' Conference Tokyo 1999" (CEDEC TOKYO 1999) will be held for September 16 - 17, 1999 at Makuhari Messe (Nippon Convention Center / International Conference Hall). This conference is the renewal version of the CESA strategic Presentation which used to be held together with the "Tokyo Game Show" to disclose the information about video-game-developing hardware. In this new conference, the software development tools and the latest technology will be introduced to the visitors.

CEDEC TOKYO 1999 is produced in the hope of serving as a stepping stone to further development by making the environment of software development better. This conference consists of two parts; the exhibition and the demonstration by software development tool makers, and the seminar of graphic designing, programming, and producing.

The exhibition and the seminar are both targeting mainly at the developers of CESA's members though, the people concerned with entertainment software industry, the students learning software programming, and the people who are interested in software development are also welcomed.
