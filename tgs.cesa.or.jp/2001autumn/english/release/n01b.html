■The theme is "Let's Play Together."

　The theme of "Tokyo Game Show 2001 Autumn" is "Let's Play Together." Communication environment are advanced, and contents and platforms including the new platform, cellular phones, PCs, and PDA, are diversified. Now, users can play games with many others anytime and anywhere. In view of these circumstances, we will send the message of "Let's play games all together."



■The 5th Japan Entertainment Software Awards wining team designed the main visual for TGS.

　We have decided to ask Japan Entertainment Software Awards wining company to design the main visual for the poster and ticket. The 5th Japan Entertainment Software Awards wining SONICTEAM, LTD. that produced "Phantacy Star Online" designed it for this time. The concept of the design is as follows.

Concept

　Along the theme of "Let's play together," the poster and ticket are designed. The designed character is putting out his hand and moving ahead. It means respectively "Let's play together" and "Don't miss the chance now."

Design Image

　A style of future video game is expressed in the design. The character is wearing many game items of portable machine, goggle, headphone, wristwatch and also shoes. In the future, these items will interface with games by network or radio transmission, and then the video game will be more active and a new style of play. The general idea that game players are facing monitors to have fun individually will be old. The games are being more fashionable to appeal wider rage of users.

■TGS to raise funds to help the victims of the devastating attacks in the U.S.

　Sales of Charity Auction which will be held at the Event Stage and moneys raised in the collection boxes installed in the venue will be contributed to the victims of the devastating attacks in the U.S. We hope for a lot of contributions and participation in the charity auction.
