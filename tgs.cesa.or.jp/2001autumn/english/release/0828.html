2001.08.28

Press Registration
for TOKYO GAME SHOW 2001 AUTUMN


We welcome qualified journalists from print, broadcast and online media to register to cover the"TOKYO GAME SHOW 2001 AUTUMN" from every corner of the world.

The press who meet the conditions below are allowed to register to cover the show.
Registration must be done in advance with application form through fax.
Please inquire for application form by e-mail: info@cesa.or.jp

Thank you very much for your understanding and cooperation.


1. Editor / reporter / camera crew from newspaper publisher or publishing company
2. Editor / reporter / staff from on-line magazine or newspaper production company
3. Reporter / camera crew from TV program production company, including BS, CS, and cable TV program
4. Reporter / staff from Radio Program production company
5. Freelance writer / camera crew entrusted by one of the companies mentioned above
6. TV or radio personality / performer of the programs


The closing date for registration is September 29.
