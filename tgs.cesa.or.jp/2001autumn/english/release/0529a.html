Information Material

Regarding "TOKYO GAME SHOW 2001 AUTUMN"

Dates : Fri. October 12, 2001 - Sun. October 14, 2001
Venue : Makuhari Messe (Nippon Convention Center)

Computer Entertainment Software Association (CESA, Chairman: Kagemasa Kozuki, location: Nishi-Shimbashi, Minato-ku, Tokyo) has finalized the schedule for "TOKYO GAME SHOW 2001 AUTUMN", for three days from Fry. October 12 to Sun. 14 will be opened to public.

The open hours for Fri. Oct. 12 will be between 10:00 and 18:00(Note: open to general public from 1pm), and for Sat. 13 & Sun. 14, it will be open from 10:00 and 17:00.

The venue will be "Makuhari Messe" (Nippon Convention Center, Mihama-ku, Chiba city). 150,000 visitors are expected to attend the show.

Tokyo Game Show Secretariat began recruiting exhibitors from Mon. May 28. For details of the show, please refer to the CESA homepage (http://www.cesa.or.jp).

The company who wish to exhibit is required to apply by Fri. June 29, 2001. Then a press conference, an explanation meeting and a drawing for the exhibition site will be held at Ginza Gas Hall (Ginza, Chuo-ku, Tokyo) on Tue. July 17. The details of the exhibition outline will be notified at the press conference.

The "Tokyo Game Show" was born in August 1996. It has established a firm position as a place to send out the latest information on the computer entertainment software industry to the world, and as a significant event for the industry in our country, which continues leading the world.

The 11th Tokyo Game Show, "TOKYO GAME SHOW 2001 AUTUMN", will introduce you all about the computer entertainment in the 21st century, which keeps expanding with the spread of new video game hardware, PC's and mobile phones, and with the improvement of communication environment. We assure you that this game show is more substantial than ever.
