Kids' Corner Outline


　Kids' Corner for primary school students and younger is set up at the Exhibition Hall 8 this time. The corner is filled with a number of children every time. At the " Touch & Try Booth," children can try new games. The game-related products for kids are available at "Sales Corner." Various events will be held by exhibitors at "Event Stage." At the photo corner, Tokyo Game Show original frames for shooting are provided. Attractions of balloon arts and juggling by circus clown are also held at the Kids' corner.
