Exhibition Regulations
*Products that may be deemed to encounter problems in terms of ethics and/or copyright must be submitted beforehand in the form of video footage or the software itself for approval.
*Only products handled by the company applying to exhibit may be exhibited.
*Products other than newspapers and magazines may not be sold outside the Product Sales Area. Sales of game software, including advance sales, must not take place under any circumstances.
*Exhibition of the Amusement machine/Arcade machine for business is not allowed.
*Neither the organizer nor the venue shall be held responsible for theft or damage to exhibits.
*Detailed regulations concerning construction and other matters will be provided at the exhibitors' briefing on July 17 (Tue.). Otherwise please refer to the Exhibition Guideline Manual.

 

 

Booth Allocation
*Due to venue space restrictions, the organizer reserves the right to make the final decision on the number of booths allocated.
*Overseas exhibitors will exhibit in the Overseas Exhibitors' Area specified by the secretariat.
