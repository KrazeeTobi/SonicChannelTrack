Allocation of booth positions Allocation of booth positions will be determined at the booth position allocation meeting held separately for each exhibition corner after the explanation meeting for exhibitors on June 19 (Wed.) 2002 in Tokyo, Japan. All exhibitors are advised to attend the briefing.

Allocation procedure In descending order from largest to smallest size planned booths, exhibitors are allowed to make a choice from several options prepared by the management office beforehand in accordance with booth area size.
When more than one exhibitor applies for the same size booth area, preference is given to the exhibitor who first meets one of the following conditions in the following order.
(1)An exhibitor who participated in the previous exhibition (Tokyo Game Show 2001 Autumn) and applied before the closing day for applications.
(2)An exhibitor who participated in the previous exhibition and applied after the closing day for applications.
(3)An exhibitor who did not participate in the previous exhibition and applied before the closing day for applications.
(4)An exhibitor who did not participate in the previous exhibition and applied after the closing day for applications.
If more than one exhibitor happens to fit the same condition, the priority among the exhibitors to choose the booth allocation would be decided by lottery.

*For exhibitors with more than 40 booths, an island booth (with no adjacent booths on both sides) will be prepared.
*The size of a booth cannot be changed at the booth position allocation meeting.
*If the positioning of the booths is not completely settled during the booth position allocation meeting, exhibitors may be asked in consultation with the management office to change the size of their booths or modify the booth dimensions. On this matter, we ask for all exhibitors' cooperation.
*Changes of booth location are not permitted after the booth position allocation meeting. However, when a cancellation by another exhibitor takes place or a conclusive reason to add more booth area arises, the booth location may be changed in consultation with the secretariat. On this matter, we ask for all exhibitors' cooperation.
*The allocation procedure may be modified in advance depending on application conditions. If this happens, the management office will inform all exhibitors on this matter before the booth position allocation meeting.

Cancellation of booth applications After the application form is submitted, cancellation of all or part of booths applied for must be made in a written form. In such a case, the following cancellation charges will become payable depending on the day of cancellation. After the cancellation form is accepted, a bill for the applicable cancellation charge will be sent. Payment must be made within 30 days.
May 30 (Thurs.) - June 18 (Tue.), 2002 50% of exhibition charge
After June 19 (Wed.), 2002 100% of exhibition charge
