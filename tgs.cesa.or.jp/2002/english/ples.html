TOKYO GAME SHOW 2002: Press Registration (media from outside Japan)

All the press covering news on TOKYO GAME SHOW is required to register in advance. Please register in advance, because we may not be able to issue a press pass for non-advance registrants at the venue.

How to register

Those who registered for TOKYO GAME SHOW 2001 Autumn
We have e-mailed a registration form and press guidelines in early August.
If you failed to receive them , or if you changed your business address, please contact:
tgs@publicity-bur.co.jp

Those who did not register for TOKYO GAME SHOW 2001 Autumn
Please e-mail the name of your media outlet and your contact information to:
tgs@publicity-bur.co.jp

Press members who are able to register in advance or to gather news are limited to those who are listed below. We hope we may have your kind understanding to this matter.


Qualified Press Includes Representatives from: Qualifying Titles Are:

Newspapers 		Editor
  Magazines Editorial     Writer
  Online Publications     Reporter
  Online News services     Correspondent
  Newswires     Photographer
  TV Stations     Director
  Radio Stations     Producer
  Other appropriate media     Camera crew

Freelance writers or photographers must submit a letter of assignment from the appropriate media.
Press Pass is not available to advertisement agencies, PR companies, and sales reps for media. Please purchase a ticket to attend the show.

If you have further questions, please contact:
Press Room, TGS Management Office
E-mail tgs@publicity-bur.co.jp
