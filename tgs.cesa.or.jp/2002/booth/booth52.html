■About Media Create
Media Create gathers and analyzes data from the digital entertainment industry, specifically focusing on the Japanese console gaming market. Business operations include publishing, market research and consulting.

■Console Game Research Data
Gathering data from retailers nationwide, Media Create provides sales figures and rankings for consoles and console software, based on sell-through figures calculated from actual unit sales. Services are not limited to mere numerical data, we also provide analytical reports, which give you full understanding about the Japanese market and its trends. These reports analyze the underlying reasons behind the trends - reasons which most publishers and developers overseas have difficulty pinning down.
