Event stage and official program timetables also announced!

Release

2025/09/09

■Record-breaking 1,138 exhibitors and 4,159 booths (as of September 9)!

As of September 9, the total number of exhibitors is 1,138 (including 28 online exhibitors), with 523 domestic and 615 overseas exhibitors. The number of physical booths at the venue has reached 4,159, greatly surpassing the previous record in 2024, making this the largest TGS ever. In addition, the number of game titles to be exhibited as of September 4 has reached 1206titles.

Exhibits will cover a wide range of genres and platforms̶including home consoles, smartphones, and PCs̶with highlights such as highly anticipated new releases, esports titles, AR/VR games, and blockchain games. Hardware, furniture, and business solutions enhancing the gaming environment will also be showcased, making TGS2025 a place to obtain and experience a wide variety of B2B and B2C information.

■Event Stage and Official Program Timetables Announced!

The timetables for event stages and official programs taking place during the show have been released today. Over the four days of TGS2025, a total of 14 stage programs (including 7 organized by exhibitors) and 21official programs are planned. Programs organized by the host will also be streamed as official programs.






■ TGS BOOSTERZ – Second Wave Announced

At TGS2025, official influencers called TGS BOOSTERZ will be appointed under the theme of “boosting Tokyo Game Showʼs appeal to the world.”
Today, the second wave of influencers has been announced: AXIS_MYCALL, Kawaii Coco, Kenki, Kuronetsu Denkyu, and Moga Mogami (in alphabetical order).
