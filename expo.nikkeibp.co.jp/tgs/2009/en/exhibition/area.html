Business Solution Area

It is possible to select either a Type-A exhibit pattern, which includes all four days of the show, or Type-B, which includes only the two business days. It is also possible to make use of the Sponsorship Sessions as additional opportunities to increase the effectiveness of exhibits.

Type-A Exhibit period September 24 (Thursday) to September 27 (Sunday)
Booth size 3m entrance x 3m depth
Booth charge 350,000 yen per booth
†The above charge only covers booth space and does not include decoration and electrical work.
Type-B
 Exhibit period September 24 (Thursday) to September 25 (Friday)
Booth size 2m entrance x 2m depth
Booth charge 200,000 yen per booth
†There is a maximum application of three booths per exhibitors.
