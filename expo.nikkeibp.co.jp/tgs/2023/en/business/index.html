Business / Press

  2. Business / Press

At Tokyo Game Show 2023, we are offering several services for business people in the game industry. We have some very useful tools for the business day, so please use them. We are also sending out information for the press, so please take advantage of this service.

Business

Business Matching

TGS2023 offers a business matching function using a dedicated system. This system is available to exhibitors and Business Day visitors (for a fee).

Business Seminar

Real seminar lectures will be held for Business Day visitors on the second day of Business Day (September 22, Friday) from 10:30 a.m. at the International Conference Hall of Makuhari Messe.
Third party provision of personal information to overseas exhibitors

Press

Press Release

Press releases related to TGS2023.

Exhibitor News

We will collect and post press releases sent out independently by exhibitors at TGS2023.

Official Supporter

We are pleased to introduce you to our official supporters who will help us promote the appeal of TGS2023.

Press Registration / Download Center

Press registration is requested for all media covering TGS 2023.

Media Partner

We are pleased to introduce you to our media partners who will disseminate information about TGS2023 to the entire world.

Call for Official Influencer

TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office are looking for "TGS2023 Official Influencers from Asia-Pacific" to represent their countries and regions, especially in Asia and Oceania, and invite them to attend this year's TOKYO GAME SHOW at Makuhari Messe. We hope that you will share the appeal of the TOKYO GAME SHOW with viewers and game fans in your own country.

TOKYO GAME SHOW 2023 Overseas Visitor Support

TOKYO GAME SHOW 2023 will provide support for trade visitors (exhibitors, press, and business day visitors)
from overseas to apply for visas to enter Japan at TGS2023.

Documents to be Supported

  • Letter of Reason for Invitation
  • Letter of Guarantee
  • Schedule of Stay
  • Documents related to the organizer of TGS

※Please check the website of the Japanese embassy/consulate in your country/region of residence for entry requirements and travel conditions, as they change daily basis.

Support fee

Charged (16,500 yen per person, tax included)
