Tokyo Game Show 2023 will offer a variety of organizer projects. You can enjoy them not only at the real venue but also online. We look forward to your participation.

Keynotes

The keynote speech will be delivered on Thursday, September 21, at 11:00 a.m. in the official program.

Official Streaming

Four days from Business Day to Public Day,
Various exhibitors will provide information on the official TGS program. Please check the program schedule and tune in.

Japan Game Awards

The Japan Game Awards will be broadcasted as an official program in four divisions: Game of the Year, Amateur, U-18, and Future.

Indie Game Project

The "Selected Indie 80" showcasing the best indie games from around the world, the "Sense of Wonder Night" pitch contest, and more.
  • Selected Indie 80
  • Sense of Wonder Night 2023 (SOWN2023)
  • Predict SOWN2023 Finalists Campaign

Steam Special Venue

TGS exhibitors' Steam-distributed game titles will be listed under tabs such as "Available" and "Coming Soon"./ Thursday, Sept. 14, 10:00a.m. (JST)～~
Tuesday, September 26, 10:00a.m. (JST)

Official Goods

TGS official goods will be on sale again this year. These goods are limited to the venue, so please stop by the official store.
