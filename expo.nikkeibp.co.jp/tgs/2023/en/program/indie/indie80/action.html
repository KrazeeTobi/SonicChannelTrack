It's a Wrap! is a challenging 2D puzzle platformer set in 1980s Hollywood, where you play both the director and the star of action-packed, low-budget films.

All you wanted to do today was shoot a nice little popcorn movie, but everything is going wrong! As the director, you need to synchronize the actors and props that are messing up their timings. Then, as the movie’s star, you need to remember your script and play out the scene… without getting crushed by that giant boulder!

Features
• Director’s Phase.
Grab your megaphone and hop into the director’s chair to puzzle your way through the timing of props, actors, and special effects to make your scene work?and keep your star in one piece.
• Action Phase.

Each level is part of a movie with its own unique genre and look inspired by the biggest blockbusters of the 80s. See if you can spot all the references to your favorite fictional professors of archaeology, space wizards, and retired soldiers on the run from the law!

Siheyuan

  • Puzzle
  • Action
Exhibitors Name :
Fantastico Studio (Italy) 
Platform :
  • Steam
  • Nintendo Switch
Movie Web

SIHEYUAN is a single and local co-op action-puzzle game of few words, but lots of tiles, designed by Italian game studio We Are Muesli (Claudia Molinari & Matteo Pozzi).
Move the falling blocks, pass them, and pass them again to fill the grid, in this minimalistic test of coordination for 1-4 players of any age, inspired by the tradition of "siheyuan", historical courtyards of Beijing, China.

It is no coincidence that SIHEYUAN was originally conceived by We Are Muesli as a site-specific installation at Beijing Design Week 2015, in collaboration with game maker Aran Koning (development) and Francesco Fontana (music and sound effects). Since then, it has been selected for several international awards and showcased at festivals like IndieCade, A MAZE., Milan Games Week, and now Tokyo Game Show!

After years of globetrotting as a playful multiplayer installation, SIHEYUAN is finally coming to home computers and consoles thanks to Fantastico Studio, which will take care of publishing the title on Steam and Nintendo Switch in 2023.
The full game features 13 different levels to unlock, 2 distinct control modes suiting your favorite playstyle, 13 special modifiers changing the game's pace from meditative to hectic, and a unique minimalistic abstract design inspired by traditional Chinese architecture, color theory, opera, and tabletop games.

What kind of game is "MOMIBOSU"?
"MOMIBOSU" is a technique-oriented, high-tempo action game with a "progressive (more advanced) 2D action" theme. It offers an experience of new action game system and high-density decision-making not only for beginners but also for experienced players. Enjoy the world of "MOMIBOSU" an action game that will make you want to try again and again, even if you fail!

What is progressive 2D action?
For example, "MOMIBOSU" uses a peaky control system in which when the player attacks an enemy character, the recoil of the attack pushes the character back strongly. In other words, the key to success in the game is to always anticipate where the character will fly in reaction to the attack. With the power of recoil on your side, you will be able to fly freely throughout the world of "MOMIBOSU".
Many other novel game gimmicks await your challenge.
