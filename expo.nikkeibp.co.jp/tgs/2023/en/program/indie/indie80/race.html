"Challenging Action" meets "Party Game" for up to four players! Introducing our latest game where players navigate using rotating sticks and control their jumps based on stick angles. The unique feature that sets this game apart is the "Jump" mechanic, which also presents its greatest challenge.

Embark on thrilling stages filled with various obstacle patterns, requiring precise stick angle adjustments for jumps, and mastering the art of combining jumps and stick rotations to tackle hooking obstacles. Get ready for an exhilarating experience!

The game offers two exciting modes: "Race Mode" for 1 to 4 players and "Capture the Flag Mode" for 2 to 4 players. Both modes encourage player interaction, guaranteeing an exciting and engaging time for everyone involved.
