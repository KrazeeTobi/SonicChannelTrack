Keynote

Inviting Mr. Pierre-Loup Griffais and Mr. Erik Peterson from Valve Corporation, the company
operating the global game distribution platform "Steam", will deliver the keynote speech. They
will also engage in a cross-talk session with Mr. Wlliam Yagi-Bacon from Capcom Inc. and
Mr. Katsuhiro Harada from Bandai Namco Studios Inc., discussing under the theme of TGS 2023,
“Games in Motion, the World in Revolution”, with a particular focus on the various changes that
have occurred in the global and Japanese game markets in the wake of the Covid-19 pandemic.

Gamera Games Now Tokyo Game Show 2023 Special Program

Gamera Games, a dynamic Chinese independent game publisher with a global reach, is gearing up to take the stage at TGS once again. The "Gamera Games Now Tokyo Game Show 2023 Special Program" is about to raise the curtain on the evolution of gaming. With global sales exceeding 2 million copies, the highly anticipated sci-fi sandbox construction game "Dyson Sphere Program," along with the battle system "Rise of the Dark Fog," will have all new content fully unveiled. In just 2 months, the overwhelmingly well-received young women's development game "Volcano Princess" has broken through 600,000 sales. The classic IP with over 20 years of history, "Xuan-Yuan Sword: Mists Beyond the Mountains," along with news on dozens of games, is set to be revealed! Let's kickstart the games and change the world together!
