TGS2021 ONLINE holds an online party "Indie Party" for exhibitors and registrants of the business matching system, and many people can hold business talks in real time.

We will send an invitation email directly to all business matching registrants, including exhibitors, so please visit the indie party venue.

In addition to the eight finalists who participated in the indie game idea contest "Sense of Wonder Night (SOWN)", SOWN judges from Japan and overseas will also participate.

Key people who can only meet here gather! Please expect us.

Date: October 1st (Friday) 13: 30-16: 00 (JST)
Location: Online venue
Participation: Except for exhibitors, registration to the following business matching system (charged) is required. The registration deadline is September 29 (Wednesday).
