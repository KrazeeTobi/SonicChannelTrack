At TOKYO2021, Online Experience Tour (fee-charged) will be held in collaboration with HIS at the "Trial / Exhibition Area" for press and influencers at Makuhari Messe.
Registrations are accepted on a first-come, first-served basis. Each tour will end when the specified number is reached, register as soon as possible.
In the Online Experience Tour, English-speaking tour guide will actually visit the exhibition booth and convey the heat of the venue to the general public who can not visit the venue by live online broadcasting.
English tour will be held on Sunday, October 3rd.
At each booth, the tour guide will report on the contents of the exhibition and communicate with the booth exhibitor while actually playing. We will also send you questions and comments from tour participants online. The details of the online experience tour are as follows.

Tour Date and Time（Planned）：

October 3, 2021（Sunday）11:00～12:30　（Japan Standard Time: UTC+9）

Tour Contents (planned):

> Introduction of booth exhibition contents by Tour Guide
> Trial play by Tour Guide
> Interview with the exhibitor by Tour Guide
> Q & A from tour participants
