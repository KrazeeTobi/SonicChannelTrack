Beware of heat exhaustion

Severely warm condition is possibly expected in and around the venue. Be sure to keep hydrated and replenish electrolytes (salt) as the crowded conditions both within and outside the exhibition halls can make you prone to excessive sweating.

Prohibited Activities

・Queuing all night for TOKYO GAME SHOW entry is strictly prohibited.

・During the Public Day, please refrain from using selfie sticks and stop using smartphone while walking.

・To prevent accidents, please refrain from running at the exhibition halls. Visitors may be refused entry or ordered to leave from TOKYO GAME SHOW by the Management Office if he/she commit an act likely to be dangerous or disruptive to other visitors or deem to interfere the event operation.

・When watching stage performances at exhibitor booths, cheering, shouting out, call-and-response and jumping are prohibited.

・Please note that a live performance itself may be terminated in the event that acts described above are detected.

・There are some booths that restrict or prohibit shooting and upload of photographs and video. Please follow the instructions of the booth attendants.

・Inside exhibition halls 1-11, please turn off mobile routers and turn off the Wi-Fi functions on smartphones and handheld game consoles. Due to increased use of the mobile devices at the venue, the network condition in the exhibition halls have been worse year by year. There is a limit to the amount of wireless LAN signals and too many devices with wireless LAN may cause harms on the demonstrations and trial game-playing at the venue. If you would like to go to wireless LAN, please go to Wi-Fi hotspot on Central Mall and Esplanade.

Baggage Inspection on Public Day

The inspection of visitors’ baggage will be carried out on both Public Days (September 14(Sat.) and 15(Sun.) ) at TOKYO GAME SHOW 2019 with the objective of ensuring the safety of visitors.

Visitors are strictly prohibited from bringing specified items to the venue (see the list below). We ask that you refrain from bringing large amounts of baggage with you to enable the inspection to proceed smoothly. If you need to bring many items with you (e.g. persons participating in cosplay), be forewarned that the inspection may require time, so we advise you to arrive early. In regards to beverages in plastic bottles, we may ask to confirm the content.

We thank you for your understanding and cooperation for the sake of safety and security in the venue.

Entering the Venue from Halls 9-11 with the Supporters Club Ticket

If you hold a Supporters Club ticket, as a general rule your entrance will be from Hall 1 -8. However, if you would like to enter the venue from Hall 9-11, we ask that you go directly to Hall 11 where you can get in an exclusive line for the Supporters Club. Note, however, that the special goods for Supporters Club ticket holders are not available at this entrance. If you would like to claim your special goods, please go to the information booth around 2nd floor Central Mall of Hall 1-8 after 11am.
