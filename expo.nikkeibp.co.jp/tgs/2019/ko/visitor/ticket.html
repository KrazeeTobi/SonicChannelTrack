All the ticket holders may enter at Priority Entrance.

Those who have the “SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket ” are entitled to use the priority entrance for 20 to 30 minutes after the gate open before general visitors make entry either on July 14 or 15 which you applied at the time of buying tickets.

"SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket" holders will enter the venue either through Hall 1-8 or Hall 9-11. Please note that you may not obtain priority tickets for trial play in case you enter through Hall 9 through 11. The numbered priority ticket will be distributed at Hall 1.

Priority Entrance waiting line starts at the time of the first train at JR Kaihin Makuhari Station. Please follow a guideboard at Makuhari Messe and event staff’s instruction to get in the line of priority entrance.

Please note that you can not move from Hall 9-11 to Hall 1-8 until 10:30.

All the SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket holders must go through the baggage check point prior to the SUPPORTERS CLUB counter. Please note that the waiting line for the SUPPORTER CLUB is different from the waiting line for the general admission.

The priority entry waiting zone for “SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket” holders will be removed after the designated time by the Management Office. After removal, enter on the same way as the general admission ticket holders.

*Priority Entrance is available only with the valid ticket. The Saturday Ticket cannot be used on the Sunday. The Sunday ticket cannot be used on the Saturday. Please check the valid date on your ticket.
