TGS Forum Application Procedure – Keynote, Global Game Business Summit, Expert Sessions

1

Complete Business Day advance registration by the advance registration ticket on the TGS website.

*You have to register to the membership of EventRegist.

2

Register to each session of TGS Forum of your choice on the TGS website.

3

Come straight to the TGS Forum venue with your Business Day Pass hanging around your neck.

*If you bring the printout of your ticket that is attached to the email confirming completion of your advance registration, you will be granted the priority entry.

Application deadline for All sessions is September 11th.

※When a number of applications come to the limit, application will be closed before September 11th.。

※Schedule, content, speakers are subject to change without prior notice.

5G Impact～Will 5G be a “Game Changer”?

“5G” - we are awaiting a launch of the service in Japan towards 2020. Special focus is now placed on “game” as one of the services where this new infrastructure can demonstrate its full capacity. In coming 5G era, how will gaming platforms change and what kind of new experiences can games deliver to users? Will we see new crustal movements then to form new economic zone and create new champions? With 5G era fast approaching, key persons from companies operating related business get together to value the trend.
