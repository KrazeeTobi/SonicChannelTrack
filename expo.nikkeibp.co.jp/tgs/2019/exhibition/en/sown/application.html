  • Deadline

    Friday, June 14 (Japan time) 2019

  • Application

    At first, Entry to Application Form for Selection Booth Indie Game Area

  • Qualification

    Open to all individuals regardless of nationality, age, or occupation (students, game producers, etc.) and to all types of corporate bodies.

  • Prize

    Grand Audience Award : 3,000 USD
    Best Game Design Award : 500 USD
    Best Arts Award : 500 USD
    Best Presentation Award : 500 USD
    Best Experimental Award : 500 USD
    Best Technological Award : 500 USD

  • Screening

    First, Entries will be reviewed for the Selection Booth [Free] of Indie Game Area by the Screening Committee.
    After, Indie Game [Selection Booth] Area Management Office will contact the selected applicants who passed the screening by mid-July.
    SOWN Screening Committee will determine SOWN Finalist [Max. 8 teams] form selected applicants of Indie Game Area [Selection Booth].

  • Schedule

    <1>Deadline：Friday, June14 (Japan Time) 2019

    <2>Results ：

    <Indie Game Area: Selection Booth> mid-July 2019
    <SOWN Finalist> mid-August 2019

  • Notice

    SOWN Finalist will be selected from Indie Game Area [Selection Booth / Free] applicants.
    Applicants who passed the screening will be given a booth in Indie Game Area. They must exhibit at TGS 2019 (Makuhari-Messe, Japan), Sep 12-15 2019.
    Transportation expenses must be paid by the selected exhibitors. No supports will be provided.

    If you are chosen as a SOWN Finalist, you can make a presentation on SOWN Stage [Sep 13, Friday]. Your presentation will go through a simultaneous interpretation.

    SOWN Stage will be video-recorded, and on the streaming in TGS Channel [Official Website]. Game play video of SOWN Finalists will be linked in SOWN Site.
