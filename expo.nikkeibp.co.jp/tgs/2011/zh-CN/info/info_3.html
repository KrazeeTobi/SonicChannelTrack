2011/11/04
Fundraising Report for East Japan Great Earthquake Recovery Relief Effort
in Tokyo Game Show 2011
Under the slogan "Cheering Up Japan with Games," Tokyo Game Show 2011 has hereby completed the fundraising for the recovery efforts in the regions affected by the Great East Japan Earthquake. We express sincere appreciation for the understanding of all of you have supported on the activity. The following statement is the completion report on the fundraising.

Thank you very much for your cooperation.

■Fundraising Total Amount: JPY 2,216,190.-
【Breakdown: Fundraising】
○Charity Auction Revenue (September 17 and 18 Total): JPY1,240,900.-
○Donations (Donation Box by Organizer and Exhibitors, Donation Efforts by Gaming Creators): JPY434,338.-
○Charity Goods Sales (consumption tax not included): JPY540, 952

The above amount of the fundraising conducted by Computer Entertainment Supplier's Association (CESA) has been donated to local governments where has been suffered tremendous damages by the earthquake.
