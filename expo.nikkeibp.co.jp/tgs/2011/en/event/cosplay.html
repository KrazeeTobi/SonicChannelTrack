Public Day Cosplay Event
On Public Days at Tokyo Game Show 2011, the “Yasuragi Mall” (the area between Makuhari Messe Exhibition Halls 3 and 4) will be designated as a cosplay area.
In addition, Cosplay Collection Night @TGS (hosted by Cure livedoor Co., Ltd.) will be held on the event stage in Exhibition Hall 8 on Saturday, September 17, beginning at 6:30 PM. We're looking forward to seeing many cosplayers there.

Cosplay Collection Night @TGS presented by Cure

Cosplay Collection Night features a cosplay fashion show by Japan's largest cosplay community Cure, and a dance party. A fusing of the Cure Cosplay Collection by charismatic cosplayer Tatsumi Inui, the producer of active and enchanting cosplay, and popular DJs and VJs will give birth to a unique space that combines game sounds and cosplayers.

Overview of the Event

Date and time Saturday, September 17, 6:30 to 8:00 PM
Location Makuhari Messe Exhibition Hall 8 Event Stage
Cast Performance cosplayers: Around 100 including KANAME☆, AKIRA and Rayna Wang (China)
DJ: King Goto, tom2
VJ: RawLight
MC: Yunmao Ayakawa
Host Cure (livedoor Co., Ltd.) http://curecos.com/


How to Participate

A numbered ticket is required for participation in Cosplay Collection Night. Tickets will be distributed on Saturday, September 17 in the Cosplay Area of the Yasuragi Mall (the area between Makuhari Messe Exhibition Halls 3 and 4) beginning at 1:00 PM.
* Admission to Tokyo Game Show 2011 will end at 4:00 PM. Participants should be sure to enter Tokyo Game Show 2011 by 4:00 PM.
* Tickets are limited. Distribution will end when the set number has been reached.
* Participation is not limited to cosplayers, but is open to everyone.
* Visit the Cosplay Collection Night ticket desk in the Yasuragi Mall with any questions on the day of the event.

Admission Procedures

After the exhibition closes at 5:00 PM, gather at the assembly point near the Dining Corner in Exhibition Hall 7. At about 6:00 PM, participants will be led by a staff member to the Exhibition Hall 8 Event Stage.
* Your ticket will be checked at the time of admission. Be sure to bring it with you.
* The cosplay changing room in Exhibition Hall 2 will be available until 9:00 PM on the 17th for Cosplay Collection Night participants only.
* Be sure to keep any valuables with you at all times.
* The organizers will not, for any reason, be liable for the loss of or damage to valuables or other personal items brought into the venue.
