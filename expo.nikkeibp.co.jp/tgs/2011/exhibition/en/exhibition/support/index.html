Online Business Matching System

TGS has been launched online business matching system for the purpose of expand business opportunities during show period. Exhibitors can approach and make appointments to other exhibitors and business day visitors prior to the show.

International Business Consultation Area

International Business Consultation Area is a meeting area for people from Japanese companies extending their operations into overseas markets and for domestic and international business persons who want to expand their endeavors in the Japanese market. Consulting experts regarding advice on international gaming business will station at the corner.

Overseas Business Meeting Corner

The corner is open meeting space for exhibitors. Complementary soft drink and interpretation service are provided.
