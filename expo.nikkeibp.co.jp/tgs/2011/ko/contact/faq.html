Exhibition Period

When is the exhibition being held?

September 15(Thu) and 16(Fri) are the Business Days, and September 17(Sat) and 18(Sun) are open to the public, Business Days program is limited to those in the game business and members of Tokyo Game Show.

What time does the exhibition open?

The exhibition opens at 10:00 and closes at 17:00. On Public Days, depending on the situation, the opening time may be revised to 9:30, at the earliest. Please note that, on Public Days (September 17 and 18), the admission gates will close at 16:00.

When will the next exhibition (2012) be held?

The date of the 2012 exhibition has not been decided. It will be announced on the official website and through other means as soon as it has been determined.

Admission tickets for Public Days

How much is a general admission ticket?

The prices for Public Days admission tickets are JPY1,200 for tickets purchased the day of the event (tax included) and JPY1,000 for advance tickets. Admission is free for elementary school children and younger.
Please note that each ticket is valid once for one day only. For more detailed information on admission tickets, see [Tickets] .

When can I buy the advance tickets?

Public Days (September 17 and 18) advance tickets are on sale from July 13 to September 16, 2011. On-the-day tickets are available at the exhibition venue.
For more detailed information on admission tickets, see [Tickets] .

Where can I buy the advance ticket?

Advance tickets are sold on-line at [Tickets] on the official website. Payment can be made by credit card only.

What time do on-the-day tickets sales begin in the morning?

The tickets sale will begin at the departure time of the first train leaving JR Keiyo Line Kaihin-Makuhari Station.

Is a Public Days admission ticket valid for two days?

No. These tickets are valid once for one day only.

* Can I reserve the ticket in advance?

Advance tickets for Public Days (September 17 and 18) advance tickets are on sale from July 13 to September 16. The tickets are available at the online ticketing of this official website.[Tickets]

Are discounted ticket prices available for senior citizens?

For citizens who are 70 years or older, specially discounted tickets (JPY100, tax included) are available from the on-the-day ticket counters at the exhibition venue. However, there are no discounted advance tickets available for senior citizens.

Are group discounts available?

There are no group discounts available for advance ticket or on-the-day ticket purchases.

Business Days

Can anyone be admitted to the Business Days program?

Admission to the Business Days program is limited to those in the game business and members of the press who will be reporting on the Tokyo Game Show. Others, who wish to attend for purposes other than business, persons less than 18 years old, and students will not be admitted. If you are a member of the public, please attend during the Public Days (September 17 and 18).

What do I need to know about the Business Days ticket?

For admission to the Business Days (September 15 and 16), a "Business Days Registration Ticket" or an "Exchange Ticket for Business Day Pass" is required. Pre-registration is required for both the ticket and the pass. Please access [Tickets (advance registration for Business Days)] to pre-register.
"Business Days Registration Tickets" are distributed by exhibiting companies. If you do not have a "Business Days Registration Ticket," please purchase an "Exchange Ticket for Business Day Pass." For details, see [Tickets (advance registration for Business Days)] .

How long is effective for Business Days ticket?

Business Day ticket is valid for two days(September 15 & 16). Since the first day pass and the second day pass are different, please stop at the reception counter at second Business Day and replace to the second day pass.Reception will confirm your name by business card.

Will students of game vocational schools be admitted to the Business Days programs?

No students of any kind will be admitted to the Business Days program. Please come on the Public Days (September 17 and 18).

I did not pre-register before the deadline. Can I still be admitted to the Business Day program?

Please show the items listed below at the admissions counter. You will be admitted after confirming that you work in the game business.

•Passport or other identification with photograph
•Employee ID card (if you do not have your employee ID card with you, you must provide a document that shows you work for your company)
•Two business cards
•Business Days Registration Ticket (if you have a ticket)
* The confirmation process may take a long while. We strongly suggest that you pre-register before coming to the exhibition venue.
* Please note that if we are unable to confirm that you are in the game business, you might not be admitted.

I do not carry business cards. Is there anything I can do?

Please show the following items at the admissions counter.
1. A form of identification, with photograph (driver's license, passport, health insurance certificate, etc.), which shows that you are not less than 18 years old; or your employee ID card with photograph
2. A document that shows you are an employee of a game-related business (front cover of your pay statement etc.)
* If you do not have any of the above documents with you, you may not be admitted even if you have a "Business Days Registration Ticket".
* Please note that we might call your work place for confirmation.

Wheelchair

Are rental wheelchairs available?

Wheelchairs are available in the Makuhari Messe parking lot. However, since the number of wheelchairs is limited, they are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Please contact Makuhari Messe (Tel: +81-43-296-0001) for inquiries. In addition, there are special gates for wheelchair users on the second floor concourse. Please check with the staff at the venue for more information.

Babies

May I use a stroller in the exhibition venue?

We expect the exhibition venue to be very crowded. For safety, we ask visitors to refrain from using a stroller at the exhibition.

Are rental strollers available?

There are no rental strollers available. For safety, we ask visitors to refrain from using a stroller at the exhibition venue.

Are there diaper-changing stations?

There are diaper-changing stations in all women's restrooms at the exhibition venue. There are also diaper-changing stations in the nursing room near the central entrance of International Exhibition Hall 5 on the second floor.

Are there nursing rooms?

There is a nursing room near the central entrance of International Exhibition Hall 5 on the second floor. The following equipment is available in the room: a nursing corner (three separate rooms), a diaper changing station, a washstand, and a water heater for preparing infant milk formula. For directions to the central entrance location, see the map [here].

Cosplay

Is cosplay allowed?

Yes, but there are restrictions, and we have some requests.

Is any cosplay okay?

We do not allow cosplay that goes against good taste or makes people uncomfortable.

May I photograph or record video of cosplay?

Recording video or taking photos is allowed only in certain areas designated by the Management office, including some areas in the Yasuragi Mall (between Halls 3 and 4). It is prohibited in the changing rooms, cloak area, around exhibition booths, busy hallways, etc.

Photos and videos

May I photograph or record video of the exhibition?

With regard to taking photographs or recording video of the exhibiting companies' booths (including any exhibits and companions), the rules set out by each exhibiting company must be observed. When taking photographs or recording video of visiting individuals such as cosplayers, please obtain the individual's prior approval to protect their privacy and portrait rights. When taking photographs or recording video of the event stage, rules will differ depending on the program. Please follow the staff's instructions.

Playing Z-rated games

What is required to play CERO Z-rated games?

If you wish to play Z-rated games (game software that include content for adults aged 18 years and older), you must present a document that shows your age (driver's license, passport, student ID card, etc.).
Your age will be checked at each booth. Please follow the booth staff's instructions.

Visitors from overseas

I need a visa to visit Japan. Can you issue a letter of invitation?

The Tokyo Game Show does not issue letters of invitation for potential visitors from overseas. Please consult your travel agency.

Can I buy a ticket from overseas?

Yes, the tickets are available online. For Public Days tickets, go to[Tickets].
For Business Days tickets, go to [Tickets (Advance registration for Business Days )].
Public Days tickets are also available at the exhibition venue on the day.

Other

Is the distribution of flyers and other maerials allowed at the exhibition venue nearby stations?

Regardless of whether you are acting as an individual or representing a corporation, exhibiting company, or non-exhibiting company, the distribution of flyers and other promotional materials at the exhibition venue or the Makuhari Messe is prohibited. (However, an exhibiting company may distribute materials at its booth. Additionally, exhibiting companies that are allowed in the sponsorship menu are exempted from this prohibition.) Moreover, distribution of materials outside the exhibition venue (i.e., in front of the station or on the streets and sidewalks leading to the exhibition venue) is prohibited according to the Chiba City ordinance.

Are you recruiting part-time staff?

No. We are not recurring part-time staff for the secretariat of the Tokyo Game Show 2011.
