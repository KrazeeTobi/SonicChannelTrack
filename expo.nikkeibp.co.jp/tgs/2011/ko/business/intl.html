Area for businesses expanding overseas and for domestic and international
business persons who want to expand their endeavors in the Japanese market


The International Business Consultation Area (open for Business Days) is a meeting area for people from Japanese companies extending their operations into overseas markets and for domestic and international business persons who want to expand their endeavors in the Japanese market. In cooperation with IMAGICA DIGITALSCAPE, counseling services regarding career developments and recruiting in the game business are provided at the corner.

In cooperation with Business Wire Japan, counseling services regarding advice on disseminating information internationally are provided at the International PR Counseling Corner.

The JETRO Project Corner will be used as a platform for international business talks, and it will be attended by firms that exhibited at the JETRO booth at Game Connection.


Area Map



Description of each corner

International Business Consultation Area

Target: Persons concerned with game development or personnel at firms/individuals involved in the computer entertainment business, particularly in the game industry regardless of nationality, and persons seeking employment or a change of jobs.
Descrption:
  • Matching and counseling service for persons considering development partnerships or corporate alliances
  • Matching and counseling service for persons considering business development while retaining some plan, technology, service, etc.
  • Consulting on corporate strategies for securing personnel or recruiting consulting for persons seeking employment
Fee:
Free of Charge（For the person who wants to consult, highly recommend to have appointments in advance with TGS Business Matching System）
Register on the TGS Business Matching System

Cooperation by: IMAGICA DIGITALSCAPE Co., Ltd.（http://www.dsp.co.jp/）


International PR Counseling Corner

Target:
  • Persons from firms/organizations involved in the computer entertainment business, particularly in the game industry
  • Companies that want to expand their own brand, information or products throughout Japan and overseas
  • Companies that want to make information about themselves known to foreign investors
Description:
  • Matching and counseling service for persons considering public relations or investor relations activities
  • Advice on public relations or investor relations activities for the domestic market
  • Advice on public relations or investor relations activities for overseas markets
  • Advice on how to write and distribute press releases
Fee: Free of Charge

Cooperation by: Business Wire Japan Co., Ltd.（http://www.businesswire.jp/）


JETRO Project Corner

Title: International Business Matching on Casual/Social Game Business
Target: Japanese Game Developers
Description: JETRO invites social game and casual game publishers from North America and introduce the latest local market outlook. In addition, JETRO coordinates the business matching with Japanese gaming developers.
Participating Companies: Big Fish Game, GameHouse, PopCap, 6Waves, airG, Roadhouse

Advance Appointments : JETRO Homepage http://www.jetro.go.jp/
