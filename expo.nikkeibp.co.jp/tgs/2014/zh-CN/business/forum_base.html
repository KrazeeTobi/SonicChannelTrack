【Part1】
Diversified Game Platforms x Globalized Games = Path to Success

Location: Exhibition Hall 8, Event Stage
It's not just home game consoles and PCs; platforms that assume a network connection such as smartphones and cloud-based systems are becoming widespread worldwide. More and more, game developers are compelled to decide which platforms to pour their resources into. In particular, markets such as the smartphone and cloud are becoming essential when organizing a business around the premise of global expansion, due to the ability to receive a direct response. Accordingly, we have invited some senior executives currently enjoying success in the domestic and foreign smartphone game markets in order to discover some clues to profiting in global business.
  • ◆Eiji Takahashi, COO and Representative Director at Alim
  • ◆Haruki Satomi, President, CEO, and Representative Director at SEGA Networks
