September 18th (Thursday) Asia Game Business Summit 2014

Free Attendance Advance registration required
  • ※Entrance to the Tokyo Game Show Exhibition Hall is limited to those who have registered in advance and been approved (advance registration fee: ¥5,000, tax included) or those in possession of a "Business Day Preregistration Application Ticket" (free, advance registration required). Individuals that have only preregistered for the keynote (free) will not be allowed access to the exhibition hall.
13:30～15:00

The scramble for Japanese content:
are Japanese game titles really that attractive?

Location: Exhibition Hall 8, Event Stage Among game titles intended for smartphone/tablet, apps recording top sales in the Japanese market are gaining attention. As the sophistication of smartphones and tablets continues, emphasis is being given to the expressive power and the nature of their games, and we are entering an era in which a quality is being demanded of game apps that is in no way inferior to games designed for home use. In line with this, software publishers from various countries are looking to join up with Japanese corporations that have achieved results in game application development, solidifying their approach toward the Japanese market. Are Japanese game apps really seen as attractive? Key business leaders representing different Asian markets will take the stage and discuss the real power of Japanese content and their visions of the future.
