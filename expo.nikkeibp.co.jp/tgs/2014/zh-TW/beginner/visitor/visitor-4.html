Points for your attention when you leave the venue / re-enter the venue

About re-entry

Even if you leave the venue once, you can enter the venue again as many times as you want during the opening hours until 4:30 p.m. Please ask the staff for a stamp that become visible by illuminating it with a black light when you leave. It may come off when you wash your hands, so please be careful.
When you enter again, have the staff check your stamp at the re-entry entrance.

About Exit Time

Visitors MUST leave the site by 5 p.m. Usually from around 4:50 p.m., the companions at some of the exhibitor's booth all line up to see the visitors off, but please make sure to leave the site by 5 p.m.

Effective days of the ticket

The ticket is only valid for a day. If you want to come on the 2nd day as well, please buy another ticket for the 2nd day.

Are there any events after TGS closes?

On September 20th (Saturday) "Cosplay Collection Night @TGS"（organized by Cure）will be held at the Event Stage of Hall 8 from 6:30 p.m. after the doors close. To see the "Cosplay Collection Night", you need to have a numbered ticket. This numbered ticket will be distributed on September 20 from the time doors open at "Cosplay Area" in Hall 9.
