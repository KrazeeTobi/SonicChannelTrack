How to apply (Entry is Closed)

  • Deadline

    Friday, June 9 (Japan time) 2018
    Friday, June 9 (Japan time) 2018 / Deadline Extended!

  • Application Method

    Fill the entry form

  • Qualification

    Open to all individuals regardless of nationality, age, or occupation (students, game producers, etc.) and to all corporate bodies

  • Results

    Entries selected by the Screening Committe and deemed presentable at the TOKYO GAME SHOW 2018 (up to 8 entries) will be regarded as the final SOWN 2017 presentation games. The nominated applicants will be directly contacted by the Management Office by mid-August 2018.

  • Inquiries

    Sense of Wonder Night 2018 Secretariat : sown@bizevent.jp​​ (Only e-mail inquiries will be accepted)
