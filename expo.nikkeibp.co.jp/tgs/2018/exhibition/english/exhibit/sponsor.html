TGS Forum Sponsorship Session

TGS Forum 2018, a conference which focuses on the latest trends in the game industry, will be held for professionals on Business Day. TGS offers 2 types of sponsorship sessions for exhibitors: “REGULAR Session” (60 min.) and “SHORT Session” (30 min.). These sessions offer the best opportunities to promote your products and services as official programs of TGS.

REGULAR Session

  • Fee:

    JPY972,000

  • Date:

    September 21, 2018

  • Capacity:

    150

  • Time:

    60 minutes

  • Application deadline:

    June 15 (Fri), 2018

  • Registration:

    Advance registration through the TGS official website

  • Equipment:

    Projector, screen and microphones

Services:

1. Announcement and visitor recruitment (Through magazines, DM, e-DM, etc.)

2. Company logo on TGS Forum Program

3. Handle Registration (pre-registration and on-site), issue Forum ticket

4. Provide list of attendees*

5. Provide download service for session documents on post-session (upon request)

6. Live Streaming Service (upon request), Live Recording Service

7. TGS 2018 Business Day invitation tickets (100 sets)
*Attendees’ list is strictly confidential. Adherence to organizer’s privacy policy is required.

SHORT Session

  • Fee:

    JPY540,000

  • Date:

    September 21, 2018

  • Capacity:

    50

  • Time:

    30 minutes

  • Application deadline

    June 15 (Fri), 2018

  • Registration:

    Advance registration through the TGS official website

  • Equipment:

    Projector, screen and microphones

Services:

1. Announcement and visitor recruitment(Through magazines, DM, e-DM, etc.)

2. Company logo on TGS Forum Program

3. Handle Registration (pre-registration and on-site), issue Forum ticket

4. Provide list of attendees*

5. Provide download service for session documents on post-session (upon request)

6. TGS 2017 Business Day invitation tickets (25 sets)

*Attendees’ list is strictly confidential. Adherence to organizer’s privacy policy is required.
