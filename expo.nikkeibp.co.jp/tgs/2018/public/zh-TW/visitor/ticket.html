    Priority Entry

    All the ticket holders may enter at Priority Entrance.

    Those who have the “TGS2018 SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket ” are entitled to use the priority entrance for 20 to 30 minutes after the gate open either on 22nd or 23rd which you applied at the time of buying ticket.

    "TGS2018 SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket" holders will enter the venue either through Hall 1-8 or Hall 9-11. Please note that you may not be obtained priority tickets for trial play in case you enter through Hall 9 through 11. The numbered priority ticket will be distributed at Hall 1.

    Priority Entrance wating line starts at the time of the first train at JR Kaihin Makuhari Station. Please follow a guideboard at Makuhari Messe and event staff's instruction to get in the line of priority entrance.

    Please note that you can not move from Hall 9-11 to Hall 1-8 until 10:30.

    All the SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket holders must go through the baggage check point prior to the SUPPORTERS CLUB counter. Please note that the wating line for the SUPPORTER CLUB is different from the wating line for general admission.

    The priority entry wating zone for "TGS2018 SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket" holders will be removed after the designated time by the Management Office. After removal, please enter on the same way as the genneral admission ticket holders.

    *Priority Entrance is available only for valid ticket. The Saturday Ticket cannot be used on the Sunday. The Sunday ticket cannot be used on the Saturday. Please check the valid date on your ticket.
