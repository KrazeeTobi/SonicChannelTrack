TOKYO GAME SHOW holds its 2nd anniversaries for e-SPORTS competitions "Cyber Games Asia (CGA)" is an international competitions in which top players and teams from different parts of Asia, including Japan.

Sudden Attack Japan-Korea Exhibition Match 2013 - Japan National Finals


Copyright © 2010 NEXON Co., Ltd. All Rights Reserved.
Copyright © 2007 GameHi Co., Ltd. All Rights Reserved.

Date & Time: September 21(Sat) 10:00 - 17:00 (planned)
"Sudden Attack" is a First Person Shooter (FPS) game that is popular in Japan. This is a Japan National Finals match in which Japanese teams will be competing for Japan - Korea Match, which will be holding at Korea in next October.
Event Operated by :

Cyber Games Asia


Copyright © 2012 NBGI

Date & Time: September 22(Sun) 10:30-13:00
Event Operated by TechnoBlood/Coordinated by Rapture Games
One of most popular fighting game "Tekken Tournament 2" in Asia will be holding an exhibition match between Japanese and Singaporean top players in a battle of high-level game play between two nations.
  • Seats are available without reservations with first-come, first-serve basis.

World of Tanks at G-Star - Japan National Final

Date & Time: September 22(Sun) 14:00 - 16:30
One of most popular online third person shooter gaming title in the world, "World of Tanks" representing by War Gaming Japan, the company has just settled in Japan last July 2013. This competion will select Japanese finalist. The winner at this event will be going to G-Star in November 2013 to participate "Japan National Finalist vs. Korea National Finalist".
Event Operated by WARGAMING JAPAN
Event Supported by e-sports SQUARE
Special Site：http://wargaming.e-sports-square.com/wot/tgs2013/index.html
