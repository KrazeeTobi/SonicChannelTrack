About Online Press Registration

The information about online press registration for TOKYO GAME SHOW 2013 (TGS2013) will be announced on TGS2013 official website or via emails after August 2013.
If you wish to receive the information about online press registration via emails, please email TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office Press (tgs2013press@w-az.co.jp) with 1) your company name, 2) media name, 3) your department 4) your name and 5) your contact information.

※If you have received the press releases from TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office Press before,
    you are already registered as the press. You do not have to email us again.
※Press registration is limited to those who fulfill “Registration Criteria” listed as below.
    We appreciate your understanding and kind cooperation.
Print Media, Newswire, & Online Media:
1. Reporter 2. Editor 3. Editorial Writer 4. Correspondent 5. Photographer
Broadcast (TV, Radio):
1. Reporter 2. Director 3. Producer 4. Camera or Sound Crew
* Online media must be commercial news websites updated regularly. Fan sites, personal web site writers,
   designers, editors or creators cannot be registered as press members.
* Advertisement agencies, PR companies, and Sales reps for media outlets are not eligible to register as press members.

※The Tokyo Game Show does not issue letters of invitation for potential visitors from overseas. Please consult your travel agency.
