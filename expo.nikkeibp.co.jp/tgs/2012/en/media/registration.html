We closed online-registration for TOKYO GAME SHOW 2012 on Friday, September 7.
TOKYO GAME SHOW 2012 is asking all members of the media interested in covering the Show, held from Thursday September 20th to Sunday September 23rd at Makuhari Messe, to show references confirming that you fulfill the media regulations listed below at the TGS2012 press counter.
1. business card
2. ID card which includes a photo of your face (employee ID card, passport, driver's license...etc.)
3. recent articles with your name, URL of your company’s page
*You need to bring all references written above.

Registration Criteria

Press members who are able to register or to cover the show are limited to those who are listed below. We ask your kind understanding in this matter.

Print Media, Newswire, & Online Media:
1. Reporter
2. Editor
3. Editorial Writer
4. Correspondent
5. Photographer
Broadcast (TV, Radio) :
1. Reporter
2. Director
3. Producer
4. Camera or Sound Crew

* Online media must be commercial news websites updated regularly. Fan sites, personal web site writers, designers, editors or creators cannot be registered as press members.
*Advertisement agencies, PR companies, and Sales reps for media outlets are not eligible to register as press members.
