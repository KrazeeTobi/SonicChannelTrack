e-SPORTS Competitions「Cyber Games Asia」(CGA）

TOKYO GAME SHOW holds the inaugural e-SPORTS games “Cyber Games Asia (CGA)" as an organizer project from Saturday, September 22 to Sunday, September 23. The CGA is an international competition in which top players and teams from different parts of Asia, including Japan.

In September 22(Sat), 「Sudden Attack Japan-Korea Exhibition Match 2012」will be held. This is a Japan-Korea exhibition match in which two Japanese teams have won national preliminaries and two teams representing Korea will be competing each other.

In September 23(Sun), 「Puyopuyo Student King Deciding Match」is a tournament of student players of the "Puyopuyo Fever" puzzle game to decide the strongest student "Puyoler." This event will be held in cooperation with e-Sports Student Promote Association (eSPA).

In September 23(Sun), 「FIFA2012 Champion Tournament in TGS」will be holding. The top 16 players of the famous soccer title "FIFA 2012" in Japan compete. The event introduces the splendor of the e-Sports through virtual football games that are played and enjoyed in the world with a wide range of generations.

For those who want to experience the fun of e-Sports, please visit the special site at Exhibition Hall 1!

Sudden Attack Japan-Korea Exhibition Match 2012

Date & Time: September 22(Sat) 10:30 - 17:00 (planned)
“Sudden Attack” is a First Person Shooter (FPS) game that is popular in Japan. This is a Japan-Korea exhibition match in which two Japanese teams that have won national preliminaries (online) challenge two teams representing Korea. The Korean players selected are among the best players in the world, and spectators can experience the true essence of e-SPORTS at the event.

Puyopuyo Student King Deciding Match

Date & Time: September 23(Sun) 12:30-14:00 (planned)
“Puyopuyo Student King Deciding Match” is a tournament that brings together student players of the “Puyopuyo Fever” puzzle game to decide the strongest student “Puyoler.” This event will be held in cooperation with e-Sports Students Promote Association (eSPA).
Operated and coordinated by: TechnoBlood/e-Sports Students Promote Association (eSPA)
* Tickets will be available on the day and we request that you come directly to the venue.

FIFA2012 Champion Tournament in TGS

Date & Time: September 23(Sun) 14:30-16:30(planned)
The “FIFA 2012 Championship Tournament in TGS” is an event in which the top 16 players of the famous soccer title “FIFA 2012” in Japan compete. The players, who have all taken part in many domestic and international competitions, will stage a professional-level competition.
Operated and coordinated by: TechnoBlood/Tokyo Game Night!! (TGN)
