Public Days (September 22 & 23)

Adults: Day of the show:
JPY1,200 (incl.tax) per day
*Tickets are available at the DAY TICKET boxes on site.

In advance: JPY1,000 (incl.tax) per day
Children: Elementary school age and under: free of charge
*Public Days Online Tickets≪Until Sep.21 23:59(JST)≫

TGS2012 SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket（end of sale）
Ticket sold in advance only: JPY3,000 (incl.tax) [comes with a special benefits]

Children:Elementary school age and under: free of charge
Business Days (September 20 & 21) Admission on Business Days (Septembe 20(Thu) and 21(Fri) ) are restricted to those people working in gaming industry and those in the press only.
Special Discount Ticket for handicapped person and senior citizens: 100 yen (tax included, day of the show only) * Individuals with disability certificates, wounded soldier certificates, or atomic-bomb survivor certificates
* One caregiver discount ticket as necessary
