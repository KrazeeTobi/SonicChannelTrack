JeSU (Japan eSports Union) STAGE

"JAPAN eSPORTS GRAND PRIX" (Japan eSports Selection Competition) will be holding. T Japan eSports Union (JeSU), a general incorporated association, was newly established to create athletes who will play an active role in the world. It is a tournament with a total prize of 5 million yen and the right to participate in the international e-sports tournament. Enjoy a new esports watching experience with a virtual studio.

Title: eFootball Winning Eleven 2021 SEASON UPDATE, Clash Royale, PLAYERUNKNOWN'S BATTLEGROUNDS(PUBG), Street Fighter V Champion Edition

Tournament Information

https://jesu.or.jp/japanesportsgp/

Tournaments (planned)

Live distribution information https://jesu.or.jp/japanesportsgp/#live

Thursday, September 24, 11: 30-12: 30 (JST/UTC+9)

JeSU Activity Presentation ・ JAPAN eSPORTS GRAND PRIX OPENING

The presentation will be updating the activities of the Japan eSports Union (JeSU). In addition, The event will hold the opening ceremony of JAPAN eSPORTS GRAND PRIX (Japan eSports Selection Competition) in which luxurious guests also participate.

Thursday, September 24, 20: 00-23: 00(JST/UTC+9)

Winning Eleven Japan's Strongest Team Deciding Match 2020 Qualifying

A tournament to decide the strongest team in Japan with the participation of J club and professional gaming team. The top players can get the right to challenge the international competition. It is the qualifying tournament.

Friday, September 25 16: 00 ~ 19: 00 (JST/UTC+9)

Street Fighter V Champion Edition JeSU Selection Tournament Qualifying

A tournament that decides the No. 1 Street Fighter V Champion Edition for athletes within a Japan eSports Pro license. It is the qualifying tournament.

Friday, September 25 20: 00 ~ 24: 00(JST/UTC+9)　

PUBG Japan National Team Selection competition

The PUBG Japan National Team Selection Committee is a selection tournament sponsored by JeSU that decides the Japan national team from the top domestic teams invited by the official domestic professional league PJS sponsored by DMM GAMES. The top players can get the right to challenge the international competition.

Saturday, September 26, 19: 00 ~ 22: 00 (JST/UTC+9)　

eFootball Winning Eleven Japan's Strongest Team Final 2020 Final

A tournament to decide the strongest team in Japan with the participation of J club and professional gaming team. The top players can get the right to challenge the international competition. It is the final tournament.

Sunday, September 27 11: 00 ~ 15: 00 (JST/UTC+9)　

Clash Royale Japan National Team Selection

It is a tournament where professionals and amateurs participate to decide the Japanese national team players of Clash Royale. The top players can get the right to challenge the international competition.

Sunday, September 27 18: 00 ~ 21: 00

Street Fighter V Champion Edition JeSU Selection Tournament Final

A tournament that decides the No. 1 Street Fighter V Champion Edition for athletes with a Japan eSports Pro license. It is the final tournament.
