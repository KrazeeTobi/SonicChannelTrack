Business

TGS Business Matching System

"TGS Business Matching System" allows General and GOLD pass attendees to make an appointment with exhibitors prior to TGS2020 ONLINE.

Exhibitor profiles, as well as the technologies, products and services they can offer, the technologies, products and services they are looking for, and meeting intentions have already been posted onto the system.

By searching through the data, you can make contact or can leave a message with the right person at any of the exhibitors you wish to hold online meetings at TGS2020 ONLINE period.

General and GOLD pass attendees can also register their own company profiles, as well as the technologies, products and services they can offer, and the technologies, products and services they are looking for.Exhibitors can search through this data, and might make contact with you.

Try approaching many different companies, and let 'TGS Business Matching System' help create your business opportunities at TOKYO GAME SHOW 2020 ONLINE."

Business Seminar (On-demand Webinar)

TGS2020 ONLINE provides an online business seminar (on-demand webinar) where you can watch exhibitor's sponsored seminar videos on TGS Business Matching System.
Every participants can watch by selecting the seminar from the tab at the bottom of the TOP screen at TGS Business Matching System.
TGS Management Office will be inform you about the contents as soon as available.

Please note that this seminar is a sponsored session. If you wish to watch the seminar, please be sure to check the ""Information for Viewing Business Seminar"" below.

[Information for Viewing Business Seminar]

Nikkei BP collects and views personal information (first name, last name, email address, work name, work address, work phone number, work department/affiliation company, work position) registered in TGS Business Matching System. The seminar will be provided to the sponsor companies of the seminar (webinar), and after the provision of the third party, the sponsor company will be responsible for managing the seminar.
Those who have viewed the seminar may be requested various information (meetings, products/services, exhibitions/seminar events, etc.) and surveys directly from the supporting companies.
The viewing is only available if the participants agree to the provision of registration information. The name of the sponsoring company is shown in the seminar title.

Indie Night Party Information

"""The Indie Night Party"", an online social exchange for the intimacy of indie game developers, Sense of Wonder Night (SOWN) finalists, and SOWN judges and sponsors, will be holding on 19:30 on September 25(Friday). It is scheduled to be holding for about 2 hours.
Everyone who registered in TGS Business Matching System (participating in online business meetings) can participate. If you wish to participate for the ""Indie Night Party"" and have not registered with TGS Business Matching System, please register TGS Business Matching System.
Exhibitors (free of charge) and people involved in the game business who have purchased a paid pass can register through TGS Business Matching System. There are two types of paid passes: GOLD Pass (27,500 yen/tax inclusive) and General Pass (11,000 yen/tax inclusive) that can participate in online business meetings. The GOLD Pass allows business meets with exhibitors and other paid participants, and the ""General Pass"" allows business meetings only with exhibitors. If you would like to participate, please proceed to the application page from the button below. "
