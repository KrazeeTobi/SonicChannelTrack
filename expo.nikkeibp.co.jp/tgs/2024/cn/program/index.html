主办方的项目​

  2. 主办方的项目​

2024年东京电玩展提供了各种组织者的项目。 你可以在网上以及在真实的会场上欣赏它们。 我们期待着您的参与。​

主旨演讲在新窗口中打开

Keynote speakers on the TGS2024 theme 'Trailblaze the World with Gaming'.

*Speakers will be announced as soon as they are confirmed.

官方视频

Four days from Business Day to Public Day,
Various exhibitors will provide information on the official TGS program. Please check the program schedule and tune in.

日本游戏奖在新窗口中打开

The Japan Game Awards will be broadcasted as an official program in two divisions.

独立游戏项目​

The "Selected Indie 80" showcasing the best indie games from around the world, the "Sense of Wonder Night", and more.

Selected Indie 80

Sense of Wonder Night 2024 (SOWN2024)

Event Stage​在新窗口中打开

EVENT STAGE will feature "Exhibitor's Stage" and other organizer contents such as "Keynote Speeches" and "Japan Game Awards".

*The timetable will be updated as soon as it is determined.

Steam特殊场所在新窗口中打开

Steam special venue will be set up within Steam to introduce exhibitors' game titles.

官方商品

TGS official goods will be on sale again this year. These goods are limited to the venue, so please stop by the official store.
