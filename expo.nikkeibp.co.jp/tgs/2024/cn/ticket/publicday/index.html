Admission Ticket (General Visitors)

1-day admission tickets (junior high school students and older only)

One-Day Ticket 3,000 yen (tax included) for each day
Tickets will go on sale from 12:00 on July 25 (Thu).

  • *Free admission for elementary school students and younger
  • *Sales will end as soon as the planned number of tickets is reached.
  • *Only available in advance online at Event Regist.

TGS2024 Supporters Club Tickets

6,000 yen (tax included) for each day
Sales start : July 25 (Thu) 12:00~

*Only available in advance online at Event Regist.

Special Tickets

100 yen (tax included) for each day

<Ticket Eligibility>

  • Those who present one of the following identification cards: Disability Certificate, War Injury Certificate, or A-Bomb Survivor's Health Certificate.
  • If you need nursing care, one caregiver must accompany you.
  • Age 70 or older. * Please present identification documents that verify your age.
    • *Only Special Tickets will be sold at the venue on the day of the event.
    • *Special Tickets will be sold only for two days, September 28th (Sat) and September 29th (Sun).
    • *After purchasing your special ticket, you will be asked to wait in the dedicated waiting line by Halls 1-8; you will be guided to Halls 9-11 after the doors open.

Public Day Tickets

One-Day Ticket : 3,000 yen (tax included) for each day
<September 28th (Sat) : 10:00-17:00/September 29th (Sun):9:30-16:30>

  • Only available in advance online at Event Regist.
  • All tickets are date-specific. Tickets cannot be changed or refunded after purchase.
  • Sales will end as soon as the planned number of tickets is reached.
  • Tickets can only be purchased online in advance. Tickets will not be sold at the venue on the day of the event.
  • Free admission for elementary school students and younger.
  • For the Family Game Park only, children of junior high school age and under and their accompanying parents or guardians are admitted free of charge. However, junior high school students and older must pay an admission fee to enter other areas, such as the general exhibition, from the Family Game Park.
  • Please note that although visitors can move from Halls 1-8 to Halls 9-11 after the show opens, they will not be able to move from Halls 9-11 to Halls 1-8 during the restricted admission period (until 10:30 on the 28th and until 10:00 on the 29th). Please note that visitors who move from Halls 1-8 to Halls 9-11 after the exhibition opens will not be able to return to Halls 1-8 during the restricted admission period.

    Re-entry will be available until 16:30 on 28th(Sat) and until 16:00 on 29th(Sun).
    Please note that both admission and re-entry times will be earlier on 29th(Sun).
  • On the morning of September 28 and 29, after the doors open, Supporters' Club ticket holders and one-day ticket holders will be admitted in that order.

This year, the TGS2024 Supporters Club Tickets, a premium ticket that combines special merchandise, will be available again.
The Supporters Club Tickets comes with TGS original goods and other benefits such as priority admission for a certain period of time from the opening of the venue in the morning.

  • All tickets are date-specific. Tickets cannot be changed or refunded after purchase.
  • Sales will end as soon as the planned number of tickets is reached.
  • Upon your purchase, you will be linked to Event Regist site where you will be required of membership registration. Credit card is required.
  • Identity verification will be conducted at the venue. If you purchase two tickets for your companion, both of you must be present at the door at the same time.
  • The name of the purchaser will be printed on the ticket. Confirmation will be made on site, so bring personal ID with you. Please be sure to bring your PhotoID such as "driver's license", "passport", "student ID", etc. that can confirm the name of the purchaser.

  • On the morning of September 28 and 29, after the doors open, Supporters' Club ticket holders and one-day ticket holders will be admitted in that order.

    Re-entry will be available until 16:30 on 28th(Sat) and until 16:00 on 29th(Sun).
    Please note that both admission and re-entry times will be earlier on 29th(Sun).
  • Tickets cannot be changed or refunded after purchase.

Special Benefit 3: Priority Entry

Priority Entrance wating line starts at the time of the first train at JR Kaihin Makuhari Station.
"TGS2024 SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket" holders may enter to the venue either through HALL 1-8 or HALL 9-11. Please follow a guideboard at Makuhari Messe and event staff's instruction to get in the line of priority entrance.
Please line up at the priority entrance lane by 9:30 on 28th(Sat) and by 9:00 on 29th(Sun).
Since the entrance start time is different between 28th(Sat) and 29th(Sun), please be sure to line up in the priority entrance lane well in advance.
After the above time, the priority admission lane will be removed and you will be asked to stand in the same admission line as the general admission tickets.
Please note that priority admission is not available from any other entrance lanes.
No refunds will be given even if you do not arrive on time.

Please note that visitors who move from Halls 1-8 to Halls 9-11 after the exhibition opens will not be able to return to Halls 1-8 during the restricted admission period.
  • *Special goods will be handed over at the exchange counter in the priority entrance lane on the Halls 1-8 side; special goods will not be handed over on the Halls 9-11 side.
    After the removal of the priority entrance lane on the Halls 1-8 side and for those who enter from Halls 9-11 side, please exchange your privilege ticket for privilege goods after 11:00 a.m. at the Information Desk located at the 2nd floor central entrance on the Halls 1-8 side.
  • *To receive Special Privileges, you will need to have the ticket even if you are students under primary school age (except infants).
