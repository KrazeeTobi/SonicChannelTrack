What’s
TGS Digital World?

TOKYO GAME SHOW Digital World is the virtual venue based on the world's largest game exhibition, TOKYO GAME SHOW.

As an avatar, you can enjoy various spaces, challenge quests and theaters, traveling around the world.

This year, it takes place on "Game Islands," a game country floating in the sky. The 17 day dream will start, making you love gaming more than before.

WORLD

The islands in the sky,
known as
"game islands".

Last year, many game lovers visited the floating islands, where the residents held festivals.
This year, the gates of the islands will open wide once again at the TOKYO GAME SHOW. One day, you received a letter.
"Come and visit us! A new journey awaits you."
These floating islands are known as the 'Game Islands'.

The inhabitants hosted this festival, adorned with glittering effects, and held up symbols to pray for the growth of gaming.
There is only one condition for entry: you must love games.
With the world map in hand, let's connect with the world.
This year's Game Island is a special stage for adventure, where the world is adorned with festive decorations and ever-changing scenery.
A dreamy 17-day experience awaits you, not to be missed by anyone who loves games.
