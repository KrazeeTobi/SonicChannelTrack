Order up! Fruitbus pulls up at Tokyo Game Show with localised demo ahead of October Steam launch

Krillbite Studio relishes their return to the Tokyo Games Show with Fruitbus ahead of its Steam launch this October 29th.

The one in a melon title will be at the event this week with a brand new trailer and a localised demo to play at the event! Players at the show will be able to take the wheel of Grandma’s Fruitbus, and set off on the adventure of a loaf-time - discovering new friends, new places and new ingredients!

Explore the Gustum archipelago to find new inspiration for your next dishes - from ancient glades to forgotten groves, refine your recipes with fresh ingredients, or try out new ones to keep tummies and souls happy. Unite friends, families and local communities through the power of food!

Change the world, one apple at a time! What will you cook up in Fruitbus?

Fruitbus will be available on Steam on October 29th, 2024 for $25. Heading to the Tokyo Games Show? Find Fruitbus at stand 02-C11 in Main Hall 2 at the Tokyo Games Show this weekend. A localised demo will also run on Steam as part of the event.

For more information on Fruitbus, visit the Steam page. For a review code, please email Rach, Senior PR Manager at rachel@neonhive.co.uk.


About Krillbite Studio

Krillbite Studio is an independent game developer who loves storytelling and creating engaging experiences. The studio was founded back in 2011, when the team were still students, and has since evolved into seasonal developers with a catalogue of award-winning titles. Common to all of our games is our desire to explore the gaming medium, and to inspire both new and experienced players alike. Our most known titles are Among the Sleep, Mosaic, and Sunlight.
