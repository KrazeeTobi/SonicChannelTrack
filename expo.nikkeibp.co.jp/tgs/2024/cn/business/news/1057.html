Discord Brings First-Ever Discordopolis to Tokyo Game Show 2024!

Tokyo, Sep 12, 2024 - Discord Inc. a global voice, video and text communications service, announced its participation at the Tokyo Game Show 2024. For the first time ever, Discord will unveil its Discordopolis booth theme at a gaming conference worldwide. Discord’s Co-Founder and Chief Technology Officer Stanislav (Stan) Vishnevskiy who will be making his first appearance on the Tokyo Game Show Main Stage.

Discord Booth: Discordopolis
At Discord’s booth #07-C03, people can see Discordopolis, the “capital city of gaming”, set in a neon-soaked future where everyone who loves and plays games can connect. Visitors can:
Play video games like Street Fighter 6 on PC or PS5 with friends
Join a legendary challenge with our special guest, Wumpus, from 10:30am-11:30am on September 28th for a chance to win a Wumpus-themed Discord Elgato WAVE:3 microphone
REWARD: Complete the Discord booth quest and receive a Tokyo Game Show special edition Wumpus gift*
GIVEAWAYS: Visit the Discordopolis Konibini to get an exclusive Discord newspaper and merchandise*
*While stocks last

Discord Talks
Main Stage
Join Discord and SIE for a deep dive into the future state of multiplayer gaming.
When: 1-2pm JST, 09/26
Where: Makuhari Messe, Hall 1 Mainstage
Business Stage
Join Discord and Liquid Advertising for an in-depth exploration of Discord Quests to connect your game to 200 million global players.
When: 2-3pm JST, 09/27
Where: Makuhari Messe, SR-05, International Conference Hall Room 301
Follow Discord at Tokyo Game Show
For any fans unable to make it to Discord’s booth or panels at TGS this year, they can follow all of the fun activities by joining its official server at discord.gg/discord-tgs from 09/16.
