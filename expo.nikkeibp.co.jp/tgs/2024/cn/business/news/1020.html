Ad-Virtua, In-Game Advertising Platform, Launches ¥1 Million Giveaway Campaign Exclusively for Tokyo Game Show 2024 Attendees

Ad-Virtua, Inc.(Headquarters: Shibuya, Tokyo; CEO: Seitaro Mizuno), the developer and operator of the in-game advertising platform "Ad-Virtua," is pleased to announce the launch of a special campaign to commemorate its debut at the Tokyo Game Show.

As part of this campaign, the company will offer advertising slots worth a total of ¥1 million advertising credit giveaway to attendees.
Be sure to visit the booth to take advantage of this opportunity.

◆ Date
September 26 (Thu.) & 27 (Fri.) 10:00a.m.-5:00p.m.
◆ Location
Hall 4, Booth 04-C12 (Business Solutions Area)
◆ Contents
Ad-Virtua, in-game advertising platform, enables monetization by displaying ads on in-game monitors, similar to billboard ads in real life.
◆ Details
A setup demo will be showcased, allowing attendees to experience the ease of implementation offered by Ad-Virtua, which enables no-code integration with a simple drag-and-drop feature that allows monitors to be placed in any desired location within the game.
※Advertising slots worth a total of ¥1 million will be provided to those who successfully complete the demo.


■ About Ad-Virtua
Key Features:
1.Non-Intrusive Advertising:
Unlike traditional banner or reward ads, Ad-Virtua’s in-game ads are non-intrusive, ensuring continuous monetization without disrupting gameplay or diminishing user experience.
2.Easy and Smooth Integration:
No scripting required! Simply place monitors anywhere in the game without coding, and start monetizing effortlessly. Ad-Virtua is also compatible with other SDKs, such as AdMob.
