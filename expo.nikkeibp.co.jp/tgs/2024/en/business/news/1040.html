Brook Gaming Unveils Exciting Lineup for Tokyo Game Show 2024

Brook Gaming, a leading Taiwanese gaming hardware brand, is set to showcase its latest innovations at the Tokyo Game Show 2024, held at the Makuhari Messe in Chiba Prefecture, Japan, from September 26 to 29, 2024.

Brook Gaming has established itself as a trusted name in the Japanese gaming market, consistently earning acclaim from the global gaming community. At recent events like EVO Japan and EVO US, Brook Gaming products were prominently featured, used by top players worldwide.

[Introducing the Gen-5X and Gen-5X mini Fighting Board]
The Gen-5X Series Fighting Board is a groundbreaking innovation that fully supports PS5, Xbox Series X, and Nintendo Switch platforms, enabling seamless control of the latest console games. Unlike current market offerings that only support PS5 fighting games, the Gen-5X Series Fighting Board is compatible with all PS5 games.

[Revolutionary Brook Fighter Starburst]
The Brook Fighter Starburst offers a modern take on traditional fighting game controllers. With its sleek, all-digital design and absence of a physical joystick, it delivers an alternative gameplay experience that enhances precision, accuracy, and speed.

[Wingman FGC 2 Converter: Breaking Platform Barriers]
Specifically engineered for the fighting game community, the newly-upgraded Wingman FGC 2 Converter is the ultimate solution for playing any PS5 games without platform restrictions.

[Pocket Auto Catch ND: The Ultimate Companion for Trainers]
The Pocket Auto Catch ND, the latest addition to the Pocket Auto Catch series, is Brook Gaming’s most powerful auto-catch device yet.

[Innovative Cat CPU Plus for The Battle Cats]
Cat CPU Plus presents a modern solution tailored to the beloved mobile game, The Battle Cats. Its customizable auto-attack function enhances the overall gaming experience.
