About Online Press Registration

Online press registration for TOKYO GAME SHOW 2015 (TGS2015) will be announced on TGS2015 official website or via emails after August 2015.
If you wish to receive the information about online press registration via emails, please email TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office Press (tgs2015press@w-az.co.jp) with 1) your company name, 2) media title, 3) your department 4) your name 5) your contact information.

*If you have received the press releases from TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office Press Room before, you are already registered as the press. You do not have to email us again.

*Press registration is limited to those who fulfill "Registration Criteria" listed as below.

Print Media, Newswire, & Online Media:
1. Reporter 2. Editor 3. Editorial Writer 4. Correspondent 5. Photographer
Broadcast (TV, Radio):
1. Reporter 2. Director 3. Producer 4. Camera or Sound Crew

*Online media must be commercial news websites updated regularly. Fan sites, personal web site writers, designers, editors or creators cannot be registered as the press.

*Advertising agencies, PR companies, and Sales reps for media outlets are not eligible to register as the press.

*Tokyo Game Show does not issue letters of invitation for potential visitors from overseas. Please consult your travel agency.
