Japan Game Awards is the event the Computer Entertainment Supplier's Association (CESA) selects and gives awards to outstanding computer entertainment software products for all types of computer entertainment platforms including home video game consoles, personal computers and mobile tools. The event is held to contribute to vitalize the computer entertainment software industry with its growth and better social recognition.
The Japan Game Awards coupled with TOKYO GAME SHOW 2015 will be engaged even more seriously in cultivation of talents and motivating game developers to the greater cause of communicating splendor of computer entertainment to ever larger people.
