TOKYO GAME SHOW holds its 4th anniversaries for e-SPORTS competitions "Cyber Games Asia (CGA)" is an international competitions in which top players and teams from different parts of Asia, including Japan.


 

September 19(Sat) 10:00－16:30

Stage A：Sudden Attack

『SAJCL (Sudden Attack Japan Champions League) 2015 Final Stage』

"Sudden Attack" is FPS (First Person Shooting) game operated by NEXON. "SAJCL (Sudden Attack Japan Champions League) 2015 Final Stage": This offline tournament will be competed among qualified top 4 clans (teams). Enjoy to watch such heated battle!
Top Prize: JPY 2 Million

『Japan vs. Korea Exhibition Match』

This exhibition match is the battle for the pride of representing countries: Japan and Korea. Korean team has defeated and overwhelmed Japan in the past. The level among Japanese teams are reaching nearly equal level with Korean teams.
Event Operated by：

http://sa.nexon.co.jp/campaign/sajcl/2015/final/outline/

Stage B：War Tales of Arado

『TENKAICHI(Best on Earth) Finals TGS2015 Autumn Qualigying Match』

NEXON holds competition event "Tenkaichi(Best on Earth) Finals TGS2015 Autumn Qualigying Match" played by online action RPG "War Tales of Arado". 16 preliminary players will compete for the right of 3 finalists for the final rounds in November.
Event Operated by：

http://arad.nexon.co.jp/campaign/duel_tournament2015_autumn.aspx

September 20(Sun) 10:00－17:00

Stage A：Alliance of Valiant Arms (AVA)

『AVA-re(Violent) ma-SHOW at TOKYO GAME SHOW 2015』

Online FPS "Alliance of Variant Arms" (AVA) is deploying more than 120 countries around the world. This competition is very unique that team (clan) member are voted and selected by vote among endusers. The selected "Dream Teams" forms All-Star Game with high-level gaming. The competitions also include "Over 30 Years Old Only" and "Women Only" games as well.
Event Operated by：

http://ava.pmang.jp/pages/offevents/tgs2015/index

Stage B：LORD of VERMILION ARENA (LoVA)

『LoVA Challenge Cup』

SQUARE ENIX unveils Japanese online PC game "LORD of VERMILION ARENA (LoVA)". LoVA holds its first time offline tournament "LoVA Challenge Cup" at TGS215. Top four qualified teams will be competing for the crown of strongest team.
Event Operated by：

http://squareenix-arena.com/
