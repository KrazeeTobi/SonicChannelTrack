【Part1】

[Introduction] The Current State of the Japanese Computer Game Industry, and the New Computer Entertainment Supplier’s Organization (CESA) (Tentative title)

Location: Event Stage, Exhibition Hall 1
  • ◆Hideki Okamura
    Chairman, CESA

Hit Contents Creation made possible by the unique status of a Creator and Corporate Executive At The Same Time

Location: Event Stage, Exhibition Hall 1 The Japanese game industry is facing ever changing situations lately. The newly re-birthed CESA has been launched on April with far reaching insight into social and smartphone game business on top of current console games. Corresponding to such shift, Tokyo Game Show 2015 has transformed itself into the new mode. Mr. Hideki Okamura, a new chairman of CESA, delineates the trend of the Japanese game industry and Mr. Hiroaki Hino, CEO of Japan’s leading hit maker, will disclose the essence of hit games.
  • ◆Akihiro Hino
    President and CEO, LEVEL-5 Inc.

-- Possibilities Created by the Utilization of Video Streaming Platforms --

  • ◆Jonathan Nagao

  • ◆Daisuke Yokosawa
