Exhibition Areas

Exhibition Areas

General Exhibition Area

General Exhibition Area on digital gaming entertainment. The area introduces digital entertainment-related products/services.

Smartphone Game & Social Game Area

Focusing on games for smart devices (smartphones, such as iPhone, Android and various tablets) and social games for PCs or mobiles.

Game Device Area

The area covers gaming devices such as headphones, gaming controllers, gaming furnitures, and other gaming devices associated with home-use gaming consoles and portable gaming devices.

Game School Area

The area is for the vocational game schools introducing their projects. The area is the must-see for future creators.

Romance Simulation Game Area

A dedicated area for female game players. You can find Romance Simulation games. Related goods will be available here too.

Indie Game Area

This area showcases totally original indie games of all platforms. New ideas and movements will be born from here!

Merchandise Sales Area

The area is an exclusive corner for merchandising of the game-related goods. On Hall 9-10.

Business Solution Area　
※Some exibitors show on Business Day only.

B toB related exhibition area to showcases a variety of solutions for the game business.

Asia New Stars Area　
※Some exibitors show on Business Day only.

This is the area targeting on the emerging Asian companies. The concept is to introduce local "new stellar companies," such as promising game venture companies and tool development companies, to Japanese game industry and the rest of the world.

Exhibition Area (Public Day Only)

Family Area

Area for the game software and related products shared and enjoyed by all the family members. Entertainment stage events and goods sales will be exciting! On Hall 1.
※Admissions for Family Area are allowed only for primary school age or younger and their parents.
Exclusive entrance is available.

Exhibition Area (Business Day Only)

Cloud/Data Center Pavilion

The pavilion for solutions of the cloud / data center to support infrastructure environment of a social game and the network game. The pavilion will be located in Business Solution Area.

Buisiness Meeting Area

Space for business meetings that allows visitors to have serious business talks in a low-key setting during Business Day. Complementary interpretation will be offered with free of charge upon availavility basis.
