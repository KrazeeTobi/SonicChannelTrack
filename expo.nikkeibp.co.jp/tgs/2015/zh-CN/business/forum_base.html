September 17th (Thu) Keynote Speech

Free Advance registration required
  • ※Entry to the Tokyo Game Show Exhibition Hall is limited to those who have registered in advance (advance registration fee: ¥5,000, tax included) or those in possession of a "Business Day Registration Ticket" (free, advance registration required). Individuals that have only registered to the keynote (free) will not be allowed access to the exhibition hall.
10:30～11:25
【Part1】

[Introduction] The Current State of the Japanese Computer Game Industry, and the New Computer Entertainment Supplier’s Organization (CESA) (Tentative title)

Location: Event Stage, Exhibition Hall 1
  • ◆Hideki Okamura
    Chairman, CESA

Hit Contents Creation made possible by the unique status of a Creator and Corporate Executive At The Same Time

Location: Event Stage, Exhibition Hall 1 The Japanese game industry is facing ever changing situations lately. The newly re-birthed CESA has been launched on April with far reaching insight into social and smartphone game business on top of current console games. Corresponding to such shift, Tokyo Game Show 2015 has transformed itself into the new mode. Mr. Hideki Okamura, a new chairman of CESA, delineates the trend of the Japanese game industry and Mr. Hiroaki Hino, CEO of Japan’s leading hit maker, will disclose the essence of hit games.
  • ◆Akihiro Hino
    President and CEO, LEVEL-5 Inc.
11:30～12:50
【Part2】

The New Era of Computer Game Marketing
-- Possibilities Created by the Utilization of Video Streaming Platforms --

Location: Event Stage, Exhibition Hall 1
New Strategy by Twitch and Amazon For Game Live Streaming and Business Development of Smartphone Apps
  • ◆Jonathan Nagao
    Director, Appstore, Amazon Japan K. K.
Game Platform Strategy Where The Real and The Net Are Integrated
  • ◆Daisuke Yokosawa
    Director, Member of the Board / Chief Creative Officer, DWANGO Co., Ltd.
The New Experience and Community Realized by YouTube and Video Creators
  • ◆Ryan Wyatt
    Global Head of Content for Gaming, YouTube (U.S.)
The video streaming platform gains the widening attention as the ever effective tool to promote the game contents globally. It is the best communication method to share lively feeling of real time video. Three major video streaming platformers who focus on the live coverage of game plays introduce their whole business concept including service options and case studies.
