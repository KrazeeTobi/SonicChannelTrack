TGS2015 Supporters Club Ticket

◆SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket BENEFITS

Special Goods 1: TGS Original T-shirt

All the ticket holders can gets TGS2015 SUPPORTERS CLUB original design T-shirt!

Pick your size when you order the ticket. (size=S, M, L, XL)
Size (length, width) *unit: cm
S (65, 47.5), M (68, 50), L (71, 52.5),
XL (74, 55)

Special Goods 2: Original Pin Badge

All the ticket holders can get TGS2015 SUPPORTERS CLUB original design badge!

Priority Entry

All the ticket holders may enter at Priority Entrance.

Those who have the “TGS2015 SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket ” are entitled to use the priority entrance for 20 to 30 minutes after the gate open either on 19th or 20th which you applied at the time of buying ticket.

"TGS2015 SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket" holders will enter the venue either through Hall 1-8 or Hall 9-11. Please note that you may not get priority tickets for trial play in case you enter through Hall 9 through 11. The numbered priority ticket will be distributed at Hall 8.

Priority Entrance wating line starts at the time of the first train at JR Kaihin Makuhari Station. Please follow a guideboard at Makuhari Messe and event staff's instruction to get in the line of priority entrance.

Please note that you cannnot move from Hall 9-11 to Hall 1-8 until 10:30.

All the SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket holders must go through the baggage check point prior to the SUPPORTERS CLUB counter. Please note that the wating line for the SUPPORTER CLUB is different from the wating line for general admission.

The priority entry wating zone for "TGS2015 SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket" holders will be removed after the designated time by the Management Office. After removal, please enter on the same way as the genneral admission ticket holders.

* Priority Entrance is available only for valid ticket. The Saturday Ticket cannot be used on the Sunday. The Sunday ticket cannot be used on the Saturday. Please check the valid date on your ticket.

Notice
  • * To receive Special Goods, you will need to have the ticket even if you are students under primary school age (except infants).
  • * No exchange or refund of T-shirt and Badge is available. Please review your size selection before submitting the order.
  • * 11:00 am or later:TGS2015 SUPPORTERS CLUB Ticket holders can pick up Special goods at Information Counter at 2nd Floor in front of HALL 5.
  • * Entry from HALL9-11:There is No exchange counter for TGS2015 SUPPORTERS CLUB Special goods. To exchange the Special goods, pick them up at Information Counter at 2nd Floor in front of HALL 5. Pick-up service begins at 11:00 am.
  • * No refunds or exchanges are available.
  • * Valid for One day only.
