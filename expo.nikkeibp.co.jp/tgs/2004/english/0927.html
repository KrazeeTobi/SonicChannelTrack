TOKYO GAME SHOW 2004 Report
Theme: A Brand New Sensation for Everyone in the World
A big success with about 160,000 visitors over 3 days—far exceeding last year!

TOKYO GAME SHOW 2004 was held from September 24 (Fri.) through 26 (Sun.) under the theme “A Brand New Sensation for Everyone in the World” organized by Computer Entertainment Supplier’s Association (CESA), and co-organized by Nikkei Business Publication, Inc. The show was very well attended over 3 days with a total 160,096 visitors. The number of visitors exceeds that of the last year’s show by approximately 10,000 people.

Total 117 exhibitors—the highest number ever—joined this 14th Tokyo Game Show. Game Software companies, game software development tools & middleware companies and game colleges from Japan, Korea, Taiwan, the United States, and China exhibited more than 500 new video game software and game-related peripherals.

Many hot new video games and game software for the new portable platforms which will debut soon or mobile phones with improved game performance drew attention in the venue. More over, partly because of increased number of overseas exhibitors, there were many overseas visitors including the press in the venue. Partly due to enrichment of the Kids’ Area, many family visitors also visited the venue on public open days.

The next Tokyo Game Show will be held from September 16, Friday, to September 18, Sunday, 2005.
