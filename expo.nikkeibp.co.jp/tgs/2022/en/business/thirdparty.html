Provision of Personal Information to Overseas Exhibitors and Sponsors to Third Parties

Sponsorship Session Sponsor

Freeverse.io

Freeverse.io is located in Spain, a country with a privacy protection system and a GDPR Article 45 Sufficiency Certification. Freeverse.io has taken measures in accordance with the "Eight Principles of the OECD Privacy Guidelines".

NetEase

The People's Republic of China, where NetEase is located, has a system for the protection of personal information that was promulgated in 2021. Please refer to the National People's Congress website for the Personal Data Protection Law. http://www.npc.gov.cn/npc/c30834/202108/a8c4e3672c74491a80b53a172bb753fe.shtml

NetEase has taken measures in accordance with the "Eight Principles of the OECD Privacy Guidelines".

PolkaFantasy

Hong Kong, where PolkaFantasy is located, has a Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, 1996. For more information, please refer to the following website

https://www.elegislation.gov.hk/hk/cap486!en-zh-Hant-HK.pdf?FROMCAPINDEX=Y

PolkaFantasy has taken measures in accordance with the "Eight Principles of the OECD Privacy Guidelines".

Moloco

The United States of America, where Moloco is located, has a personal data protection system and is a member of APEC's CBPR system; the United Kingdom has a personal data protection system and is a recipient of a sufficiency certification under Article 45 of the GDPR; the Republic of Korea has a personal data protection system and is a recipient of a sufficiency certification under Article 45 of the GDPR; and is a member of APEC's CBPR system.

The People's Republic of China, where Moloco is located, has a system for the protection of personal information that was promulgated in 2021. Please refer to the National People's Congress website for the Personal Data Protection Law.http://www.npc.gov.cn/npc/c30834/202108/a8c4e3672c74491a80b53a172bb753fe.shtml

Moloco has taken measures in accordance with the "Eight Principles of the OECD Privacy Guidelines".
