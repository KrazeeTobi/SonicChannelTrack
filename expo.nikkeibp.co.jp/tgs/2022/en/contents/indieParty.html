SOWN Pre-Party

The online networking event, which has been held after SOWN for the past two years, will be held this year as the SOWN Pre-Party. In preparation for the next day's presentation, we will introduce the profiles of the SOWN finalists to create excitement, while also conveying the fun and uniqueness of indie games.

We will prepare a place where you can interact with SOWN finalists, “Indie Game Selected Exhibit” exhibitors, SOWN judges, etc. using the online meeting Gather.

  • ◆Eligibility Requirements

    ・All applicants for the 2022 Indie Game Selection Booth

    ・SOWN Judging Committee/Stakeholders

    ・TGS2022 exhibitors

    ・ Business Day Gold Pass purchasers

    An invitation to the Gather venue will be sent to the registered email address.

    ◆ Date and time

    September 15, 2022 (Thursday) 18:00 OPEN
