Business Matching

"TGS Business Matching System (Business Meeting)"

TOKYO GAME SHOW 2022 will be holding a business meeting matchmaking scheme in the event. We are now accepting applications for paid participants.

By purchasing a "GOLD Pass" or "General Pass," you will be invited to register in "TGS Business Matching System" and will be able to have business meetings with TGS2022 exhibitors. This is a valuable opportunity for those involved in the game business that was previously available on the TGS Business Days.

Business meetings at TGS2022 will be conducted using TGS Business Matching System, which will be available both in real(in-person) and online for four days during the exhibition. Exhibitors' introductions, the technologies, products, and services they can offer, and the technologies, products, and services they are seeking are posted on TGS Business Matching System, and visitors can search for this information and contact the person in charge.

In addition, TGS Business Matching System has a "Recommendation" function, which picks up the most suitable business partners based on the profile information of registered exhibitors, etc., and displays them in the system. This function increases the probability of business negotiations.

As in previous years, TGS2022 will be attended by many exhibitors from Asia and other countries, making it possible to conduct international business meetings that would otherwise be difficult to realize.

Look forward to seeing you there.

GOLD Pass - access to all exhibitors and attendees

TOKYO GAME SHOW 2022 offers "GOLD Pass" that allows you to have business meetings not only with exhibitors but also with other attendees, giving you access to over 300 exhibitors as well as the opportunity to interact with other attendees who are willing to have business meetings with you.
