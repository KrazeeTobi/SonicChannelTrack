官方商品

TGS2022 official goods are based on the main visual drawn by KUKKA, with a lineup of T-shirts, towels, mouse pads, clear files, and more. This year, we have increased the number of products such as masks with original designs, and have prepared a total of 37 items including variations in color and design. The "TGS Official Shop" where you can purchase official goods will be set up on the north side of Hall 4 and on the Central Mall on the 2nd floor (in front of Hall 5). Payment can be made by credit card (VISA, Mastercard) in addition to cash.
