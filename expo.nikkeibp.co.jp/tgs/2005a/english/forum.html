TGS Forum 2005, the conference for industry involved persons
"A Developers Session - CEDEC Premium" - Special Offer for International Attendees

TGS Forum 2005, held on September 16 (Fri), the opening and business day of Tokyo Game Show 2005, will offer a number of sessions for those in game-related industries, focusing on the latest technological and business trends in the game industry.

The keynote speaker is going to be Mr. Robert J. Bach, Chief Xbox Officer and Senior Vice President, Home and Entertainment Division, Microsoft Corp. and Mr. Satoru Iwata, President of Nintendo Co., Ltd.

For those of you who are from overseas, we have "A Developers Session - CEDEC Premium, "a seminar equipped with Japanese-English simultaneous interpretation.

"CEDEC Premium" is a session offered in conjunction with CEDEC, Japan' s largest seminar for game developers organized by CESA. Supported by the International Game Developers Association (IGDA), an international NPO for game developers, the session will have two speakers from Electronic Arts Inc. (U.S.) and, under the theme of "The next-generation development strategies of Electronic Arts - what makes the company the world' s largest publisher," explore the future course of this increasingly globalized industry.

We have special offer of 7,000 yen (general advance purchase: 8,000 yen) for international attendees.
