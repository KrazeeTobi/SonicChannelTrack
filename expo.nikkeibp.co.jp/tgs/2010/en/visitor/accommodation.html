Access to Makuhari Messe

By train 30 minutes from Tokyo Station to Kaihin Makuhari Station on the JR Keiyo Line
By bus 30 minutes from Narita Airport
40 minutes from Haneda Airport
15 minutes from Makuhari Hongo Station

For further information,
http://www.m-messe.co.jp/en/access/index.html
