Title TGS Forum 2010
Dates September 16 (Thu) - 17 (Fri), 2010
Venue Makuhari Messe [official site]
Organizered by Computer Entertainment Supplier's Association (CESA)
Co-organized by Nikkei Business Publications, Inc.
Open Positions Speaker / Panelist to participation in the TGS Forum 2010
Number of Positions Available A few
Qualification Requirements Those involved in the computer entertainment fields
Speech Subject Games in general, online / mobile entertainment, finance / market analyses, development technology, and other computer-entertainment-related subjects
(Presentations for the purpose of publicizing a particular company are ineligible for application)
How to Apply Please e-mail the following materials to the Management Office below:
  1. Profile, including your name, name of company/organization, address, and phone number where you work, email address, and brief biography
  2. Theme and summary of your speech (written in Japanese or English in 1 sheet of A4-size paper)
  3. Other material related to the speech theme (article / paper, speech material, etc.)
Application Deadline must be received by the Management Office by May 28 (Fri.), 2010
Announcement of Result The applicants who passed the selection will be contacted by the end of June.

†There will be no remuneration for the speeches.
†Travel and accommodation expenses will not be borne by the Management Office.
†The speaker pass for the TGS Forum 2010 and the VIP pass for the expo will be provided to the selected speaker.
†The selected speakers will be asked to agree to the TGS Forum Speech Policy.
