Call for TGS2023 Official Influencer

TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office are looking for "TGS2023 Official Influencers from Asia-Pacific" to represent their countries and regions, especially in Asia and Oceania, and invite them to attend this year's TOKYO GAME SHOW at Makuhari Messe.
