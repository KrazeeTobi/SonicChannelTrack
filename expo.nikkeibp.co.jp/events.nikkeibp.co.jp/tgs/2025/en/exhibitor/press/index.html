Press Registration

The "press registration" for media representatives who wish to cover the TGS2025 venue will begin in early August.

  • Registration methods will be announced separately.

Prior to the start of press registration, media representatives who wish to receive press releases and other information about the Tokyo Game Show should contact the following.

Tokyo Game Show PR Office (Sunny Side Up Inc.)

E-mail：tgs2025_prssu.co.jp

  • Please write "TGS2025: Request for coverage".
  • Please include information that shows you meet the following coverage guidelines, such as your affiliation and media name.

After checking the contents at the secretariat, we will proceed with the press release distribution and other procedures.

  • If you have already received a press release from the secretariat, there is no need to contact us.
  • As of February 6, 2025, the contact details for the Tokyo Game Show PR Secretariat have changed.

Press Coverage Rules and Regulations

Press pre-registration for TOKYO GAME SHOW 2025 is limited to those who fall under any of the following categories ① through ⑥ below.

  • ①Editors, reporters, and photographers of newspapers and publishing companies
  • ②Editors, reporters, and staff members of Internet news or information website production companies established by corporations
  • ③Television program production companies, reporters, and filming staff, including BS, CS, and cable TV
  • ④Radio program production companies, reporters and staff
  • ⑤Writers and cameramen who have a contract with the above editing/production companies or editorial departments for news gathering
  • ⑥Filming models and performers involved in news gathering and program production
  • Advertising agencies, business and advertising sales departments of newspapers, publishing companies, TV stations, and radio stations, PR firms, research firms, securities analysts, and those who distribute or post on SNS or video channels are not eligible.
  • TGS Press Room reserves the right to refuse registration for coverage if it determines that the applicant does not meet the coverage rules.
  • In addition, influencer registration is available for those who distribute and post on social networking sites and video channels and meet the conditions set by TGS (fees may apply).
    The conditions for influencer registration and the registration start date will be announced on the official website around early August.

Download Center

You can download the TGS2025 logo, photos, etc. from the "Download Center".

Media representatives who do not know their login information for the Download Center should contact the Tokyo Game Show PR Office (tgs2025_prssu.co.jp) by email.

Please include information that shows you meet the media coverage regulations, such as the name of the media outlet you are affiliated with or your position.
We will check the details you provide and then send you your login information.
