SELECTED INDIE 80 (1.5m in width X 2m in depth)

  • Fee FREE (Screening Required)
  • Entry Closing May 16 (Fri.) JST 23:59, 2025

TOKYO GAME SHOW(TGS) 2025, with the support of our sponsors, will set up "SELECTED INDIE 80" slot where indie game developers can exhibit their games in real for free at the Indie Game Area.

  • Only individuals and organizations that have been selected through a screening process after submitting the required application can exhibit at SELECTED INDIE 80.
＜Conditions to submit SELECTED INDIE 80＞
  • ●Only those who can exhibit at Makuhari Messe for 4 days
  • ●For annual sales of the developer (individual or corporate) of the submitted title:
    Company: Less than USD 500,000.-
    Individual: Less than USD 10,000.-
  • ●If the developer of the submitted title is a corporation, it must be fully independent in terms of capital.
    [For example, you cannot apply if you are a subsidiary of a major game company.]
  • The application process is different from the regular exhibition.

In order to create opportunities for indie games to attract more attention, TOKYO GAME SHOW will once again provide a framework for indie game developers, regardless of whether they are corporations or individuals, to exhibit at TGS for free. This project is made possible through the support of our sponsors. If you are a game platformer, game publisher, PC/monitor company, etc., we would appreciate it if you could consider sponsoring us.
Please refer to Guide to Indie Game Project Sponsorship for the details of the basic decoration.
