The 8th Creators' Training Symposium


　We regularly hold the Creators Training Symposium for game school teachers on the first day of Tokyo Game Show.
At this 8th symposium, in the first part, we welcome Mr. Yoshiki Okamoto from CAPCOM for a lecture about his experiences, how he overcame difficulties on his business or how he refined his skills. In the second part, lectures about recruitment in the field of program and graphic are given. Examples of evaluation standards for written, skill, and interview test will be introduced. We will welcome, Digital Media Lab.,inc., FromSoftware,Inc. and KONAMI CORPORATION as the speakers.
We welcome the press to cover the fist part of symposium.
Date & Hours October 12 (Fri.) 13:30 ~ 16:00
Place Makuhari Messe International Conference Hall 3F Room#301
Fee Free for members / \8,000 for the general (Requested pre-registration)
Contents The 1st Part
"To attain the goal ~ from my experience~"
Speaker: Mr. Yoshiki Okamoto, CAPCOM
