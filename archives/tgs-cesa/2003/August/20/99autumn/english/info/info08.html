Daily Report : September 18 (General Admission Day)

The visitors experienced the latest games with the latest digital technology by trying the demo games or by watching the images on the large screen. Many exhibitors held booth events, including talk-shows, concerts. Visitors appeared to really enjoy these events.

"NAVI PLAZA", so called communication zone, was established in the east side of the main entrance. The information about exhibitors was available there, and the stage events and booth events were displayed on the large screen. This area was also used as rendezvous point.

8 schools exhibited at the School Corner, drawing youngsters who are wishing to be software creators in the future. Each school displayed their students' works and conducted questionnaire surveys.

The traditional event, "CESA Charity Auction & Lottery" was held at the event stage. The bidders were very excited to get the goods at a premium. The proceeds will go to the United Nations Children's Fund.
