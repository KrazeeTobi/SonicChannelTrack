Information for Exhibitors

Guidelines for Exhibitors
 * Overseas manufacturers that are not members are welcome to exhibit (Japanese manufacturers must be CESA members, however).

No. of Booths Available
Minimum No. of Booths: Approx. 1,500 Booth size: 3 m x 3 m (9 m2)

Exhibition Regulations
 * Products that may be deemed to encounter problems in terms of ethics and/or copyright must be submitted beforehand in the form of video footage or the software itself for approval.
* Only products handled by the company applying to exhibit may be exhibited.
* Products other than newspapers and magazines may not be sold outside the Product Sales Area. Sales of game software, including advance sales, must not take place under any circumstances.
* Neither the organizer nor the venue shall be held responsible for theft or damage to exhibits.
* Detailed regulations concerning construction and other matters will be provided at the exhibitors' briefing on January 18(Thurs.). Otherwise please refer to the Exhibition Guideline Manual.

Booth Allocation
 * Due to venue space restrictions, the organizer reserves the right to make the final decision on the number of booths allocated.
* Overseas exhibitors will exhibit in the Overseas Exhibitors' Area specified by the secretariat.

Inclusions in Exhibition Booth Charges
Inclusions in these charges can be collected from any of the application areas.

2.Cost of electrical work to provide a single phase main line
(2 100 V AC, 15A single phase lines per booth)
* Does not include cost of electricity used and cost of additional main line electrical work
3.Admission tickets
4.Exhibitor badges and publications
* Will be explained in detail at the exhibitors' briefing
