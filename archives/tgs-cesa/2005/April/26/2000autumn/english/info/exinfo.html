Exhibitor's Information


The 9th time the show has been held, is expected to be the scene of unprecedented excitement as an event bringing everyone closer to the new future of computer entertainment.
We hope you utilize Tokyo Game Show as a starting point for expanding into the global market and as an opportunity to communicate with users.

No. of Booths Available
Approx. 1,500 booth unit
Booth size: 3 m x 3 m (9 m2)

Booth Allocation
Due to venue space restrictions, the organizer reserves the right to make the final decision on the number of booths allocated.
Overseas exhibitors will exhibit in the Overseas Exhibitors' Area specified by the secretariat.

Exhibition Areas
Overseas Exhibition Area Area for overseas software manufacturers. *Exhibition Booth Charges 260,000yen per booth (plus 5% tax)

For more information regarding exhibiting Tokyo Game Show, Please inquire Tokyo Game Show Secretariat info@cesa.or.jp
