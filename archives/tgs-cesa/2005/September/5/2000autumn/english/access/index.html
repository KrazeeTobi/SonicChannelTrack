Venue
MAKUHARI MESSE (Nippon Convention Center / Mihama-ward, Chiba City)
>> http://www.m-messe.co.jp/index.html

Access by Train
*JR Keiyo Line Kaihinmakuhari Station (5min. walk)
[30 min. from Tokyo station to Kahinmakuhari station, 12 min. from Soga station]
*JR Soubu Line Makuharihongo Station (Route Bus for Makuhari Messe or Kaihinmakuhari station, approx. 15min.)
[40min. from Akihabara station to Makuharihongo station, 15min. from Chiba sta.]


Access by Car
*Approx. 40 min. drive from downtown Tokyo through Capital Express Way
From Wangan Narashino IC of Higashi Kanto Express Way
or Makuhari IC of Keiyo Express Way [Approx. 5 min. to Messe]
*Approx. 30 min. from Narita area
From Wangan Chiba IC of Higashi Kanto Express Way [Approx. 5 min. to Messe]
Parking
*Messe Big Parking Lot: 5,500 cars *Messe Mall Parking Lot: 1,100 cars

There are some City Hotels around Messe and the limousine buses are available
directly from Narita Airport.
