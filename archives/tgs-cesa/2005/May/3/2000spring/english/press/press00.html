TOKYO GAME SHOW 2000 SPRING
Press/Media Registration
Welcome to TGS 2000 SPRING Press/ Media Registration. We invite qualified journalists from print, broadcast and online media to register for a complimentary media badge that provides:
*Exhibit floor access
*Use of TGS 2000 SPRING Press/ Media Center facilities

Following press team would to be allowed to report ;
  1. Reporter / writer / cameraman from newspaper publishers / publishing conpanies
  2. On-line web site production companies
  3. TV program production companies
  4. Reporter / writer / cameraman to be entrusted the company above.
  5. Reporter / performer for programs.


How to register

TGS 2000 SPRING Press/Media Registration is open to working journalists with commercial news organizations only.

  • To obtain a media badge for TGS 2000 SPRING, please inquire for registration request form by e-mail(info@cesa.or.jp).
    The press registration request form cannot be considered as a confirmed press registration.
    You will be asked to fill out the registration request form and present back-up documentation to verify your editorial media status. Please make sure you send the information requested back on time.
  • If you are entrusted by publishing companies, TV stations etc., the application should be made by your client.
  • Mass media / advertisement agencies / PR companies working for exhibitors do not need to register as the press. Please use exhibitors' pass offered from your client or purchase a current ticket.
  • Once we have received your registration form with credentials via fax, you will receive a confirmation sheet via fax in approximately confirming your registration.
  • The management of TGS 2000 SPRING reserves the right to deny any applicant a press/media badge.


Deadline for application: March 20, 2000(Mon)
