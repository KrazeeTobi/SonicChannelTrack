Overview

Name TOKYO GAME SHOW 2014
Dates
Business Day
10:00-17:00, September 18 (Thursday) & September 19 (Friday), 2014
※Admission on Business Day is restricted to those people working in the game industry and those in the press.
※Advance registration for Business Day will start around early August.
Public Day
10:00-17:00, September 20 (Saturday) & September 21 (Sunday), 2014
※Entry for general visitor will be closed on 16:00.
※Opening time may be shifted earlier for some types of ticket
Venue Makuhari Messe  ［official site］
Organizer Computer Entertainment Supplier's Association (CESA)
Co-organizer Nikkei Business Publications, Inc.
Admission Fees
Business Day (September 18 and 19)
Business Day Ticket sold in advance: 5,000 yen
※Please be noted in advance that screening will be conducted to confirm attendees' qualification to buy Business Day ticket on site.
Public Day (September 20 and 21)
Adults: Advance Ticket/1,000 yen per day, On-the-day Ticket 1,200 yen per day
※Tickets are available at the DAY TICKET boxes on site.
※Ticket sold in advance: 1,000 yen per day.
※Children (elementary school age and under): free of charge.
