The TGS Business Matching System is an appointment system for Business Day visitors to talk business with exhibitors during the show. The system is free and available for anyone to use, so please take advantage of this opportunity.

Exhibitor company profiles, as well as the technologies, products and services they can offer, and the technologies, products and services they are looking for have already been posted onto the TGS Business Matching System. By searching through this data, you can make contact with the right person at any of the exhibitors.

Visitors can also register their own company profiles, as well as the technologies, products and services they can offer, and the technologies, products and services they are looking for. Exhibitors can search through this data, and might make contact with you.

Try approaching many different companies, and let the TGS Business Matching System help your business.

Advantages of using the TGS Business Matching System

  • 1. Anyone can use the system for free.
  • 2. You can make appointments prior to the show, and then hold business talks during the show. Saving on time, costs and effort, you will be able to achieve business outcomes more efficiently.
  • 3. Meeting booths are free to use and can be booked in advance.

Register on the TGS Business Matching System

Notes

1. To use one of the meeting booths during the show, you will need either an [Entry Ticket (Public Day)] or an [Entry Pass (Business Day)].
2. Both parties need to agree for an appointment to take effect. You should be aware that not all appointments may be accepted.
3. When registering for the first time, your User ID and Password will be sent to the email address you register. Please be careful not to lose them.
