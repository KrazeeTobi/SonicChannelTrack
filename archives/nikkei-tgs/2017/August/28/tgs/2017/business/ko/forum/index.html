ThemeThe Future of e-Sports in Japan

e-Sports has stirred a global movement. It is rumored to be one of new Olympic events while it has been established as pro-sports game with many pro-leagues on the rise. ThemeDigital distribution of Video Game

Global companies in the game industry have been shifting to digital distribution year by year. Although in Japan digital distribution is at the nascent stage, the portion of digital distribution in major Japanese publishers has increased steadily.
Given the situation, this panel invite major platformers and game publishers to discuss the current status and future trend of digital distribution.
