Press Registration for non-Japanese Media

Welcome to TOKYO GAME SHOW 2007. TGS Press Registration is only open to working journalists with commercial news organizations. All the press covering news on TOKYO GAME SHOW 2007 is required to register in advance.
The registration deadline for press coverage: Monday, September 10, 2007

■ Those who have covered TGS 2006 and/or before

We have sent an email information regarding the press coverage of TGS 2007 to a person who represents a department that exhibitors, reporters, cameramen, and so on belong to on August 17. We do not accept any individual registration, and therefore the person who represents the department need to register all of your members who plan to visit and cover TGS 2007.

■ Those who plan to cover TGS for the first time

If you do not receive the information by then, we would like the person in charge to email or fax us references that verify their qualification as press to cover TGS 2007. References that we would like to have include ID card issued by a company, business card, recent articles with a writer's name, URL, and a page that includes the list of staff names, and so on. We would send the registration form to the person in charge after we check the eligibility of applicants for TGS 2007 press coverage.

■Registration Criteria

Press members who are able to register or to cover the show are limited to those who are listed below. We ask your kind understanding in this matter.

Print Media, Newswire, & Online Media: 

 Reporter
Editor
Editorial Writer
Correspondent
Photographer

Broadcast (TV, Radio) : 

 Reporter
Director
Producer
Camera or Sound Crew

* Online media must be commercial news websites updated regularly. Fan sites, personal web site writers, designers, editors or creators cannot be registered as press members.
*Freelance journalists must submit a letter of assignment from the appropriate media.
*Advertisement agencies, PR companies, and Sales reps for media outlets are not eligible to register as press members.
