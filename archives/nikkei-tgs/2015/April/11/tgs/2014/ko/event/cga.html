TOKYO GAME SHOW holds the 3rd anniversary e-SPORTS competition event called "Cyber Games Asia (CGA)" by inviting Top rated game players and teams from around Asia including Japan.
Please enjoy the high level game plays competed among the best players.

Sudden Attack「SAOMT 2014 Summer」Off-Line Final Tournament
Date & Time: September 20(Sat) 10:00 - 17:00 (planned)

NEXON holds off-line final competitions for its PC Online FPS Game "Sudden Attack" called「SAOMT 2014 Summer(Sudden Attack Official Master Tournament 2014 Summer)」.
The selected 4 clans (teams) will be competing for national championship on top of 1,026 clans(teams).

Winner Prize： JPY 1-Million
Event Operated by：

Alliance of Valiant Arms Gameon Cup(AGC) 2014 - Escort International Friendship Match　Date & Time: September 21(Sun) 10:00 - 17:00 (planned)

Cyber Games Asia at 2nd Day will be holding an international friendship match playing by online FPS "Alliance of Valiant Arms" Gameon Cup 2014.
Top-rated "Escort" representative clans (teams) from China, Japan, South Korea and Taiwan will be challenging for the toughest battle for the regional championship.
