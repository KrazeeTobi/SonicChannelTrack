Access / Accommodations

Venue

Makuhari Messe （Mihama-ku, Chiba City）

http://www.m-messe.co.jp/

View Larger Map

Access by Train

JR Keiyo Line Take the train to KAIHIN MAKUHARI Station (about 29 minutes from TOKYO Station and about 12 minutes from SOGA Sation), then walk 5 minutes.
JR Sobu Line From MAKUHARI HONGO Station (about 40 minutes from AKIHABARA Station),
  • ※Getting on the bus for QVC Marine Field or IRYO(Medical)CENTER and get off at Town Center(takes about 17mins), then walk 3 minutes.
  • ※Getting on the bus for MAKUHARI MESSE CHUO and get off at last stop (takes 17mins).

Access by Highway Bus

From/To Narita International Airport

【Operated by Keisei Bus】

Stopped at: Narita Airport Terminal 1/ Terminal 2, Hotel Springs Makuhari, JR Kaihin Makuhari Station,Hotel New Otani Makuhari, Hotel Francs, Hotel The Manhattan, Hotel Green Tower Makuhari, APA Hotels & Resorts Tokyo Bay Makuhari
Fare: JPY900 for One-Way

Timetable (Keisei Bus)

From/To Haneda International Airport

【Operated by Keisei Bus】

Stopped at: Haneda Airport New International Terminal/ Terminal1/Terminal 2, Hotel New Otani Makuhari, Hotel Francs, Hotel The Manhattan, Hotel Green Tower Makuhari, APA Hotel & Resorts Tokyo Bay Makuhari, Hotel Springs Makuhari, JR Kaihin Makuhari Station, Makuhari Messe Chuo
Fare: JPY1,150 for One-Way

Timetable (Keisei Bus)

【Operated by Airport Limousine】

Stopped at: Haneda Airport New International Terminal/ Terminal1/Terminal 2, Hotel Springs Makuhari, JR Kaihin Makuhari Station, Hotel New Otani Makuhari, Hotel Francs, Hotel The Manhattan, Hotel Green Tower Makuhari, APA Hotels & Resorts Tokyo Bay Makuhari, Makuhari Messe Chuo
Fare: JPY1,150 for One-Way

Timetable (Airport Limousine)

※Note: Fares and schedule are subject to change without notice.

Access by Car

  • ・Eastern Kanto Expressway: About 5 minutes from Coastal Narashino (WANGAN NARASHINO) or Coastal Chiba (WANGAN CHIBA) Interchange
  • ・Keiyo Road: About 10 minutes from Makuhari Interchange
  • ※Makuhari Messe Parking with 5,500 capacity（Car JPY1,000/day）
  • ※For more information on parking at Makuhari Messe:http://www.m-messe.co.jp/en/access/index.html
  • ※For more access information to Makuhari Messe: http://www.m-messe.co.jp/en/access/index.html
