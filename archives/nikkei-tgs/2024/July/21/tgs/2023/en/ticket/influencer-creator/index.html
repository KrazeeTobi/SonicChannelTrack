At the TOKYO GAME SHOW 2023 (TGS2023), which will be held for the first time in four years using the entire Makuhari Messe, influencers and creators who distribute gameplay on video posting sites and SNS for the purpose of improving information dissemination can enter the venue from Business Day. (* Entry conditions apply / Pre-registration required / Registration fee required).

In addition, with the support of YouTube Gaming, we will newly prepare a dedicated area "Creator Lounge" where influencers and creators can use it as a base for live distribution and video editing.

We hope that all influencers and creators will come and experience the forefront of gaming, and share with as many people as possible the information, experiences, and feelings you have gained at the venue.

Regarding registration

<Acceptance period for influencer/creator registration>

  • "General Influencers/Creators": August 4 (Fri.) to 6:00 P.M. on September 13(Wed.) *JST →to 6:00 P.M. on September 18(Mon.) *JST(Extended period)
  • "Invited Influencers": August 4 (Fri.) to 6:00 P.M. on September 18(Mon.) *JST

Influencers and creators who wish to attend are requested to register in advance.
When you register, we will set the entry conditions.
General influencers/creators and influencers/creators invited by exhibitors (hereinafter referred to as "Invited Influencers") are requested to register the required information.
The secretariat will check the contents and approve the visit as soon as it is confirmed that the entry conditions are satisfied.

Click here for pre-registration

Period during which influencers/creators can visit

September 22nd (Friday) to September 24th (Sunday), 2023

  • *Some special invitation influencers can enter from September 21 (Thursday)
  • *During the above period, it is possible to visit multiple days.

Registration fee

  • Registration fee of 1100 yen (tax included) is required for General Influencers/Creators.
  • Up to 1 companion can be registered per influencer. Accompanying persons are limited to the person in charge of the talent agency, photography, editing, streaming, hair and makeup, and interpretation.
  • A separate registration fee of 1650 yen (tax included) is required for those accompanying general influencers.
  • If your registration is not approved, there is no registration fee.
  • There is no registration fee for "Invited Influencers" who have obtained a code (Promotion Code) from the exhibitor (the registration fee is borne by the exhibitor). Please enter the code in the corresponding field of the web registration form (released on August 4) and proceed to pre-registration.
  • Regarding "Invited Influencers", the code (promotion code) is different for [Only the person] and [The person + accompanying person]. Please confirm which ticket (code) to the exhibitor in advance.

Entry/Registration conditions

General influencer/Creator

  • The number of SNS registrants/followers alone must exceed “30,000”.
  • Or, the total number of registrants/followers on multiple SNS must exceed 50,000.
  • The posted content does not cause any trouble (including infringement of rights) to third parties and does not violate public order and morals.
  • *In the examination of the content of the entry by the secretariat, if it is confirmed that there is a posting or distribution that causes inconvenience to a third party (including infringement of rights) or a posting or distribution that is contrary to public order and morals, the secretariat will decide to cancel the entry.
  • *If it is determined that it is significantly contrary to the basic principles of the organizer, Computer Entertainment Supplier's Association (https://www.cesa.or.jp/compliance.html), we will ask you to refrain from visiting.
  • *After approval of registration, if it is found that the content of the post causes a nuisance to a third party (including infringement of rights) or is contrary to public order and morals (including points pointed out by a third party), we will cancel the registration. We may ask you to withdraw the posted content.
    The registration fee will not be refunded even if the registration is cancelled. Please note.

Invited Influencer

  • "Invited Influencers" are those who have been invited by exhibitors and have obtained a code (promotion code) for the online registration form (released on August 4).
  • The posted content does not cause any trouble (including infringement of rights) to third parties, and does not violate public order and morals.
  • *In the examination of the content of the entry by the secretariat, if it is confirmed that there is a posting or distribution that causes inconvenience to a third party (including infringement of rights) or a posting or distribution that is contrary to public order and morals, the secretariat will decide to cancel the entry.
  • *If it is determined that it is significantly contrary to the basic principles of the organizer, Computer Entertainment Supplier's Association (https://www.cesa.or.jp/compliance.html), we will ask you to refrain from visiting.
  • *After approval of registration, if it is found that the content of the post causes a nuisance to a third party (including infringement of rights) or is contrary to public order and morals (including points pointed out by a third party), we will cancel the registration. We may ask you to withdraw the posted content.

Main target SNS

YouTube / Twitter / Instagram / Twitch / Niconico / TikTok / Facebook / Threads

Precautions for visiting and shooting

  • Please be sure to bring the following items to the reception on the day you visiting . Failure to do so may result in refusal to issue the pass.
    [Influencer / Creator]
    • PDF ticket (output or QR code screen presentation)

      *The reception staff may ask you to present your SNS management screen.
      Thank you for your understanding.

    [Companion]
    • Business card and personal identification (ID with photo, etc.)

      *Please be sure to check in and enter with the influencer/creator.

  • It is possible to take photos and videos inside the Makuhari Messe venue, but if you can identify the person in the image, please be sure to obtain permission from the person or process the image so that the person cannot be identified.
  • There is a possibility that you may be accidentally reflected in photos and videos taken by others.
  • At the exhibitor's booth, please follow the regulations and rules of each company when filming, streaming, etc.
  • Posting on malicious SNS, shooting, distributing, posting, etc. that may cause discomfort to exhibitors and visitors are strictly prohibited.
  • The Secretariat will not be held responsible for any troubles that may occur during filming, distribution coverage, or trial play at exhibitor booths. Please respond by yourself.
  • Please strictly observe the following rules regarding the use of selfie sticks. (Only influencers/creators are allowed to use selfie sticks. Please be sure to wear the “Influencer Badge” clearly.)
    • Shooting while walking is prohibited
    • Please use a selfie stick with a length of less than 50 cm
    • Shooting with the selfie stick above your head is prohibited
  • The secretariat and exhibitors may retweet or introduce the content posted/distributed.
  • We may ask you to leave the venue if you find any nuisance in the venue. In addition, depending on the situation, we may ask you to confirm the content of the photos and videos you have taken. In the unlikely event that it is determined that there is a problem with the content, please cancel the publication and delete the photos and videos.
  • Please pay all expenses for attendance, recording, distribution, etc. by yourself.
  • Wearing a mask is optional.
  • We will prepare disinfectant at the entrance of the venue, so please cooperate with hand disinfection.
  • Please refrain from visiting if you are feeling unwell, such as having a fever of 37.5 degrees or higher, or having cold symptoms.

Creator Lounge supported by YouTube Gaming

The Creator Lounge supported by YouTube Gaming, under the special sponsorship of YouTube, is a dedicated area for influencers and creators to use as their hub while visiting the in-person venue of TGS2023. The Lounge is exclusively available to official influencers and creators who have pre-registered or are special guest influencers invited by TGS2023. It provides a cozy space where they can socialize, a booth for trying out games and uploading videos, and a workspace. Additionally, there will be an experimental booth for shooting YouTube Shorts within the area for general visitors to enjoy.
