Covid-19 Safety Regulations

TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office will operate Makuhari Messe and record and distribute official programs in accordance with the "COVID-19 Guidelines for Prevention of the Spread of Infection in the Exhibition Industry" established by the Japan Association of Exhibition Organizations, while taking measures to prevent infection.

We would appreciate it if you would read and understand this before visiting the exhibition.
Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

●Please wear a face mask, take a temperature, and disinfect with alcohol thoroughly.

●TGS2022 recommends that you install the Contact Confirmation Application for New Coronavirus (COCOA)app.

●Please wash your hands frequently.

●Shaking hands and other forms of contact with other visitors are prohibited.

●Please ensure social distance between visitors.

●The following persons are NOT allowed to enter the venue:

Persons who fall under any of the following categories.

・Fever of 37.5℃ or higher is observed.

・Visitors with symptoms such as breathlessness, strong lethargy, or even mild symptoms such as coughing or pharyngeal congestion

・If you are under the influence of alcohol

・If you have been in close contact with a person who is positive for Covid-19 infection

●Admission of junior high school students and younger is prohibited.

・This is due to safety considerations, as the vaccination status of young people is still unclear.

●Prohibition of call and response on stage
