Haneda Girl, team up with the combat robot M.O.T.H.E.R to save the Data Empire from the threat of the Hackernauts! Haneda Girl is a 2D pixel game with frenetic action and platforming. Switch between Haneda Girl and M.O.T.H.E.R in a cooperative exchange game, approach each situation in infinite ways. The goal is not just to achieve, but to aim for a high score with the beauty of the strategy!

Platform :

Mecha Macho is an action/platform video game, in which you play as Luchin, a 3-year-old baby that is the reincarnation of the legendary wrestling champion "El Super Macho". You will travel through the universe in your robot suit to avenge his death, and recover his champion belt using wrestling techniques and powers, that you start to remember as you make your way through the game

Robot action game. Great action x battle graphics, looks like a lot of fun!
