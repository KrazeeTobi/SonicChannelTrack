Hotel Accommodations

Dear Participants of Tokyo Game Show 2010,

On behalf of the Secretariat of Tokyo Game Show 2010 held at Makuhari Messe held in Makuhari, KNT, the official travel agency for hotel accommodations, will assist participants in reserving their hotel room.
Official hotels have been specially reserved for the participants.
However, we advise participants to book hotel reservations early to ensure their stay while rooms are available.

  • Payment should be made by credit card.
  • This hotel booking site allows participants to book their hotels online (multiple rooms at one time), and confirmations are issued instantaneously online.
  • Hotel reservations will close on August 30th 2010
  • If you require additional accommodations in other cities during your stay in Japan, please feel free to visit Japan Traveler Online at;
    http://japantraveleronline.com

Proceed to reservation site
