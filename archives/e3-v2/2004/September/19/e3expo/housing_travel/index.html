Contact Convention Management Resources (CMR), E3's official housing and travel company, to make all your travel arrangements for E3 2004. As a full service travel organization, CMR is your one stop for exclusive discounted rates from hotels, major airlines and car rentals.

Take advantage of a full range of locations and some of the best hotel rates in Los Angeles. No pre-payment is necessary to book a reservation, however, cancellation penalties will apply. Book online and receive immediate confirmation by email, as well as make changes to or update your reservation. Shuttle bus service is available only to attendees booking through E3 housing, and special bus passes will be issued to attendees who book through CMR.

E3 Shuttle Service
Please note that E3 Shuttles are available only to attendees who reserve their accommodations through the E3 Housing department.

Click here to view the 2004 Shuttle Service schedule.

Access to the E3 shuttle service will be limited to individuals who make their hotel reservation through the official E3 Housing Service. You will receive a wristband when you check-in to your hotel only if your reservation is in the official E3 room block. This wristband will allow you to utilize the shuttle service and you must show it to board the shuttle buses throughout the duration of E3.
