Deadline for Exhibitor Registration - April 9, 2004

1. Web

All edits and additions made after the deadline must be picked up on-site at Exhibitor Registration located in South Hall Lobby, Los Angeles Convention Center.

Exhibitor Badges
For each name submitted prior to the April 9th deadline you will receive a printed name badge and badge holder. The Exhibitor badge allows access to the show floor Wednesday, May 12 through Friday, May 14. It also allows access to the show two hours BEFORE the opening and one hour AFTER the close of the show floor on show open dates. Exhibitor badges and badge holders are strictly for exhibitor personnel working in your company's booth during the show. For security reasons, these badges are not to be utilized for clients, friends, etc., who are not working the booth. No exhibitor badges will be mailed until show management has received A Certificate of General Liability Insurance with the proper coverage, See Section #28 of the Exhibit Space Contract.

Set-Up/Tear Down Wristbands
In addition to your Exhibitor Badges, you will be eligible to receive an allotment of Set-UP/Tear Down wristbands for your exhibitors and exhibitor-appointed contractors (EAC's). These wristbands are to be used during both Set-Up and Tear Down. Exhibitor badges will NOT allow access to the floor before or after show open days. You may order these wristbands in advance by completing the Set-Up/Tear Down Wristband Order Form located behind the Registration/Lead Retrieval/Travel Information tab in the Exhibitor Service Manual. These wristbands will also be available on-site in the Security offices in both the West and South Halls and will be given to only EAC's and Exhibitors who have turned in their liability insurance certificate. The wristbands will be valid during the show Set-Up/Tear Down days only. If you have any questions, you may contact the E3 Exhibitor Help line at 877.216.6264.

What If I Don't Have All The Names of the Individuals Working In My Booth By The Deadline?
If you would like to make additions/edits to your exhibitor list after the April 9, 2004 deadline, you can do it online. Badges available for pick-up at the Exhibitor Registration counter located in both West and South Halls lobbies of the Los Angeles Convention Center.

How Do I Get Badges For My Customers?
Each exhibiting company will be sent an allotment (based upon total paid square footage) of Guest Passes in January of 2004. A sample letter of redemption instructions that you can insert in your mailings will also be included with the Guest Passes. Your customers and clients may register online or complete the registration form in advance to avoid delays. Two forms of industry identification must accompany the Guest Pass Registration form. Since the review/approval process of the Guest Pass credentials can take 7 to 10 days, please distribute your passes IMMEDIATELY. Once your guest has been approved, they will receive an e-mail confirmation that must be brought on-site to receive their COMPLIMENTARY exhibit floor pass. Guest Pass recipients that do not process the registration in advance will have to complete this process on-site at the Guest Pass Verification/Validation Counters located in the South Hall Lobby. Each exhibiting company will also receive 5 complimentary conference session passes. To purchase conference program passes at a 20% discount, please visit the Exhibitor Registration counter at the show.

What If I Have Children Under 18 Who Will Be Doing Demonstrations In My Booth?
E3 strongly discourages anyone under 18 on the exhibit hall floor. Children who are demonstrating your product in your booth must be badged as Underage Exhibitors and must be pre-approved. No children under 18 will be allowed to register at the show. Those individuals under 18 years of age working in your booth are permitted in your booth only and are not to be in other parts of the Exhibit Hall. To gain pre-approval, please e-mail Mary Dolaher, Show Director, at mdolaher@e3expo.com.

What Do I Do If I Want To Hold A Meeting in My Booth Pre or Postshow?
For security and safety reasons, unauthorized personnel and/or events will not be permitted on the show floor without Show Management approval before or after official show hours. For further information, please contact Pat Phillips, Director of Show Operations, at pphillips@vnuexpo.com.

Exhibitor Badge Pick-Up Procedures
All badges requested prior to April 9, 2004 will be sent to the Exhibitor Contact specified upon registration. These badges will be mailed 2-day Federal Express and a confirmation including your FedEx tracking number will be sent to your contact. All badges mailed will include a badge holder. No exhibitor badges will be mailed until show management has received A Certificate of General Liability Insurance with the proper coverage. See Section #28 of the Exhibit Space Contract. These badges will be valid only during show open dates so please remember to request your Set-Up/Tear Down wristbands via the form found behind The Registration/Lead Retrieval/Travel Information tab in your service kit.

Any badges requested after the April 9, 2004 deadline will need to be picked up on-site at Exhibitor Registration in both the West and South Hall Lobbies of the Los Angeles Convention Center. Photo identification must be presented before obtaining your name badge.

It is important that you communicate to your booth personnel and contractors you hire, when and where they must pick up their badges. Without a badge and badge holder during show days or a move in wristband during set-up/tear down, they will NOT be allowed on the show floor under any circumstances.

To avoid waiting in lines, we strongly suggest registering your booth personnel by the April 9, 2004 deadline and requesting your set-up/tear down wristbands in advance. ONLY the first day of wristbands will be sent out. Each additional day you will need to pick up your wristbands daily at the security offices.
