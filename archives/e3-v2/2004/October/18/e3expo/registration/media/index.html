Welcome to E3 2004 Media eRegistration. We invite qualified working journalists from commercial news organizations to submit a registration request for a complimentary media badge that provides:

  • Exhibit floor access
  • Use of E3 2004 Media Center facilities
  • Access to the ESA Media Briefing & Breakfast (on a first-come, first-served basis)
  • Access to the Media Hospitality Suite

Please Note: Media badges do not provide access to workshops or conferences. Please email the E3 2004 Media Relations Team at media@e3expo.com to submit a conference or workshop request.
