Into the Pixel:
A Digital Game Art Exhibition.
In celebration of its 10th Anniversary, E3 hosted an exhibition of art from video and computer games with the LA County Museum of Art's Graphic Art Council and the Academy of Interactive Arts and Sciences. A distinguished panel of jurors representing the traditional art world and the videogame industry selected sixteen artworks from more than 120 submitted. View the Selected works online at www.e3insider.com.
