E3 On-Site Media Center
The E3 2004 Media Center offers convenient support for journalists covering the show free of charge. It features a large working newsroom equipped with:

  • Macintosh and PC computers
  • High-speed Internet access
  • Wi-Fi network access
  • Standard office equipment
  • Telephones
  • Fax machines
  • Photocopy machines
  • An exhibitor news conference room
  • Exhibitors' media kits
  • A media-only hospitality suite
