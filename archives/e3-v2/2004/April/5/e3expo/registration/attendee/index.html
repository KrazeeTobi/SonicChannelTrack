Welcome to the E3 2004 Attendee Registration. Please note that E3 is a trade-only event and is not open to the general public. Individuals who are not able to document their direct, professional affiliation to the interactive entertainment industry are not qualified to attend. Applications will not be processed without complete information and identification. The Management of E3 2004 reserves the right to deny any applicant admission.

Package Descriptions and Pricing Information

There are 4 ways to register for E3 2004:

1. Web
Conference Program registrations made online after April 9, 2004 will be charged at regular pricing. New for 2004: Attendees registering for Package 5 may now begin the registration process online. Upon completion of the online application, you will still need to submit two forms of industry identification before your application is processed and confirmed. Package 5 is available only to qualified members of the interactive entertainment industry, and is available until April 9, 2004.

Please Note: In order to register online, you must be using Netscape 4.0 or higher or Internet Explorer 4.0 or higher. If you are not, please update your browser or register by mail or fax.

4. Phone
877.216.6263 by May 7, 2004. Registration for Package 5 is NOT Available by phone. Registrations received after April 9 will be charged at regular pricing. A $5 service fee applies to all phone registrations. TTY Service is available for the hearing impaired at 800.735.2929.

Remember - Package 5 is only available to qualified members of the interactive entertainment industry. All applications for Package 5 must include 2 forms of industry identification. Forms will not be processed without complete information and industry identification.

Do not pay twice for this event. You will be charged for all registrations, and you will not be refunded for duplication registrations. All registrations are non-refundable and non-transferable.

Receiving your badge
New for 2004: All attendee badges and badge holders will be distributed on-site at Express Registration counters.

U.S. Attendees
Upon confirmation of your registration, you will receive an electronic confirmation containing a unique bar code ID number. Simply bring this confirmation on-site to an Express Registration counter to pick up your badge and badge holder. Please note that the bar code will only scan once - do not duplicate your confirmation. In addition, all E3 attendees will be required to show government-issued photo I.D. (such as a driver's license or passport) in order to receive the 2004 badge and badge holder.

International Attendees
Upon confirmation of your registration, you will receive an electronic confirmation containing a unique bar code ID number. Simply bring this confirmation on-site to the International Attendee Registration counter to pick up your badge and badge holder. Please note that the bar code will only scan once - do not duplicate your confirmation. All E3 attendees will be required to show government-issued photo I.D. (such as a driver's license or passport) in order to receive the 2004 badge and badge holder.

If you have questions about your registration, contact Customer Service at 877.216.6263, outside the U.S. please call 805.654.0171 or e3expo@rcsreg.com.
