         BOOTH APPROVAL GUIDELINES 

    All exhibitors occupying 400 or more square feet of booth space must complete the Booth Approval Request Form. Booth heights and limits vary according to the size of the exhibit space rented and the location of the booth. Please refer to the LACC Exhibit and Building Permit Information. All multi-story exhibits, regardless of whether people will occupy the upper area or not, and all exhibit features must have drawings available for inspection.

    Exposition Management, the installation and dismantling contractor, and governmental authority reserve the right to review structure during the time the exhibit is being erected, exhibited and dismantled at Show site.

    The drawing should include a signature or stamp of the reviewing structural engineer indicating that the structure is properly engineered for its proposed use, and a signature of an authorized official of the exhibit building company indicating that the structure is built in compliance with the details and specifications set forth on the drawings. Signs must be posted indicating the maximum number of people the structure will hold. The Los Angeles Convention Center requires additional permits for certain types of building structures used for exhibit purposes. Please refer to the LACC Information Packet located behind the Booth Construction/ Storage/Signage tab.

    Two paper copies of design layout/specifications are required for all booth approvals to be sent to Tom Fields, Building Superintendent, Show Operations, LACC, 1201 S. Figueroa St., Los Angeles, CA 90015, Tel: 213-714-1151 ext 5609 - Fax: 213-765-4267 e-mail: tfields@lacclink.com . All booth approvals must be submitted by March 31, 2004. Installation of booths cannot begin before Show Management has notified the exhibitor of plan approval.

    Only In-Line booths should refer to the IAEM guidelines located under the Booth Construction/Storage Signage tab. Exposition Management must approve any exceptions to the guidelines no later than April 19, 2004. The Booth Approval Form is located behind the Booth Construction/ Storage/Signage tab in the exhibitor service manual.
