E3: Where the Interactive Entertainment Industry gets down to Business

  

The industry's annual forum for the launching of new products and the exchange of ideas on interactive entertainment content, E3 draws over 55,000 professionals - no kids and hobbyists - for three days of intense deal-making.

As successful new titles become more expensive to produce, you need an efficient, cost-effective way to expand your market, increase sales, grab media attention and attract top developer talent and investors.

Your company's presence at E3 can help you do all this and more. You can accomplish more of your sales, networking and recruitment goals in three days at E3 because all of the interactive entertainment industry's key players are gathered together, ready to do business.
 

Who Should Exhibit at E3?

  

Every company that aims to be a force in interactive entertainment. Your participation at E3 shows that you're a force to be reckoned with in this ever-growing industry. Use your exhibit to generate sales leads, close deals, grab media attention, reach new international markets and identify new distribution channels. Your strongest competitors are undoubtedly here - can you afford to miss E3?
