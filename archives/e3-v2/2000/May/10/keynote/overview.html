America Online, Inc. (AOL) President and COO Bob Pittman to Keynote E3 Expo 2000!

We're pleased to announce that AOL President and COO Bob Pittman will share his unique insight on how interactive entertainment and the Internet online industry will unfold in the new millennium, at 9 a.m. on Thursday, May 11, in Room 515 at the Los Angeles Convention Center.

Note: Access to the keynote room will be on a first-come, first-served basis. An on-site overflow room will be available for additional attendees; however; individuals in the overflow room will not be able to ask questions of the speakers.
