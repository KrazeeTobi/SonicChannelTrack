I have pre-registered. When will I receive my badge?
Badges for registration received on or before April 7, 2000, will start mailing via U.S. mail April 24, 2000. If you registered after April 7, 2000, you must pick up your badge at the Attendee Will Call counter at the Los Angeles Convention Center.

US attendees' badges will be mailed beginning April 17, 2000. ALL INTERNATIONAL ATTENDEES (including Canada) must pick up their badges at the Attendee Will Call counter at the Los Angeles Convention Center.
