Exhibitor News


Business Wire
Business Wire (http://www.businesswire.com) - Business Wire is the leading electronic distributor of exhibitor trade show news releases and photos to media, analysts, online services and the Internet. For E3 2000, Business Wire has custom distribution packages and a special Web site dedicated to E3-related news releases. For more information, contact Jim Liebenau at 800/237-8212 or liebenau@bizwire.com, or visit the Business Wire staff in the working press room of the Media Center at the show.



Virtual Press Office
Virtual Press Office (http://virtualpressoffice.com) - The Virtual Press Office will be automating the working press room at E3 2000 by uploading exhibitor press materials (press releases, company and product backgrounders, photos, white papers, etc.) to a single homepage. The service has been designed to help journalists obtain quick and efficient access to breaking news stories as well as comprehensive background information. The Virtual Press Office E3 2000 service is free of charge to reporters and is online year round.
