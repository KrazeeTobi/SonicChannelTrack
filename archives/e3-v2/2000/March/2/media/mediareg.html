Welcome to E3 2000 Media Registration. We invite qualified journalists working from commercial news organizations to register for a complimentary media badge that provides:

  • Exhibit floor access
  • Use of E3 2000 Media Center facilities

PLEASE NOTE: media badges do not provide access to workshops or conferences.

We ask that you carefully read the following information before proceeding to the registration system.

  • The management of E3 2000 reserves the right to deny any applicant a media badge.
  • E3 2000 is a trade-only event. No one under age 18 is permitted, including infants.
  • E3 2000 Media Registration is open to working journalists with commercial news organizations only.
  • Submitting the registration request form does not automatically qualify you for a media badge at E3 2000.
  • After submitting the registration request form, you must fax the following credentials to E3 2000 Media Registration at 310-967-2912:

      - Copy of your business card with name, editorial title and media outlet's logo as well as one of the following:
      - Copy of a current masthead, which clearly displays your name and editorial title.
      - A bylined industry-related article written by you and published within the last 6 months.

  • In addition, several media groups have the following supplemental requirements:

      Online Media: Only journalists with commercial news organizations qualify for media badges. Unfortunately, fan sites, personal web site writers, editors or creators cannot be registered as media. To qualify as online media, fax both:

      • A PRINTED copy of your online publication with your name and title appearing in an editorial capacity AND
      • A copy of the web site's business license or official documentation showing the business's federal tax identification number.

      Freelance Writers: In order to register as media, we require that you fax a letter from a publisher, editor or producer (on their letterhead) stating that you are covering E3 2000 on assignment or under current contract.

      Broadcast Journalists: We require that each member of a broadcast crew register separately. In order to qualify for a media badge, you must also fax a letter from a producer (on company letterhead) stating that you are covering E3 2000 on assignment.

      Industry and Financial Analysts: Please fax a recent report or bulletin on interactive entertainment with your byline.


  • Once you have completed the online application and we have received your credentials via fax, you will receive an email in approximately two weeks confirming or denying your registration.
