Overview | On-site Services | News Center 

E3 2000 invites qualified journalists working from commercial news organizations to register for a complimentary media badge. After April 14, media may submit a registration request to expedite the on-site registration process, but MUST finalize registration by providing credentials as outlined below at E3 2000 Media Registration on site at the Los Angeles Convention Center (LACC).

PLEASE NOTE: media badges do not provide access to workshops or conferences.

We ask that you carefully read the following information before proceeding to the registration system.

  • The management of E3 2000 reserves the right to deny any applicant a media badge.
  • E3 2000 is a trade-only event. No one under age 18 is permitted, including infants.
  • E3 2000 Media Registration is open to working journalists with commercial news organizations only.
  • Submitting the registration request form does not automatically qualify you for a media badge at E3 2000.
  • Please note that media badges do not provide automatic access to workshops or conferences. Please contact E3 2000 Media Relations at e3expo@cohnwolfe.com or 310-967-2946 to reserve a session. Click here for a complete list of workshops and conferences.

On site Media Registration

If you are submitting a media registration request form after April 14, you MUST bring your credentials to Media Registration on site to the LACC (Room 502) to finalize the media registration process.

Media Registration Hours
Wednesday, May 10 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Thursday, May 11 7 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Friday, May 12 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Saturday, May 13 8 a.m. to 4 p.m.

All working journalists MUST provide:

  • Copy of your business card with name, editorial title and media outlet's logo as well as one of the following:
     
    • Copy of a current masthead, which clearly displays your name and editorial title.
    • A bylined industry-related article written by you and published within the last 6 months.

  • In addition, several media groups have the following supplemental requirements:

    • Online Media: Only journalists with commercial news organizations qualify for media badges. Unfortunately, fan sites, personal web site writers, editors or creators cannot be registered as media. To qualify as online media, bring both:

      • A PRINTED copy of your online publication with your name and title appearing in an editorial capacity AND
      • A copy of the web site's business license or official documentation showing the business's federal tax identification number.

    • Freelance Writers: In order to register as media, we require that you provide a letter from a publisher, editor or producer (on their letterhead) stating that you are covering E3 2000 on assignment or under current contract.

    • Broadcast Journalists: We require that each member of a broadcast crew register separately. In order to qualify for a media badge, you must also provide a letter from a producer (on company letterhead) stating that you are covering E3 2000 on assignment.

    • Industry and Financial Analysts: Please bring a recent report or bulletin on interactive entertainment with your byline.


    If you have any questions on the process above, please contact the E3 2000 Media Relations Team at e3expo@cohnwolfe.com or 310-967-2946.

    Please note: E3 is a trade event. No one under 18 will be admitted (including infants). This policy is strictly enforced.
