On-Site E3 2000 Media Center
The E3 2000 Media Center offers convenient support for journalists covering the show. It features a large working media room equipped with Macintosh and (Pentium) PC-compatible computers and high-speed Internet access. Standard office equipment, such as local access telephones, fax and photocopy machines, will also be made available to the media free of charge. The Media Center also includes a press conference room, a media kit room and media-only hospitality suite.
