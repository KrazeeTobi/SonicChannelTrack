W3 - The New Advertising Review Council of the ESRB: An Overview
9:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.
Wednesday, May 10

The Entertainment Software Rating Board (ESRB) has created the Advertising Review Council (ARC) to ensure that advertisements placed by entertainment software companies are appropriate, responsible, truthful and accurate. On January 31, 2000, ARC began monitoring and enforcing ARC's Principles and Guidelines and Advertising Code of Conduct to ensure that computer and video games are marketed appropriately to all audiences. This workshop will feature a review of ARC's new Principles and Guidelines as well as new major changes to the Advertising Code of Conduct. The role of various industries in assisting ARC in implementing these Principles and Guideslines will also be discussed.

Moderator: Dr. Arthur Pober, ESRB
Panelists: Lee Uniacke, Ziff Davis; Bill Kelchner, Imagine Media; Riley Russell, Sony Computer Entertainment America; John Rousseau, IDG Games Media Group
