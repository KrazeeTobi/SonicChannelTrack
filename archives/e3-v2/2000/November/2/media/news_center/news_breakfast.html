MEDIA ADVISORY

Exclusive Media Invitation for the E3Expo 2000 Media Briefing & Breakfast

WHAT:Kick off E3Expo 2000 -- the world's premiere interactive entertainment showcase -- with the fourth annual Media Briefing & Breakfast hosted by Interactive Digital Software Association (IDSA) President Douglas Lowenstein. Media professionals are invited to this exclusive event at which Lowenstein will offer a first-look at IDSA's 2000 Video and Computer Game Consumer Survey, as well as discuss the latest trends and opportunities ahead for this booming $6.1 billion U.S. industry. The Briefing & Breakfast also offers a unique opportunity to meet leading industry executives and exhibitors prior to the show floor opening. Media attendees will receive complimentary continental breakfast and an official E3Expo 2000 media baseball hat.

Immediately following the breakfast, Bob Pittman, AOL president and COO, will deliver the E3Expo 2000 keynote address.
 
WHEN:7:30 a.m., Thursday, May 11, 2000.
 
WHERE:Los Angeles Convention Center, West Hall, Room 515
 
WHO:Douglas Lowenstein is president of the Interactive Digital Software Association (IDSA), the U.S. association that owns E3Expo and is dedicated to serving the business and public affairs needs of companies that publish games for video game consoles, personal computers and the Internet.
