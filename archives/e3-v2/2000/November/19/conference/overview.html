Track 1
Development: From Garage to Million Dollar Decisions
Development issues have never been so critical and decisions so cost-intensive. You cannot afford to miss out on what Game Gods and Masters are thinking. What alternative OS's and platforms will drive the next generation markets? What technologies and skill-sets will you need to survive and thrive? Learn the success stories and how to approached issues affecting development.

Track 2
Wired and Online: All the Time and Everywhere We Go
The foundation has been built. Now it's time to expand the market to fill the bandwidth. Are we inexorably heading to an online world -- if so, what will your role be? How are communities built and find out exactly what users want today and what they might want tomorrow. Learn successes directly from the pros that have built a thriving world. Soon, e-commerce will be everywhere. Are you ready?

Track 3
A Buzz in the Air: The Issues
Learn to deal with complex policy and business issues facing our industry. Hear from experts and industry leaders about the issues we are faced with. Learn firsthand the secrets of recruiting the talent you need to build your team in the changing marketplace, and what tradeoffs (if any exist) in developing for a "mass-market" vs. the core market.

Track 4
Consumers: The Masses are Hungry
How does your branding and selling message survive the clutter and media bombardment every man, woman and child faces every waking moment? And how do you go about getting the girl gamer? Experts will showcase what's worked for them and where the industry is headed. And now for something completely radical: a panel of gamers and parents will provide their perspective.

Track 5
Business Trends: The Future, 2.0
Each day seems to brings convergence cloaser as we approach an all-entertainment-all-the-time wired world. How do you decide where to go next? Will your licenses take you to the big dance with the Media Giants…and endless revenue streams? Maybe, but all the deals in the world won't matter if your products aren't on shelves. Hear first-hand how titles are selected and stocked by the biggest distributers and Retailers. We'll show success stories that answer all that and more. Plus: how to make yourself a Media Giant.
