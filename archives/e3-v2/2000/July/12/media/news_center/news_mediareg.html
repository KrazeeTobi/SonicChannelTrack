MEDIA ADVISORY

Electronic Entertainment Expo 2000 (E3) Media Pre-Registration Now Available Online

WHAT:Online Media Pre-Registration for E3 2000
 
WHEN: Online Pre-Registration: January 5 - April 7
Exhibit Dates: May 11 - May 13
Conference Dates: May 10 - May 12
 
WHERE:Los Angeles Convention Center
 
WHY: Themed "Beyond Imagination" for its first show of the millenium, E3 2000 is the world's largest trade event exclusively dedicated to showcasing interactive entertainment and educational software and related products. Under one roof, attendees will meet the most influential people in the industry, discover trends, see groundbreaking new technologies, and get a sneak peak at next generation products for computers, video game consoles and the Internet.
 
ATTENDEES:
  • Leading interactive entertainment professionals from around the world including developers, software publishers, retailers, distributors and industry executives
  • Members of the international financial community including investment bankers, venture capitalists, analysts and financiers
  • Worldwide electronic, print and broadcast media
 
HOW TO REGISTER: Members of the media may register at the online Media Center. Required credentials for a media pass, as outlined on the site, must be faxed or mailed before the application is considered complete. Questions? Call the E3 2000 Media Hotline at (310) 967-2946 or send an email to e3expo@cohnwolfe.com.
 
WHO OWNS E3 2000: E3 2000 is wholly owned by the Interactive Digital Software Association (IDSA), the U.S. association exclusively dedicated to serving the business and public affairs needs of companies that publish games for video game consoles, personal computers and the Internet.
