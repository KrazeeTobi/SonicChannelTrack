Shuttle Service to the Los Angeles Convention Center and Official E3 Hotels

May 17-19, 2001

Complimentary shuttle service is provided between the Los Angeles Convention Center and the hotels listed here. Shuttle information signs are in the lobby of each hotel.

Please note that shuttle times and schedules vary between routes. Check the sign in your hotel lobby for additional information, frequency of service, specific boarding locations and changes.

If you have questions regarding the shuttle or to make an advance reservation for a shuttle with a wheelchair lift, please call KUSHNER & ASSOCIATES at (310) 274-8819.
