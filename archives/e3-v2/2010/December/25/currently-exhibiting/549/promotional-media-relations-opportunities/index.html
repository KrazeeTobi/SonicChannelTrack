Promotional & Media Relations Opportunities

Take advantage of the following opportunities to maximize your exposure at E3 Expo 2011 - before, during and after the show!

Media List

Whether you are unveiling a new product or hosting a special event or celebrity at your booth, this list offers you an exclusive opportunity to gain direct access to the media attending the show and covering your business all year long, and to make sure those key editors are personally invited to see your products at E3 Expo. The next updated pre-registered media list will be posted in Spring 2011. For any questions, please email the Media Team at media@e3expo.com.

Exhibitor Activities

Let the E3 Expo Media Team support and complement your PR efforts. Often times we are contacted by members of the media asking about E3 Expo's special events or needing a quote on a specific topic. By sending us your planned activities, we will be able to direct media right to you. To keep the E3 Expo 2011 Media Relations Team in the loop, email us at media@e3expo.com.

News Wall

Promote your company’s events and activities on E3 Expo’s News Wall located in the on-site Media Center. The News Wall serves as a convenient information resource for the thousands of media that pass through the Center during the show. (We can also include your news in our online Media Center!) The E3 Expo 2011 Media Relations Team is more than happy to help you at media@e3expo.com.

PR Contact Guide for Media

Each year we provide a list of exhibiting companies' PR contacts to qualifying journalists in advance of the show. Media use this helpful tool to contact exhibitors directly. To be included in the guide, please contact the E3 Expo 2011 Media Relations Team at media@e3expo.com.

First Looks

First Looks is E3 Expo's exclusive program designed to spotlight exhibitors showcasing never-before-seen titles and technologies prior to and during the show. If you have brand-new products making their debut at E3 Expo 2011, this is a great way to build buzz and media anticipation. Before the show, we will distribute a compilation of new product descriptions to qualified media and retail buyers. During the show, First Looks products are highlighted through a variety of dedicated on-site signage, as well as through a special First Looks product directory that is distributed to media and retailers, making it simple for them to locate exhibitors with debuting products.

To include your company's debuting products in the First Looks Directory, please log into your MyE3Expo account, click through to the First Looks section, and enter your complete product listing, including the product name, description, and media contact information. Should you have any additional questions regarding the First Looks or MyE3Expo Program, please email Kristin Dacey.

Please note: we reserve the right to edit any product submission longer than four sentences per product.

Event Marketing and Sponsorship Opportunities

Both the Los Angeles Convention Center and our host city overflow with advertising and sponsorship opportunities. No matter what you would like to accomplish during E3 Expo -- big or small -- you can make it happen through a wide variety of sponsorships and customized marketing programs [link to EM page in EO section]. For further information, please contact Shanna Cito, 203-259-3355 and Ellen Moorehead, 508-988-7830.

Celebrities and Special Events

Do you have a special guest appearance or event at your booth? Let Show Management help in making the execution flawless and unforgettable. We will help you to create a plan so problems don’t arise, such as attendees blocking the aisles or obstructing the visibility of another exhibitor. We can also pre-register celebrities for show badges and make special arrangements for entering and exiting the show. Please contact Jacklyn Mellen for further information. For additional E3 Expo PR opportunities, please contact the E3 Expo Media Relations Team at media@e3expo.com.
