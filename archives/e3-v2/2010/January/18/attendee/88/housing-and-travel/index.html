Housing and Travel

Take advantage of a full range of locations and some of the best hotel rates in Los Angeles. No pre-payment is necessary to book a reservation; however, cancellation penalties will apply. Book online and receive immediate confirmation by email, as well as make changes to or update your reservation.

Super Shuttle Airport Transfers

Super Shuttle is offering the following discounts to E3 Expo 2010 Exhibitors and attendees. Please click here to make your reservation.

Super Shuttle offers 15% off shared ride transfers, 10% off Business Class transfers, and 15% off all ExecuCar sedan transfers.

The discount is valid from all the local area airports (LAX, BUR, LGB, SNA & ONT) to the Downtown, Hollywood, Beverly Hills, Century City & Santa Monica hotels.

Los Angeles Convention Center

Located conveniently at the intersection of the Santa Monica and Harbor Freeways, the Los Angeles Convention Center offers a technologically advanced facility that combines incredible functionality in a unique LA atmosphere.

Many amenities at the Los Angeles Convention Center include:
  • State-of-the-art power and date/network systems
  • Fiber Optic Network
  • Wireless internet
  • Full Service Business Center
