Media Center

Thank you for your interest in covering E3 Expo 2010! We look forward to seeing you. Our online Media Center allows you to submit your request for media registration for a complimentary media badge that provides E3 Expo exhibit floor access. The Media Center also provides the latest E3 Expo 2010 news and information for covering the show, June 15-17, 2010, at the Los Angeles Convention Center.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the registration process or the show in general, please contact your E3 Expo Media Relations Team at media@e3expo.com.

E3 Expo Online Media Registration

Online media registration is now open and will be available through May 3, 2010. Before getting started, first read over our credentialing requirements.

After completing your online registration, please scan and email the requested credentialing requirements to media@e3expo.com. You will receive an email confirming or denying your registration request after the E3 Expo 2010 Media Registration Team receives your required industry credentials.

Please provide your direct contact information should we need to contact you concerning your registration request. (Please note: E3 Expo is a trade event and is not open to the public. No one under the age of 17 will be admitted including infants.)

E3 Expo On-Site Media Center

The E3 Expo Media Center, in Petree Hall, offers convenient support free of charge for journalists covering the show. It features a large working newsroom equipped with:

  • Apple and PC computers
  • High-speed Internet access
  • Wi-Fi network access
  • Standard office equipment
  • Phones
  • Fax machine
  • Photocopy machine
  • A "News Wall" featuring important exhibitor news and information
  • Media-only hospitality suite
  • Lockers
