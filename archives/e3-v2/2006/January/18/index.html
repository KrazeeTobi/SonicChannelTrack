One place. One entire industry waiting for you. It's E3 2006.


The interactive entertainment event of the year. See all the newest computer and video game products and technologies that the industry has to offer. Spot the latest trends. Every company you need to see and every category you want to learn about is represented here. Want to know what's new and in what direction the industry is headed? Come to E3 2006.

And that's not all. Register for the Conference Program and be a part of a dynamic educational experience that will guide you toward the most promising opportunities within the interactive entertainment industry today. Industry executives and renowned creative talent will share their insight and views of the future, and supply you with tips and strategies to perfect your business plans and keep you one step ahead of the competition. Whether you're a designer, manager or strategist, there are more than 30 sessions covering a wide variety of topics specifically designed to meet your needs and interests. Register today. Three days that could change your whole year.

Visit this site often for updates.

See you in May!
