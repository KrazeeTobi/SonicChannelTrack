The Official E3Expo 2006 Show Daily, published this year by Future Publishing, is the daily newspaper eagerly anticipated by thousands of attendees as the essential resource for show news.

Pitch your stories directly to the Show Daily editorial staff.
Contact Colin Campbell 650-872-1642, ccampbell@next-gen.biz

We understand that Oser Communications has contacted some of you to try to sell you advertising space in the Interactive Entertainment Daily News, which Osar intends to distribute around the E3Expo 2006. Please be advised that this publication is NOT connected with E3Expo 2006 or the ESA. E3Expo 2006 publishes the only official Show Daily each year in connection with E3Expo 2006. The Show Daily is the only publication that is distributed on LACC premises during the show (including in the LACC parking lot, entrance ways, sidewalks and inside the LACC) or at authorized hotels during the show. We wanted to bring this matter to your attention so that you do not inadvertently purchase advertising space in this publication under the false impression that it is connected with E3Expo 2006.

    Industry Invitations

Industry Invitations will be mailed to each confirmed exhibitor the second week of February. As an E3Expo 2006 exhibitor you have the unique opportunity to provide your key buyers, developers and analysts with a complimentary admission to the exhibit halls. You don’t have to request the Industry Invitations, each exhibiting company will receive 10 invitations per 100 sqft of exhibit space with a maximum quantity of 500 invitations per company.

We will again offer industry invitation recipients the opportunity to register in advance and register online! By offering this service we hope to minimize the on-site registrations lines and improve the process for your guests, while continuing to qualify all industry invitation recipients.

   Exhibitor Appointed Contractors

All exhibiting companies utilizing an Exhibitor Appointed Contractor must complete the “Notification of Intent to use Exhibitor Appointed Contractor” form found in the Exhibitor Service Manual behind the “Booth Construction & Storage/Signage” tab and submit it to Show Management as soon as possible. Show Management will send an Exhibitor Service Manual upon request along with updated information to all the EAC companies officially registered by the exhibiting company. This will ensure proper communication to all the EAC companies working on your booth. If you have already submitted information on your I&D House via the “Exhibitor Contacts” form, you are still required to submit the “Notification of Intent to use Exhibitor Appointed Contractor” form from the service manual.

    Official Security Contractor - RA Consulting

To provide our E3Expo exhibitors with a complete security program that not only meets but exceeds expectations, Show Management has again selected RA Consulting as our official security management provider for E3Expo in May 2006.

With a tremendous amount of experience in handling all aspects of trade shows and conventions nationwide, from pre-planning to on-site supervision, RA is your one stop shop to provide access control, crowd management, individual exhibit security, VIP escorts/transportation, and special event security (on or off-site). RA Security also provides rental equipment such as two-way radios and accessories, surveillance cameras, and safes. Please feel free to contact RA with any security/safety needs or concerns. Please contact Larry Lopez or Peter Alexan at the numbers/e-mail addresses below.

    Official International Freight Forwarder

E3Expo 2006 has appointed Rogers Worldwide as the official international shipper, customs broker, freight forwarder and provider of related transportation services. Their services include the arrangement of transportation from your country of origin to deliver either to the advance-receiving warehouse or show site, including all clearance formalities. Rogers Worldwide on-site staff will also assist in the outbound shipping of your exhibit material at the conclusion of the show. Significant savings may be achieved by shipping early. Please contact the Rogers Worldwide operations coordinator for this event for a cost estimate or further information about their services:
