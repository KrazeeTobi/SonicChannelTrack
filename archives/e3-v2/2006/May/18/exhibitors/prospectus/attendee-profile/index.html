Attendee Profile

Buyers. Analysts. Media.
They're All Here. And They're All Within Your Reach.

Do you know why E3 is regarded as such a big time business booster? Because no other event can equal the quality of the E3 audience. E3 is acknowledged as the must-attend event for everyone who matters in interactive entertainment. For the past 11 years, this is where new products debuted. The press is at E3 ready to report on what's happening. Retailers are at E3 ready to replenish their inventories with exciting new products for the holiday season and months to follow. Influential business analysts are at E3 ready to discover hot new market trends and be the first in the know on important industry news.

The people you want to reach most are all here at E3.

Who else attends? Virtually everyone you need to meet: the decision-makers in key sales and marketing positions; venture capitalists; industry and financial analysts; and representatives from the development, creative and publishing communities. There are outstanding business opportunities at E3 on a variety of fronts.

If you believe in numbers, you'll love this one: approximately 40% of all show attendees are retailers, distributors, financial analysts and media representatives. Here's another: 50% of all attendees made purchase decisions within six months of attending the 2005 show. In fact, a recent survey conducted by Exhibit Surveys Inc. revealed that E3 has the highest buying power of any show in the country within the trade show industry. According to the survey, E3 has the largest percentage of attendees who plan to purchase products exhibited at the show within six months.

E3. There’s no other way to reach such a highly qualified audience with such unparalleled buying power. This is the audience that can make your year. They want to see what’s new and what's next. Here's your opportunity to show them.
