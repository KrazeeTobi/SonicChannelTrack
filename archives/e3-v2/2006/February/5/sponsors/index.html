INTERNATIONAL MEDIA SPONSORS:
The Market for Home Computing and Video Games is the weekly trade publication for the interactive entertainment market, with subscribers in 57 countries.
Develop is the only international magazine dedicated to the entertainment software development community. It is an essential business tool for programmers, producers, artists, musicians and investors.
OFFICIAL SHOW DAILY SPONSOR:
Future is the leading US publisher of cutting-edge games, computing, action sports and musician magazines, which sell in excess of 2.2 million copies per month. Its market-leading games titles include Official Xbox Magazine, PSM: 100% Independent PlayStation 2 Magazine, PC Gamer, CheatPlanet.com, Next-Gen.biz and the forthcoming GamesRadar.
TRADE PARTNERS:
Animation Magazine reports on all aspects of animation and is the most comprehensive source about technology, film, television, gaming, commercials, events and trends. Our subscribers include animators, producers, directors, executives, broadcasters, distributors, publishers, recruiters, teachers and students. In addition, we provide subscribers with daily news emailed directly to them.
Computer Graphics World provides digital content creators information on the production tools and applications necessary to make them successful. The award-winning editorial covers the convergence of technology: what it means to film, TV, gaming, graphic arts and the web.
KidScreen is the leading online destination for game developers, providing the best place on the Internet to learn about and keep up with the world of game development. We feature over 1500 articles, hundreds of book and product reviews, industry event coverage, community forums, industry news, job listings, and more.
License!, the leading trade publication for intellectual property licensing and merchandising, provides award-winning editorial focusing on business deals, trends and marketing strategies on every category of consumer products. License! magazine's 25,000 subscribers are 100% personal direct request and representing every category in the industry and are active buyers.
Multiplayer.it is the first Italian website, related to Electronic Entertainment. Spreading worldwide coverages about the most important Media Events and a massive download area, offers a complete service to final users.
Mobile Games Analyst examines the entire mobile games sector, from service issues and the emerging mobile gaming value chain, to competitors strategies, to reveal how companies are making money from mobile games across the world. Each fortnightly online issue provides news analysis, substantial research and exclusive industry data which is not available from any other source.
