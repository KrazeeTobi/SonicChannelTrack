Exclusive Event Marketing Opportunities For Exhibitors.


When you become an exhibitor at E3 you become our partner in a very real and meaningful way. It is in our mutual interest that E3 be a highly rewarding marketing experience for your product and company.

As an exhibitor at E3, you'll benefit before, during and even after the show itself. For instance, the E3 advertising campaign that runs before the show helps draw thousands of industry professionals to our doors and to your exhibit. During the three days of the show, there are many creative opportunities available to help you stand out, and our Event Marketing team is ready to help you find the program that's just right for you. After the show, other specialized programs keep your message visible for a long time. The end result is you reach your key partners, important contacts and customers, and even others in the industry who were unable to attend in person.

How can you leverage your presence as an exhibitor at E3 2006?

We’ll help you create a tailored marketing program for the show to:

  • Generate press coverage for your new product releases
  • Create heightened awareness for your company brand
  • Drive qualified professionals to your exhibit
  • Impact thousands of attendees with your product message
  • Gain a tactical advantage over your competition

Event marketing gives you a whole host of ways to achieve your objectives for the show:

  • Customized Marketing Programs: The only limits are the borders of your imagination. You've got a great product - make the entire convention center the focus of your marketing communications plan. Think Big! We offer a host of options to fit even the most ambitious marketing objectives. Naturally, the programs that create the really big splashes are the first to go and must be reserved far in advance. Don't wait. Call today to lock in your ideal location.
  • On-Site Signage: Connect with the industry’s key people through more than 100 signage locations available to choose from. Options include banners, column wraps, ad walls, light boxes, custom floor space, door/floor/stair riser graphics, and more.
  • Sponsorships: Want a more targeted audience? Sponsorship programs from Badge Cords and Badge Inserts to VIP Retailer Gifts and Show Bags enable you to reach everyone at the show or just a small segment of the audience.
  • Exhibit Guide: The E3 Exhibit Guide is a one-stop reference of exhibitor lists, floor maps, shuttle schedules, product lists, conference and workshop schedules, speaker bios, and general show information. Run your ad in here and it will never leave the attendees’ hand.
  • Web Site Advertising: When E3 attendees seek information about the upcoming show, they go online. Shouldn’t you be there to meet them? Last year, tens of thousands of unique visitors to the e3expo.com site tallied millions of page views. E3expo.com averages over 10 million hits a month, and more than 120+ million page hits the month of the show.
