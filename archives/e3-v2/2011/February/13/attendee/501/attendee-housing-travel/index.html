Attendee Housing & Travel

E3 Expo Housing is now live. Please click here to begin.

Take advantage of a full range of locations and some of the best hotel rates in Los Angeles. No pre-payment is necessary to book a reservation; however, cancellation penalties will apply. Book online and receive immediate confirmation by email, as well as make changes to or update your reservation.

Travel Discounts

To make your air travel arrangements for E3 Expo 2011, please call the airlines directly using the Travel Discount information and codes below.

Airline Discounts are available on United Airlines and American Airlines. Reservations can be booked on-line or by calling the phone numbers listed below. When making your reservations, be sure to mention the discount numbers below or pass this information to your travel consultant to take advantage of these special E3 Expo fares.

Car Rental

Special rates have been arranged with Enterprise Rent-A-Car, for reservations please Contact Enterprise Rent-A-Car at 1-800-593-0505 using code 32C6606, or online by clicking here.

Super Shuttle Airport Transfers

The Super Shuttle is offering the discounts listed below to E3 Expo 2011 Exhibitors and Attendees. Please click here to make your reservation.

The Super Shuttle offers 15% off shared ride transfers, 10% off Business Class transfers, and 15% off all ExecuCar sedan transfers. These discounts are valid from all the local area airports (LAX, BUR, LGB, SNA & ONT) to the Downtown, Hollywood, Beverly Hills, Century City & Santa Monica hotels.

Airport Shuttle Available June 9, 2011

Airport shuttle will be offered on the last day of the show, June 9th, for $10.00. Tickets can be purchased outside West Lobby at the Shuttle Bus Information desk.

Los Angeles Convention Center

Located conveniently at the intersection of the Santa Monica and Harbor Freeways, the Los Angeles Convention Center offers a technologically advanced facility that combines incredible functionality in a unique Los Angeles atmosphere.

Amenities at the Los Angeles Convention Center include:
  • State-of-the-art power and date/network systems
  • Fiber Optic Network
  • Wireless internet
