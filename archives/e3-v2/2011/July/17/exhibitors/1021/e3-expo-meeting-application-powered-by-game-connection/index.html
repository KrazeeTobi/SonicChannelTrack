E3 Expo Meeting Application Powered by Game Connection

Enhance your E3 experience by taking advantage of the complimentary E3 Expo Meeting Application powered by Game Connection. The Meeting Application provides an exclusive match-making software that allows E3 exhibitors to network and schedule meetings weeks before the show begins.

Game Connection is a one-stop shop to meet face to face with dozens of targeted partners involved in all stages of the production pipeline, from concept to outsourcing, publishing, distribution and financing, all in 30-minute meetings. Game Connection’s unique and efficient Online Meeting Planner allows you to identify business partners, send meeting requests and arrange your schedule with people that matter the most to your business. The application is all about matchmaking and online scheduling that facilitates connections between exhibitors and attendees well before E3 begins.

Registering creates the opportunity to network with industry professionals by connecting with them through meetings scheduled during E3. The Meeting Application also showcases your products, expertise and services to a highly-qualified and highly-valued audience who are looking for the next big product or industry deal. Start making connections with key decision makers who can impact your future.
