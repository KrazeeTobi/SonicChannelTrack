Attendee Registration Policies

E3 Expo is a professional trade event and is not open to the general public. Individuals who are not able to document their direct, and current professional affiliation to the interactive entertainment industry are not qualified to attend.

Attendee Registration Pricing and Deadlines:

Type Description Before April 25, 2011 Beginning April 26, 2011
Expo Pass 3-day Show Floor Pass $400 $500
Industry Pass 3-day Show Floor Pass available to qualified Industry Professionals Free* Not Available

*For Qualified Industry Professionals ONLY

Industry Identification Requirements

To apply for free admission as a member of the interactive entertainment industry, attendees must submit two of the following credentials. At least one document must contain both the attendee’s name and the name of their company.

Acceptable forms of Industry ID:

  • Resale Tax Certificate
  • Insurance Card
  • Paycheck Stub
  • Company Credit Card (with Attendee’s name)
  • Business License
  • W-2 Form

International attendees should submit similar credentials unique to their country that confirm their employment within the interactive entertainment industry.

Age Requirement

Individuals under the age of 17 including infants will not be admitted to E3 Expo.

Badge Information

Attendees who have a U.S. mailing address and who are registered and confirmed by April 25, 2011, will receive their badge in the mail.

Attendees who have an address outside of the U.S. and/or are NOT registered and confirmed by April 25, 2011 must pick up their badge and badge holder on-site at Attendee Will Call located in the South Hall Lobby of the Los Angeles Convention Center.

All attendees are required to pickup their badge holders on-site and must present a valid government issued photo ID (driver’s license or passport).

International Attendees

Badges will NOT be mailed to international attendees, including Canadians. International guests can pick up their badges at the International Registration counter located on-site in the South Hall Lobby of the Los Angeles Convention Center. Translation services will be available.

Cancellations and Refunds

All registrations are non-refundable and non-transferable.

Attendees with Special Needs

Attendees who require special services should contact Show Management at 877-216-6264 to identify their special needs. All requests should be received by May 13, 2011, in order to ensure the availability of services.
