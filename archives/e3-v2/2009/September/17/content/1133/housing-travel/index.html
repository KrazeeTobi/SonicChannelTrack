Housing & Travel

To make all your travel arrangements for E3 Expo 2009 you must call the airlines directly using the Travel Discount information and codes below.

Take advantage of a full range of locations and some of the best hotel rates in Los Angeles. No pre-payment is necessary to book a reservation, however, cancellation penalties will apply. Book online and receive immediate confirmation by email, as well as make changes to or update your reservation. Please note: Shuttle bus service is available only to attendees booking through E3 Expo housing, and special bus passes will be issued to attendees who book through CMR.

Click here for Attendee Housing

To speak directly with an E3 Expo Travel specialist, please call 800.804.4337 or fax to 415.979.2270 beginning February 9, 2009. For calls originating outside the U.S., call 415.979.2295. If you prefer to mail or fax your housing request, click here to download the Attendee Housing form.

Click here to view the official hotel listing for E3 Expo 2009.

E3 Expo Shuttle Service: Please note that E3 Expo Shuttles are available only to attendees who reserve their accommodations through the E3 Expo Housing department.

Los Angeles Convention Center

Located conveniently at the intersection of the Santa Monica and Harbor Freeways, the Los Angeles Convention Center (LACC) offers a technologically advanced facility that combines incredible functionality in a unique LA atmosphere.

Many amenities at the LACC include:

  • State-of-the-art power and date/network systems
  • Fiber Optic Network
  • Wireless internet
  • Full Service Business Center
