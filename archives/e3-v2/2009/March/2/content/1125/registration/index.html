Registration

E3 Expo 2009 Attendee Registration

Welcome to Attendee Registration. Please note that E3 Expo is a trade-only event and is not open to the general public. Individuals who are not able to document their direct, and current professional affiliation to the interactive entertainment industry are not qualified to attend. If you are from the retail sector, you must be selected by your parent company to attend.

Registration Options


4 Ways to Register

To be considered for registration, two forms of Industry ID must accompany your application. At least one form of industry ID must contain both your name and the name of your company. Incomplete applications will not be processed. The Management of E3 Expo 2009 reserves the right to deny any applicant admission.


Online
Complete the online registration application and submit two forms of Industry ID.

Mail
To mail your registration, click here to download the registration form. Mail registration is available until May 1, 2009. Please mail the completed registration form along with two forms of Industry ID to:

E3 Expo 2009 Registration
4025 Fair Ridge Drive
Suite 250
Fairfax, VA 22033

Fax
To fax your registration, click here to download the registration form. Fax registration is available until May 1, 2009. Please fax the completed registration form along with two forms of Industry ID to: (866) 581-0214.

Phone
To register by phone, please call (877) 216-6263. A $10 service fee applies. Complimentary exhibit hall passes for Qualified Industry professionals are not available by phone.

ALL SALES ARE FINAL. Do not pay twice for this event. You will be charged for all registrations, and no refunds will be issued for duplicate registrations. All registrations are non-refundable and non-transferable. No Exceptions.


Industry Identification

Your registration will not be complete until you submit two forms of Industry ID. A list of acceptable forms of Industry Identification are:

  • Resale Tax Certificate
  • Company Credit Card (with Applicant's name)
  • Insurance Card
  • Business License
  • Paycheck Stub
  • W-2 Form

In order to apply for free admission as a member of the interactive entertainment industry, you must submit two of credentials listed above. Your registration will not be complete until you submit two forms of Industry ID. At least one form of industry ID must contain both your name and the name of your company. Incomplete applications will not be processed. The Management of E3 Expo 2009 reserves the right to deny any applicant admission.

Fax your industry ID to (866) 581-0214. Include your name, email address and telephone number on ALL documentation.

- OR -

Mail your industry ID to:

E3 Expo Registration
4025 Fair Ridge Drive
Suite 250
Fairfax, VA 22033

Badge Information

All E3 Expo attendees will be required to show a government issued photo ID (driver's license or passport) in order to pick up their badge holder on-site. Do not use a fictitious name. The name on your ID and the name on your badge must be the same.

If you are registered and confirmed by May 1, 2009 and have a U.S. mailing address, you will receive your badge in the mail and must pick up your badge holder on-site.

If you are registered and confirmed after May 1, 2009 or have an address outside of the U.S., you must pick up your badge and badge holder on-site at Attendee Will Call.

If you have questions concerning your registration, please contact Customer Service at E3Expo2009@rcsreg.com or call 877.216.6263. Outside the U.S. please dial 805.654.0171.

Please note: E3 Expo is a trade event. No one under 17 will be admitted (including infants). This policy is strictly enforced.
