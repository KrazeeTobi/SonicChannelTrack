Promotional and Media Relations Opportunities

Take advantage of the following opportunities to maximize your exposure at E3 Expo 2009 - before, during and after the show!

Media List – Free!

Check back in mid-April for the first installment of the E3 Expo 2009 pre-registered Media List. Please contact the E3 Expo 2009 Media Relations Team at media@e3expo.com with any additional questions.

Exhibitor Activities – Free!

Let the E3 Expo Media Team support and complement your PR efforts. Often times we are contacted by members of the media asking about E3 Expo's special events or needing a quote on a specific topic. By sending us your planned activities, we will be able to direct media right to you. To keep the E3 Expo 2009 Media Relations Team in the loop, email us at media@e3expo.com.

News Wall – Free!

Promote your company’s events and activities on E3 Expo’s News Wall located in the on-site Media Center. The News Wall serves as a convenient information resource for the thousands of media that pass through the Center during the show. (We can also include your news in our online Media Center!) The E3 Expo 2009 Media Relations Team is more than happy to help you at media@e3expo.com.

News Distribution

Business Wire is the official news release distribution service for E3 Expo. In addition, Business Wire will also be offering all exhibitors a free distribution of a 100-word exhibitor profile. Please email them directly at tradeshow@businesswire.com or call 310-820-8473 ext. 605 to learn more about all the distribution options.

Digital Media Kits

Virtual Press Office can transform your bulky, expensive and dated press kits into state-of-the-art, always-updated digital media kits. These kits are automatically linked to the show’s online Media Center. Please contact VPO for further information at sales@virtualpressoffice.com.

PR Contact Guide for Media

Each year we provide a list of exhibiting companies' PR contacts to qualifying journalists in advance of the show. Media use this helpful tool to contact exhibitors directly. To be included in the guide, please contact the E3 Expo 2009 Media Relations Team at media@e3expo.com.

Show Daily

The E3 Expo Show Daily is the daily newspaper eagerly anticipated by thousands of attendees for daily show news and a comprehensive resource of participating companies and their products.

  • Show Daily Advertising Opportunities
    Make your presence known by advertising in the Show Daily. The Show Daily operates like any other news outlet and all ad-buys must be coordinated through their advertising department. Contact Marci Yamaguchi Hughes at GamePro for further information. Deadline to submit Show Daily Advertising is May 5th.

  • The E3 Expo Official Show Daily
    The E3 Expo Official Show Daily, published this year by GamePro, is the daily publication eagerly anticipated by thousands of attendees as the essential resource for show news and is now accepting story pitches for consideration. Increase coverage of your company’s news and products by pitching your stories directly to the Show Daily editorial staff. Due to space limitations, it is strongly advised that you contact the Show Daily as soon as possible. The earlier you pitch, the better your chances of having your story idea accepted for final publication. Please contact George Jones or Mike Weigand for more information.

  • Show Daily Listing
    Provide vital product and contact information for complimentary placement in the E3 Expo 2009 Show Daily. Please contact exhibits@e3expo.com for more details. Deadline to submit Show Daily Listing is April 17th.

    • First Looks

    First Looks is E3 Expo's exclusive program designed to spotlight exhibitors showcasing never-before-seen titles and technologies prior to and during the show. If you have brand-new products making their debut at E3 Expo 2009, this is a great way to build buzz and media anticipation. Before the show, we will distribute a compilation of new product descriptions to qualified media and retail buyers. During the show, First Looks products are highlighted through a variety of dedicated on-site signage, as well as through a special First Looks product directory that is distributed to media and retailers, making it simple for them to locate exhibitors with debuting products.

    To include your company's debuting products in the First Looks Directory, please email a four-sentence description of each product you would like included. This Directory will first be sent out to media and buyers on May 19th, and an updated version will subsequently be distributed at the show. Be sure to include media contact information with each product description, and email your submission to Kristin Dacey.

    Please note: we reserve the right to edit any product submission longer than four sentences per product.

    Deadline to submit First Looks description is May 8th.

    Event Marketing and Sponsorship Opportunities

    Both the Los Angeles Convention Center and our host city overflow with advertising and sponsorship opportunities. No matter what you would like to accomplish during E3 Expo -- big or small -- you can make it happen through a wide variety of sponsorships and customized marketing programs. For further information, please contact Ellen Moorehead, 508-988-7830 (non ESA Member companies), or Deborah Paul, 508-424-4820, (ESA Member companies.

    Celebrities and Special Events

    Do you have a special guest appearance or event at your booth? Let Show Management help in making the execution flawless and unforgettable. We will help you to create a plan so problems don’t arise, such as attendees blocking the aisles or obstructing the visibility of another exhibitor. We can also pre-register celebrities for show badges and make special arrangements for entering and exiting the show. Please contact Jackie Vella for further informaition. For additional E3 Expo PR opportunities, please contact the E3 Expo Media Relations Team at media@e3expo.com.
