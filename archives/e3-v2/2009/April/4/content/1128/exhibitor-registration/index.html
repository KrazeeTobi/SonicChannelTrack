Exhibitor Registration

The people, the products, the place.

The Electronic Entertainment Expo (E3 Expo) is the North American event of the year for everyone involved in the global interactive entertainment industry. This must-attend event is all about the business, innovation, fun and excitement of the fastest-growing and most compelling sector of the entertainment industry: video and computer games.

E3 Expo is where the industry comes together, where start-ups mingle with corporate giants and where the demos and deals impact the interactive entertainment business for months to come. If your company is part of this dynamic industry, E3 Expo is where you need to be.

Exhibitor Profiles Include:

Console, PC, online, and mobile game publishing and developing, makers of video game hardware, technologies and peripherals, as well as qualified business partners.

Bringing the interactive entertainment industry together, in one place, at one time, E3 Expo 2009 is your best opportunity to connect with those that matter most to your bottom line.

The E3 Expo 2009 highly-qualified and targeted audience will include:

  • Publishers
  • Developers
  • Retail Buyers
  • International and U.S. Based Trade & Consumer Media
  • Industry and Investment Analysts
  • Venture Capitalists
  • Community Influencers

E3 Expo delivers the key players that can impact your future success.

Retail Buyers - they can make your year!

As retailers are making purchasing decisions for the holiday season, E3 Expo exhibitors are able to effectively and efficiently display their products to key retailers.

VIP Buyer Program
Extending invitations to only the most qualified members of the retail community, E3 Expo's VIP Retailer program ensures that the buyers with the most purchasing power are turning out in force to see your products.

Media - generating billions of media impressions worldwide!

News from the show is seen and heard around the globe. E3 Expo is not only the must-see event for the trade media covering the industry, but it commands the attention of global consumer press - everyone wants to know what all of the excitement is about! Journalists from a wide variety of game-enthusiast publications, both print and online, radio, and broadcast outlets will be looking for the next big story from the E3 Expo show floor, and as an exhibitor you have a unique opportunity to capture the media spotlight.

There are many great reasons for you to exhibit at E3 Expo 2009:

  • Reach a highly-qualified and targeted audience
  • Meet with key buyers planning purchases - helping you to realize your ROI & ROO
  • Network with key industry contacts
  • Benefit from the exposure of billions of media impressions
  • Observe the latest competitive products and trends
