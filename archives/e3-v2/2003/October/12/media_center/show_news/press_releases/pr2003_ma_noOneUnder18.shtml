E3 2003 Welcomes Interactive Entertainment Industry Professionals May 14 - 16

WHAT: E3 2003: Where Business Gets Fun, the world's largest trade event exclusively dedicated to showcasing interactive entertainment and educational software and related products, will open its doors at the Los Angeles Convention Center (LACC) in one week.
