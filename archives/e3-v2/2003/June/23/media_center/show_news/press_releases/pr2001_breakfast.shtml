***IMPORTANT MEDIA INVITATION***

Save the Date For The E3 2001 Media Briefing & Breakfast

WHAT: You are invited to attend the E3 2001 Media Briefing & Breakfast hosted by Interactive Digital Software Association (IDSA) President Doug Lowenstein. An annual tradition, this exclusive event offers media the opportunity to preview findings from the IDSA's yearly Video and Computer Game Consumer Survey and get an in-depth briefing on the state of the dynamic $6 billion interactive entertainment industry. Media attendees will receive a complimentary breakfast and an official E3 2001 media baseball cap.
WHEN: 7 a.m., Thursday, May 17, 2001 - Breakfast served
7:30 a.m., Thursday, May 17, 2001 - Presentation
WHERE: Los Angeles Convention Center, West Hall, Room 515 (2nd Floor)
WHO: Doug Lowenstein, president of the IDSA, the U.S. association representing computer and video game publishers and owner of E3.
WHAT ELSE: Media in attendance will have preferred seating for the keynote panel immediately following the briefing.

Keynote panelists include:
  • Robert J. Bach, senior vice president, games division and chief Xbox officer, Microsoft Corp.
  • Peter T. Main, executive vice president, sales and marketing, Nintendo of America Inc.
  • Kazuo Hirai, president and chief operating officer, Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc.
