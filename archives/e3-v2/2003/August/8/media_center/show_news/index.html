Business Wire
Business Wire is the leading electronic distributor of exhibitor trade show news releases and photos to media, analysts, online services and the Internet. For E3 2003, Business Wire has custom news and multimedia distribution packages and a special Web site dedicated to E3-related news releases. For more information, contact Leon Harbar or Jim Liebenau at 310/820-8473, ext. 602 or tradeshow@businesswire.com. Business Wire staff also will be available in the on-site newsroom of the Media Center at the show.

Virtual Press Office
The Virtual Press Office will be automating the on-site newsroom at E3 2003 by uploading exhibitor press materials (press releases, company and product backgrounders, photos, white papers, etc.) to a single homepage. In addition to the online materials, you may complete a profile to track areas of interest, create categories to filter releases and have news emailed to wherever you are. The VPO E3 service is completely free and online year round. If you have questions/suggestions, please email us at press@virtualpressoffice.com.
