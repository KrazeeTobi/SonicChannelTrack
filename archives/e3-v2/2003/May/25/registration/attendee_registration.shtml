Don't pay twice for this event. You will be charged for all registrations, and you will not be refunded for duplicate registrations. All registrations are non-refundable and non-transferable.

Receiving Your Badge
U.S. Attendees: For attendees who register on or before April 4, 2003 badges will be sent to you in the mail beginning March 15, 2003. Attendees who register after April 4, 2003 must pick up their badges at the Attendee Will Call counter located in the South and West Lobby areas of the Los Angeles Convention Center.

International Attendees: All international attendees (including Canadians) must pick up their badges at the International Attendee Registration Counter in South Hall beginning May 12, 2003.

All registrants must be prepared to show government issued photo I.D. (such as a driver's license or passport) in order to obtain the 2003 E3 badge and/or badgeholder required for entrance to the Exhibit floor.

If you have any questions about your registration, contact Customer Service at 877-216-6263, outside the U.S. please call 805-654-0171, or e3expo@rcsreg.com.
