Reach Consumers, Buyers, Analysts
and the Media in One Convenient Location
Nothing can boost your business like E3 because nobody can match the depth and quality of the E3 audience. For ten years running, everyone in the business of games has come to show off new and enhanced products and do business at E3. An interested and informed press corps is always ready to report what’s happening here. Motivated retailers come to build up their inventories of great new products for the next six to twelve months, including holiday season merchandise. Analysts attend E3 in record numbers to identify hot new market trends and be the first to hear other important industry news. All this means that E3 is truly a win-win-win-win situation.

The E3 audience is much more diversified than that though. Decision makers in key sales and marketing positions, venture capitalists, industry and financial analysts, and representatives from the development, creative, and publishing communities attend as well, providing outstanding business opportunities across the board.

You can rest assured that the people you need to see the most will be at E3. Almost a third of all show attendees are retailers, distributors, financial analysts and media representatives, and half of all attendees made purchase decisions within six months of attending the 2003 show. In fact, the results of a recent industry survey revealed that E3 has the highest buying power within the trade show industry. According to a survey by Exhibit Surveys Inc., E3 has the largest percent of attendees who plan to purchase products exhibited at the show within six months.

In short, there’s no better way to reach a highly qualified audience with the unparalleled buying power that can really make things happen for exhibitors. They come to E3 to see what’s next in this dynamic, rapidly changing industry. They come to get a sneak peek at the games that consumers will be buying, playing and talking about. They come to be “wowed” – and this is your chance to do just that.

Meet the Top Decision Makers
For an even more quantitative breakdown of the quality of the E3 audience, take a look at the Net Buying Influence (NBI), which identifies attendees who have purchasing influence for at least one of the products or services exhibited at any given show. The NBI for E3 is consistently high, so you can rest assured that first-rate decision makers are looking at your offerings.
