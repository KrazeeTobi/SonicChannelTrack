E3 2006 Call For Speakers

Deadline for submissions is November 18!

As a faculty member, you have the opportunity to educate and share your knowledge of what's going on in today's game industry with a core group of industry professionals. Over 2,000 dedicated attendees attend E3 not just for the excitement of the show floor, but also for this opportunity to improve their business and creative skills.

Our ground rules for participating in the Conference Program are simple and few:

  1. Your participation should be in the spirit of commitment to your peers in the computer game industry.
  2. All faculty members are responsible for their own travel and lodging. E3 Show Management does not offer any fees or remuneration for appearances on panels or in workshops.
  3. This is not a sales opportunity. Our audience, the moderator, and show management will not tolerate nor invite back anyone who uses this presentation time to sell themselves or their company.
  4. As an E3 Conference Program faculty member, we consider you a VIP guest of E3. Registration is complimentary and a speaker badge allows you to attend all conference sessions and gives you access to the show floor and all its activities. Your affiliation as a speaker will be used in all appropriate marketing materials.

If you're interested in this exciting opportunity, please complete the following electronic proposal form and submit by November 18th - and don't forget to visit our E3 site for show and program updates.
