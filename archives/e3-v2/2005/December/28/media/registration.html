Media Registration

Online media registration will be available December 15 through April 13. Only qualified working journalists from commercial news organizations may submit a registration request to receive a complimentary media badge for E3 2006.

NOTE: E3’s Early Access hours – only pre-registered media (media who have submitted credentials to the E3 Media Relations Team by April 13) will be allowed on the show floor during E3’s Early Access hours on Wednesday, May 10 from 9 – 11 a.m. We strongly encourage all media to take advantage of the online media registration to secure qualification by April 13.

Online Media Registration Verification
If you have already filled out a registration request form for E3 2006 and would like to check the status of your media registration, please visit our online media registration verification system.
