Westin PeachtreeCMR Discount Rate: $185sgl/$200dbl
210 Peachtree Street NE
Telephone: 404-659-1400
Facsimile: 404-589-7424

Since opening in 1976, the Westin Peachtree Plaza has been Atlanta's landmark hotel. The sleek glass cylinder, which towers seventy-three stories above downtown, is North America's tallest hotel. The five story atrium lobby is a favorite gathering place of Atlanta's business leaders. Accommodations include health and fitness center, business center and swimming pool.
