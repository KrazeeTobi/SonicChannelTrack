Atlanta Delivers
Start with over 750,000 contiguous square feet of exhibit space in ONE facility - the Georgia World Congress Center. Add easy access into Atlanta...lower labor rates for services than most U.S. cities...and plenty of hotel rooms within walking distance. Then add the presence of every top retailer in the business (95% of VIP retailers who attended in 1997 plan to return in 1998). It all adds up to a world-class event that's profitable and convenient for exhibitors and attendees alike.

Traveling Made Easy
Atlanta is easy to get to from most locations in the U.S. and overseas. Thirty-one airlines fly into Hartsfield Atlanta International Airport, the world's largest passenger terminal complex. Eighty percent of the U.S. is within a two-hour flight to the city, and 80 U.S. cities and many major international cities enjoy non-stop service to Atlanta.

An Affordable Destination
The official travel service company for E3, Convention Management Resources (CMR), has obtained exclusive discounts for E3 exhibitors on hotel, airline and car rentals. Call today to secure special travel rates for everyone in your company who needs to attend E3.

Hotel Accommodations
Atlanta is one of the most convenient and charming cities in the country. Take a short walk from the convention center to any one of 16 downtown hotels with nearly 9,000 rooms. Atlanta expanded its hotel space for the 1996 Olympics, and now has more than 55,000 guest rooms in 340 properties, from elegant, first-class business hotels to friendly, well-maintained mid-priced chains. In the evening, enjoy exquisite cuisine and southern hospitality at the many fine restaurants throughout the city.
