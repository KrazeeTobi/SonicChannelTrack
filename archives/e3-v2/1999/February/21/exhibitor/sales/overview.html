The Electronic Entertainment Expo (E3) is the premier annual event presented by the interactive entertainment industry for the interactive entertainment industry. Each year, E3 brings together the key players from around the globe who are shaping the future of this multi-billion dollar industry. Retailers and distributors who sell the products... talented developers who create them... media who report on them... investors who provide critical support. You'll showcase your products to tens of thousands of the most influential professionals in the industry at E3 '99.

E3 is all business -- admission is strictly limited to trade professionals in the interactive entertainment industry. The exhibit floor is dedicated solely to interactive entertainment, educational, on-line and special interest software and related products. No one in the industry can afford to miss E3. It's where the trends begin... where the connections are forged... where the deals are made.

Grab Your Share of the Booming Interactive Entertainment Market with an Exhibit at E3

Interactive entertainment just keeps getting hotter!

Already in the first six months of 1998, sales are up over 30%. Last year, the U.S. interactive entertainment software industry had record revenues of $5.6 billion. Sales outside the U.S. add another $5 billion to this robust market. All market indicators point to continued growth, as the fourth quarter traditionally accounts for more than 50% of annual sales.

The industry is steadily expanding to reach new audiences as older adults, women and girls are joining the ranks of people who list interactive entertainment as one of their top leisure-time activities. Breakthrough technologies also contribute to increased sales. Next-generation consoles, DVD and on-line gaming all create new revenue streams as they increase their market penetration. No wonder interactive entertainment is now the fastest-growing segment of the U.S. entertainment industry, outstripping even the motion picture industry.

In an industry as dynamic and fast-changing as interactive entertainment, an exhibit at E3 places you at the center of the marketplace, letting you see what's new and be seen by everyone who is important in the industry. Establish your presence in the market and increase awareness of your company and products among your key audiences in three days of intensive marketing activity.

Of course, E3 introduces you to the buyers who are driving the market. VIP retailers are a select group of retailers who buy for 80% of the interactive entertainment market. These power buyers are present at E3 to find the products that will fill their shelves in the coming months.

  • Ninety-five percent of retailers rank E3 as the most valuable show they attend.
  • One hundred percent of retailers attending E3 are actively involved in purchase decisions; 72% authorize purchases, 16% recommend purchases and 8% specify purchases.
  • Sixty-two percent intend to purchase products seen at E3 within six months. (Statistics based on 1998 Market Perspectives Survey.)

The payback is substantial. You'll gain access to new retail channels in the U.S. and abroad. You'll be positioned to garner invaluable attention from thousands of media representatives covering the show. You'll have the opportunity to forge relationships with investors, developers and other influential industry players vital to your continuing success.

Make May 13, 14 and 15 the most productive days of 1999 - spend them exhibiting at E3!
