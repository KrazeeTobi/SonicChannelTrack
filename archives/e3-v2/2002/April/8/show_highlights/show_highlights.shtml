Enter the Electrifying World of E3 Expo
After seven successful years, E3 is acknowledged as the must-attend, must-exhibit event for everyone who matters in interactive entertainment. Over 62,000 of the world's most influential retailers, distributors, developers, investors and media gather here for three days of intensive deal-making. There's nothing else like it in the world. The most products, the biggest crowd, the hottest action: E3 brings it all together under one roof.

E3 is the most fun you can have and still call it work! Test-drive hot titles for all the new platforms, including GameCube, Xbox and PS2. Plus, check out the latest in PC games and advances in wireless and on-line games.

Explore the dazzling possibilities of the newest technology, learn about market forces, open your mind to fresh options--strolling the aisles at E3 is an education in itself. Better yet, attend the E3 conferences and workshops for incisive instruction in dozens of topics.

The E3 educational program caters to the very specific needs of developers, marketers and start-up executives. 2002 is a "don't miss" year--explore where the industry is going next.

You'll find unbiased guidance from industry experts on a multitude of topics, including broadband adoption, wireless and online gaming, and the next generation of massively multiplayer online role-playing titles. You'll also gain insights on starting your own development studio, recruiting and retaining talent and finding investors.

Look for details on sessions and speakers in the Conference Program section after the New Year.
