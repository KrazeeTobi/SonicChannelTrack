Is the show open to the public?
No, E3 is a trade event and only professionals from the industry will be allowed to attend.

[top]

I would like to bring my child to the show. Is there an age restriction?
E3 is open to trade only. No one under the age of 18 (including infants) will be admitted. THIS RULE IS STRICTLY ENFORCED.

[top]

When and where is the show?
The show is being held May 22-24, 2002 at the Los Angeles Convention Center in Los Angeles, California. Please note: For the first time in E3 history, the show will run from Wednesday through Friday due to the Memorial Day holiday. Click here for general show information.

I have pre-registered. When will I receive my badge?
For attendees who register on or before April 12, 2002, badges will be sent to you via U.S. mail beginning on March 18, 2002. If you registered after April 12, 2002, you must pick up your badge at the Attendee Will Call South Hall counter at the Los Angeles Convention Center.

International Attendees: International attendee badges will not be mailed in advance (excluding Canadians). International Attendees can pick up their badges at the International Attendee Registration counter at the Los Angeles Convention Center Starting Monday, May 20 at 8:00 AM. You must be prepared to show a government issued photo id in order to pick up your badge.
