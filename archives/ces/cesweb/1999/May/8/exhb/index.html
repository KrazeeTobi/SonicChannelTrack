Meet the Millennium at CES
Exhibiting at CES is a yearly ritual for over 1,500 companies every year. Why? Because CES offers you BUYERS. Over 40,000 of them, all prepared to buy. Consumer electronics sales are expected to top $70 billion this year. CES buyers represent over $68 billion in purchasing power!

Workstyles and lifestyles are converging and changing at a pace never seen before. Notebook and hand-held computers, mobile phones, remote access, portable printers and GPS were once exotic. Now, they're standard equipment.

This spells opportunity for you, the consumer electronics professional.

Savvy Buyers and Consumers

Where do you find the buyers and consumers of new technologies and products?

At 2000 International CES, January 6-9, 2000 in Las Vegas, Nevada. It's where savvy buyers and consumers look for technologies that will enhance their lifestyles. Technological advances occur almost daily, from semi-conductor chips to home theater to DTV, DSS, wireless and video. Buyers want to work these products into their sales and distribution channels. And they need you at CES to do it!

  • IT Buyers
  • Corporate (non-retail) buyers
  • International Professionals
