Forecast Penetration
of Digital Sets

Penetration, First Eight Years:
Analog vs. Digital TV
  • CEMA conservatively forecasts penetration of digital TV sets will reach 30% by the year 2006.
  • The first 10 million sets will be purchased from 1999 to 2003.
  • The next 10 million will be sold in 2004 and 2005.
  • Annual unit sales will exceed 10.8 million in the year 2006.
  • In comparison, color TV penetration rose to only 10% during the first eight full years on the market.
  • Penetration of VCRs reached 30% in their first eight years on the mass market.
  • CD players reached 30% penetration in their first eight years.
  • Beyond 2006, digital TV penetration will increase even more rapidly.
