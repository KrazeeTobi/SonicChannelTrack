1999 Exhibitor Info

1. Exhibit Space Investment
A special rate is given to exhibitors participating in priority space selection. This rate is valid from January 8 - 11, 1998. Exhibitors submitting applications after January 11, 1998 are invoiced at the standard rate.

Standard Rate
Open Booth Space $29.00 per sq. ft.
"M" Rooms (On Exhibit Floor) $33.00 per sq. ft.
Outdoor Space* $10.50 per sq. ft.

*(the rate only applies if contracted for 400 square feet or more of indoor exhibit space.)

CEMA Members will receive a $2.00 per nsf discount off of the above priority space selcction rates or $1.00 per nsf discount off the above standard rate.

  • Company identification sign (7"x 44")

For 400 square feet and over (Facility restrictions apply):

  • One (1) hour hanging sign assistance

  • Light gray stucco walls
  • 12-foot high ceilings

Main Level (N109 - N114) These permanent rooms contain the following:

  • Purple/mauve/gray mix carpeting
  • Light gray stucco walls
  • 20-foot high ceilings

Upper Level (N201-N250) These permanent rooms contain the following:

  • Purple/mauve/gray mix carpeting
  • Light gray carpet on inside and outside walls with green graphic stucco walls
  • 12-foot high ceilings

Sands Exhibit Rooms will include the applicable complimentary exhibitor services listed in section II.

  • Blue/pink/purple/gray mix carpeting
  • Light rose walls
  • 13' to 15' high ceilings

LVCC Exhibit Room Complex and Sands Meeting Room: exhibitors may not affix anything to the walls in a manner causing damage to the surface. All exits must be kept clear. Storage closets are not usable and doors are not removable.

Modular Demonstration rooms include:

  • One(1) smoke detector
  • One (1) fire extinguisher
  • One(1) door with lock
  • Four (4) light tracks with two (2) halogen lights (150 watt bulbs) on each track
  • One (1) 2,000 watt duplex electrical outlet

All interior and exterior walls are of a neutral color (graphics may be used on outside walls). Carpet is not included, but may be ordered at a special price to include installation and removal.
