Cisco Systems
In-House Design Team
Cisco Internet Home Gateway Cisco iHG 2005
The Cisco Internet Home Gateway 2005 enables smart homes by bringing high speed ADSL connection to the home and distributing that access within the home with no new wiring. Over single pair of telephone wire, the CISCO iHG can support 4 voice lines—each with a unique telephone number, 1 Ethernet port, and 4 Home PNA ports. The seamless integration of voice and data over single pair of copper wire, at very high bandwidth, unleashes multimedia applications, voice communications, home networking, and an endless list of new world applications. The Cisco iHG 2005 is poised to grow with new features and capabilities to enable the home of the future.
