Call For Speakers
You can secure a speaking opportunity at the 2002 International CES for your company or your client by answering the 2002 International CES CALL FOR SPEAKERS.

More than 122,000 industry professionals from 120 countries came to Las Vegas to see the cutting edge in digital technology at the 2001 International CES, featuring four days of conference sessions, keynote speeches and exhibits of consumer technology. It is the showcase for financial analysts, venture capitalists, developers, consumer technology manufacturers, corporate buyers and retailers. A speaker slot in the CES conference program is your opportunity to reach this audience.

The 2002 International CES will host approximately 100 conference sessions addressing wireless technology, home networking, mobile electronics, emerging technologies, retailing trends, e-commerce, digital content and much more. Please see the CALL FOR SPEAKERS entry form for a full list of conference subjects.

To be considered for a speaker opportunity at the 2002 International CES you can either submit your entry online or in hard copy by printing the PDF file (one applicant per form). YOU MUST USE THIS FORM. Incomplete submission forms or entries not submitted on this form will NOT be considered in the review process.

All submissions should include a 50-75 word biography, a photo, and at least 3 elements that you can address should you be chosen to speak at the 2002 International CES.

Please contact Conference Coordinator Kartraice D. Hooper at khooper@ce.org if you have questions regarding the submission process.

Thank you in advance for your entry.

NOTE: All entrants will be contacted by August 31, 2001.

1. I submitted a Call For Speakers form, when will I be notified if I was chosen to speak at CES?

All Call For Speakers entries will be answered by August 31, 2001.

Return to Question List


2. How are speakers selected?

Speakers are selected based on their topic knowledge, industry knowledge and the ability to identify and recruit other experts from the topic area. They must be able to define, develop, and deliver an interesting, unbiased, educational and technical program.

Return to Question List


3. Does CES provide speaker honorariums?

Unfortunately, CES does not provide speaker honoraiums.

Return to Question List


4. Does CES cover speaker travel and hotel expenses?

No. CES speakers are responsible for their own hotel and travel expenses.

Return to Question List


5. Who should I contact to assist me with hotel and travel arrangements?

We recommend that you make your travel arrangements by calling Housing On-Line directly at 1.800.397.0939. You may also contact them via Email at hol@pgi.com or by going to their website at http://www.houseol.com.

Return to Question List


6. Are panelists to bring laptops loaded with their PowerPoint presentations or will this be taken care of by CES?

All panelists who wish to show a PowerPoint presentation must provide their own laptop.

Return to Question List


7. Do I register myself for CES?

No, as a speaker at CES, you will automatically be registered.

Return to Question List


8. Is there speakers' lounge on-site where I can practice my presentation?

Yes, there will be Speaker Ready Room located in the Las Vegas Convention Center in (room TBD). Speaker ready rooms will be equipped with a computer, access to the Internet, fax machine, photocopier (for light photocopying only), telephones and light refreshments.

Return to Question List


9. You requested my bio. Will it be published in any show materials?

If you are selected to speak your biography will be posted on the CES Web site and will be used to introduce you at your session.

Return to Question List


10. When will I receive my show badge?

Your badge will be available in the Speaker Ready Room (location TBD).

Return to Question List


11. What A/V will be available in the conference sessions?

The standard A/V in each conference session will be:
  • LCD Data/ Data Projector
  • Podium and podium microphone
  • Panelist microphones
  • 8 x 8 Screen
  • VGA Switch Box
All other A/V equipment must be ordered and approved in advance. A/V equipment cannot be ordered on-site.

Return to Question List


12. I'm a speaker and I would like to attend several conference sessions. I see that many CES sessions require a fee. As a speaker, do I have to pay?

All speakers will be given up to 5 complimentary tickets.* You will be able to attend up to 5 separate conference sessions of your choice.

*Some restrictions apply.

Return to Question List


13. I'm a speaker at CES and I would like a colleague to attend my conference session. I'm aware of the cost of the session, but he only wants to attend my session, is that possible?

Your 5 complimentary speaker tickets may be given at your discretion to fellow colleagues.

Return to Question List


14. I'm very eager to hear my panel discussion. Where can I get an audiotape?

All CES Speakers will receive an audiotape of their conference sessions within a month of the show. If you require a copy immediately, you may purchase audiotapes on-site at the tape sales booth in the Grand Lobby of the LVCC.

Return to Question List


15. Is using the CES slide template required for the presentation? Is there any way we can use our own slides?

No, you are not required to use the CES template, but we strongly encourage that you do so.

Return to Question List


16. I want to provide handouts to those attending my conference session. Does CES handle production?

CES is happy to produce handouts for conference sessions, but we must receive the handout by December 1, 2001. Speakers are responsible for making copies if the handout is not received by CES before December 1, 2001.

Return to Question List


17. I'm a speaker for CES and I would like to have access to your press list.

Unfortunately, we do not distribute that information to CES Speakers.

Return to Question List


18. What date and time is my session? Where is it being held?

CESWeb lists all of the session times and locations through an online conference search tool.
