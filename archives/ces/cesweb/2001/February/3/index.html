International CES - The World's Largest Annual Technology Tradeshow

With the new expansion of the Las Vegas Convention Center, the 2002 CES will have room to host exhibitors all under one roof, making it easier for attendees to see the entire show. More than 1,040 companies have signed up for more than 1.1 million square feet of 2002 CES exhibit space. The show floor will be filled with the most exciting technologies in consumer electronics.

2001 CES Highlights Digital Technology

More than 122,000 industry professionals from 120 countries came to Las Vegas to see the cutting edge in digital technology at the 2001 International CES, featuring four days of conference sessions, keynote speeches and exhibits of consumer technology.
