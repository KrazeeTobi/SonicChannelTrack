Top Internet Resources for Consumer Technology

Getting Connected: So you're interested in learning the basics about buying and connecting a multitude of consumer electronics products - or maybe you have some specific questions about just one or two. The Consumer Electronics Association has compiled a Buying Guide to help you do just this. Lots of ePublications to choose from here. www.ce.org/epublications

Buying a PC: It's easier than ever before. Here are some how-tos for the novice.www.techtv.com

Digital Photography: You're not exactly a serious videographer, but you're interested in getting a good camcorder at a good price? Read all about the leading models. Send your questions to "Ask Ida" or talk with the experts at etown. www.etown.com

DTV: Everything you need to know about digital television all in one place. www.DTVweb.org

MP3: Still don't know what it is, exactly? Not to worry. Read all about it at TechTv. www.techtv.com

DVD: You've given up on learning how to program the VCR, now you want to buy a DVD. eTown has all of the information you need. www.etown.com

Computer terms: Don't know what it means, don't know where to go to look it up: Lots of these are related to technologies connected to consumer electronics. Here's our favorite dictionary site for computer terms: www.ZDWebopedia.com Even includes links to related articles, pages and primers.

Consumer Electronics Association (CEA)
The Consumer Electronics Association is dedicated to serving the manufacturers, retailers and consumers involved in the development, manufacturing and distribution of audio, video, mobile electronics, communications, information technology, multimedia and accessory products as well as related services sold through consumer channels.

Other CEA websites you may find of interest:
Antennaweb. Find the type of outdoor antenna to use to receive your local television broadcast channels. Whether the antenna you need is for use with a home satellite system, high-definition television or a traditional analog set, this site, based on geographical maps and signal strengths, will show you what you need to know to buy the right antenna.

TechHome. Your complete source for information on products that will make your home safer, more comfortable and more enjoyable. TechHome will show you how to create an integrated home system using products that you may already own.
