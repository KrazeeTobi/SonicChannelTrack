ExactSet™ Travel Alarm RM-826
The Oregon Scientific ExactSet™Travel Alarm Clock is the smallest-ever "atomic clock" that automatically sets its own time, day and date. It features an internal radio receiver and antenna that automatically tunes the time signals from the US Atomic Clock, one of the most accurate timepieces in the world, operated by the US Commerce Department in Boulder, CO. Its small travel-alarm form factor makes it ideal for travel and for bedside display. It can be set to any of the four US time zones and automatically adjusts for daylight Savings Time and Leap Year.
