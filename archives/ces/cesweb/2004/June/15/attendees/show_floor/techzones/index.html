Connected Home Entertainment
LVCC South 1-2

Learn more about the benefits of whole-house audio and video. Whether you're a homebuilder, distributor or installer, check out the innovative, hard-wired, professionally installed systems in the CONNECTED HOME ENTERTAINMENT TechZone. Demo new multi-room networked entertainment, including audio, structured wiring, whole-house video, no-new-wires and wireless products.

What to expect
Plan to meet manufacturers or suppliers of products such as:
  • Audio, structured wiring or home networking solutions
  • Whole-house video entertainment systems, including video source, delivery and display
  • Video adapters and set-top boxes, including networked PVRs and thin clients
  • Multi-room audio equipment and source controllers
  • Amplifiers and in-ceiling, in-wall and outdoor speakers
  • Networked monitors and displays
  • Infrared and other wireless distribution systems
  • Music storage and management systems
  • User interfaces

SD Card

More memory in a smaller chip! The Secure Digital (SD) Memory Card has a powerful influence on advanced digital storage technology of information, entertainment and communications.   • SD-enabled products
  • Products compatible with SD memory cards

Ultrawideband
Innovations Plus

Wireless connectivity has enabled a new mobile lifestyle filled with conveniences for mobile computing users at work and home. Ultrawideband (UWB) technology offers a solution for the bandwidth, cost, power consumption and physical size requirements of next-generation consumer electronics devices. UWB enables wireless connectivity with consistent high data rates across multiple devices and PCs, making it easy to create highspeed WPANs that connect devices.

What to expect
Plan to meet manufacturers or suppliers of products such as:
  • Consumer electronic manufacturers
  • Silicon/software developers
  • Personal computing companies
  • Industry organizations/ecosystem participants
