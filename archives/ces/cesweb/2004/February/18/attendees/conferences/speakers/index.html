Speak at the 2005 International CES
Thank you for your interest in speaking opportunities for the upcoming 2005 International CES.
The International CES conference program, the world’s largest industry education forum, exchanges ideas among presenters and participants and helps attendees discover the newest trends, strategies and profit opportunities in the consumer technology and IT industries.

The official 2005 International CES Call for Speakers starts this May. If you would like to be notified when the call for speakers opens, please e-mail Kartraice D. Hooper.

Because of the number of sessions and the need for a balanced program, a limited number of presenters will be accepted. We consider all proposals based on industry representation, professionalism, prior public speaking experience and session topic relevance.
