Electrical Service Information
Trade Show Electric (TSE) is the exclusive provider of electrical service in the Las Vegas Convention Center (LVCC) exhibit halls and meeting rooms, as well as the Las Vegas Hilton.

General overhead hall lighting is provided to exhibitors without charge. Where electrical services for special lighting, outlets, spotlights and the operation of exhibitor’s products are required, please complete the electrical order form included in this section. Forms should be completed and returned as soon as possible. If 24- hour electrical service is required in your booth, please be sure to order such service. A discount applies to advance orders. See individual order forms for prices.

Meeting Room Exhibitors: Existing electrical service is limited to installed outlets in each room. To utilize these outlets, exhibitors should order service and labor from TSE Electrical.

TSE ELECTRICIAN LABOR RATES (LVCC, Hilton)
Straight Time - $79.40 per hour
Overtime* - $150.45 per hour
*Before 8:00 a.m. and after 5:00 p.m. weekdays and all hours Saturday, Sunday, and Holidays.

The TSE electrical information brochure and order forms are located online at www.GESinterkit.com.

Telephone, Internet / Network Service (LVCC Exhibitors)
NEW THIS YEAR:
  • Anyone showing up at CES with devices/computers that do not have the latest virus definitions, operating system patches, etc. and cause network problems will be disconnected from the network until their devices/computers are free of problems. SmartCity will have automatic port monitoring that will close down ports when it detects a problem. SmartCity should also have CDs with the latest fixes that they can give to customers to fix their devices.

  • Planning on showcasing wireless products or services in your booth this year?
    As the exclusive provider of wireless services at the Las Vegas Convention Center, Smart City requires all exhibitors showcasing wireless products and services to contact them three weeks in advance of the show in order for Smart City to engineer a cohesive operating network. Due to the extensive coverage Smart City provides for the facility and the high number of exhibitors that showcase wireless at CES, this a necessary step to ensure that everything runs smoothly.

    Exhibitors are not permitted to set up their own access points and/or run their own wireless network if they are not specifically showcasing or demonstrating wireless as part of their exhibit. This is also a necessary step in order eliminate interference with Smart City’s network and other wireless exhibitors.

    Questions? Download the Wireless Internet Information Sheet(pdf) or contact Smart City at (888) 446-6911, (702) 943-6000 or snewcomb@smartcity.com.

Smart City is the exclusive provider of Telephone, Internet and Data Network service in the Las Vegas Convention Center. Contact Smart City at (800) 446-6911, (702) 943-6000 or e-mail snewcomb@smartcity.com

Orders placed and paid for after December 9, 2003 are not guaranteed by the opening of the Show. You may pick up your telephone instrument(s) at the Exhibitor Service Center in your exhibit hall. Telephone service will be disconnected at the close of the show on Sunday, January 11, unless you notify the appropriate telephone supplier otherwise. Telecommunications, network service and antenna drops may be ordered using the Smart City forms in this section.

A $75 per line late charge for Telephone service and a $250 per line late charge for Internet/Network service will be applied by Smart City for lines ordered and paid for later than December 9, 2003. There is an additional late charge if you order on-site or after move-in has started (see order form for details).

Each phone line ordered comes with an actual instrument (telephone) that is yours to keep. If you would like to use your own instrument in order to reduce costs, note this on your telephone service order form by ordering the line only. This applies to single phone line only, not multi-line orders.

IMPORTANT: Exhibitors are prohibited from personally distributing hard wire phone service to other exhibiting companies/booths. The work must be done by Smart City.

Downloadable order forms for telephone and internet service are located above.
