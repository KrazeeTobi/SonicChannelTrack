Special Displays

Experience CEA: One Digital World
 LVCC Grand Lobby
Come and see what CEA does the other 51 weeks of the year. Featuring 34 HDTV sets, a 20-foot CE industry timeline, the all-new CES Stage, plus interactive electronic gaming and digital imaging exhibits, the Experience captures the excitement of CES from the minute you walk in the Grand Lobby.
Download a map (pdf) of Experience CEA: One Digital World and learn more about what this new display offers, including:

  • Accessorize Industry Promotions
    See just how much accessories bolster the bottom line and improve the technology experience of every consumer. Plus, learn about AntennaWeb.org and preview CEA's Online Connections Guide.
  • Best of Innovations Awards Showcase
    Experience the highest form of genius. Now in its seventh year, the Best of Innovations Awards are given to the most highly honored products in all facets of the consumer technology industry.
  • CEA Stage
    There's never a dull moment on the CEA Stage. Gameshows, contests, awards, interviews from industry notables and more. See the lineup.
  • CEA Training: Real-time Retail Training for the Real World!
    CEA will unveil a comprehensive online training program for front-line retail staff. Check out CEKnowhow.com, the series of FREE training modules for wireless communications, audio, mobile electronics and home networking. Brief demonstration sessions will also take place on the CES Stage throughout the show.

  • The CES Store, sponsored by
    Gear, gifts and other specialty items are available at the CES Store. Stock up on 2004 International CES clothes and buy your favorite high-tech products here or at the CES Store located in the LVCC, Upper South Hall.

  • TechHome Demo
    Displaying the home networking technology for the kitchen, bedroom, utility room and recreation room, CEA's TechHome Demo tours the country to demystify home technology for consumers.

  • TechKnow Overload
    CEA's mock-up of the ultimate college dorm room arrives at CES from a national college tour to showcase the latest in audio, wireless and home theater technology to the Generation Y market. Visit www.tkotour.com.

  • TechnoCar
    TechnoCar generates excitement for aftermarket automotive electronics and promotes quality installation by MECP-certified professionals with a mock car interior loaded with the latest in mobile technology.

  • Technology is a Girl's Best Friend Showcase
    With 73 percent of female consumers involved in CE purchasing decisions, CEA's TechGirl program examines the needs of this lucrative market. The program includes an exclusive product showcase, a conference SuperSession, and a section of the CES store. Read more.

  • Teen Tech Campaign
    Tap into new trends in the 12- to 18-year-old age bracket. The Teen Tech Campaign includes recent research to shed considerable light on market forces and the buying patterns of this tech-savvy group.























Academy of DTV Pioneers
 Cast your vote at the HDTV Sports Bar
Each year the Academy of DTV Pioneers honors excellence in all aspects of digital content development and delivery, analog to digital transition leadership, and the manufacture of HDTV. Make sure to stop by the HDTV Sports Bar to cast your vote in the People's Choice Award for favorite HDTV program.






Acura: Official Automotive Sponsor of the 2004 International CES
 LVCC Silver Exhibits
If you haven't experienced the Acura TL, now's your chance. As the official automotive sponsor of the 2004 International CES, Acura will feature the 270-hp Acura TL for demonstrations of technological firsts, including Bluetooth® hands-free wireless technology and the world's first multi-channel surround-sound system with DVD-Audio—both standard. Visit www.acura.com.






Audio Café
 LVCC Silver Exhibits, Booth #71428
To help attendees realize the advances in digital surround sound, CEA's Audio Division is hosting its first Audio Café at CES. Relax, have a cup of java, and experience the power of surround sound.






2004 Consumer Electronics Hall of Fame
 The CE Hall of Fame honors the pioneers that have made the consumer electronics industry what it is today. New inductees will be showcased at www.CE.org/HallofFame as well as throughout the Las Vegas Convention Center during the show.






HDTV Sports Bar
 LVCC, Upper South Hall
Sit back and relax while taking in the best in high definition sports programming at the HDTV Sports Bar, located in the lobby of the upper level South Hall. Also be sure to cast your ballot for the DTV Academy's People's Choice Award for favorite HDTV program.






Innovations 2004 Design and Engineering Awards Showcase
 LVCC Silver Exhibits
Cut to the chase. See the innovations to top all innovation—in the annual competition that culminates at the International CES. See the year's best designed and engineered products in consumer technology on display in the Silver Exhibits.
Press Preview Reception: Tuesday, January 6, 5-8 p.m.






NextGen04 Demonstration Home
 LVCC Silver Exhibits
Sponsored by CEA's TechHome Division, the NextGen04 Demonstration Home illustrates the practical application of home automation and whole house broadband to the next generation of home buyers.






2004 Select Products MECP Installer Challenge
 LVCC Gold Exhibits
Come watch as the best-of-the-best installers compete in this four-day mobile installation contest to build the best car.



  

TechTV's Best of CES Awards
 LVCC Grand Lobby
TechTV's Best of CES Awards honor the most outstanding new consumer technology products that debut at the International CES. See which products are deemed the best when the winners are announced online and on the CES Stage in the Grand Lobby during the show.






NEW!  U.S. Export Pavilion
 Las Vegas Hilton Ballroom, Booth #54003
If you're interested in international trade, visit the U. S. Export Pavilion. Meet several U.S. government agencies who can provide assistance with international services such as market research, financing, regulations, locating business partners and shipping. This is a good opportunity for small and medium-sized companies to learn how the U.S. government can help you increase your international profits or get started in international trade.
