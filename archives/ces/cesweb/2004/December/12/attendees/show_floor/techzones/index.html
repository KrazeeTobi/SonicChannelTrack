MultiMediaCard Association
Innovations Plus
Sponsor(s): MMCA

The MultiMediaCard Association (MMCA) (http://www.mmca.org/) is the open standard memory card organization, promoting worldwide adoption of postage-stamp size, removable storage cards designed for mobile phones and digital imaging. These convenient, reliable, rugged and lightweight standardized data carriers store up to 1 Gbyte, or approximately 640,000 book pages.  The cards are fast for excellent system performance; energy efficient for prolonged battery life in portable products; and cost-efficient for use in systems sold at consumer price points.  

What to expect
Meet manufacturers and suppliers of:
  • Removable solid-state memory solutions for mobile applications, such as MP3 music players, portable video games, laptop computers, personal digital assistants (PDAs), mobile telephones and digital cameras
  • ROM technology for read-only applications
  • Flash technology for read/write applications
  • energy efficient products for prolonged battery life in portable products
  • Digital communications devices
  • Internet music players
  • Digital video cameras
  • Global positioning systems
  • Security devices
