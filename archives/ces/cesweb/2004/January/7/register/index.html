The 2004 International CES is just around the corner!
Free advance registration for the 2004 International CES is closed, but don't worry - you can still attend the show! Once you arrive in Las Vegas for the world's largest consumer electronics show, you may purchase an Exhibits Plus Pass for only $75.00 (U.S.). Conference sessions will still be available at on-site rates. Please visit one of our many on-site registration areas and we will be happy to help.

If you are a 2004 International CES Exhibitor and still need to register your booth personnel or pick up your badges, you may do so onsite. Please visit our exhibitor registration areas.

If you have questions or need assistance, please contact the International CES Customer Service by e-mail at CESinfo@CE.org or by phone at 1-866-233-7968 to process your request. (Outside the U.S., call 011-301-631-3983).

Thank you and we'll see you in Las Vegas!
