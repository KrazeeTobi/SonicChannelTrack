Maximize your marketing and sales momentum
The Consumer Electronics Association (CEA) offers many events and programs to spur the consumer technology industry all year long.
Take advantage of:
  • Membership. CEA members enjoy many benefits, including reduced exhibit rates at the International CES. Visit www.CE.org/join_cea.

  • Events. CEO summits, webcasts and industry forums keep you in touch with market trends and industry executives. Visit www.CE.org to view upcoming events.

  • Training. New online training from CEA helps retail sales professionals increase their product knowledge, selling skills, and identify customers' needs. Visit www.CEKnowHow.com.
