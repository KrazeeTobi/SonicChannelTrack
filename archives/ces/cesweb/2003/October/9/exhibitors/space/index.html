Exhibit Space
At the International CES, global business leaders form new relationships at the one event that embraces the high-tech demands of consumers the world over. Will they see you there?


Plan your 2004 International CES space strategy
Our business development team is ready to tailor a comprehensive exhibit package. Download the 2004 International CES Space Agreement and contact an International CES business development representative to review options and availability.

Exhibit Booth Space
Choose from a variety of exhibiting options, from open space on the show floor to turnkey designer packages. Divided into easy-to-navigate categories, the show floor's design offers your company maximum exposure to the attendees you need to meet.

TechZones
Showcase your company in one of our many TechZones. These high-energy, show-within-a-show zones thrust your technology into the limelight.

Meeting Place
Conduct private meetings and product demonstrations in hard-walled CES Meeting Place hospitality suites—right on the show floor! Customize yours. Access the concierge area. Receive food and beverage service. It's that easy.

Other Meeting Room Options
Options, such as Hilton hospitality suites and permament Convention Center meeting rooms, are available. Contact an International CES business development representative to review availability.
