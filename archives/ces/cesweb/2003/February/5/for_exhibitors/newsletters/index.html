Dan Cole
Vice President, Exhibitor Services, Sales and Business Development, International CES

At the 2003 International CES this past month, our customers were neck-deep in the energy of the world’s largest consumer technology show. Your innovations took center stage on the massive show floor, giving more than 115,0000 people a chance to hear your story, immerse in your brand and learn how you can make a difference in their business. There’s no denying it: The show revived a sense of urgency, excitement and hopes for good fortune for the upcoming sales year.

Behind the scenes, exhibitors once again participated in the space selection process for The 2004 International CES. The energy rivaled the heart of the show at hand. Once again, your confidence in the show and the results it continues to bring you spurred a record 86% re-sign for next year’s event.

I congratulate you if you’re among the group that already secured space—we’re delighted that you’ll be returning as an exhibiting alumnus.

But if you’re among those who are waiting to see what your marketing budget can handle before committing to ride the 2004 International CES success wave, I need to convey an earnest sense of urgency to you. Ten months until show time does NOT mean 10 months to make a decision that will ultimately guarantee you exhibit space. We already fill 1.25 million net-square-feet of space—including the entire Las Vegas Convention Center (inside and out!), the Alexis Park Hotel, and the Las Vegas Hilton—and our room to grow in the physical sense is limited. All signs point to a packed house again, and the earlier you’re aboard, the better we can ensure that you have the room you need in the location you need it.

If your company and executive team is like us, you want to see the hard numbers that prove the return on investment is there. The news is positive, but you want undeniable evidence that the people right for you are really there—at the International CES—to help you grab a critical slice of your market.

If so, please touch base with us immediately to discuss your 2004 International CES participation and other opportunities to further leverage your experience at the best event experience our fast-paced industry offers. We can get you detailed information and tailor a plan for you. Just call (703) 907-7613 or e-mail us at CESinfo@ce.org.
