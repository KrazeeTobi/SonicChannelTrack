Computer Entertainment Software Association (CESA, Chairman: Kagemasa Kozuki, location: Nishi-Shimbashi, Minato-ku, Tokyo) has finalized the schedule for "Tokyo Game Show 2001 Spring," March 30(Fri)to April 1,2001. The show will be open from 10 am to 17:00 on all days.

Exhibition Overview
Name: Tokyo Game Show 2001 Spring
Dates: March 30, 2001 (Fri.) to April 1 (Sun.)
(10:00 to 12:30 on March 30 will be opened to press/media, people involved only)
Open: 10:00 to 17:00
Venue: Makuhari Messe (Japan Convention Center) Exhibition Halls 1 to 8
2-1 Nakase, Mihama-ku, Chiba City, Chiba Prefecture
Organizer: Computer Entertainment Software Association (CESA)
Official Support from Government Offices: Ministry of International Trade and Industry (Anticipated)
No. of Visitors: 150,000 (expected)
Admission Fees:

adults (junior high school students and older) \1,200
(including consumption tax)
pre-purchased tickets: \1,000
elementary school students and younger admitted free of charge
