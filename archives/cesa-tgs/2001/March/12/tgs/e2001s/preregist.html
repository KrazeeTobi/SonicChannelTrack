TOKYO GAME SHOW 2001 SPRING
Press Pre-Registration

"Tokyo Game Show 2001 SPRING" will be held by Computer Entertainment Software Association (CESA, Chairman: Kagemasa Kozuki, location: Nishi-Shimbashi, Minato-ku, Tokyo) at Makuhari Messe (the Nippon Convention Center) March 30 (Fri.) through April 1 (Sun.), 2001. On March 30, 10:00 am ~ 12:30 pm, the entry is allowed only for the press and the video game manufactures and distributors concerned.

We welcome only qualified journalists from print, broadcast and online media to register to cover the "TGS 2001 SPRING" during the session. Please refer the attached "Note" about covering the show.
Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

The press allowed to apply for registration
1. Editor / reporter / cameraman from newspaper publishers and publishing companies
2. Editor / reporter / staff from on-line magazine or newspaper production companies
3. Reporter / cameraman from TV program production companies, including BS, CS,and cable TV program
4. Reporter / staff from Radio Program production companies
5. Freelance writer / cameraman entrusted by the companies mentioned above
6. TV or radio personality / performer of the programs

We would appreciate if you could pre-register to cover the show.
Please ask for an application form by e-mail(info@cesa.or.jp).

In addition, a keynote lecture (the keynote speaker: Bill Gates, Chairman and Chief Software Architect of Microsoft Corporation, U.S.) is scheduled for March 30, Friday at the event hall of Makuhari Messe. Even if you cover it only, you are required to pre-register.

How to register
  • Please fill in the application form and then fax it to Tokyo Game Show Press Registration by March 14 (Wed.)
  • After we complete your registration, we will send you a confirmation sheet. Please bring the confirmation sheet to the Overseas Press Registration Counter on the first day of your covering the show. Without the confirmation sheet, "Press Pass" will not be issued
  • If you are freelance writer, cameraman, or subcontractor entrusted by publishing companies or, TV stations etc., the application should be made by your supervisor of the company.
  • Mass media / advertisement agencies / PR companies working for exhibitors do not need to register as the press. Please use the exhibitor's pass offered by your client, or purchase a current ticket.
