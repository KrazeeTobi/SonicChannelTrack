Press Conferences and Events @ CES

CES Press Day - January 8, 2003
Wednesday, January 8, 2003, Las Vegas, NV
While press and analysts are not allowed generally on the exhibitor show floor on the 8th, numerous exhibitors will hold press conferences throughout the day. Journalists and analysts are encouraged to arrive in Las Vegas by Tuesday, January 7th to take advantage of all the CES press conferences and events. Of special note, press and analysts are invited to the Innovations Reception and Open House, the afternoon of Jan. 8. Innovations honorees will be on-hand to demonstrate their cutting-edge products. Cocktails and hors d'oeuvres will be served.
