Attendee Lead Retrieval System
Attendees will receive a coded badge that will enable you to quickly capture pertinent information for follow-up after the show. The ExpoExchange badge reader / lead retrieval unit should be ordered by December 1, 2002. This service is very important to develop good prospect records, and it is strongly recommended that exhibitors take advantage of it. Visit the Supplemental Services section to download the order form.

Liability and Insurance
According to the 2003 International CES Application and Exhibit Space Contract, exhibitors are advised to carry extraterritorial insurance covering theft, public liability, and property damage. Exhibitors do not need to provide International CES with a copy of the insurance certificate, but must make sure that it is available upon request on-site. Exhibitors are required to have a commercial general liability policy of not less than $250,000, naming International CES, Las Vegas Convention and Visitors Authority, Las Vegas Hilton and Alexis Park Resort as additional insureds.

The Consumer Electronics Association and all organizations and individuals employed by or associated with the 2003 International CES will not be responsible for injury or damage that may occur to an exhibitor, his/her employees or agents, nor to the safety of any exhibit or other property due to theft, fire, accident, or any other destructive causes.
