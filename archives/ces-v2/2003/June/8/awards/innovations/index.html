The hottest, coolest products are at the 2003 International CES.



Catch the buzz from The Innovations 2003 Design and Engineering Showcase, honoring this year’s most innovative products in 20 categories. Honorees will be highlighted at the Innovations 2003 booth #55083, Las Vegas Hilton at the 2003 International CES, January 9-12, 2003.

Innovations Judging
Since 1989, the prestigious Innovations Awards program has given consumer technology manufacturers and developers an opportunity to have their newest products judged by a preeminent panel of independent industry designers, engineers and journalists.

For the eighth year, the Industrial Designers Society of America (IDSA) is endorsing and acting as advisors to the Design & Engineering Showcase. Product evaluations are based on these weighted criteria:
  • User value
  • Aesthetics
  • Contributions to the quality of life
  • Innovative qualities
