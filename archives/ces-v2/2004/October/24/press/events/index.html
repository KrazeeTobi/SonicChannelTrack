New York Press Preview
Wednesday, November 10, 2004
Time Warner Center
10 Columbus Circle
New York, NY

Kick of the CES season and catch all the latest show news, plus the unveiling of the CES Innovations Design and Engineering Showcase honorees and the Consumer Electronics Association's (CEA) 5 Technologies to Watch, at the 2005 International CES New York Press Preview. This year's CES New York Press Preview features an expanded format with sponsor tabletops and suites and CEA's Holiday Showcase. Network with some of today's top tech companies one-on-one to see what they're unleashing for the holiday selling-season and what they plan to debut at the 2005 International CES.

New York Press Preview Schedule of Events:
  • Exhibitor-only Luncheon, 12:30-2 p.m., Lotus Room, Mandarin Oriental Hotel
  • Tabletops and Suites, 2-4 p.m., Time Warner Building and Mandarin Hotel, Sponsors and media/analysts only
  • Press Conference, 4-5 p.m., Lotus Room, Mandarin Hotel
  • Exhibitor/Press Reception, 5-7 p.m., Stone Rose, Time Warner Building

Press may register for the New York Press Preview by contacting Jamie Lilly or calling 703-907-7650.

CES Unveiled: The Official Press Event of the International CES
Tuesday, January 4, 2005
4:00-7:00 p.m.
Las Vegas Convention Center, Room S226

Kickoff your coverage of the largest consumer technology tradeshow in style at CES Unveiled: The Official Press Event of the International CES. Be the first to see the new year's hottest products and get a sneak peek at the 2005 Innovations Design and Engineering Showcase honorees—before the show officially opens. Enjoy cocktails and hors d'oeuvres, while checking out exclusive tabletop demonstrations and networking with the biggest names in technology. Only credentialed media and analysts are allowed to attend this exclusive event. It's the best reason under the sun to arrive in Las Vegas a day early.

Press may register for CES Unveiled while registering for the 2005 International CES. For more information on event registration, please call 703-907-4365 or e-mail press@ce.org.
