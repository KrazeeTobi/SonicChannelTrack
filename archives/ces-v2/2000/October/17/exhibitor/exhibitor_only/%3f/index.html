Guarantee Meeting Room Requests
Don't Be Caught Empty-Handed Act now!

If you're interested in hosting a meeting at the 2001 show, be sure to submit an "Exhibitor Meeting Room Request" form immediately.

Reserve your space before it's too late! Request forms are available via Fax-On-Demand, 888 333 9237. If you wish to host an in-booth event, to take place either before or after show hours, contact CES Operations at 703 907 7984. Exhibitors are required to submit a signed form, outlining rules and regulations, no later than December 1, 2000.

Sponsor the 2001 International CES Press Luncheon

Sponsor the CES press luncheon and have one-on-one access to the media during their lunch hour. Sponsors who provide a meal will have two of their hottest products displayed at the luncheon. CES will provide signage and napkins with your logo and allow up to five of your company's representatives an opportunity to meet and greet the journalists. The luncheons will be held in the Silver Pavilion of the Las Vegas Convention Center, Saturday through Tuesday, January 6 - 9, 2001.

CES staff will make all catering, logistical and promotional arrangements. This unique opportunity is $27,500 per lunch or $95,000 for exclusive sponsorship for all four days. Place your order now! For more information on this press sponsorship opportunity, contact Carole Russo at 703 907 7795 or crusso@ce.org.
