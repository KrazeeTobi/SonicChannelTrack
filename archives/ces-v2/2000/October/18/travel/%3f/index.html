Parking Information

Parking is available this year, however, due to construction at the LVCC, parking is not available in the lots immediately adjacent to the LVCC. Shuttle buses will be available to transport attendees between the convenient parking lots and exhibit facilities. Attendees also are encouraged to take advantage of the complimentary CES shuttle buses from the hotels. The Hilton, Riviera and Alexis Park will have available parking in their lots.

Shuttle Bus Service

International CES offers complimentary shuttle bus service between official hotels and the LVCC, Alexis Park, and Riveria. Look here in August for schedules and route numbers.

ADA accessible buses are available for those with special needs. Please call CMAC at (401) 826-4100 to arrange your transportation.
