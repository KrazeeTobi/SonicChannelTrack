Innovations 2000 Innovations is the Consumer Electronics Association’s (CEA) prestigious competition, which honors product excellence in Design and Engineering.

The Design & Engineering Honors Program recognizes the most innovative products in the categories of Audio, Blank Media, Computer Hardware, Electronic Gaming, Home Appliances, Home Theater, Integrated Home Systems, Mobile Electronics, Mobile Computing Workspace, Online/Internet, Personal Electronics, Photographic Equipment, Retail Resource, Satellite Systems, Small Office/Home Office, Specialty Audio, Telephones, Video and Wireless Communications.

Entries in the Innovations 2000 Design & Engineering Honors Program were judged by a panel of 22 prominent industry experts. For the sixth year, the Industrial Designers Society of America (IDSA) endorsed and acted as advisors to the Design & Engineering Showcase. This year the program also received endorsement from the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Consumer Electronics Society.

Product evaluations are based on weighted criteria: value to user, aesthetics, contributions to the quality of life as well as the product's innovative qualities.

All who visit Innovations 2000 walk away with a sense of the excellence that can be achieved in consumer technology.
