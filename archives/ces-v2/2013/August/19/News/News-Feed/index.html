2014 CES: New Year. New Badges.

Jul 15, 2013
The 2014 CES will mark new badges and new badge pickup process for show attendees. Badges will feature near field communication technology, providing additional efficiency on-site at CES. Attendees will no longer receive badges in the mail prior to CES, but will easily be able to pick up their badge when they arrive in Las Vegas. Visit CESweb.org/BadgeInfo for more information, or CESweb.org/register to register today.
