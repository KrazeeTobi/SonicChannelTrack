8/20/2013

CES Implements New Badge Process

There’s always something new at the International CES, and this year is no exception. Not only have we added new TechZones, conference sessions and events, this year CES has also implemented a new badge process. Badges will no longer be mailed to attendees in advance of the show. In addition, business credentials will need to be submitted during the registration process, rather than on-site. This new process will allow for an easier and more efficient badge pick up process on-site. Visit CESweb.org to register today, and CESweb.org/BadgeInfo to learn more about this new process.

The Innovations Design and Engineering Awards is the industry’s most esteemed awards program and we welcome all qualified candidates to serve as a judge for the Innovations Awards at the 2014 International CES. To learn more about becoming a judge, please visit CESweb.org/innovations. Act now – call for judges ends on August 16.

7/15/2013

2014 CES: New Year. New Badges.

The 2014 CES will mark new badges and new badge pickup process for show attendees. Badges will feature near field communication technology, providing additional efficiency on-site at CES. Attendees will no longer receive badges in the mail prior to CES, but will easily be able to pick up their badge when they arrive in Las Vegas. Visit CESweb.org/BadgeInfo for more information, or CESweb.org/register to register today.
