10/21/2013

CEA Releases the 2014 Edition of Five Technology Trends to Watch

Released during a panel discussion at CEA’s Industry Forum in Los Angeles, Five Technology Trends to Watch is an annual publication that examines five prominent technology trends that will influence the consumer electronics industry in the years ahead. Using the latest consumer research and market forecasts, the 2014 issue looks at the Internet of Things (IoT), driverless cars, digital health care, robotics and the future of video distribution and consumption. Five Technology Trends to Watch is a special supplement to CEA’s bi-monthly flagship publication, It Is Innovation (i3). Access the full report.

10/7/2013

CEA Announces Expansion of Eureka Park and Two New TechZones

The Eureka Park TechZone at the 2014 CES will expand by 30 percent and will feature more than 200 entrepreneurs and startups. In partnership with UP Global (formally the Startup America Partnership and Startup Weekend), the National Science Foundation and General Electric Company, Eureka Park is a dedicated area showcasing budding startups and entrepreneurs.

Also new in 2014 is the Academia Tech TechZone, which will be within Eureka Park, showcasing technologies emerging from colleges and universities. CEA also announced the launch of Eureka Park: NEXT TechZone, an area for mid-stage startups that have launched a product within the past year.

Read the full press release and learn more about CES TechZones. 

10/7/2013

Latest in Ultra HD to Be Featured at the 2014 CES

Ultra HD is the next generation of display technology for the home and delivers four times the resolution of today’s full High-Definition TVs, with more than eight million pixels of resolution. Ultra HD was launched at the 2013 International CES and the 2014 CES will discuss bringing Ultra HD to market through conference programming. CEA will produce “Ultra HD Content: What Will We Watch in 2014 and Beyond?” on January 7, 2014. “Seeing is Believing – 3D, 4K and UHD,” will take place on January 9, 2014 and will be produced by the International 3D and Advanced Imaging Society, an official CES conference partner.  See the full press release to learn more about Ultra HD at the 2014 CES.

Digital Fitness Exhibit Area Expands by 30 Percent for 2014 International CES

The digital fitness footprint at CES has seen 30 percent growth for 2014. The FitnessTech TechZone, produced in partnership with Living in Digital Times, will focus exclusively on the newest technologies for the sports and fitness enthusiast.
