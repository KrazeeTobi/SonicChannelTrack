          Digital Health Summit


          The 5th Annual Digital Health Summit focuses on the latest products and consumers' growing demand for high-tech health services, particularly through the lens of the Affordable Care Act. See solutions for diagnosing, monitoring and treating a variety of illnesses -- from obesity to ADHD, from poor vision to high blood pressure. Learn about games that reinforce healthy behaviors, body sensors that let people take more responsibility for their own health, affordable gene sequencing, real-time medicine monitoring, and more. You'll gain an understanding of the digital health infrastructure and how your organization can capitalize on this hot market.
