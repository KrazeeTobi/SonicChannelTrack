          CES has partnered with several airlines to save you money. Check back often, as airline partners are added throughout the fall.

          American Airlines


          Visit American Airlines and enter Promotion Code 1314AI to receive up to a five percent discount. Note, discount is not applicable on all fare types. Valid on travel to Las Vegas between January 4-13, 2014

          Virgin Atlantic


          Virgin Atlantic is an award winning UK based airline. It is the second largest long haul airline and flies to 36 international destinations worldwide. Through their new joint venture with Delta, travelers are offered 91 additional routes within North America and between the UK and USA.
           
          Virgin Atlantic operates daily to Las Vegas and is convenient for attendees travelling from the UK. To book discounted airfare, visit Virgin Atlantic and receive 10% off of the lowest published rate (this applies to all cabins and classes). Travel valid between January 4-15, 2014.

          SkyTeam Global


          SkyTeam comprises 19 leading international carriers: Aeroflot, Aerolineas Argentinas, Aeromexico, Air Europa, Air France, Alitalia, China Airlines, China Eastern, China Southern, Czech Airlines, Delta Air Lines, Kenya Airways, KLM Royal Dutch Airlines, Korean Air, Middle East Airlines, Saudia, TAROM, Vietnam Airlines and Xiamen Air, serving over 1,000 destinations in 187 countries with over 15,000 flights daily.  To book your discounted airfare, visit the CES SkyTeam page. Travel valid between January 2-14, 2014. 

          McCarran's New T3 Terminal

          Las Vegas McCarran airport opened a new terminal, T3, in June 2012. Terminal 1 continues to be open and operational but Terminal 2 is permanently closed. See below for a full list of affected carriers and see more details here. Some features in the 1.9 million square foot expansion include space for two Transportation Security Administration checkpoints with up to 31 lanes, more than a dozen stores and restaurants, and on-board Wi-Fi for those passengers that are parked at any of T3’s gates. Further, international travelers will experience an expanded US Customs and Boarder Protection port of entry that uses multiple Global Entry kiosks to expedite entry into the United States.
