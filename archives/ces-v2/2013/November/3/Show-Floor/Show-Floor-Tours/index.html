          Get The Most Out of CES: Take an Official Tour of the Show Floor

          We are pleased to offer our attendees exciting show floor tours during the 2014 International CES. From self-guided to completely customized, we’ve got something for every attendee. Take a look at the myriad of options below and choose the tour that’s right for you and your business needs.

           

           





          General Overview Tour

          2-hour guided floor tour
          Produced by ShellyPalmer, in partnership with the International CES

          A specially trained docent will guide you through thoughtfully curated exhibits and tour stops where you will see key technologies and innovations and hear expert insights and descriptions by Shelly Palmer and his team of industry thought leaders.  This experience is perfect for anyone who wants to pack the best of CES into two action-filled hours.  If you're a CES first-timer or attending the CES Conference sessions -- this tour is for you! 

          Price: $300 (Only $150 with the purchase of a conference pass)

          The Tour Experience Includes:

          • Small Groups led by specially trained docents and expert tour guides

          • An abstract from Shelly Palmer's 2014 Connected World™ CE Report

          • Wireless Headsets so you won't miss a word*

          *Please note: There is a mandatory $250 deposit required for each wireless headset receiver, which will be added to your total and charged to your credit card at the time of your reservation. This deposit will be fully refunded if the unit is returned at the conclusion of your tour. The earbuds are yours to keep.
           
          Tours leave from the North Hall Lobby and the South Hall Lobby of the Las Vegas Convention and World Trade Center (LVCC) several times each day. Space is limited and tours fill up quickly, so register now!

          Questions about this tour? Contact CEStours@shellypalmer.com

          Executive Tour

          2-hour guided floor tour
          Produced byShellyPalmer, in partnership with the International CES

          You will be guided through the best of CES by a senior tour guide from the Shelly Palmer team. This 2-hour floor tour is perfect for executives and professionals who want deep insights into consumer behavior and industry trends. Our expert guides will help you identify and understand key technologies and services, differentiate offerings, and create action items and strategic take-aways from the show. The Executive Tour will make you an industry expert in 2 hours – it's simply the best way to see CES.
           
          Price: $995 

          The Tour Experience Includes:

          • Small Groups led by specially trained docents and expert tour guides

          • An abstract from Shelly Palmer's 2014 Connected World™ CE Report

          • Wireless headsets so you won't miss a word*

          *Please note: There is a mandatory $250 deposit required for each wireless headset receiver, which will be added to your total and charged to your credit card at the time of your reservation. This deposit will be fully refunded if the unit is returned at the conclusion of your tour. The earbuds are yours to keep.
           
          Tours leave from the North Hall Lobby and the South Hall Lobby of the Las Vegas Convention and World Trade Center (LVCC) several times each day. Space is limited and tours fill up quickly, so register now.

          Questions about this tour? Contact CEStours@shellypalmer.com

          Platinum Tour

          Customized guided floor tour
          Produced by ShellyPalmer, in partnership with the International CES

          Experience a custom-curated, executive overview of CES created specifically for you and your colleagues.  Shelly Palmer and CES will craft a unique "best of CES" tour designed for your group.

          Please contact us for details: Contact CEStours@shellypalmer.com
