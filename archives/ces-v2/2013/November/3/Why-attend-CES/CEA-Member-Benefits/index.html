          CEA Member Benefits for CES Exhibitors and Attendees

          CEA members get incredible benefits on-site at CES. To start, members get a $100 discount on registration as well as an Exhibit Plus Pass and a 25 percent discount on conference passes. They also get exclusive access to member lounges with private meeting rooms, refreshments and lots of seating and an invitation to the CEA Member Cocktail Party.

          Member Lounges

          • Private meeting rooms

          • Wi-Fi

          • Refreshments

          • TV, PCs, printer

          • CEA Staff will be available in the member lounges for assistance.


          Lounge Locations

          • Las Vegas Convention Center (LVCC), North Hall, Upper Level, Outside Room N250

          • LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S220

          • The Venetian, Tower Suite 30-140


          Member Packet

          • Free LVCC member parking pass*

          • Tickets to the VIP Luncheon*

          • Special recognition on badges, plaques and directory listings


          Exhibit Space Discount/Selection Priority and Other Exclusive Resources

          • $100 discount on registration and an Exhibit Plus Pass

          • 25 percent discount on conference passes

          • Exhibitor discounts on booth space* (To receive the CES membership discount, the exhibiting company must become a member with fully paid dues within 60 days of submitting the CES Application and Exhibit Space Contract or by July 1, 2013, whichever is the sooner of those two dates. Discount not available after July 1, 2013.)

          • Member priority for 2014 International CES space and on booth change waiting list*

          • Up to 60 percent discount on Innovation Design and Engineering Awards entry fees

          •  20 percent discount on CES attendee postal list rentals*

          •  Free International CES press list*

          • 10 percent discount on BusinessWire U.S. news distribution circuits

          *Exhibiting member companies only


          CEA Member Cocktail Party
          6–8 p.m. Tuesday, January 7, 2014
          LVCC, South Hall 1 Lobby, Aces (formerly the International Food Market)
          CEA Members and their guests are welcome.
           
          Visit CE.org/join, contact membership@CE.org or call 703-907-7600 for questions about CEA membership.
