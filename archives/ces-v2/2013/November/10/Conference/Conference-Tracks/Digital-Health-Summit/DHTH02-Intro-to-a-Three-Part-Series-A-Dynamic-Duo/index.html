          A Dynamic Duo: How Digital Health and ACA Will Catalyze Opportunity (Intro to Three Part Series)

          January 8, 2014, 10-10:05 a.m.
          LVCC, North Hall N250

          Join us for a three-part series to discover how the ACA and the digital health industry can work together to leverage each other to achieve mutual success. The following three sessions will cover the key parts of the ACA as it applies to this industry and how digital health businesses can capitalize on these new opportunities.
