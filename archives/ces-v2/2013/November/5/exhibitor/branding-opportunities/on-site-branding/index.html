          When the doors open on Tuesday, January 7, 2014, have your company’s name, brand and products seen first.

          Choose between any of our front-and-center opportunities to have your brand featured onone or many of our exterior and interior banners; shuttle bus exterior and interior wraps, covers and banners; outdoor promotional space for any custom designs, cars or experiences. Want your product or service to be put to work? Become an official equipment sponsor.

          Digital Signage
          During the International CES, the lobbies and escalators at the Las Vegas Convention and World Trade Center (LVCC) are some of the busiest areas throughout the show. Take advantage of these prime locations by advertising on the new digital signage screens now available at the LVCC.

          Coming soon: Download the 2014 CES Digital Signage flyer for more information
           
          Sponsorship Investment: $5,000 per eight (8) second spot.
           
          Contact us at ltardif@CE.org or 703-907-7681 to secure this opportunity.


          Interior and Exterior Banners
          Undoubtedly among the most prominent on-site promotional opportunities at CES, banners guarantee exposure to key decision-makers in the consumer technology industry. Exterior banners are often the first impression CES attendees have of their experience at the show while the interior banners grab attention and become the most talked about forms of marketing. They're used as directional guides ("Go past the Sony banner in the Central Concourse."), meeting points ("Meet me under the Panasonic banner in the Grand Lobby.") and what many other marketing campaigns at CES are measured by.

          Looking to capture the attention of attendees at The Venetian? Use The Venetian PlanTour menu to browse a variety of high-traffic and targeted areas of the venue avialble for branding and providing you with the greatest exposure.

          Sponsorship Investment: Call for details
           
          Contact us at ltardif@CE.org or 703-907-7681 to secure this opportunity.


          Official Equipment Sponsor
          Put your technology to work with this in-kind sponsorship opportunity. Supply computers and printers for use at the email stations and other high-visibility show management locations such as information counters and registration areas. Sponsor provides equipment and technical support. 
           
          Sponsorship Investment: Call for details

          Outdoor Promotional Opportunity Space
          Space is available for promotional displays outside and in the lobby areas for static promotional products such as custom inflatables, cars, interactive kiosks and more.
           
          Sponsorship Investment: Call for details

          Contact us at ltardif@CE.org or 703-907-7681 to secure this opportunity.


          Shuttle Bus Advertising Programs
          There are over 30 hotels on the official housing list for International CES and we offer complimentary shuttle service between the LVCC and all hotels, all four days of the show through our partners at RPMs, Inc. Target your clients with high-impact transportation-related advertising. The shuttle program provides you with the most captive audiences available for marketing at CES. With 40,000 attendees riding the shuttles each day, your message will be delivered in a unique and powerful format. 
          2014 CES Shuttle Routes include:

          • Hotel Shuttle Program: The hotel shuttle program provides service from all official CES hotels to the LVCC.

          • LVCC Shuttle Loop: This shuttle runs every 10 minutes providing the only on-property mobile exposure while making 5 key stops on the LVCC campus.

          • The Venetian Express: Express shuttle service runs every 10 minutes between the Venetian and the LVCC, accommodating a daily peak ridership of 20,000+.

          • Mandalay Bay Press Express: Monday only, shuttle service will be provided to and from Mandalay Bay for press attendees.

          • LVCC Golf Cart Intraconnect: Golf Cart service will be provided every 10 minutes between the South Hall 1 & 2 and Central Hall 5.

          Choose between any of the options below, exclusivity is available:

          Exterior On-Bus Opportunities
          Choose between a full wrap of the shuttle, a half banner or multiple window banners. All advertisements are produced in full color with design upgrades available.

          Interior In-bus Opportunities
          Promote your brand or messaging inside the bus through custom headrest seat covers, passenger-facing front window banner, individual window banners or a looped video promotion. Multiple designs and upgrade options are available for the printed promotions.

          General On-Site Opportunities
          Put your brand on the signage every CES attendee will read to find their route – the transportation program brochure, hotel lobby signage and exterior side signage. All options provide colorful and convenient placement.


          Sponsorship Investment: $5,000 and up

          Contact our partner at RPMS, Alex Connor at AlexC@rp-ms.com or 401-639-0993/877-836-5846 for reservations, pricing and custom programs.
