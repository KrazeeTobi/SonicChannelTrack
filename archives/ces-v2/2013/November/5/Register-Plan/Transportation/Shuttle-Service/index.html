          The International CES offers complimentary shuttle service on all four days of the show.

          Full Shuttle Schedule


          Hotel Shuttle Buses
          The International CES offers complimentary shuttle service Monday to Friday to and from the Las Vegas Convention Center (LVCC) and most official CES hotels. 

          NEW! Limited Shuttle Service to Venetian
          We are now offering limited service from CES hotels on routes 2-7 to The Venetian. Timed departures will be at 7:30 am., 9 a.m. and 10:30 a.m., Tuesday- Friday. There will be no return service to hotels from The Venetian. 

          LVCC Shuttle Loop
          If you need to go from one side of the LVCC to the other, consider this shuttle to save time and to give your legs a break. The shuttle runs every 10 minutes and stops at five key locations around the building. 

          LVCC Golf Cart Intraconnect
          Use the complimentary Golf Cart Intraconnect service at the LVCC to travel from the back of Central Hall 5 to the back of South Halls 1-2 during show hours.

          Venetian Express
          Express shuttle service runs every 10 minutes between The Venetian and the LVCC, Monday through Thursday from 7:30 a.m. to 6:30 p.m. and Friday 7:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. 

          Mandalay Express
          On Press Day, Monday, January 6, the Mandalay Express will run to and from the LVCC from 7:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. 

          Airport Shuttle Service
          Shuttle service to McCarran International Airport is available from the LVCC and The Venetian (bus loading area). The one-way trip cost is $5 per person and is available every 30 minutes on Thursday and Friday, the last two days of the show.

          CES does not offer shuttle service fromMcCarran Airport to show venues or your hotel. However, there are several companies that do. Limo service is also available. Limos and shuttles are located on the west side of baggage claim, outside door exits 7-13. Find a full list of limo/shuttle companies here.
