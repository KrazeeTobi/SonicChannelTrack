            What's New with Badges in 2014

            As the world’s leading innovation event, we strive to leverage technology to improve your experience at the International CES. This year, we have new badges which will result in shorter lines, more badge functionality and greater ease of use. The new badges will have near field communication (NFC) technology, which enables us to access and update information on the badges quickly and effectively.

            One important change for 2014 CES is that badges will NOT be mailed in advance. Instead, once you register and receive your confirmation, you will pick up your badge when you arrive in Las Vegas. A full list of badge pickup locations is listed below. However, for faster, more efficient check-in, you should not wait until you get to a CES show venue to retrieve your badge. Simply pick up your badge at McCarran Airport while you wait for your baggage or after you check in to one of our official CES hotels.

            Remote Badge Pickup Locations:

            • McCarran Airport, Terminal 1 Baggage Claim

            • McCarran Airport, Terminal 3 Baggage Claim

            • ARIA

            • Circus Circus

            • Excalibur

            • Mandalay Bay

            • MGM Grand

            • Mirage

            • Riviera

            • Westin

            • Wynn

            On-site Badge Pickup Locations:

            • LVH, Ballroom Foyer

            • LVCC, North Hall 3 Lobby

            • LVCC, Central Lobby, Patio

            • LVCC, Central Hall 2, Entrance

            • LVCC, South Hall 1, Patio

            • LVCC, South Hall 1, Front Lobby

            • LVCC, South Hall 2, Lobby

            • LVCC, South Hall Connector, Outside S229 (Press Only)

            • Venetian, Level 1, Meeting Space

            • Venetian, Level 2, Foyer

            • Venetian, Tower Elevator Bank Entrance

            This year, you only need to show a government issued ID (driver’s License or passport) and the QR code on your confirmation page (via printout, phone, tablet or laptop) to verify on-site and receive a badge. Please note that all attendees can pick up their badge at any remote or on-site pickup location, regardless of their attendee type.
