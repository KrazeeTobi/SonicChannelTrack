            Brand Matters
            The Brand Matters program premiered at the 2013 International CES® to provide a tailored experience to the senior level marketing and advertising audience at the show. In its inaugural year, the program attracted over 5,000 executives in this community from notable companies like Coca Cola, Samsung, Unilever, YouTube, Facebook, Tumblr, Amazon, Twitter, and Google.

            Brand Matters connects the International CES and the advertising and marketing executives looking for the latest consumer technology products that are important to their client’s bottom line. The program educates and assists them with navigating the exhibits, conference sessions and events critical to their community at the most influential technology trade show in the world.

             
            As a Brand Matters sponsor, your brand will be front and center to this exclusive group of influencers.


            CEA Member Reception
            Reach top executives and industry leaders through a sponsorship of the Consumer Electronics Association’s (CEA)® annual membership networking reception at CES. Align your brand with the preeminent authority and voice for the consumer technology industry and build awareness with a critical audience of key decision-makers. As the sponsor, you’ll receive two (2) sponsor signs at the entrance to the reception and inside; company/product literature distributed in high traffic areas and access to the post-show attendee mailing list.

            Sponsorship Investment: Call for details.
             
            Contact us at byb@CE.org or 703-907-4324 to secure this opportunity.


            CEA Entrepreneur Club (eClub) Networking Cocktail Party
            Connect your brand, products and services with industry entrepreneurs when you sponsor this exclusive, invitation-only event. Held at Surrender nightclub in the Wynn on Thursday, January 9, sponsorship of this event includes opportunity to provide branded amenities (beverage napkins, etc), signage during the event, company logo included on event invitation, a reserved table and up to ten party invitations for sponsors' guests, dependent on sponsorship level.

            Sponsorship Investment: $7.500 - $20,000 (based on exclusive or non-exclusive agreement) 

            Contact us at ltardif@CE.org or 703-907-7681 to secure this opportunity.


            CEA VIP Buyers Program
            Make your company name and logo visible to a select group of presidents, owners, CEOs, merchandisers and buyers from the top 150 consumer electronics retail companies. The sponsorship for the VIP Buyers Program includes the VIP Buyer Car Service and the VIP Luncheons.

            The car service helps you reach more than 1,200 of the most important buyers in the business, those who represent an incredible 95 percent of the $100 billion consumer electronics retail market. VIP buyers use the car service to go to and from the show, as well as many other Vegas locations during the four days of the show.

            The invitation-only VIP Luncheons attract 700-800 guests per luncheon. Past guests include VIP buyers from Best Buy, RadioShack and Target, as well as VIPs, state and federal government representatives and policy directors from the FCC, FTC and other government agencies.
             
            As the sponsor of this program, your company will receive:

            • Sponsor signs on both sides of each car in the VIP Buyer car service (approximately 100 cars)

            • Logo on luncheon invitation (subject to print deadline)

            • Display table at entrance to lunch room and product information in the room

            • Logo on signs inside luncheon room and on directional signs leading to lunch room

            • Sponsor logo on promotional materials

            • Brochure insertion in VIP Buyer Welcome Package (subject to mailing deadline)

            • Logo and website listing on CESweb.org

            • One-time use of VIP Buyer mailing list, through a third-party mail house

            • Lunch invitations for up to five company reps or sponsor guests

            Sponsorship Investment: Call for details
             
            Contact us at ltardif@CE.org or 703-907-7681 to secure this opportunity.

            Entertainment Matters at CES
            Entertainment Matters at CES (EM) premiered at the 2011 International CES®, and has been a brilliant success ever since. In 2013, EM attracted more than 30,000 entertainment industry professionals to the show, including influencers from the Back Lot to the Boardroom. 
             
            Designed by and for Hollywood professionals, EM connects the entertainment and technology industries and allows these executives to experience the products and technologies that deliver entertainment to the consumer, offers education focused on the emerging business models resulting from new technologies, and defines new ways for consumers to experience their entertainment. From keynotes and panels to exhibits and special events, EM packages together what’s critical to the Hollywood community.
             
            As an Entertainment Matters sponsor, the EM program at CES is your connection to lucrative partnerships and groundbreaking innovations at the world's largest annual innovation event.
             
            Sponsorship Investment: Call for details.
             
            Contact us at ltardif@CE.org or 703-907-7681 to discuss traditional and customizable opportunities.


            Innovations Design and Engineering Awards
            Since 1976, the prestigious Innovations Design and Engineering Awards has given consumer technology manufacturers and developers an opportunity to have their newest products judged by a preeminent panel of independent industrial designers, engineers and members of the trade press. At CES, the Innovations Showcase is  the place to see, touch, feel and understand the future of consumer technology.

            Tie your logo and brand to the part of the show floor that draws the most press coverage at CES. As a premier sponsor of the award program considered among the most prestigious in the CE industry, you’ll receive inclusion in the Call for Entries direct mail and electronic mail campaigns; general CES show promotional materials including the attendee newsletter and on-site official show publications; on-site directional signage and the graphic panels of the Innovations showcases, as well as countertop signage for honoree’s booths; and inclusion in the 2014 sponsor logo and much more.
             
            Sponsorship Investment: Call for details.
             
            Contact us at ltardif@CE.org or 703-907-7681 to secure this opportunity.


            International Reception
            The International Reception, held the first night of the show, hosts an average attendance of 300 international attendees, exhibitors and press each year. The event is promoted through various targeted electronic direct mailings (EDMs) to our international audience and delegation leaders. Invitations are also distributed on-site in the International Registration area and the International Commerce Center. As a sponsor, your company receives:

            • Electronic invitation sent to all pre-registered international attendees (approximately 25,000) with your name and logo included 

            • Your name and logo on the printed invitation for the international reception that is distributed on-site in the International Registration area and the International Commerce Center

            • Reception sponsor recognition on CESweb.org

            • Name and logo on signage at the reception

            • Opportunities to distribute and display literature within the reception room

            • Ten invited guests in addition to staff 

            • Recognition during brief remarks by CEA

            Sponsorship Investment: Call for details
             
            Contact us at byb@CE.org or 703-907-4324 to secure this opportunity.


            Leaders In Technology Program
            The Leaders in Technology (LIT) program at the International CES allows high-level policymakers and technologists to see first-hand the dynamic industry that is driving the American economy. The program also provides information and insight to assist officials in evaluating federal policies that affect the consumer technology industry. The program culminates at the International CES LIT Dinner. Aligning your company name and logo with this extremely exclusive, invitation-only event signifies excellence and prestige. Choose from five possible sponsorships:

            LIT Dinner Reception (exclusive)
            Prior to dinner, guests are invited to a cocktail reception to network with colleagues. As the exclusive sponsor, your logo and acknowledgement will appear on beverage station signage, you may also provide branded cocktail napkins. You will receive an acknowledgement and your logo in the dinner program; recognition during dinner welcome remarks by Gary Shapiro, president and CEO of CEA and five (5) complimentary dinner registrations.
             
            Sponsorship Investment: $12,500*

            LIT Dinner
            The most distinguished event at CES, the Leaders in Technology (LIT) Dinner attracts 500 of the most influential industry leaders, including executive-level government representatives and policy makers. As a co-sponsor, you’ll receive recognition through the following:

            • Logo on 1,500 print and electronic dinner invitations

            • Logo recognition on directional signage to the dinner and promotional signage during the event

            • Logo and/or website in the dinner program

            • Recognition in dinner welcome remarks made by Gary Shapiro, president and CEO of CEA

            • Logo recognition on title presentation slide for the multimedia presentation shown before the dinner (if used)

            • Five (5) complimentary dinner registrations, including one (1) seat for an appropriate company executive at a head table.

            • One (1) print ad in It Is Innovation (i3)

            Sponsorship Investment: $25,000*, two sponsorships available.

            LIT Dinner Dessert Reception (exclusive)
            Immediately following the dinner, an assortment of desserts, coffee and tea are set in the pre-function room to allow guests to continue networking with colleagues. As the exclusive sponsor of this reception, you’ll receive:

            • Logo and acknowledgement in the dinner program

            • Recognition in dinner welcome remarks made by Gary Shapiro, president and CEO of CEA

            • Logo and acknowledgement on dessert reception signage

            • Five (5) complimentary dinner registrations

            • Opportunity to provide cocktail napkins with logo for use during dessert reception

            Sponsorship Investment: $12,500*

            LIT Partner Sponsorship
            Designed for a company looking to support the program and receive quality recognition, as a partner sponsor you’ll receive:

            • Logo and acknowledgement in the dinner program

            • Four (4) complimentary dinner registrations

            Sponsorship Investment: $2,500
             

            *Sponsor must be a CEA member and all agreements are subject to federal ethics guidelines. All items are subject to Sponsor meeting deadlines as outlined in the contract.

            Contact us at ltardif@CE.org or 703-907-7681 to secure any of these opportunities.
