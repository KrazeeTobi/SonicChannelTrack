            New Badge Pickup Process

            New for the 2014 CES, attendee badges will not be mailed in advance. All attendees will need to pick their badges up onsite at the 2014 CES. Learn more about the new badge process
            .

            International Commerce Center (ICC)

            LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S224-S225

            The International Commerce Center offers business services exclusively for our international attendees and exhibitors. Services include private meeting rooms, reserved on a first-come, first-served basis; laptop hookups and computers with Internet access so you can stay connected to your office back home and a lounge area where you can meet casually with colleagues or rest after a busy day on the show floor. Multilingual interpreters are available to assist you as needed.
             

            Global Matchmaking and International Reception

            Tuesday, January 7, 2014
            6:00-7:30 p.m.
            LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S222

            All international attendees, exhibitors and media are invited to the Global Matchmaking and International Reception. This reception is for international visitors only. Don’t miss this opportunity and enjoy light refreshments after an exciting day on the CES show floor. This event is open to all international registrants and their invited guests.
             
            The U.S. Department of Commerce will once again host a matchmaking program, for U.S. companies seeking to do business abroad, at this reception. Are you a U.S. based CES exhibitor interested in participating? Contact us for additional information. We look forward to seeing you there!
             

            Interpreters

            A multilingual staff is available in the LVCC ICC and in the following areas to assist with registration: LVCC, CES Central Plaza, Main Registration Area and in The Venetian, Level 2, Venetian Ballroom Foyer, Exhibitor Registration Area.
            Interpreters are available to assist you in several languages, including Spanish, French, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean. In the Main Registration Area, look for the counter titled, “International Language Interpretation” if you need assistance.

            International Buyer Program

            The International CES is a proud participant in the International Buyer Program, sponsored by the U.S. Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration. International Trade Specialists from the U.S. Department of Commerce are available in the ICC (LVCC South Hall Connector, Room S224/225) to provide matchmaking assistance and export trade counseling to international attendees and exhibitors.
