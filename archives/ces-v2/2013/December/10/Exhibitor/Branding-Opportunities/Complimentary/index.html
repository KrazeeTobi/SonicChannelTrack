            Complimentary Branding Opportunities

            Whether you have the money to invest in additional branding opportunities or not, International CES is committed to providing all of our exhibitors with a range of free benefits to build awareness of your company, brand and products. From speaking opportunities to free registration for your key accounts, you’re sure to find at least one opportunity that will amplify your exhibiting experience.

            2014 International CES Official Directory Listing
            Completing your directory listing is your gateway into the MyCES online directory and the printed International CES Official Show Directory. Attendees use the portal and online floor plan to research exhibitors and products and create their agendas for the show. This tool is also referenced year-round by attendees and prospects. It's important that the information they see about your company is accurate.

            By logging into the Exhibitor Dashboard you’re able to maintain all of your information, as well as update your product categories, company contacts and export interests. Follow these steps to quickly review and update your information:
            1. Simply go to: http://ces14.exh.mapyourshow.com
            2. Enter in your personalized login information
            3. Select “APPROVE DATA” if all information appears correct

            Deadlines:
            Inclusion in the printed Directory: October 11
            Inclusion in the online Directory: No deadline; update your information year-round! 

             
            Email Map Your Show or call 888-527-8823 for your login and password, additional information or assistance.


            CES Registration Link on Your Site
            Help your customers register for CES! Place a link on your corporate website to online registration for the International CES using this address: www.CESweb.org/register.
            Contact Christina Corrado with any questions.

            Customer Invitation Tool
            Get the word out about your presence at the 2014 CES to those that matter most. New and improved for 2014, we’ve worked with our new registration vendor, ITN International, to develop two easy-to-manage options for inviting your clients and prospects to visit your booth during the show. Choose a CES-branded email template, with room to add a personalized message about your company. Or simply copy your company’s unique registration URL to send in a message through your own email client. Whichever option you choose, this free promotional tool will help ensure your audience is coming to your exhibit.

            To access this tool and start sending invitations:
            1. Log into your Exhibitor Dashboard
            2. Click on the "Register Staff/EAC, Invite Clients" tile
            3. Once inside the registration portal, select the "Invitations" tab and follow the directions to send your invitations!

            Contact us with any questions.


            Exhibitor Profile Distributed via Business Wire
            Business Wire offers exhibitors free electronic distribution of a 100-word exhibitor profile to attending media and related audiences worldwide. 

            Email Business Wire or call 888-381-9473 for more information.
             

             
            International CES Logo
            Leverage the power of your participation in the International CES. Download the official International CES logo, and include it in your marketing and booth promotions (login required). 
             
            Send us an email or call 703-907-4324 for more information or assistance.

             
            International CES Speaking Opportunities
            Share your knowledge and build your reputation as a leader in the consumer electronics industry. Visit the Call for Speakers section for information on International CES speaking opportunities.

            Call for Speakers Opens: May 1
            Submissions Due: 5 p.m. EDT, June 12
