            Keynote Announcement: Blue Button Comes to the Rescue

            January 8, 2014, 12:10-12:20 p.m.
            LVCC, North Hall N250

            A new technology is being launched to help solve one of the biggest problems in healthcare - gaining access to our medical records and allowing us to share them with whomever we choose, while remaining secure and up-to-date. Join us to discover the Blue Button possibilities and learn how your company can leverage this technology to improve client experience by integrating access to their most current health information.

              Keynote:
            • Lygeia  Ricciardi, Director of Consumer eHealth, ONC, US Dept of Health & Human Services
