            The CMO Club is an exclusive, invitation-only VIP program open to a select list of marketing and advertising executives. Participants in the CMO Club at CES experience the most influential technology show in the world from a unique executive level perspective.

            CMO Club benefits include:

            • Free access to the CES show floor.
            • Access to the CMO Club lounge on the show floor (located just inside the N4 entrance of the LVCC, North Hall Lobby).
            • Meeting room in the lounge, reserved on a first-come, first-served basis.
            • Customized, guided tours of the show floor (space is limited).
            • Priority VIP passes to keynote addresses (keynote passes available in the CMO Club Lounge - quantities limited).
            • 20 percent discount on the Entertainment Matters Conference Pass.
            • Access to a hotel block at the Bellagio available on a first-come, first-served basis through November 26.

            CMO Club Show Floor Tours

            Bespoke to the media and marketing communities, MediaLink is offering highly curated CES show floor tours, led by industry experts. For more information on these customized tours, contact CEStours@medialink.com.
