            An Update on 2014 CES Presenter Attire Policy

            Be sure to check out “An Update on 2014 CES Presenter Attire Policy,” a recent blog post by Karen Chupka, senior vice president, International CES and corporate business strategy, CEA, about the new exhibitor/presenter attire policy at the 2014 CES: CES Show Management reserves the right to make determinations on appropriate exhibitor/presenter attire. If for any reason an exhibit and/or its contents are deemed objectionable by Show Management, the exhibitor may be asked to alter the attire of its employees, booth staff and/or models. If necessary, the exhibitor may be asked to remove the individual(s) in question at the exhibitor’s sole expense. Exhibitors with questions about compliance with these guidelines should consult with Show Management in advance of the show. Read More

            Schedule Your 2014 CES Exhibitor Press Conference

            If your company is planning to make a major product launch or company announcement at CES, hosting a press conference on-site is an excellent way to brief the media on your big news. All CES exhibitors are eligible to hold a newsworthy press conference and have their event included on the official CES Exhibitor Press Events list. Events can be held in a CES-provided press conference room in Mandalay Bay, The Venetian or the LVCC, as well as the exhibitor’s booth or in an exhibitor’s paid meeting room. Reservations generally run in two-hour increments and come with a standard room set including theater-style seating; three draped tables; a stage riser, a podium with a light and a wired microphone, and two easels for signage. To schedule an exhibitor press conference, download the Press Conference Reservation Request Form and return to PRcontacts@CE.org. Read More

            The Official International CES Daily, produced by TWICE

            All CES exhibitors are invited to submit new product information to The Official International CES Daily , produced by TWICE.

            When submitting new product information, please keep the following criteria in mind:
            ·  Submit press releases describing the products (not pre-printed sales sheets or ads)
            ·  Your product(s) must be “new” – meaning they are making their debut at the 2014 CES
            ·  Your official booth number must be included
            ·  Email no more than three high-resolution images (300 dpi)
            ·  If new products are sent in without a usable picture, they will only be considered for appearance online at www.TWICE.com on its International CES page and not in print

            Information can be submitted to TWICECESDAILY@nbmedia.com by November 13. QUESTIONS? Contact TWICE editor-in-chief Steve Smith at 917- 281-4754 or ssmith@nbmedia.com.
