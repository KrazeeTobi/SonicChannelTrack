CE Industry Revenues to Reach Record-High $209 Billion in 2013, According to CEA

Revenues for the consumer electronics industry are projected to grow nearly three percent, reaching a new record-high of $209.6 billion, according to the semi-annual industry forecast released today by the Consumer Electronics Association (CEA), producer of the 2013 International CES. The forecast also shows 2012 industry revenues reached $204 billion, up five percent from the previous year. “CEA’s forecast once again confirms that CE products play an increasingly indispensible role in consumers’ lives,” said Steve Koenig, CEA’s director of industry analysis. “Consumer adoption of smartphones and tablets continues to expand briskly, as mobile connected devices take center stage in today’s connected, digital lifestyle.” [Read more]
