Press Events

The 2013 International CES hosts a variety of events specifically for members of the press long before the show doors open. Join us in London and New York — and then in Las Vegas — to see what's new and next in the world of consumer technology.

CES Unveiled STOCKHOLM

Monday, October 29, 2012
World Trade Center Stockholm
Stockholm, Sweden

With the launch of CES Unveiled STOCKHOLM, the Consumer Electronics Association (CEA)® brings the excitement of CES to the Nordic region. Executives from CEA’s market research department will present holiday sales and hot tech trends forecasts to top journalists from Denmark, Finland, Lithuania, Norway and Sweden along with CES announcements. Exhibitors will showcase ground-breaking technology products during the networking reception to targeted media, analysts and industry affiliates.
 
Schedule:

12:15-1 p.m.        CEA Market Research Presentation
1-2 p.m. 2013 International CES Press Conference
2-5 p.m. CES Unveiled NEW YORK STOCKHOLM Networking Reception

For press inquiries, contact Allison Fried at afried@CE.org.

Interested in exhibiting? Contact Tira Gordon at tgordon@CE.org.

CES Unveiled NEW YORK

Monday, November 12, 2012
Metropolitan Pavilion
New York, New York

A kick-off to the 2013 International CES - Get a sneak peek at this year's hottest products two months before the show floor even opens! This annual event features CEA’s annual holiday sales and hot trends forecast, the announcement of the honorees for the Best of Innovations Design and Engineering Awards, the International CES press conference and a networking reception that lets press and exhibitors network and see the tabletop displays of new products.

3:15-4 p.m.        Holiday Sales and End-of-Year Trend Analysis
Presented by: CEA's market research experts
2nd Floor- The Metropolitan Suite
4-5 p.m. 2013 International CES Press Conference with Innovations Award Announcement
2nd Floor - The Metropolitan Suite
5-8 p.m. CES Unveiled NEW YORK Showcase and Networking Reception
1st Floor - Pavilion

Registration for the New York Press Preview featuring CES Unveiled NEW YORK is now live!

Questions about registering? Contact Erica Corley at 703-907-7621.

Interested in exhibiting or sponsoring? Contact a business development representative.

CES Unveiled LONDON

Thursday, November 15, 2012
Inmarsat Conference Center
London, United Kingdom

The Consumer Electronics Association has taken the CES Unveiled property international with the second annual CES Unveiled LONDON. Exhibitors will be on hand with their ground-breaking technology products to showcase to key UK and European media and analysts. It’s the perfect time to capture your headlines before the holiday selling season.

Registration for CES Unveiled LONDON is now live!

For press inquires, contact Allison Fried at afried@CE.org.

Interested in exhibiting? Contact Tira Gordon at tgordon@CE.org.

CES Unveiled LAS VEGAS: The Official Press Event of the International CES

Sunday, January 6, 2013
Mandalay Bay, Level 3, South Seas Ballroom C
Las Vegas, Nevada

Be the first to see the new year's products at CES Unveiled LAS VEGAS with a pre-show look at what will be making news headlines before the show officially opens. Catch all the latest products in one room. Plus, get a sneak peek at the product debuts from our Innovations Design and Engineering Award Honorees.

Registration for CES Unveiled LAS VEGAS is now live!

Questions about registering? Contact Erica Corley at ecorley@CE.org.

Interested in exhibiting or sponsoring? Contact a business development representative.

State of the Consumer Tech Industry and 2013 CES Trends to Watch

January 6, 2013 2 – 3 p.m.
Mandalay Bay, Level 2, Surf A
Las Vegas, Nevada

At the onset of CES, there are two questions on everyone’s mind: What does the year ahead look like? And what lasting trends will emerge from the next several days? Before the doors open for the most important tech event in the country, get a glimpse into what everyone will be talking about in the year to come. Attend this presentation by CEA analysts to gain an understanding of the business trends, market forces and consumer dynamics behind the technology on display at the 2013 International CES. 

State of the Global CE Industry

January 6, 2013 3 – 4 p.m.
Mandalay Bay, Level 2, Breakers H
Las Vegas, Nevada

Countries with fast-growing economies and large emerging middle classes will lead the way in new CE revenue growth. This session provide a comprehensive look at global CE market trends.
