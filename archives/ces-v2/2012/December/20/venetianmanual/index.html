General Information for Venetian Exhibitors

If you are exhibiting at The Venetian, you will find everything you need to know specific to The Venetian on this page including the Venetian Exhibitor Manuals, information on show vendors, move-in and move-out schedules and rules and regulations.
 
If you are beginning to work on your show budget, please keep in mind that GES rates for material handling, furniture and other décor will be increasing by up to three percent. A full list of their services and pricing, as well as other vendor pricing, will be available in your Venetian Exhibitor Manual in September.
 
2013 GES material handling rates for Venetian exhibitors are as follows:

Venetian Exhibit Space (Hall D, Venetian Ballroom, Venetian Meeting Rooms):
Advance freight to the warehouse: $41.07 per 100 lbs. ($123.21, 300 lbs. minimum)
Direct freight to show site: $69.53 per 100 lbs. ($208.59, 300 lbs. minimum)
 
Venetian Exhibit Suites:
Advance freight to the warehouse: $56.41 per 100 lbs. ($169.23, 300 lbs. minimum)
Direct freight to show site: $77.54 per 100 lbs. ($232.62, 300 lbs. minimum)
Back to warehouse fee: $17.74 per 100 lbs. (53.22, 300 lbs. minimum)

Hospitality Suites General Information
Exhibitors have the opportunity to contract for hospitality suites during CES at The Venetian. Hospitality suites are to be used for meetings or for hospitality purposes only. Absolutely no exhibits may be constructed or installed and GES is not permitted to deliver freight to these suites. Further, signage is not permitted to be displayed outside of these suites in the hallways, on doorways, foyers, etc. Hospitality suite exhibitor locations will be printed on signage created and placed by CES Operations, as well as distributed at info desks and offices.

Reservation and Check-in/Check-out Procedures
Hospitality suite exhibitors may check-in after 3 p.m. on Sunday, January 6 at The Venetian’s Invited Guest Check-in desk in the front lobby. The exhibitor’s actual suite number will be assigned at this time. The suite number cannot be assigned prior to January 6. If you are checking in later than January 6, please advise Gaiya Berube so that your suite is held for you.
Exhibitors must check-out by 11 a.m. on Saturday, January 12.
 
Please be sure to fill out and submit the Multiple Guest Authorization Form and submit it Bobby Baumler, CES senior sales coordinator, by December 1.

Deliveries
No freight shipments are permitted to be delivered to a hospitality suite. Freight, crates, pallets or shipments are not permitted, as hospitality suites cannot be utilized for exhibiting equipment or products. The Venetian’s Package Center will only accept one (1) single box with a weight limit of 50 lbs. and an entire, cumulative shipment cannot exceed 200 lbs. Otherwise, the Package Center will not accept the shipment from UPS, FedEx, etc. Further, GES is not permitted to deliver packages or freight to these suites. Please keep this in mind when planning your meetings.

Furniture Removal or Additions
The hotel is unable to remove or move any furniture from hospitality suites. Exhibitors may not ship or bring outside furniture (couches, televisions, coffee tables, etc.). Exhibitors are not permitted to move furniture around within the suite. This policy is strictly enforced and, if violated, furniture movement fees ($450) will apply at The Venetian’s discretion.
