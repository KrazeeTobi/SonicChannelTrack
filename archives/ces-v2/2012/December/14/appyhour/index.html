Sales Navigator

The Casey Group

Sales Navigator helps users better plan and manager their schedules by seamlessly integrating the contact and calendar applications in a geo-centric view. Sales Navigator's contact grouping feature makes it easy for sales and service representatives to organize their customers and prospects, while the filtering feature allows them to focus on specific contacts. Sales Navigator plots users daily schedule as a route letting them know if they will be late or on time to appointments. Smart Time, an iPad feature, enhances planning by estimating the time needed to travel and builds an intelligent appointment schedule based off end time of one appointment and travel time to the next appointment.

Directed

Viper SmartStart 3.0.1 enables the "cloud- connected car" like never before, providing more powerful 2-way interaction with your vehicle and features like the patent-pending SmartSchedule and retrieval of diagnostic codes. As of October 18, 2012, the award-winning app is now integrated with Siri, making it the first mobile app to ever let users start their car with Siri on the iPhone.
