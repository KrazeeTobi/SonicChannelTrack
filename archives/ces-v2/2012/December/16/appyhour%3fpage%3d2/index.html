Iris

Lowes
The Iris app lets you control your home remotely from anywhere via smart phone or tablet. Remotely adjust your thermostat, turn the living room light on at night while away on vacation or check on your puppy throughout the day by viewing streaming video from your home directly in your app. The Iris app controls the DIY friendly Iris smart home system recently launched by Lowe's for greater control and management of your home. What is really innovative about the Iris offering is that basic monitoring and control of your home through the Iris app is free - no high monthly fees or long term contracts associated with most home monitoring/management solutions. Customer must have one of the Iris systems in their home to use the app.
