CNET Best of CES

CNET expert editors scour the International CES to find the most buzz-worthy, category-defining, innovative and promising products at the show. See their picks at the live awards presentation on the CNET stage.

CES Innovations Awards

The Innovations Design and Engineering Awards has given consumer technology manufacturers and developers an opportunity to have their newest products judged by a preeminent panel of independent industrial designers, independent engineers and members of the trade press. It's the place to see, touch, feel and understand the future of consumer technology.

IAWTV Awards

The red carpet rolls out at CES, thanks to the IAWTV Awards. The IAWTV Awards is the  first award show within the Web television industry to be presented by content creators, for content creators.

2013 CES Hot Stuff Awards

Stuff, the world’s best-selling gadget magazine, is back at the CES to announce the third successive CES Hot Stuff Awards, showcasing the 10 most desirable, most exciting and most innovative products to launch at the show. 

The 2013 What HI-FI? Sound and Vision Stars of CES

The 2013 International CES once again plays host to the Stars of CES—the Awards scheme from the world’s leading home entertainment buyer’s guide, showcasing the music and movie-boosting heroes of the world’s greatest technology show. Centered on the audio exhibits in the Venetian, Stars of CES will highlight the show’s most impressive debuts, from the latest high-tech streaming solutions to the very best in traditional hi-fi and AV. 

Technology & Engineering Emmy® Awards

Launched in 1948, The Technology and Engineering Emmy® Awards honor development and innovation in broadcast technology and recognize companies, organizations and individuals for breakthroughs that have a significant effect on television engineering.
