CES Government Conference
ICG Government, FedSources and the Consumer Electronics Association have joined forces to bring you the CES Government conference to be held January 4-5, 2006, at the Las Vegas Convention Center in Las Vegas, Nevada.

It may be the most significant conference today on government technology and management, and it is being held in conjunction with the International CES. CES Government brings together more than 400 senior executives from the Executive Branch, Congress, state government and industry in a high energy environment of program content and networking.

CES Government 2006 focuses on the challenges governments face in efficiently and securely moving and managing information across boundaries and jurisdictions of government. The conference highlights government priorities of information management in homeland security, social services and health care. Come to hear from Congressional leaders, Executive Branch management and policy executives and state and local technology and management leaders.
