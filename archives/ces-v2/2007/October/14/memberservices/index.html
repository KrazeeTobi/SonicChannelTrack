CEA Member Services at the 2007 International CES



As a CES member exhibitor...
Your company is eligible to receive the CES Member Services Packet that includes a parking pass, lunch tickets, and a plaque to display in your booth at the 2008 International CES.

In an effort to provide you with efficient customer services, please complete the following by COB Monday, November 5 so that your packet is mailed to the appropriate person within your company:

* First Name  
* Last Name  
* Company Name  
* Street Address  
* City  
* State     (within the United States)
* Zip/Postal Code  
* Country  
* Telephone  
* E-mail Address  
 
Your company is entitled to one (1) parking pass per venue. Please complete the following:
My company is exhibiting at the Las Vegas Convention Center (LVCC)
My company is exhibiting at the Sands Convention Center / Venetian
My company is exhibiting at the LVCC and the Sands Convention Center / Venetian
 
NOTE: Free public parking is provided at the Sands Convention Center / Venetian; therefore, your company will not need a parking pass. If this does not meet your needs, please contact membership@CE.org.

The CES Member Services Packet will be shipped out and tracked in late November or early-December. If you have any questions, feel free to contact membership@CE.org. E-mails will be returned within two business days.
