CESWeb Help
CESWeb is the hub of all information related to the Consumer Electronics annual tradeshow for consumer technology, 2001 International CES. It has been designed to help you find all the information you need to attend the show, exhibit at the show, speak at the show and cover the show as a member of the press.

The CESWeb Help System

As you can imagine, sometimes all of this information can get a little confusing, so for your benefit, we have created a system to provide you with help how, when and where you need it.

Wherever you see a blue question mark, clicking on it will provide you with a quick tip for whatever process or term you are currently using on the site.

In addition to the question marks, we have created a set of step by step instructions which are each designed to walk you through a specialized aspect of CESWeb, including registering for CES, searching for exhibitors, reserving housing or travel arrangements and using our conference planner.

Getting More Help

If you can't find help for your specific question here or would like personalized assistance please feel free to e-mail us at cesinfo@ce.org. We return most e-mails within 24 hours. Or to speak with a Customer Service Representative, call our Customer Service Center at 703 907 7605. We are available to answer calls during normal business hours (8am to 5pm EST Monday through Friday).
