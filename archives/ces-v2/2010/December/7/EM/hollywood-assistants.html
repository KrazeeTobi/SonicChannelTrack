Hollywood Assistants

(By invitation only)
As tomorrow’s studio chiefs, mega-producers, leading actors, agents and lawyers are today’s assistants, CES has created a custom experience for Hollywood’s next generation of leaders.

We've designed a program to introduce this important, highly-networked and influential community of professionals to the CES experience. Hollywood Assistants Program benefits include:


  • Free access to the CES show floor
  • Access to discounted block of rooms at the Cosmopolitan and Vdara at City Center* (through November 19)
  • Customized guided tour of the show floor*
  • Free access to select Entertainment Matters conference sessions on Saturday
  • 20 percent discount on full Entertainment Matters conference pass
  • Access to invite-only evening event on Saturday, January 8*

    * Space is limited.
