APPNATION VII

presented by APPNATION
  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Tracks
Share This Print This Page

APPNATION VII focuses on the global app economy and how leading developers and consumer brands are driving app discovery, acquisition, consumer engagement and monetization and will also look at the burgeoning Internet of Things (IoT) app ecosystem and development opportunities as the billions of connected devices and gadgets coming online in the coming years all need apps.

Top Three Topics:
  • Best practices about app discovery, acquisition, and monetization from leading app developers and publishers.
  • Global brands, agencies, and media companies are leveraging app-based marketing, in-app advertising and emerging IoT apps to drive consumer engagement.
  • Leading app companies, global brands, and research firms to discuss the state of the global app economy.
