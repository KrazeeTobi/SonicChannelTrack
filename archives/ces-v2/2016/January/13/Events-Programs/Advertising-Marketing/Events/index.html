    We have you covered from major celebrations lasting through the night to low-key, yet lucrative networking happy hours. Look below to see how to connect with colleagues, clients and prospects at any of these opportune events.


    C Space Opening Reception
    Presented by Rocket Fuel
    4-7 PM Tuesday, January 5
    ARIA, Level 1, Pinyon 4 Ballroom
    Open to all CES attendees

    You don’t want to miss this! Grab your colleagues and industry friends and head to the premiere C Space networking event. The Opening Reception is where you’ll enjoy drinks, food, and a live musical performance by the Best Indie Rock Band in America, Houndmouth. Named an artist to watch on Spotify's 'Spotlight on 2015,' check out Houndmouth on Spotify today!  


    MediaLink CES Kick-off Party
    8 PM-12 AM Tuesday, January 5
    Wynn, XS Nightclub
    By invitation only

    The premier, invitation-only industry event on the eve of CES. The MediaLink CES Kick-Off Event is a “check-your-arms-at-the-door”-type event, with attendees from all areas of the media, advertising, entertainment, technology, and finance communities. In attendance are the senior-most executives who buy and sell media from creative and media agencies, chief marketing officers, media inventory holders, investment banking, private equity, and venture capitalists, leading entertainment studio and entertainment executives and select talent.

    Hulu Cocktail Party
    4-8 PM Wednesday, January 6
    ARIA, barMasa
    By invitation only

    Check out what's new at Hulu. Join us for an exclusive evening featuring an open premium bar and small bites in the heart of C Space.

    Nielsen C Space Networking Lunch
    11:30 AM-12:45 PM Wednesday, January 6
    ARIA, Bristlecone 8
    Open to all CES attendees

    Don’t lose your chips – Total it up with Nielsen at their kickoff CES networking luncheon. Join the leading global measurement company and its top media professionals for networking and conversation. 

    Google Content Cocktail Party
    4-8 PM Wednesday, January 6
    Bellagio, Hyde
    By invitation only

    #CESafterdark Party, hosted by Twitter, Spotify and Muzik
    Thursday, January 7
    By invitation only

    Twitter is your live connection to culture. For what will be the hottest ticket of the night, Twitter and partners Spotify and Muzik will host an exclusive, private party with Tweet-worthy DJ sets and surprise celebrity guests.
