    Entertainment Matters Party
    presented by TAO Asian Bistro and Nightclub
    10 PM-Close Thursday, January 7
    Palazzo, LAVO Nightclub
    Exclusive for Entertainment Matters Attendees

    TAO Asian Bistro and Nightclub has garnered worldwide acclaim as the hottest restaurant, lounge and nightclub in Las Vegas transporting guests from the City of Sin to the Pacific Rim. Enjoy Complimentary admission from 10 PM to close, and an exclusive wristband for service to their well open bar from 10:30-11:30 PM. For bottle service/table reservations in advance, call 702-388-8588. Attendees must bring their CES badge or a printed copy of the event pass.
     
    * All guests must be 21+ w/ ID and compliant with dress code, including no shorts, sneakers, flip flops, t-shirts or hats permitted. Slacks/jeans, collared shirt and closed-toe dress shoes for gentlemen are required. TAO cannot admit patrons if the facility is at an admission hold due to capacity issues. All entry is at doorman discretion.

    • Brands Working with Startups presented by ANA
    • Chief Digital Officer Forum (CDX), presented by M2 Events
    • Content and Monetization, presented by Digital Hollywood
    • Digital Hollywood
    • Entertainment Summit, presented by Variety
    • Gaming Trends, presented by Digital Hollywood
    • High Dynamic Range, presented by the Advanced Imaging Society
    • High-Resolution Audio
    • The Future of Media
