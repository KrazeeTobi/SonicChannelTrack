With more than four decades of success, CES reaches across global markets, connects the industry and enables consumer technology innovations to grow and thrive.

Every year CES draws attendees from around the globe ready to do business. They’re senior-level executives and key decision-makers who are all looking to develop new business partnerships. 

Explore why CES is where innovation does business; the 2015 CES Audit Summary provides comprehensive, verified information on who attends CES, why they attend and what product categories and services they represent.

Benefits of Exhibiting
The following benefits are available to all official CES exhibitors, regardless of exhibit size and location. All opportunities are deadline permitting and subject to change at the discretion of the Consumer Technology Association (CTA). 

  • Five (5) complimentary exhibitor badges for every 100 square feet of exhibit space (minimum 10)
  • Five (5) complimentary single-session conference tickets
  • Opportunity to offer your customers and prospects complimentary registration to CES (up to 1,000 complimentary invitations)
  • Complimentary listings in the printed Official CES Show Directory and Show Guide & Maps 
  • Complimentary use of the Exhibitor Dashboard, powered by Map Your Show, to generate leads and promotions prior to CES
  • Use of the CES logo in your CES-related marketing and exhibit promotions
  • Exhibitor Profile distributed via Business Wire
  • Access to the CES pre-registered media list
  • Opportunity to host a press conference at CES
  • Complimentary, paperless press kit distribution in the CES press rooms
  • Opportunity to submit a product for inclusion in The CTA National Media Tour, aka the “Digital Answer Man Tour”
  • Opportunity to submit a 30 Second Press Pitch Video about product launches at CES; which will be featured in the Press section of CESweb.org
  • Opportunity to be listed or quoted in regional press releases
  • Discount on CES Innovation Awards entries
  • Discount on CES Unveiled events
  • Access to all CES Keynote Addresses on a first-come, first-serve basis
  • Opportunity to be considered for inclusion in the Official CES Show Floor Tours
  • Standard listing in the official CES 2016 App
    • Opportunity to upgrade to other levels of enhanced listing in the app or become a featured exhibitor

 
The following additional benefits are available to CTA member exhibitors only. Learn more and get the details on CTA member benefits.

  • Discounts on exhibit space
  • Member priority for CES 2016 space selection and booth change waiting list
  • 20 percent discount on CES attendee postal list rental
  • Up to 60 percent discount on CES Innovation Awards entry fees
  • Free LVCC parking pass
  • Tickets to VIP Networking Luncheon

  
Show Dates
The growth of CES has required us to adjust dates for the 2016 show to ensure we had availability for our exhibit venues. CES 2016 will run Wednesday, January 6-Saturday, January 9.
