    Maximize Your CES Experience

    We provide an array of pre-event and onsite services to help maximize your CES experience (and get your business done).

    International Business Center (IBC)

    LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S224
    Sands, Level 2, Lobby

    The International Business Center (IBC) offers commercial services exclusively for our international attendees and exhibitors. Services include private meeting rooms, reserved on a first-come, first-served basis; laptop hookups and computers with Internet access so you can stay connected to your office back home and a lounge area where you can meet casually with colleagues or rest after a busy day on the show floor. Multilingual interpreters are available in the LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S224 to assist you as needed.

    International Matchmaking Reception

    Wednesday, January 6, 2016
    6-7:30 PM
    LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S222

    All international attendees, exhibitors and media are invited to the International Matchmaking Reception. Don’t miss this opportunity and enjoy light refreshments after an exciting day on the CES show floor. This event is open to all international registrants and their invited guests.

    Interpreters

    Interpreters are available to assist you in several languages, including Spanish, French, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean. In the Main Registration Area, look for the counter titled, “International Language Interpretation” if you need assistance.

    International Buyer Program

    CES is a proud participant in the International Buyer Program, sponsored by the U.S. Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration. International Trade Specialists from the U.S. Department of Commerce are available in the IBC (LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S224) to provide matchmaking assistance and export trade counseling to international attendees and exhibitors.
