Media Briefing: Trends in Home Audio and Custom Installation
3-4 p.m. Saturday, January 5
The Venetian, Bellini 2106
Consumer demand for great sound is being driven by the increasing affordability and performance of audio products. Changes in technology and product design are ushering in dramatic new ways for people to enjoy music—including multi-room solutions. As a result, consumer interest in home audio is more acute than ever. This session, created especially for our media attendees, provides an update on trends in home audio from the diverse perspectives of manufacturing, retailing, custom installation, and industry research. 

Moderator: Todd Thibodeaux, SVP Industry Relations, CEA

Panelists:
Manufacturer: Kathy Gornik, President-THIEL Audio
Manufacturer: Petro Shimonishi, Vice President, Marketing, NetStreams
Retailer: Steve Caldero, Vice President, Sales, Ken Crane’s Home Entertainment
Installer: Ric Johnson, President, RL Johnson Construction Services
Market Research: Steve Koenig, Sr. Industry Analyst, CEA
