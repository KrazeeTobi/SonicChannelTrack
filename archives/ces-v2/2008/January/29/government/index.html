CES Government Conference
The Advisory Board of CES Government 2008 invites you to participate in the third annual CES Government Conference, to be held at the Ritz-Carlton Lake Las Vegas on January 9-10, 2008.

CES GOVERNMENT 2008: ELECTION IMPACT
The political season is about to begin….

Senior technology and management executives from government and industry will gather in Las Vegas next January to hear from, meet and share ideas with elected officials, candidates and political experts at CES Government 2008.  The conference focus this year is the November ‘08 elections, politics and their impact on government technology and management agendas.

Focused discussions, substantive plenary sessions and unique networking opportunities will provide conference participants’ distinctive opportunities to match the critical intersect of government management and technology applications with significant government challenges including immigration, healthcare, social services, homeland security and law enforcement, and social services.

The world’s largest and most exclusive technology showcase – International CES –stands as the high-energy backdrop and partner for the CES Government conference and this year’s premiere program.

Total attendance for this executive conference is strictly limited to 250. If you are interested in participating in the CES Government 2008 conference, please e-mail or call conference coordinator Caroline Boyd at cboyd@cesgovernment.com or (804) 359-1631 to receive an invitation or for additional information and to answer any questions you might have.
