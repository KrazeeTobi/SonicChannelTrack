              Stop Fighting and Fix It: Making Music Licensing Work for Innovators, Artists and Consumers

              January 7, 2014, 11 a.m.-12 p.m.
              LVCC, North Hall N254

              The music licensing system is broken. Different technologies are assessing different licensing rates seemingly at random and investors shy away. Some artists benefit from new revenue streams. Some claim underpayment. How can we modernize our anachronistic music licensing system to benefit entrepreneurs, musicians and consumers?

                Moderator:
              • Molly  Wood , Technology Reporter, Independent
                • Panelist:
              • Dave  Allen, Director of Interactive Strategy, NORTH
              • Hank  Shocklee, Music Producer, Founder of Public Enemy and President, Shocklee Entertainment
              • Michael  Weinberg, Acting Co-President, Public Knowledge
              • Steven M.  Marks, Chief, Digital Business and General Counsel, RIAA
