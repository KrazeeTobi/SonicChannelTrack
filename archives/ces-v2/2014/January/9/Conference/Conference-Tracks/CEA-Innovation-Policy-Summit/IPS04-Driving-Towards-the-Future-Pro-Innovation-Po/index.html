              Driving Towards the Future: Pro-Innovation Policies and the Connected Car

              January 7, 2014, 2-3 p.m.
              LVCC, North Hall N254

              Produced in Partnership with Global Automakers and Auto Alliance
              Connected cars are appearing on the road, presenting new opportunities for motorists and new challenges for policymakers. Government regulators are in position to promote, or delay, the car of the future. As automobiles and consumer electronics converge, how do we ensure a pro-innovation regulatory environment for connected automobiles?

                Presenter:
              • David  Strickland , Administrator , National Highway Traffic Safety Administration
                • Moderator:
              • John  Quain, Contributor, New York Times, CBS News
                • Panelist:
              • Hilary  Cain, National Manager, Technology and Innovation Policy, Toyota Motor North America, Inc.
              • Kevin  Link, Sr. VP, GM China, Verizon Telematics
              • Thilo  Koslowski, Vice President, Distinguished Analyst, Founder, , Gartner, Inc.
              • Mike  Stanton, President and CEO, Association of Global Automakers, Inc.
              • Mitch  Bainwol, President and CEO, Alliance of Automobile Manufacturers
              • Andrew  Brown, VP and Chief Technologist, Innovation and Technology Office, Delphi
