                You can follow CES via social media while you are at the show and all year long.

                Get breaking news, win cool prizes and learn more about the world’s most important technology event. Want to join the conversation? Be sure to use our official hashtag #CES2014.

                The Social Media Command Center at the 2014 CES, located in the Grand Lobby of the LVCC, will be powered by FleishmanHillard's black box, an interactive, portable, human-powered technology platform that integrates business data – including traditional, digital and social conversations – into real-time insight dashboards and visualizations. At the 2014 CES, black box will sift through the mountains of information being generated across the show floor and beyond. Show attendees will be able to walk up to the touchscreen populated with rotating topics based on conference content and click on a subject to see a dashboard featuring all the relevant content bubbling to the top, including conversational trends and the most influential conversation streams.

                Also, the brand new Social Media Vending Machine will be located in the Experience CEA display in the LVCC Grand Lobby. Attendees who use the vending machine to issue a tweet using the official CES hashtag (Remember...it's always #CES2014) and Twitter handle (@intlCES) will receive an exclusive CES branded t-shirt. Attendees who wear the t-shirt on the show floor will have the opportunity to win gift cards.
