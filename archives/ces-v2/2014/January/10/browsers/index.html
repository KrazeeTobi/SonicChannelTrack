              We endeavor to support a wide range of browsers on as many devices as possible. We redesigned the CESweb.org site with this goal in mind, however we had to make decisions regarding web technologies more than two years old. The browser you are using falls into this area. While the overwhelming majority of CESweb.org can be viewed on older browsers not all pages and not all functionality can be accessed with this older technology. We therefore recommend upgrading to one of the following browsers:

              The most recent versions of:
              Google Chrome, Firefox or Apple Safari 
              As well as Internet Explorer 10 or newer.
