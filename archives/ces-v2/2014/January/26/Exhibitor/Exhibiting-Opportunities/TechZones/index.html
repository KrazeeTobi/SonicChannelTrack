                Looking for more of a community setting? Participate in one of 20 CES TechZones, our themed areas featuring hot products, technologies, solutions and more. Again, you can opt to work with your team to create a custom booth design or select a turnkey package. As an exhibitor in a CES TechZone, you’ll receive the same benefits as a traditional booth space exhibitor, as well as amplified marketing to attendees promoting the TechZone.

                Below you’ll find a list of our current TechZones for the 2014 CES. Find your match or contact us about creating your own.

                3D Printing
                LVCC, South Hall 3


                Additive manufacturing has become one of the fastest growing printing processes in the twenty-first century – virtually making an object appear out of thin air by generating successive layers of material to create a three dimensional product. Showcasing leaders in the field and jaw-dropping demos, you’ll see the future of printing come to life on the show floor of CES in the all-new 3D Printing TechZone, sponsored by TCT Mag.

                Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

                 

                Academia Tech
                The Venetian, Level 1, Casanova

                Located within Eureka Park, the all-new Academia Tech TechZone will showcase game-changing developments, all stemming from some of the country’s leading educational institutions. Whether your students and staff have developed the latest cutting-edge software or engineered the next must-have gadget, strut your inventiveness and school pride! Take your place within Academia Tech.

                Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

                 

                Digital Health Summit
                LVCC, South Hall 2

                The Digital Health Summit focuses on the intersection of technology, health and wellness. Here, you'll see solutions for diagnosing, monitoring, and treating a variety of illnesses, from obesity to asthma, from poor vision or hearing to high blood pressure. Learn how wireless infrastructure, regulatory bodies, brilliant engineering and medicine converge to create this new world.

                Contact Living in Digital Times at summitinfo@digitalhealthsummit.com or 201-564-7900 for exhibiting opportunities.

                 

                Eureka Park
                The Venetian, Level 1, Casanova, Marco Polo, Galileo

                In partnership with UP Global, the National Science Foundation (NSF), GE and AT&T,  Eureka Park is a specialized TechZone that provides a unique exhibiting opportunity to launch a new product, service or idea. It is the premier CES destination where retailers, venture capitalists, manufacturers and other key attendee groups will find budding entrepreneurs, fledgling startups and home grown innovation.

                Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

                Eureka Park: NEXT
                The Venetian, Level 2, Venetian Ballroom

                Building off the success of Eureka Park, the home for startups at the International CES®, Eureka Park: NEXT hosts the progressing stage of Eureka Park startups. Retailers, venture capitalists, manufacturers and more will discover established startup companies looking to expand their growth. 

                Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.
                 

                 

                FashionWare
                The Venetian, Level 2, Venetian Ballroom

                FashionWare is an all-new lifestyle TechZone that is taking place at the 2014 CES. We look at the newest innovations—body sensors, mind and gesture controlled devices, personalized headsets, and the latest wrist-wear and eyewear. Style, function and technology intersect in this wow-factor, high-tech exhibit space, including an after party and live runway show.

                Contact Living in Digital Times at summitinfo@fashionwareshow.com or 201-564-7900 for exhibiting opportunities.

                 

                Fitness Tech
                LVCC, South Hall 2

                Today there are hundreds of ways to use technology to make your workouts and outdoor activities more fun, safer, and more effective. The FitnessTech TechZone will focus exclusively on the newest innovations and tools for the sports enthusiast and spotlight key trends impacting the collective multi-billion dollar sports, fitness and outdoors markets.

                Contact Living in Digital Times at summitinfo@sportsandfitnesstech.com or 201-564-7900 for exhibiting opportunities.


                G.hn
                LVCC, South Hall 1

                As service providers and consumers clamor to test new G.hn home networking technologies, the super speed Gigabit networking and services can be achieved in the home with the simplest of Plug and Play installations.  Acting as a robust backbone, G.hn works over any existing home wiring and enables multiple streaming of video content, including HDTV, 3DTV, audio and VoIP, video-surveillance, gaming and other performance-intensive applications possible in any room of the house. 

                Contact Home Grid Forum at administration@homegridforum.org or 503.619.0676 for exhibiting opportunities.

                 

                Gaming Showcase
                LVCC, South Hall 2

                The Gaming Showcase is the prime destination where gaming meets business. Here you’ll find gaming hardware, software and accessories — from online PC,HD gaming and plasma HDTV to surround sound and power conditioning hardware. Don’t miss your opportunity to experience the newest in gaming technologies.

                Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.


                GoElectricDrive
                LVCC, North Hall

                The GoElectricDrive TechZone, in partnership with the Electric Drive Transportation Association, is a dynamic showcase of electric drive technologies, products and services.  Electric vehicles and their associated charge stations – for homes, condominiums, public facilities – and a range of electric mobility products and services can be found in this thriving area of the show floor.

                 

                International Gateway
                The Venetian, Level 2, Venetian Ballroom and Hall D

                Expand your global network by visiting the International Gateway. The International Gateway features more than 800 exhibitors showcasing the latest innovative technologies and products around the world.

                Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

                 

                Kids@Play
                The Venetian, Level 2, Venetian Ballroom

                If you're a retailer, content provider, distributor, game developer, educator, policy maker or manufacturer of CE devices that appeal to this important market,  join us in the Kids@Play TechZone as we explore the way being digital today creates a smarter world tomorrow. And more important, we prepare you to serve them on their own terms.

                Contact Living in Digital Times at summitinfo@kidsatplaysummit.com or 201-564-7900 for exhibiting opportunities.

                 

                Mommy Tech
                The Venetian, Level 2, Venetian Ballroom

                The “mommy” audience, a code name for families using a variety of high-tech mobile products, is the fastest, largest and most vocal group of technology adoptees; the market is founded on good products, good service and trusted relationships. In the MommyTech TechZone you’ll see how a new generation of products is making homes smarter and safer, creating better entertainment, fostering safer driving experiences and more.

                Contact Living in Digital Times at summitinfo@mommytechsummit.com  or 201-564-7900 for exhibiting opportunities.

                 

                MotionTech
                LVCC, South Hall 2

                MotionTech, the all-new TechZone at the 2014 CES, sponsored by the MEMS Industry Group (MIG), will showcase some of the leading innovations in the progressive field of sensor technology, including the tiniest motion-sensing devices of all: MEMS. From gesture recognition and augmented reality to eye-tracking technology, attendees will discover connections to their devices they may have never known existed.

                Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

                 

                PMA@CES
                LVH, LVH Pavilion

                PMA@CES is the place for the most-concentrated exhibits of digital imaging/photography products and services. From capture to output, PMA@CES at the LVH is the place to be to learn about profitable products and services, reconnect with industry friends and establish vital new business connections.

                Contact Jeff Frazine at jfrazine@pmai.org or 517-788-8100 x101 for exhibiting opportunities.

                 

                Robotics
                LVCC, South Hall 1

                The robot revolution is here! Robots are finding their way into our homes, our offices, under our seas, in our factories and in our hospitals! In fact, they may soon be caring for our loved ones!
                The 2014 CES Robotics TechZone will feature a wide range of robotics and intelligent systems applications.

                Contact us at ecotton@ehpub.com or call 978-460-3408 for exhibiting opportunities.

                 

                Silvers Summit
                LVCC, South Hall 2

                The Silvers Summit showcases the products and services that keep boomers engaged, entertained, connected and healthy. Exhibitors in this destination meet with distributors, journalists, research firms and manufacturers that demonstrate the products and services that help mature consumers maintain a high quality of life.

                Contact Living in Digital Times at summitinfo@silverssummit.com  or 201-564-7900 for exhibiting opportunities.

                 

                TransformingEDU
                The Venetian, Level 2, Venetian Ballroom

                TransformingEDU, formerly HigherEdTECH, showcases how K-20 education is being reinvented through ground-breaking entrepreneurial innovation and corporate investment. Mixing leaders from the for-profit and non-profit sectors including institutions, organizations, government, corporations, and media, TransformingEDU is the TechZone where innovation meets education.

                Contact Kerry from Living in Digital Times at kerry@transformingedu.com or 214-356-1752 for exhibiting opportunities.

                 

                WristRevolution
                LVCC, South Hall 3

                The all-new WristRevolution TechZone at the 2014 CES is where style, sensors and connectivity intersect. From customizable and downloadable watch faces to internet-connectivity and app-based programs, the standard wristwatch transforms into a GPS, music player, health monitor, alert and message indicator and more. 


                Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

                 

                ZigBee
                LVCC, South Hall 1

                ZigBee offers green and global wireless standards connecting the widest range of devices to work together intelligently and help you control your world.  ZigBee gives consumers new ways to control CE devices, energy use, lighting, heating/cooling, appliances and security systems, to name just a few. ZigBee gives consumers reliable, low-cost products that are easy to use and install.

                Contact Krista Davi at kdavi@zigbee.org or 925-275-6658 for exhibiting opportunities.
