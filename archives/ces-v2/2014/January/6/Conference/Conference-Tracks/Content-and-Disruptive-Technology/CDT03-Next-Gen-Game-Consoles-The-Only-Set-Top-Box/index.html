            Next-Gen Game Consoles: The Only Set-Top Box You'll Ever Need?

            January 8, 2014, 12:30-1:30 p.m.
            LVCC, North Hall N259

            Mind-blowing graphics aside, the new generation of game consoles are advanced entertainment hubs that put a wealth of content options at our fingertips. Consider how these super powerful, smartphone-friendly consoles will change not only gaming, but also the entire entertainment landscape and possibly the way consumers receive all their video content in the home.

              Moderator:
            • Chris  Morris, Contributing Writer , CNBC.com
              • Panelist:
            • Steve  Carlin, Sr. Director, Ubisoft
            • Nanea  Reeves, COO, Machinima
            • Marc  Whitten, Chief Product Officer for Xbox, Microsoft Entertainment and Digital Media
            • Matthew  DiPietro, VP of Marketing , Twitch
