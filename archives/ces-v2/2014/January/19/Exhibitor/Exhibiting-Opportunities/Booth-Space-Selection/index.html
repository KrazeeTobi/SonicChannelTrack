              We are pleased to provide you with your company's complete 2015 International CES Exhibit Space Selection online information packet. As in the past, exhibit space selection for the 2015 International CES will take place on-site during the 2014 show in January.

              Please review all of these important documents to help facilitate your selection process:

              • YOUR COMPANY'S PRODUCT CATEGORY, SELECTION NUMBER, SELECTION DATE & TIME, AND SESSION NUMBER (located at the bottom of your space selection letter that was sent to you)

              • 2015 INTERNATIONAL CES BENEFITS & SERVICES SHEET
                An outline of your investment and complimentary exhibitor services.

              • THE 2015 INTERNATIONAL CES EXHIBIT SPACE SELECTION PROCESS
                A summary of the Exhibit Space Selection process, designed to help familiarize you with the procedures of Exhibit Space Selection.

              • THE 2015 INTERNATIONAL CES EXHIBIT SPACE SELECTION MAPS
                Shows the location of the Exhibit Space Selection Rooms. The Las Vegas Convention Center Exhibit Space Selection Room will be located in Room S223, in the Meeting Room Connector between the Central Halls and the South Halls. The Venetian Exhibit Space Selection room will be located in Titian 2306 (for Venetian Ballroom exhibitors only on Friday, January 10). Follow the directional signs!

              • PROXY STATEMENT/ALTERNATE DESIGNATION FORM 
                To be completed should you be unable to personally attend the Exhibit Space Selection process during your company’s scheduled selection time.

              • NEW THIS YEAR! PLEASE NOTE! ​2015 EXHIBIT SPACE CONTRACT – **Please take note of our  NEW ON-SITE CONTRACT ELECTRONIC PROCESSING PROGRAM this year:  You will NOT need to complete your contract before space selection!

              Once you have selected your space, you may proceed to the processing area as in previous years past. Your Exhibit Space Contract will then  be electronically generated by a CES representative for your review.  You will then be asked to submit your approval (electronically).  We’ll furnish a paper copy of your contract after the show, but in the meantime, should you request one on-site, we’re happy to provide.  

              • 2015 APPLICATiON AND EXHIBIT SPACE CONTRACT AND 2015 EXHIBIT SPACE CONTRACT TERMS AND CONDITIONS

              Please note existing share policy: "Share" companies in the LVCC and LVH will NOT accumulate points starting with the 2008 International CES. Share companies will keep their accumulated points as long as they become a contracted primary exhibitor and do not miss two consecutive shows.

              CES calculates your company's Exhibit Space Selection Number based on the following criteria (listed in order of significance):

              1. The number of years your company has participated in the International CES.

              2. CEA Membership Status (must be a current member in good standing to qualify).

              3. Size of exhibit space (used to determine tie-breaker situations).

              Companies will not be able to select exhibit space unless all financial and contractual commitments to CEA have been met.

              We urge you to report to the Exhibit Space Selection Room at least 15 minutes prior to your scheduled selection date and time. Upon arrival to the Exhibit Space Selection Room, you will be able to preview the 2015 floor plans to see which exhibit spaces have already been selected by other companies. In addition, please review the 2015 International CES Exhibit Space Selection Process. This outline is a summation of what to expect when you arrive at the Exhibit Space Selection Room and it will assist you in your Exhibit Space Selection planning.

              The International CES is segmented by specific product categories and technologies; each product category exhibits in a specific area or hall. Your exhibit location is dependent upon the products your company exhibits and/or intends to exhibit. At least 60 percent of the products shown in your booth must be dedicated to the respective product category and product category location. When determining your company's 2015 exhibit location, please refer to the floor plan that corresponds to your product category.

              PLEASE NOTE: Floor plans and the above product category locations are for general reference only and are subject to change.

              If you have any questions regarding the 2015 Exhibit Space Selection process, please feel free to contact your CES Account Manager or appropriate product category coordinator. We also recommend that you contact your sales coordinator prior to arriving on-site in January to resolve any concerns or address any questions.
              Thank you for your patronage and support of CES events. We look forward serving you for another successful year. See you in Las Vegas!
