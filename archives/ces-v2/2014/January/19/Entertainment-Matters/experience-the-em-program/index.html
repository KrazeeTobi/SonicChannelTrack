                CES reflects the dynamic consumer technology industry by evolving along with it. We reshape CES each year to keep the show fresh and relevant for attendees. This year we've expanded all things content and entertainment to address the growing demand from Hollywood and the entertainment community.

                Events and Conference Programming

                MediaLink CES Kickoff Event
                7:30-11:30 p.m. Monday, January 6
                Wynn Las Vegas, Tryst Nightclub
                By Invitation Only

                The premier invitation-only industry event on opening night of the CES. The MediaLink kickoff is a “check-your-arms-at-the-door”-type event, with numerous competitors from all areas of the media, advertising, entertainment, technology, and finance communities. In attendance are the senior-most executives who buy and sell media from creative and media agencies, chief marketing officers, media inventory holders, investment banking, private equity, and venture capitalists, leading entertainment studio and entertainment executives, and select talent.

                IAWTV Awards Gala
                6-8 p.m. Tuesday, January 7
                The Rio, Brasilia 4&5

                Celebrities, glitz, glam and the best of web video celebrating the 3rd annual International Academy of Web Television (IAWTV) Awards for excellence in web television. The IAWTV Awards is the official web television industry awards celebration established for digital content creators, by digital content creators, and voted on by the organization’s membership comprised of accomplished digital entertainment industry professionals. At the gala event, producers, directors, talent, studios, and networks will present awards honoring the best in web video and talent, both in front of the cameras and behind-the-scenes. Purchase tickets today. 

                Lavo: Entertainment Matters Party
                11p.m.-Close Wednesday, January 8
                The Palazzo, Lavo Nightclub
                By Invitation Only

                Lavo Nightclub is one of the premiere nightclubs on the Las Vegas Strip from the creator of Tao Asian Bistro, Tao Nightclub & Tao Beach. Enjoy complimentary admission from 11 p.m. to close, and an exclusive wristband for service to their well open bar from 11 p.m.-12 a.m. For bottle service/table reservations in advance, call 702.791.1818. Attendees must bring a printed copy of the invitation.

                * All guests must be 21+ w/ ID and compliant with dress code, including no shorts, sneakers, flip flops, t-shirts or hats permitted. Slacks/jeans, collared shirt and closed-toe dress shoes for gentlemen are required. TAO cannot admit patrons if the facility is at an admission hold due to capacity issues. All entry is at doorman discretion.

                Breakthrough of the Year Awards
                Red carpet: 630 p.m.; Show starts: 7:30 p.m.
                Thursday, January 9
                LVH, LVH Theatre

                Variety’s 2014 Breakthrough of the Year Awards is a unique red carpet event that will celebrate a diverse range of extraordinary talent who have excelled in the fields of film, television, sport, music, comedy, technology, philanthropy and social media. The show will be anchored with live musical and comedic performances and these innovators and pioneers in their fields will be presented with awards live on stage at the only red-carpet event at CES. Learn more on the award page. 


                Technology & Engineering Emmy Awards
                6:30 p.m. Thursday, January 9
                The Bellagio, Bellagio Ballroom

                The 65th Annual Technology & Engineering Emmy® Awards will take place at the 2014 CES, marking its eighth consecutive year presenting these honors during CES. Join us for this reception and presentation as we celebrate innovation from the likes of Netflix, Amazon, Google and others. Please visit the full award page for more information. 



                Entertainment Matters Conference Pass
                If you're in the content and entertainment arena, the Entertainment Matters Conference Pass is designed with you in mind. At only $750 in advance and $1,000 on-site, tracks within this pass include:

                • Cable Conference at CES

                • Connecting in the Cloud

                • Content and Disruptive Technology

                • Digital Hollywood

                • Digital Music at CES

                • Seeing is Believing - 3D, 4K and UHD

                • Smartphones and Tablets Conference

                • StoryTech Impact! Conference

                • Variety’s Entertainment Summit at CES

                Learn more about these tracks at CESweb.org/conference.

                The IMPACT! Show Floor Tour

                A contextually relevant floor tour with the StoryTech team of experts who hail from the fields of lifestyle tech, storytelling, marketing, social media strategy and production. You will see the array of technologies enabling the 5 most relevant trends of 2014 that you will be using to build audience and drive community around any business endeavors. StoryTech is a strategic consulting and packaging firm dedicated to informed collaboration between storytellers, including brands, and technology companies resulting in profitable business and content ventures.  To register, go to CESweb.org/register, click on the Tours tab and choose your StoryTech tour.
