            3 - 7 p.m.
            Tuesday, October 1, 2013

            OXO2 Level Two, Oxo Tower Wharf

            The third-annual CES Unveiled LONDON featured the very best UK-based consumer technology companies, including budding startups and established international brands, showcasing cutting-edge innovations. Executives from CEA announced a CES keynote speaker and gave a sneak peak of what’s to come at the 2014 CES in January and CEA’s chief economist Shawn DuBravac presented trend forecasts for media, analysts and other industry affiliates. The conference portion concluded with a panel discussion from top UK tech influencers View the schedule of events and exhibitor line up from the 2013 event and check out the media coverage to see the products and technologies that were showcased.
