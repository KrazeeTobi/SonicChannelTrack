              5 - 6 p.m.
              Wednesday, January 8, 2014

              International CES

              Winners Announced 5 p.m. 
              www.Stuff.tv

              Stuff, the world's best-selling gadget magazine, is back at the 2014 CES to announce the fourth successive CES Hot Stuff Awards, showcasing the 10 most desirable, most exciting and most innovative products to launch at the show. Stuff's international team of editors will be considering newly launched products from every category in order to unearth the very best that the show has to offer, resulting in a Hot Stuff Awards roster that's guaranteed to get gadget fans worldwide hot under the collar. Like Stuff itself, there will be nothing bland about the 2014 CES Hot Stuff Awards. The 2014 CES Hot Stuff Awards will be announced via press release and online at www.Stuff.tv at 5 p.m. PST on Wednesday, January 8. For early product submissions, please email hotstuff.awards@haymarket.com
