              Looking for more of a community setting? Participate in one of our CES TechZones, our themed areas featuring hot products, technologies, solutions and more.


              Again, you can opt to work with your team to create a custom booth design or select a turnkey package. As an exhibitor in a CES TechZone, you’ll receive the same benefits as a traditional booth space exhibitor, as well as amplified marketing to attendees through promotions of the TechZone.

              Below you’ll find a list of our current TechZones for the 2015 CES, plenty more are in the works. Find your match or contact us about creating your own.

               

              3D Printing

              Showcasing leaders in the field and jaw-dropping demos, you’ll see the future of printing come to life on the show floor of CES in the all-new 3D Printing TechZone.

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

               

              Academia Tech

              Located within Eureka Park, Academia Tech showcases game-changing developments, all stemming from some of the country’s leading educational institutions.

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.


              Driverless Car Experience

              Innovative technologies that support the future of autonomous/automated driving including parking assist, collision avoidance and emergency braking and much more. 

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.
               

              Eureka Park

              Eureka Park is a specialized TechZone that provides a unique exhibiting opportunity to launch a new product, service or idea. 

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.


              Eureka Park: NEXT

              Building off the success of Eureka Park, the home for startups at the International CES®, Eureka Park: NEXT hosts the progressing stage established startup companies looking to expand their growth. 

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

               

              Gaming Showcase

              Thee prime destination where gaming meets business. Here you’ll find gaming hardware, software and accessories — from online PC,HD gaming and plasma HDTV to surround sound and power conditioning hardware.

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

              Hi-Res Audio Experience

              A showcase of clearer, crisper audio on the most advanced high resolution audio (HRA) devices that are more convenient, compatible and cost-effective than ever before.

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

              International Gateway

              The International Gateway features more than 800 exhibitors showcasing the latest innovative technologies and products around the world.

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

              Motion Tech

              A display of leading innovations in the progressive field of sensor technology, including the tiniest motion-sensing devices of all: MEMS.

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.

              Wrist Revolution

              Where style, sensors and connectivity intersect. From customizable and downloadable watch faces to internet-connectivity and app-based programs, the standard wristwatch transforms into a GPS, music player, health monitor, alert and message indicator and more. 

              Contact a CES Sales representative at exhibit@CESweb.org or 703-907-7645 to get started.
