            • Hotel Reservations
            • Share This
            • Print This Page

            To assist you in your travel plans, CES negotiated discounted room rates at select Las Vegas hotels. 

            See www.CESweb.org/hotel (in English) for the full list of hotels and rates. To obtain the CES rate, all rooms must be booked directly with the hotel by visiting www.CESweb.org/hotel and selecting a hotel name. You will be taken directly to that hotel’s reservation page where you can enter your credit card information.

            If you need a block of 20 or more rooms, you may submit a request directly to your hotel(s) of choice. Your request will go directly to the hotel(s), and the hotel will have three business days to respond.

            Please note: There is no CEA/CES housing agency or bureau. Claims by housing agencies/bureaus that they are the official CES housing agency are untrue.

            Any questions? Please email CEShotels@CE.org.
