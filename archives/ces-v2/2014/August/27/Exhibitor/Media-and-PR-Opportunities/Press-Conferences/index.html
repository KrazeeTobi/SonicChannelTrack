                      Have a Major Annoucement to Make at #CES2015?

                      If your company is planning to make a major product launch or company announcement at CES, consider hosting a press conference or event on-site to brief the media on your big news.

                      IMPORTANT NOTE: OUTBOARDING

                      Outboarding is a serious violation of CES policy for which there are serious consequences. Any company found to be hosting a CES press event at a non-official CES venue during any of the official CES press events listed below is considered to be outboarding. CEA is unable to detect all instances of outboarding in a given year. However, when CEA determines that outboarding has occurred, CEA will vigorously enforce the rule against outboarding and may impose penalties for past as well as current violations.

                       
                      Official 2015 CES press event dates/times:
                       
                      Sunday, January 4:  

                      • 2-3 PM State of the Consumer Tech Industry and 2015 CES Trends to Watch

                      • 3-4 PM State of the Global CE Industry

                      • 4-7 PM CES Unveiled Las Vegas

                      Monday, January 5:

                      • 8 AM-5:45 PM 2015 CES Press Day

                      • 6:30-10 PM Official 2015 CES Press Events - TBA

                      Tuesday, January 6:

                      • 8-10 AM Official 2015 CES Press Events - TBA


                      CES press events are scheduled on a first-come, first-served, space-available basis and all CES exhibitors are eligible to host a CES press conference. CES offers the following press conference opportunities during the week of the show:


                      CES Press Day Conference Room
                       
                      8 AM-4:45 PM
                      January 5, 2015
                      Mandalay Bay, South Convention Center, Levels 2 & 3
                      Investment: Varies
                       
                      Open exclusively to registered CES media and industry analysts, Press Day is a day of back to back exhibitor press conferences featuring major product and company news the day before the 2015 CES show floor opens. Two (2) 45-minute Press Day conferences take place per hour. Press Day space is sold for 10 cents per square foot per hour, with the first four hours free, up to 4,750 square feet of reserved space. The formal Press Day space selection process took place in July for eligible exhibitors, but a few slots remain open. Press Day events will be included in the official CES Press Events Schedule.
                       
                      If you’re interested in this paid-for opportunity or have any questions, contact the CES PR Team.


                      CES Press Day Power Session Room 

                      8:30 AM-5 PM
                      January 5, 2015
                      Mandalay Bay, South Convention Center, Level 3
                      Investment: FREE

                      CES offers complimentary, one hour room reservations for companies looking to break their news quickly and directly to CES media. Mandalay Bay, Level 3, South Seas Ballroom J (seating for 172) will be reserved for Press Day Power Sessions and exhibitors will be given 15 minutes for set up, 30 minutes for the event and 15 minutes for tear down. Press Day Power Sessions will be included in the official CES Press Events Schedule.


                      PLEASE NOTE: The Power Session room will have a standard room set and exhibitors may not make any alterations to the existing set or order additional services, such as A/V or Internet. View base package details.
                       
                      If you’re interested in hosting a Press Day Power Session, please fill out the 2015 CES Press Conference Request Form. The final submission deadline is Friday, December 19, but events are scheduled on a first-come, first-served basis.
                       

                      CES Press Conference Room 
                       
                      January 6 &  7, 2015
                      Venetian, Galileo Ballrooms 902, 903, 904 & 905
                      LVCC, South Hall Connector, S227 A, S227 B & S228
                      Investment: FREE*
                       
                      All CES exhibitors are eligible to hold a newsworthy press conference during the show and to have their event included in the official CES Press Events Schedule. Events can be held in a CES-provided press conference room at The Venetian in Galileo Ballrooms 902, 903 (available both Tuesday, January 6 and Wednesday, January 7)  904 & 905 (available Tuesday, January 6 only) or at the LVCC in South Hall Connector Room S227A, S227B or S228 (available both Tuesday, January 6 and Wednesday, January 7).
                       
                      Reservations run in two-hour increments, giving exhibitors 30 minutes for set up, one hour to host their event and 30 minutes for tear down. Rooms come with a standard base package, including theater-style seating, three draped tables, a stage riser, a podium with a light, a wired microphone and two easels for signage. View base package details specific to each ballroom.
                       
                      To schedule an exhibitor press conference, fill out the 2015 CES Press Conference Request Form. The final submission deadline is Friday, December 19, but events are scheduled on a first-come, first-served, space-available basis.
                       
                      *Exhibitors are responsible for ordering and paying for catering, additional furniture, Internet or A/V services above and beyond the base package. 
                       

                      CES Press Conference in Meeting Rooms or Exhibits
                       
                      January 6-9, 2015
                      Investment: FREE*
                       
                      All CES exhibitors are eligible to hold a newsworthy press conference in their exhibit or paid meeting space during the show and to have their event included in the official CES Press Events Schedule, Events can be held anytime during the show.
                       
                      To schedule a press conference in your exhibit or meeting space, fill out the 2015 CES Press Conference Request Form by Friday, December 19.
                       
                      *Events held on the show floor before or after show hours require advanced security forms and hired security guards. If your request form indicates that you’d like to host your event before or after show hours, the CES PR team will send you the appropriate paperwork.
                       
                      Interested in booking a meeting room? Contact your sales representative today.
                       

                      If you have general questions about hosting a press conference during the 2015 CES, please contact the CES PR team.
