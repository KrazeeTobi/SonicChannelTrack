                      2015 CES Asia

                      CEA recently announced the launch of a brand new tradeshow: The 2015 International CES Asia. Produced by CEA, the event will take place in Shanghai, a top global city for technology innovation. CES Asia will run May 25-27 at the Shanghai New International Expo Center (SNIEC) and will span roughly 8,500 net square meters of exhibit space. Registration will launch at the end of this year and booth sales will start in August. To keep up with the latest information, bookmark www.CESasia.com.

                      CES Innovation Awards Call for Entries Opens August 11

                      Entries for the 2015 International CES Innovation Awards will be accepted August 11-September 15, 2014. The annual competition honors outstanding design and engineering across 28 consumer technology categories, including one new product category: Safe Driving. Honorees will be announced at CES Unveiled New York on November 11 and showcased at the 2015 International CES. Questions? Contact InnovationAwards@CE.org.

                      About CEA

                      The Consumer Electronics Association (CEA) is the technology trade association representing the $211 billion U.S. consumer electronics industry. CEA owns and produces the International CES, the world’s gathering place for all who thrive on the business of consumer technologies. CES attracts the world’s business leaders and pioneering thinkers to a forum where the industry’s most relevant issues are addressed. Learn more.
