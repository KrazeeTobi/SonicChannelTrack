                    Pinyon Ballroom Complex within ARIA

                    New for 2015, C Space at ARIA is the official CES destination for creative communicators, brand marketers, advertising agencies, digital publishers and social networks. C Space will tell the story of how content, creativity, technology, brand marketing, influencers and the consumer come together as part of the CES universe.

                    Your CES badge provides access to all three official show locations: C Space at ARIA, Tech East and Tech West. At C Space you can enjoy activities and displays listed below.


                    Participants:

                    GroupM, Mindshare, MEC, Maxus, Xaxis, Modi Media, Amazon Media Group, Hulu, Neustar, Rubicon Project, Meredith Corp., USA TODAY | Gannett, MediaLink, Taboola, Samba TV, HookLogic, BrightLine, Factual, Landmark Ventures/ShellyPalmer and Allant. See them here.

                    Tailored Conference Programming:

                    Included with your CES Exhibit Plus Pass.

                    • C Space Headliners
                      Neal Mohan, VP, Display and Video Advertising, Google and
                      Scott Burke, SVP, Advertising, Yahoo

                    • Digital Disruption, presented by ANA
                    • Marketing and Engagement, presented by CEA

                    View the full list of conference sessions and pass options.
                     

                    Events and Activities Include:


                    Exclusive for Brand Matters, Entertainment Matters and C Space participants and the content, marketing and advertising communities
                    4-7 PM Tuesday, January 6
                    ARIA, Level 1, Pinyon 4
                    Gather with your colleagues and friends across the creative landscape to kick off a week of storytelling.


                    Exclusive for Brand Matters, Entertainment Matters and C Space participants and the content, marketing and advertising communities
                    4-8 PM Wednesday, January 7
                    ARIA, Level 1, barMASA
                    Check out what's new at Hulu. Join us for an exclusive evening featuring an open premium bar and small bites from three-star Michelin chef, Chef Masa Takayama, in the heart of C Space.

                    • Grab a refreshment, network with colleagues and clients and view imaginative displays in the Pinyon 4 Ballroom.
                    • ARIA meeting suites


                    Transportation:

                    • C Space Shuttle, presented by HookLogic 
                      This dedicated transportation service will run between C Space at ARIA, CES Tech East and Tech West. From ARIA attendees can choose whether they want to go directly to Tech East (LVCC stop) or Tech West (Venetian stop) as their destination. Shuttle service runs every 15 minutes from 8 AM-6 PM Tuesday-Wednesday and 8 AM-4 PM on Thursday. Drop off and pickup locations are as follows
                      ARIA: Level 1, North Valet Entrance
                      Tech East: LVCC, Grand Patio
                      Tech West: Venetian, Underground Bus Loading Area

                    • Bellagio - CityCenter- Monte Carlo Tram
                      This tram runs daily every seven minutes from 8 AM-4 AM. Stops include the Bellagio Spa Tower, Crystals and Monte Carlo's Street of Dreams shopping center. 


                    C Space Hours:

                    Please note the hours for C Space at ARIA are slightly different than the other CES venues. C Space closes early on Thursday (4 PM) and all day Friday. 
                    Tuesday, January 6: 10 AM-6 PM
                    Wednesday, January 7: 9 AM-6 PM
                    Thursday, January 8: 9 AM-4 PM
                    Friday, January 9: CLOSED
