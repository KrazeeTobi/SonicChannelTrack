First-Timer ResourCES


We're pleased to welcome you to your first International CES®!

Every year, one event brings together the innovators on technology's cutting edge. And for those four days, Vegas glows brighter. To make planning and attending your first CES a success, we've put together a list of helpful First-Timer ResourCES just for you.

Hotel and Travel


Stay and save with the official CES hotels. You’ll also receive complimentary CES shuttle service between these properties to and from the LVCC.

Planning and Navigation


Know before you go – Create a MyCES account to start planning and organizing your 2012 International CES experience. You can keep track of exhibitors of interest as well as organize sessions and appointments with your own calendar.

Take it on-site – Use Follow Me, the official mobile app of CES, to sync your MyCES account for on-site use. Not only will you have access to all of your preshow planning efforts, but also important show alerts, late-breaking CES news, and real-time interactive floor maps.

Guidebook – For attendees, BY attendees. This First-Timer ResourCES Guidebook covers all topics from what to wear, bring, see, do, eat and more. Check back soon to download your copy.

Show Floor


  • Official CES Venues – Spread across 1.7 million net square feet of exhibit space, you’ll see technology come to life throughout the LVCC, Hilton and The Venetian.
  • TechZones – More than 25 uniquely themed TechZones group new technology markets together and feature up-and-coming products, services and companies for the industry.
  • Product Categories – want to search exhibitors based on their specialty market? With 15 product categories represented at CES, you’ll find more options than anywhere else.


[return to question list]


Events and Awards


CES and prestigious partners host awards programs, inspiring keynote addresses and special events to honor both industry leaders and groundbreaking products and technologies.
  • Keynote Addresses – Take a front row seat at the future of technology! Attend CES keynote addresses delivered by the industry’s leaders. Keynote addresses will take place in both The Venetian, Palazzo Ballroom and the Las Vegas Hilton Theater.
  • Awards – From CNET’s Best of CES to the International Academy of Web Television (IAWTV) to the Emmy Awards (yes, The National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences kind of Emmy), the 2012 CES is full of some very noteworthy people, products and companies!
  • Events –The 2012 CES capitalizes on the sensational Las Vegas nightlife to host some of the industry’s most extraordinary afterhour affairs.
