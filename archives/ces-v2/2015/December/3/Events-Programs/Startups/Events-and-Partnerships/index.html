    The Consumer Technology Association (CTA), the owner and producer of CES, offers a year-long schedule of events that focus on innovation and the startup community.

    As a Startup Member, these events and strategic partnerships provide excellent opportunities to network and learn from our industry’s leaders.

    CTA Innovate!

    Innovate! is CEA’s annual meeting place for both startups and established consumer technology companies. The conference is open to members and non-members alike and features a special registration rate for startups. Startup opportunities include focused mentorship with business leaders and VCs, as well as multiple networking events over three days of programming. Content tracks, like Media Training and Funding 101, are specifically geared towards small business interests. Additionally, the coveted CTA Innovation Entrepreneur Awards are presented at Innovate!

    CTA Startup Member Month

    Throughout August each year, CTA celebrates our unique members, highlighting startup member interviews and success stories and featuring blog posts about member benefits and initiatives particularly valuable to startups, including our mentor program, advocacy initiatives and much more.

    Startup Industry Partners

    CTA has established important partnerships with leaders in the startup space to help us create dynamic experiences that connect our members with the field’s brightest minds. We partner with organizations like:

    • 1776
      A global incubator and seed fund that helps engineer the success of the world’s most promising startups tackling important global challenges. 1776 hosts an annual competition the Challenge Cup, engaging Startup Federation partners in 16 cities across the world to identify the most promising new startups and connect them with the information, mentors and resources they need to succeed – regardless of their geographic location.

    • Tech.co
      Formerly Tech Cocktail, Tech.co is a media company and events organization for startups, entrepreneurs and technology enthusiasts. Tech.Co dedicates itself to covering news, how-to’s, up-and-coming startups and industry trends online and hosting events in over 30 cities in the U.S. and abroad. CEA members are invited to get involved in Tech.co’s and Celebrate events happening across the country.

    • Startup Grind
      The world’s startup community, actively educating, inspiring and connecting founders in over 170 cities through events, media and partnerships with organizations like Google for Entrepreneurs. To date, Startup Grind has helped 170,000 entrepreneurs find mentorship, connect to partners and hires, pursue funding and reach new users.

    To take advantage of these events and partnerships, join CTA through our Startup Membership.
