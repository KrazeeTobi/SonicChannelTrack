Innovating Privacy: New Frameworks for Changing Technology

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 7
LVCC, North Hall, Room N254
Add to Calendar 1/7/2016 11:30:00 AM 1/7/2016 12:30:00 PM America/Los_Angeles Innovating Privacy: New Frameworks for Changing Technology Consumers are enjoying the benefits of connected devices while (grappling) navigating new privacy issues. Industry and regulators alike are working to understand consumer preferences while preserving creativity and flexibility to innovate with data. How can we adapt existing frameworks to respond to consumer concerns?

  • Chris Wolf, Partner, Hogan Lovells
