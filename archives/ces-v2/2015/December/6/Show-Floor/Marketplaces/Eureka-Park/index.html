    Sands, Level 1

    Eureka Park is the flagship startup destination at CES, providing a unique opportunity to launch a new product, service or idea. See the latest ideas and innovations from an international breadth of budding entrepreneurs. Home-grown innovation ranging from fledgling prototypes to progressing startups will span the entire consumer technology spectrum in this unique Marketplace.

    Use this map to find your way around Eureka Park and discover places like: 

    • University Innovations Marketplace
    • Eureka Park Lounge, presented by Arlington, VA, Home of CTA
    Visit the Eureka Park Lounge and recharge, refuel and relax. Or take a moment to check email and get caught up on work between sessions. Eureka Park Lounge is presented by Arlington Economic Development. Arlington, VA, home to CTA, is where some of the world’s top technological innovations, ranging from GPS and voice-recognition software to the Internet, were developed. It’s easy to see why businesses chose Arlington as one of America’s preeminent places to do business.
