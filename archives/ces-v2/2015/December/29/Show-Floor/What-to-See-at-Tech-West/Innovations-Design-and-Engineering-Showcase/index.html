    Sands, Level 1, Hall G, Booth #83319

    Discover the CES 2016 Innovation Awards Honorees within the hands-on display in the CES 2016 Innovation Awards Showcase, presented by Gracenote. Touch and experience the products that makes this one of technology's most exclusive lists.
     
    Each year the CES Innovation Awards give consumer technology manufacturers and developers an opportunity to have their newest products judged by a preeminent panel of independent industrial designers, independent engineers and members of the trade press. The showcase at CES displays the honorees of the program, giving attendees a first-look at the future of innovative design.
