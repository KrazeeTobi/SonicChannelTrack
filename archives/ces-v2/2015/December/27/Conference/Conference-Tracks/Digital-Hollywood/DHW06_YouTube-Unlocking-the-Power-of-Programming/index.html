$ Additional Registration Required

3:30-4:30 PM January 5
LVCC, North Hall, Room N262
Add to Calendar 1/5/2016 3:30:00 PM 1/5/2016 4:30:00 PM America/Los_Angeles YouTube: Unlocking the Power of Programming YouTube has officially evolved from being a massive repository of extraordinary video content to a massive repository of content channels. These channels bring viewers millions of hours of global video production and spontaneity. YouTube is re-writing the future of television. LVCC, North Hall, Room N262 CES CES@CE.org false MM/DD/YYYY
