                  Call for Authors

                  We are now accepting applications to join Gary's Book Club at the 2016 International CES. Gary's Book Club showcases the works of prominent authors in the tech industry. Authors will share their insights during an interview on the CEA Stage followed by a book signing. Attendees have the opportunity to meet the authors, purchase books through Barnes & Noble and have their copies signed onsite. There is no charge to take part in this program.

                  To be admitted into the book club, books need to be launched within the past year (January 2015-January 2016). In addition, the book should cover technology, innovation, technology or innovation policy. To be considered, please fill out the form below. The call for authors ends on May 1, 2015.
