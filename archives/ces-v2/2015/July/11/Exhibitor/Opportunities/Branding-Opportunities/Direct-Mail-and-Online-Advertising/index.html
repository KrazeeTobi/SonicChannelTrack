More than four million impressions of electronic direct mail to more than 200,000 unique readers keep CES attendees up-to-date.

Reach CES' community through online directory sponsorships, spotlights in the CES Now attendee newsletter, dedicated email blasts and attendee postal mailing list rentals.

Attendee Newsletter Sponsorships
CES Now is the regularly scheduled attendee newsletter that deploys to our attendees and prospects. Sponsor one or several newsletters to generate brand recognition for your company. Newsletters are emailed to over 200,000 readers, including 2016 pre-registered attendees, 2015 verified attendees, prospects, international attendees, VIP buyers and more.

Contact Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681 to learn more and secure this opportunity.


Dedicated Email Blasts
Send a dedicated email blast, fully branded from your company to CES attendees. These blasts are created entirely by you and will be sent out directly by CES on your behalf. Through each email blast, you will have the opportunity to reach more than 200,000 readers, including CES 2016 pre-registrants, 2015 verified attendees, prospects, international attendees, VIP buyers and more.

Contact Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681 to learn more and secure this opportunity.


Attendee Postal Mailing List
The CES attendee mailing lists, available only to official CES exhibitors, help you reach last year's attendees and the coming year’s pre-registered attendees. We'll help you target specific demographics of attendees you want to see at your exhibit.

Contact us to learn more and secure this opportunity.


Official CES App
Get your products and services in the palms of CES attendees’ hands through our official app. With several enhanced placement opportunities, the CES app can help set you apart and highlight your brand. Packages range from enhanced Exhibitor Directory listings to a presenting sponsor opportunity. 

Contact Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681 to learn more and secure this opportunity.


Online Directory Sponsorships
Powered by our partners at Map Your Show, the official online exhibitor directory for CES is referenced throughout the year to connect attendees to your products and services before, during and long after CES. All CES exhibitors receive a free directory listing, but you can choose from a variety of packages to help you boost your bottom line and connect to the attendees you want to meet.

Contact Brett Glatfelter or call 908-399-6245 for more information and to secure any of these online directory opportunities.
