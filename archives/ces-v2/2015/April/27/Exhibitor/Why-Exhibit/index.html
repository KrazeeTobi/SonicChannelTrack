                With more than four decades of success, the International CES reaches across global markets, connects the industry and enables consumer technology innovations to grow and thrive.

                Every year CES draws attendees from around the globe ready to do business. They’re senior-level executives and key decision-makers who are all looking to develop new business partnerships. 

                Explore why CES is where innovation does business; including highlights from the 2015 Pre-Audit Flyer, which provides comprehensive, verified information on who attends the International CES, why they attend and what product categories and services they represent. The 2015 CES Audit Summary will be available in May 2015.

                Benefits of Exhibiting
                The following benefits are available to all official CES exhibitors, regardless of exhibit size and location. All opportunities are deadline permitting and subject to change at the discretion of CEA. 

                • Five (5) complimentary exhibitor badges for every 100 square feet of exhibit space (minimum 10)
                • Five (5) complimentary single-session conference passes
                • Opportunity to offer your customers and prospects complimentary registration to CES (up to 2,000 complimentary invitations)
                • Complimentary listings in both the online and print versions of the Official International CES Show Directory, Map Guide and Show Guide
                • Complimentary use of the Exhibitor Dashboard, powered by Map Your Show, to generate leads and promotions prior to CES
                • Use of the International CES logo in your CES-related marketing and exhibit promotions
                • Exhibitor Profile distributed via Business Wire
                • Access to the International CES pre-registered media list
                • Opportunity to host a press conference at the International CES
                • Complimentary, paperless press kit distribution in the CES press rooms
                • Opportunity to submit a product for inclusion in The CEA National Media Tour, aka the “Digital Answer Man Tour”
                • Opportunity to submit a 30 Second Press Pitch Video about product launches at CES; which will be featured in the Press section of CESweb.org
                • Opportunity to submit a product for consideration in CESTV
                • Opportunity to be listed or quoted in regional press releases
                • Discount on CES Innovation Awards entries
                • Discount on CES Unveiled events
                • Access to all International CES Keynote Addresses on a first-come, first-serve basis
                • Opportunity to be considered for inclusion in the Official CES Show Floor Tours Standard listing in the official 2016 CES App
                  • Opportunity to upgrade to other levels of enhanced listing in the app or become a featured exhibitor

                 
                The following additional benefits are available to CEA member exhibitors only. Learn more and get the details on CEA member benefits.

                • Discounts on exhibit space
                • Member priority for 2016 CES space selection and booth change waiting list
                • 20 percent discount on CES attendee postal list rental
                • Up to 60 percent discount on CES Innovation Awards entry fees
                • Free LVCC parking pass
                • Tickets to VIP Networking Luncheon

                  
                Show Dates
                The growth of International CES has required us to adjust dates for the 2016 show to ensure we had availability for our exhibit venues. The 2016 CES will run Wednesday, January 6-Saturday, January 9.
