                  Eureka Park is the flagship startup destination at CES, providing a unique opportunity to launch a new product, service or idea. At the 2015 CES the latest ideas and innovations from more than 20 countries were found throughout  this Marketplace. It celebrated the international breadth of budding entrepreneurs and home-grown innovation from fledgling prototypes to progressing startups, spanning the entire consumer technology spectrum. 

                  Also featured in Eureka Park:

                  • University Innovations Marketplace
                  • Concentrated exhibits from startups originating in France and Israel
                  • Exclusive exhibits from Indiegogo and TechCrunch's Hardware Battlefield finalists
                  • Startup Stage presented by UP Global in partnership with Magna

                  Check out our 2015 CES startup highlight video and stay tuned for what's to come at the 2016 CES within Eureka Park.
