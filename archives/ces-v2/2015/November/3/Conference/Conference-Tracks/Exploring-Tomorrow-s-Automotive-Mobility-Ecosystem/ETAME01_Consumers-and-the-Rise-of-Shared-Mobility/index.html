Consumers and the Rise of Shared Mobility

9-10 AM January 8
LVCC, North Hall, Room N261
Add to Calendar 1/8/2016 9:00:00 AM 1/8/2016 10:00:00 AM America/Los_Angeles Consumers and the Rise of Shared Mobility The shared economy is expanding. Ride-sharing, car-sharing, and integrated multi-modal mobility systems are driving consumer preference towards personal transportation options that provide access versus require ownership. Enjoy this discussion exploring how consumer engagement is changing in the expanding shared economy. LVCC, North Hall, Room N261 CES CES@CE.org false MM/DD/YYYY

The shared economy is expanding. Ride-sharing, car-sharing, and integrated multi-modal mobility systems are driving consumer preference towards personal transportation options that provide access versus require ownership. Enjoy this discussion exploring how consumer engagement is changing in the expanding shared economy.
    Moderator:
  • Craig Giffi, Vice Chairman, U.S. Automotive Industry Leader, Deloitte

  • Scott Corwin, Director, Deloitte
