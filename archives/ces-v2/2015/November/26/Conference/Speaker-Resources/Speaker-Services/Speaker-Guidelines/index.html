10 Things You Need to Know About Speaking at CES

These helpful tips will help you prepare for speaking at CES 2016. 

  • CES speakers are responsible for their own travel arrangements and hotel accommodations.
  • Speakers should coordinate materials and arrival times with their session moderators. Speakers are responsible for providing their own laptops for presentations. A list of provided AV equipment will be available through the Speaker Handbook.
  • Press and analysts are admitted to conference sessions free of charge. There are no specific press services offered for speakers. If your company is exhibiting at CES, we do have public relations resources available. 
  • If selected to speak at CES, your information including headshot and bio will be promoted on CESweb.org once you have accepted your speaker agreement in your speaker profile. If you have any questions regarding your speaker profile, please contact CESconference.
  • All conference session times and locations are listed online in the Session Schedule. Please note that the schedule is subject to change.
  • Handouts can be uploaded to the official CES App. More details will be available later this Fall.
  • Promotional materials may not be distributed in session rooms. This is a privilege offered to paying sponsors only. Get more information on these sponsorship opportunities.
  • We do not share our attendee or press lists with speakers.
  • All CES speakers have access to the Speaker Ready Rooms. These rooms are located in the LVCC, North Hall, N263, Venetian, Level 4, Marcello 4401, and ARIA, Platinum Lounge, Level 1, Pinyon 3. These rooms are equipped with Wi-Fi and refreshments and are the perfect spot to meet up with your fellow panelists.
  • Each speaker receives up to five (5) complimentary session tickets to attend CES produced conference sessions (exclusions apply). These can be picked up in the Speaker Ready Rooms when retrieving speaker badges.
