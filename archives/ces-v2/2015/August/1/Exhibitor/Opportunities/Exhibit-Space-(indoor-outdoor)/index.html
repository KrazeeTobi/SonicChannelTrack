    Showcase your innovations, deliver inspiring experiences and hold important meetings.

    Indoor
    Each hall of the official CES venues are divided into product categories to ensure no company is overlooked. Your exhibit’s hall location will be determined by the product category your technology represents. We have options for footprints for any budget. You can also create a custom booth design or choose a low-cost, turnkey (finished) exhibit package.

    Our indoor space includes not only traditional exhibit space, but meeting rooms and suites as well.

    Current CEA members: Visit CE.org/membership for information on your discounted exhibit space rate.

    Outdoor
    Complement your indoor exhibit space or create a unique footprint outside, front and center at Tech East. Be as imaginative and innovative in this space as you are with your product – exhibit in the open air or use a canopy, tented or dome structure; build a stage, bring a car, host a happy hour. Whatever fits your needs. This space is ideal for exhibitors looking to strengthen their experiential marketing.
