As in the past, exhibit space selection for CES 2016 will take place on-site during the 2015 CES, January 6-9. Although a packet is mailed mid-December to the contact information we have on file for each exhibitor, below you’ll find the documents needed for CES 2016 Exhibit Space Selection process.

Please review all of these important documents to help facilitate your selection process.
 

Selection Appointment Details
Found at the bottom of your space selection letter mailed mid-December. This letter includes your company's product category, selection number, selection date & time and session number. If you have any questions, please reach out to the appropriate CES Sales Representative.

Space selection for suites at the Renaissance Las Vegas, Westgate Las Vegas, Wynn Las Vegas and Encore at Wynn, as well as exhibits at The Venetian for audio (including High-Performance and the High-Resolution Audio Marketplace) will take place in March and April. A CES representative will contact current exhibitors in these spaces prior to offering space to additional exhibitors.


Exhibit Space Selection Process
We urge you to report to the Exhibit Space Selection Room at least 15 minutes prior to your scheduled selection date and time. Upon arrival to the Exhibit Space Selection Room, you will be able to preview the 2016 floor plans to see which exhibit spaces have already been selected by other companies. In addition, please review the CES 2016 Exhibit Space Selection Process. This outline summarizes what to expect and how to plan for your space selection.


CES 2016 Exhibit Space Contract and Terms & Conditions
CES 2016 Exhibit Space Contract and our Terms & Conditions are available for your reference, but you do not need to complete this form prior to space selection.

Once you have selected your space, you may proceed to the processing area where your Exhibit Space Contract will then be electronically generated by a CES representative for your review.  You will then be asked to submit your approval (electronically).  A copy of the contract will be sent to the contact information provided after the 2015 CES concludes. Copies are available on-site during your selection process, upon request.

CES 2016 Floor Plans
The International CES is segmented by specific product categories and technologies; each product category exhibits in a specific area or hall. Your exhibit location is dependent upon the products your company exhibits and/or intends to exhibit. At least 60 percent of the products shown in your booth must be dedicated to the respective product category and product category location. When determining your company's 2016 exhibit location, please refer to the floor plan that corresponds to your product category.

PLEASE NOTE: Floor plans and the above product category locations are for general reference only and are subject to change.

Please note existing share policy: "Share" companies in the LVCC, Wynn/Encore, Renaissance and Westgate will NOT accumulate points starting with the 2008 International CES. Share companies will keep their accumulated points as long as they become a contracted primary exhibitor and do not miss two consecutive shows.

CES calculates your company's Exhibit Space Selection Number based on the following criteria (listed in order of significance):

  1. The number of years your company has participated in the International CES.

  2. CEA Membership Status (must be a current member in good standing to qualify).

  3. Size of exhibit space (used to determine tie-breaker situations).

Companies will not be able to select exhibit space unless all financial and contractual commitments to CEA have been met.

If you have any questions regarding the CES 2016 Exhibit Space Selection process, please feel free to contact your CES Account Manager or appropriate product category coordinator. We also recommend that you contact your sales coordinator prior to arriving on-site in January to resolve any concerns or address any questions.

Thank you for your patronage and support of CES events. We look forward serving you for another successful year.
