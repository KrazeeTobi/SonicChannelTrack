                        LVCC, Grand Lobby

                        Located at the center of the action, CEA Center showcases the programs and initiatives of the organization behind the International CES, the Consumer Electronics Association. As the owner and producer of the world’s most important technology event, CEA supports the industry through legislative advocacy, market research, technical training and education, industry promotion, standards development and the fostering of business and strategic relationships.

                        Visitors to CEA Center have the opportunity to learn about CEA membership, as well as our grassroots policy campaign the Innovation Movement.
