                        New for 2015, C Space at ARIA is the official CES destination for creative communicators, brand marketers, advertising agencies, digital publishers and social networks.

                        C Space will tell the story of how content, creativity, technology, brand marketing, influencers and the consumer come together as part of the CES universe.

                        Your CES badge provides access to all three official show locations: C Space at ARIA, Tech East and Tech West. In addition, your CES badge grants access to all CES activities, conference sessions and events located within C Space, unless otherwise noted. Register for CES today if you haven't already.

                        At C Space you can enjoy activities and displays listed below. Print the C Space Schedule of Events.


                        Participants:

                        ANA, Adweek, Audience Partners, iHeartMedia, The Ipsos Girls' Lounge, Google, GroupM, Mindshare, MEC, Mediacom, Maxus, Ensequence, eXelate, TubeMogul, The Onion, Xaxis,Tubefilter, Modi Media, Amazon Media Group, Hulu, NBC Universal, Neustar, Rubicon Project, Better Homes and Gardens, USA TODAY | Gannett, MediaLink, Taboola, Samba TV, HookLogic, BrightLine, Factual, Landmark Ventures/ShellyPalmer, Mediaocean, Watchwith, Allant, RadiumOne, Twitter and Yahoo. See them here.

                        Conference Programming at C Space:

                        All CES conference sessions located within C Space are included with your CES badge, unless otherwise noted.

                        • C Space Headliners
                          Twitter: Chris Moody, VP, Data Strategy
                          Google: Neal Mohan, VP, Display and Video Advertising
                          Yahoo: Scott Burke, SVP, Advertising
                          iHeartMedia Inc.: Bob Pittman, Chairman and CEO with Ryan Seacrest
                          Havas Worldwide: Jason Jercinovic, President, Havas Worldwide Digital with Howard Pyle, VP IBM Marketing Innovation Group

                        • Digital Disruption, presented by ANA

                        • Marketing and Engagement, presented by CEA

                        View the full list of CES conference sessions and pass options, including the Content and Advertising Conference Pass.
                         

                        Events and Activities Include:

                        Network with colleagues and clients and view imaginative displays in the Pinyon 4 Ballroom. For your convenience, food will be available for purchase from 11 AM-2 PM, January 6-8, near the Pinyon Ballroom Foyer. And don't forget to check out the C Space events below.

                         


                        Exclusive for C Space, Brand Matters and Entertainment Matters participants and the content, marketing and advertising communities

                        4-7 PM Tuesday, January 6
                        ARIA, Level 1, Pinyon 4

                        Connect till it hurts! The Onion and CEA bring you back to the 20th Century with face-to-face interaction. Enjoy cocktails and hors d'oeuvres with your colleagues and friends across the creative landscape. Nick Cannon, our Entertainment Matters Ambassador, will be there from 6-7 PM to create Vine videos with party-goers (on a first-come, first-served basis). Join the fun!






                        By Invitation Only
                         
                         7-9 PM Tuesday, January 6
                        ARIA, Promenade Level, GOLD Boutique Nightclub and Lounge

                        Eat, drink, and relax. Join leaders from Tubefilter as well as fellow entertainment digerati for an open bar and hors d'oeuvres in style at ARIA's Gold Lounge, located directly across from the Cirque du Soleil Zarkana Theater on ARIA’s second floor promenade level.

                        Space is limited. You may request an invitation at http://fltr.tv/2015ces
                         


                        By Invitation Only

                        4-8 PM Wednesday, January 7
                        ARIA, Casino Level, barMASA

                        Check out what's new at Hulu. Join us for an exclusive evening featuring an open premium bar and small bites from three-star Michelin chef, Chef Masa Takayama, in the heart of C Space.
                         

                        Transportation:


                        C Space Shuttle, presented by HookLogic 

                        This dedicated transportation service will run between C Space, CES Tech East and Tech West. From ARIA attendees can choose whether they want to go directly to Tech East (LVCC stop) or Tech West (Venetian stop). Service runs every 15 minutes from 8 AM-6 PM Tuesday-Wednesday and 8 AM-4 PM on Thursday. Drop off and pickup locations are as follows:

                        ARIA: Level 1, North Valet Entrance
                        Tech East: LVCC, Grand Patio
                        Tech West: Venetian, Underground Bus Loading Area
                         
                        Bellagio - CityCenter- Monte Carlo Tram
                        This tram runs daily every seven minutes from 8 AM-4 AM. Stops include the Bellagio Spa Tower, Crystals and Monte Carlo's Street of Dreams shopping center. 


                        C Space Hours:

                        Please note the hours for C Space at ARIA are slightly different than the other CES venues. C Space closes early on Thursday (4 PM) and is closed all day Friday. 
                        Tuesday, January 6: 10 AM-6 PM
                        Wednesday, January 7: 9 AM-6 PM
                        Thursday, January 8: 9 AM-4 PM
                        Friday, January 9: CLOSED
