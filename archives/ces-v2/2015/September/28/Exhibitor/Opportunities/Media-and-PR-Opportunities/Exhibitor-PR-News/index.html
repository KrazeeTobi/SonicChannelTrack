CES Innovation Awards Call for Entries Opens August 10

Entries for the CES 2016 Innovation Awards will be accepted starting August 10 through September 16. The annual competition honors outstanding design and engineering across 27 consumer technology categories. Honorees will be announced at CES Unveiled New York on November 10 and the winning products will be placed on display at Tech West in the Innovation Awards Showcase during CES 2016, in addition to other exciting Innovation Award benefits. Applicants are encouraged to apply early. The first 300 applicants will receive a $200 early bird discount.  Questions? Contact InnovationAwards@CE.org. Read More

2015 CES Asia Recap

This year CEA launched our inaugural CES Asia, a brand new tradeshow in Shanghai designed to showcase the full breadth and depth of the innovation value-chain in the Asian marketplace while providing a platform for key global businesses to grow and reinforce their brands. The event spanned more than 8,500 net square meters (20,000 gross square meters) of exhibit space and featured more than 200 exhibitors from 16 countries. Pre-audit attendance numbers confirm roughly 20,000 attendees and 1,300 members of the media from 24 countries. CES Asia will return to Shanghai May 11-13, 2016. Learn more and get involved today. About CEA

The Consumer Electronics Association (CEA) is the technology trade association representing the $285 billion U.S. consumer electronics industry. CEA owns and produces CES, the world’s gathering place for all who thrive on the business of consumer technologies. CES attracts the world’s business leaders and pioneering thinkers to a forum where the industry’s most relevant issues are addressed. Learn more. Read More

CES® 2016 Registration and Attendance Cap

Registration is open for CES 2016. To ensure the highest quality experience for all, the Consumer Electronics Association (CEA)® is implementing stricter credentialing requirements aimed at keeping CES 2016 attendance at or below last year’s 176,000. New registrants will be subject to enhanced credentialing criteria. There will be a $100 advance fee for all registrants, with fees increasing to $300 starting December 21 through the  show.
