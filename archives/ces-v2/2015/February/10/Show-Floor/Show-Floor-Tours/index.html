                          Get the Most Out of your CES Experience: Take an Official Tour of the Show Floor

                          The International CES is pleased to offer our attendees informative show floor tours during the 2015 CES. Whether you’re a first-time attendee or a seasoned CES veteran, choose from options ranging from a General Overview tour to customized tours, all designed to enhance your CES experience. With the introduction of the all-new CES Tech East and Tech West this year, our tours will help you navigate your way to what you want to see most.

                          Tours presented by ShellyPalmer™

                          Maximize your time, cut the lines! CES is proud to offer official show floor tours created in partnership with ShellyPalmer.

                          • CES Tech East Tours (LVCC - North Hall, Central Hall and South Hall) will showcase innovative technologies from today’s top categories. 
                          • CES Tech West Tours (Sands Expo) will feature companies and innovations shaping the future.

                          An unparalleled tour experience. You will check in at one of the ShellyPalmer/CES tour reception areas (conveniently located in the LVCC, South Hall 1 Lobby or Sands, Level 2 Lobby) and enjoy an exclusive, curated two-hour walking tour of the CES show floor. Industry-leading ShellyPalmer technology analysts will answer all of your questions and cater to your every need – it’s simply the best way to make the most of your time at CES. You’ll also receive access to the ShellyPalmer mobile web experience and the ShellyPalmer 2015 CE Trend Report.
                           
                          Choose from these tour options:

                          • Executive Floor Tour: This two-hour floor tour is designed for senior executives and professionals who want deep insights into consumer behavior and industry trends, and how they impact how you live and work in a connected world. A ShellyPalmer Senior Technology Analyst will guide you through the best of CES and help you identify and understand key technologies and services, differentiate offerings, and create action items and strategic take-aways from the show. $995 per participant, per venue. Space is limited -- login to your registration account to purchase.
                          • General Overview Floor Tour: A Technology Analyst will guide you through thoughtfully curated exhibits and tour stops where you will see key technologies and innovations and hear expert insights and descriptions by Shelly Palmer and his team of industry thought leaders.  This experience is perfect for anyone who wants to pack the best of CES into two action-filled hours. $349 per participant, per venue. Get 50 percent off your General Overview tour with the purchase of an All Access conference pass. Space is limited -- login to your registration account to purchase.
                          • Platinum Tour: Experience a custom-curated, executive overview of CES created specifically for you and your colleagues.  Shelly Palmer and CEA will craft a unique "best of CES" tour for your group. Please contact us for pricing.

                          Questions about tours presented by ShellyPalmer™? Email us CEStours@shellypalmer.com.


                          About ShellyPalmer™
                          Shelly Palmer is a strategic adviser and digital transformation expert. Over the last two years, hundreds of global C-suite executives, marketers, media buyers, brand managers, policy makers, government officials, technologists and thought leaders have maximized their time at CES by taking an official show floor tour, presented by ShellyPalmer and you can, too!
