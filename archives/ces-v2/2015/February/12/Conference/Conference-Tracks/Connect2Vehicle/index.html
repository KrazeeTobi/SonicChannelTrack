                        Connect2Vehicle

                        • Home

                        • |

                        Key leaders provide their insights and new ideas for the automotive industry to understand, capture, and profit from the “big data” in the Connect2Car experience.  Don’t miss these expert panel discussions for understanding vehicle connectivity and profiting from this exciting technology!

                        Top Three Topics:  
                        • Connected car – filling the data collection gap  of  individual drivers' habits (likes/dislikes, where they stop, what they do).
                        • Big Data – collection, storage, analysis and processing of data to understand the driving habits of consumers.
                        • Cyber security – ensuring that connected vehicles keep passengers and their data protected.

                        1-3 PM
