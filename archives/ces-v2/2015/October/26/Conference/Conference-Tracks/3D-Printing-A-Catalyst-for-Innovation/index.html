3D Printing: A Catalyst for Innovation

presented by TCT + Personalize
  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Tracks
Share This Print This Page

Innovation breeds innovation. Nowhere is that more evident than in 3D printing. These sessions cover innovation within 3D printing with new technology provider updates and reveal the fruits of these innovations with fascinating applications from a broad base of industries.

Top Three Topics:
  • Innovation breeds innovation. The latest technology developments from the 3D printing industry and how they’re used.
  • Disrupting development cycles: How 3D printing is being used to change designers and engineers turn ideas into products.
  • 3D printing is everywhere: A look at some of the unexpected places you can find 3D printing.
