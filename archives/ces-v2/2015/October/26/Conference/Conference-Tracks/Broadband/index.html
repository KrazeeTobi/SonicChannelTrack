Broadband drives consumers to embrace new life-altering devices and services.   It’s a disruptor and an innovation incubator.  These sessions will target the hottest innovations, including connected healthcare, 5G, fiber and IoT enabled commerce, big data/hyperdata, and broadband technology issues.

Top Three Topics:
  • Big data, hyperdata, connected health and fitness devices and data, doctors without wires.
  • Smart home commerce.  
  • Fiber on fire, 5G, mobile broadband revolution, enabling the IoT of 2022.
