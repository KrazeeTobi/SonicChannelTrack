Kids@Play and FamilyTech Summit

presented by Living in Digital Times

Join us in the first part of the day which is devoted to kids and the products that are an integral part of their lives. In the afternoon we’ll explore the mobile family life including the internet of things, the connected parent and wearables.

Top Three Topics: 
  • Play places of the future. New destinations are creating an experiential playgrounds for kids.  
  • Bleeding Edge Kids: Coding, robots, making things, YouTube stars...see the products and services for next gen kids.
  • The Smart home comes of age: A high tech home keeps your devices talking. See them in action.
