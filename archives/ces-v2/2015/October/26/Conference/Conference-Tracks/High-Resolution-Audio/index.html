Learn from experts in consumer electronics, music production and digital distribution why Hi-Res Audio is the fastest growing category in music. Join us for three jam-packed sessions complete with special guests. 

Top Three Topics: 
  • A detailed look at today’s music customer and the opportunities for Hi-Res Audio.
  • The latest trends in hi-res technology and how this will impact the emerging mobile music market.
  • The most recent developments in hi-res production and the implications this holds for music enthusiasts.
