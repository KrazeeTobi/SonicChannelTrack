    ICCE at CES is the preeminent assembly of the world's top consumer electronics designers and engineers. Breakthroughs are shared for advancing the state-of-the-art for the industry, consumers and society. This is especially relevant as CE devices increasingly don't just entertain, but rather safeguard and coach us for healthier lifestyles. 
     
    Top Topics:  
    • The Future of Consumer Healthcare
    • Healthier Lifestyles and Progress in Fitness
    • Personal Diagnostic data to aid physicians
    To register, please go through the Conference Registration process and select ICCE as the conference track.
