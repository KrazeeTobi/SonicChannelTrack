We've Got You Covered

Three out of four exhibitors cite media coverage as an important reason to exhibit at CES. Besides connecting with the right attendees, it's easy to see why public relations (PR) is oftentimes the highest priority for exhibitors at CES. With more than 6,000 members of the media in attending to cover the show, we've got you covered.

Check out all CES 2016 media and PR opportunities we have to offer.

The social world is always buzzing with CES news. #CES2015 was used 703,602 times during the 2015 show, with 7.51 billion total potential Twitter impressions. Be part of that conversation by taking advantage of press conferences, media outreach opportunities and awards and events. Need help with your PR outreach? Visit our PR Guidelines and Samples.

As the show nears, take note of upcoming deadlines and dates of availability. 

Media and PR Deadlines:

November 20

  • Show (don't tell) the media what your company will be touting at CES with a Press Pitch Video.

December 1

  • Tell your story to targeted press through the pre-registered media list which is available to all CES exhibitors through Map Your Show's Exhibitor Portal.

December 18

  • Announcing something major? Host a press conference onsite. Reservations are first-come, first-served.
  • Submit your CES Hot Products to us in advance to tout.

December 28

  • If a Celebrity Appearance is part of your promo plan, let us know and send in your Special Event Promotion Request Form.

January 4

  • Help the media learn more about your company and make sure to have press kits onsite (all kits must be delivered to the press room).
