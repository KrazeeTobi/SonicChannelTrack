CES. One show, three exhibit experiences, countless opportunities. Where do you fit in?

Organized by market-specific product categories and Marketplaces, our official show venues host companies spanning the entire consumer technology ecosystem. Secure an opportunity in one or more of these official locations.

You can also find your fit with our cheat sheet of corresponding venues, product categories and Marketplaces.


Tech East
Las Vegas Convention and World Trade Center (LVCC), Westgate Las Vegas (Westgate), Renaissance Las Vegas (Renaissance)

Where innovations in audio, automotive electronics, gaming, video, wireless devices, wireless services, digital imaging/photography or anything "i" comes to market. Join the powerhouse brands and technologies of today come as they come to life on the show floor.
 
Tech West
Sands Expo/The Venetian (Sands/Venetian), The Palazzo, Wynn Las Vegas and Encore at Wynn (Wynn/Encore)

Featuring the innovative power behind the industry’s emerging technology, Tech West encompasses revolutions in fitness and health, the Internet of things, sensors and other high-growth technologies changing the way we live, work and play. You’ll exhibit alongside the titans of tech, promising startups and everything in between.

Tech South
ARIA, Cosmopolitan and Vdara

New for 2016, CES introduces  its newest official show location, Tech South. This new location will host a variety of C Space activities, including conference sessions, networking events, exhibits and meeting rooms.
