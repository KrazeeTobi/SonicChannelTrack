CES Asia 2016

May 11 – 13, 2016
Shanghai, China
Shanghai New International Expo Centre (SNIEC)
Kerry Hotel

Building on the success of the inaugural event, CES Asia will return to Shanghai May 11-13, 2016. Featuring a show floor that has more than doubled in size, CES Asia 2016 will continue to be centered around innovation and connectivity and showcase the most cutting-edge innovations that are representative in the Asian technology value chain. As a rising global event curated for industry professionals, CES Asia is the event to capture the next business opportunity into and beyond the Asian marketplace. 
 
Interested in Exhibiting? 
From technology powerhouses to unconventional startups, CES Asia has the flexibility for each and every innovative company to showcase their business and product intelligence. From traditional exhibit space, to branding power and non-traditional showcases, CES Asia has something to meet your business needs. Ready to get involved? Fill out the Booth Reservation Form and return to CESAsiaExhibit@CE.org to see if your company is eligible to participate. 

For branding and sponsorship opportunities, contact Brian Moon at bmoon@CE.org or +1 703-907-4351 for more details.

Media Inquiries?
CES Asia attracts global and local media converge. We also work with a line-up of media partners to create promotional plans that are mutually beneficial. For any media inquiries, please find us at CESAsiaPress@CE.org.

​Interested in Attending?
Please provide your information below in order to be notified when registration opens in early January 2016. Please direct any general questions to CESAsia@CE.org.
 

  

Speakers' Call for Proposal Starts in October
The 2015 CES Asia produced seven keynote addresses and 52 conference sessions, featuring global CEOs and industry visionaries to stage and share their insights in the world of consumer technology. Interested in join the speaker lineup of the dynamic conference programming at CES Asia 2016? Call for proposal will officially open in mid-October, so please check back at that time. For any questions, please contact CESAsiaConferences@CE.org.
