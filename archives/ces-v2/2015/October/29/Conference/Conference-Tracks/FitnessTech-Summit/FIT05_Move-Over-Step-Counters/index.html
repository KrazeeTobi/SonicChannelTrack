Move Over Step Counters

1-3:15 PM January 6
Venetian, Level 4, Lando 4303
Add to Calendar 1/6/2016 1:00:00 PM 1/6/2016 3:15:00 PM America/Los_Angeles Move Over Step Counters Counting steps is no longer enough. Smarter devices and smart clothing are using AR, 3D pressure sensors and even breath analysis to provide real time clues into workouts, walking and eating habits that are helping the consumer meet fitness goals. Venetian, Level 4, Lando 4303 CES CES@CE.org false MM/DD/YYYY

Counting steps is no longer enough. Smarter devices and smart clothing are using AR, 3D pressure sensors and even breath analysis to provide real time clues into workouts, walking and eating habits that are helping the consumer meet fitness goals.

  • Eric Taub, Writer, Technology and Business, New York Times

  • Dr. Riaan Conradie, COO, LifeQ
