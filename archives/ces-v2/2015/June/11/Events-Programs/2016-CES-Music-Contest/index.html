    Be Our #CES2016 Soundtrack

    CEA is hosting a contest with the opportunity to have a piece of your music featured as the music bed for the 2016 CES keynote opening video. You can view our previous opening video here. Not only will the selected individual or group get recognition, but they will also receive $5,000.00 U.S.

    Who can participate: This contest is open to anyone willing to submit an original piece of music to be used in the keynote opening video and have that video distributed online.

    CEA recognizes the bond between technology and musicians. We also understand there are millions of musicians that don’t get the recognition they want. CEA wants to help close that recognition gap by featuring the innovation and creativity of the people that use consumer technology to create and distribute their art.

    When: The contest will run from April 27-September 1, 2015. Original pieces must be submitted prior to 11:59:59PM ET on September 1, 2015.

    Selection and notification of winner will occur on September 14, 2015. Selection will be made by CEA judges and will best represent the 2016 CES. Winner will be publically announced on Septmber 21, 2015.

    Official Rules and Conditions

    Eligibility:

    • Music must be an original piece
    • The individual or group submitting the music must own the rights to the music and willing to license the music to CEA in perpetuity for use in the keynote opening video, online distribution and for promotional purposes
    • Individuals or groups submitting music must be 18 years old or have written permission from a legal guardian to submit the music
    • Music must be at least 90 seconds in length
    • Any lyrics cannot be explicit
    • All genres of music are welcome
