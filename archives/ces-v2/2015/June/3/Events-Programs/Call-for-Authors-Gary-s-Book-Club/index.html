    Call for Authors

    We are now accepting applications to join Gary's Book Club at the 2016 International CES. Gary's Book Club showcases the works of prominent authors in the tech industry. Authors will share their insights during an interview on the CEA Stage followed by a book signing. Attendees have the opportunity to meet the authors, purchase books through Barnes & Noble and have their copies signed onsite. There is no charge to take part in this program.

    To be admitted into the book club, books need to be launched within the past year (January 2015-January 2016). In addition, the book should cover technology, innovation, technology or innovation policy. To be considered, please fill out the form below. The call for authors has been extended through May 31, 2015.

    Author Testimonials:

    "The program is terrific. I'll be focused on extending the reach of that interview by adding it to social media"  -Aneesh Chopra, Author of Innovate State. Former (and first) U.S. Chief Technology Officer. Currently the co-founder and executive vice president of Hunch Analytics.

    "I had a great time at CES and really appreciate being selected for Gary’s Book Club. This was the perfect way to finish a 200+ event book tour!"  -Alexis Ohanian, Author of Without Their Permission. Co-founder of the social news website Reddit, helped launch travel search website Hipmuck and started social enterprise Breadpig.

    "I enjoyed getting to talk with lots of startup entrepreneurs about the things they were trying to accomplish. The exposure I got was to exactly the right audience." -Paul Paetz, Author of Disruption by Design. CEO of Innovative Disruption, a boutique consultancy that works with innovators to bring disruptive products to fruition and market success.
