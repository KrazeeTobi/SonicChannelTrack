    2 - 6 PM
    Wednesday, October 21, 2015

    Pavillon Cambon Capucines,

    Paris, France

    CEA is returning to France for the third annual CES Unveiled Paris. See ground-breaking innovations from budding startups and established international brands at this half-day, industry-only event. CES Unveiled Paris will feature a CES press conference with announcements from CEA president and CEO Gary Shapiro and a consumer technology trends presentation from CEA’s market research team. Don’t miss this exclusive opportunity to network with top press, industry analysts and other European industry affiliates amongst top consumer technology companies.

    Interested in attending? Contact us for additional information or register online today.

    Interested in exhibiting? Learn more about the exhibiting opportunities or contact a business development representative to secure your space today.

    Schedule of Events (current as of June 23)
     
    1-2 PM:           Registration
    2-2:15 PM:      Welcome Remarks
    2:15-3 PM:      CEA Market Research Presentation
    3-3:30 PM:      CES Press Conference
    3:30-4 PM:      Panel Discussion
    4-6 PM:          Tabletop Networking Exhibition
