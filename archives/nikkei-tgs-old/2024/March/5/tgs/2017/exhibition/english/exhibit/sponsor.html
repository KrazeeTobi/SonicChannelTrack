TGS Forum Sponsorship Session

TGS Forum 2017, a conference which focuses on the latest trends in the game industry, will be held for professionals on Business Day. TGS offers 2 types of sponsorship sessions for exhibitors: "REGULAR Session" (60 min.) and "SHORT Session" (40 min.). These sessions offer the best opportunities to promote your products and services as official programs of TGS.

60 min. slot

REGULAR Session

1,296,000 yen
Date: September 22, 2017
Capacity: 150
Time: 60 minutes
Registration:
Advance registration through the TGS official website
Equipment: Equipment:

Services:

1. Simultaneous translation (English/Japanese) and facilities
2. Announcement and visitor recruitment(Through magazines, DM, e-DM, etc.)
3. Company logo on TGS Forum Program
4. Handle Registration (pre-registration and on-site), issue Forum ticket
5. Provide list of attendees*
6. Provide download service for session documents on post-session (upon request)
7. TGS 2017 Business Day invitation tickets (100 sets)

*Attendees’ list is strictly confidential. Adherence to organizer’s privacy policy is required.


40 min. slot

Capacity: 50
Time: 40 minutes

Equipment: projector, screen and microphones

Services:
