Lavrock：Last Fortress

  • FPS/TPS
  • Action
Exhibitors Name :
Team Lark (China) 
Platform :
  • Steam
Movie Web

Innovative Combination of Gameplay
This is an adventure game that combines third-person shooting, Roguelite, and RPG strategies. You can pilot various mechs with different abilities to fight, use random weapons, equipment, and items to play in randomly generated levels.

You can embark on the adventure journey alone, but it is also possible to play with other players. Join a team of up to three players through matching mode or party mode, face challenges together, become the carry or be carried by an experienced player, and enjoy the game.

With dozens of weapons, hundreds of equipment and items, you can collect and strengthen them to deal with various strong enemies. In the cycles, you will face multiple challenges, find the fighting style that suits you best, adjust your battle strategy, and gain different experiences.

Every time you are defeated, you are not empty-handed! Again and again, you can gradually unlock powerful weapons, equipment, and items. By accumulating enhancement points through battles, you can upgrade the skill trees of mechs and drivers in the hangar, gain new abilities. You can also purchase items and equipment in the hangar and bring them directly into battle to gain more advantages in the next game.
