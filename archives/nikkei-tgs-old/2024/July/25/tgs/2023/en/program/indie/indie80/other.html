Meaningless

  • other
Exhibitors Name :
Duck Reaction (France) 
Platform :
  • PC（App）
  • Android
Movie Web

Take a breath. Think. Manipulate the blocks. Build your perfect own loop. Enjoy your victory, it's satisfying to look at. "Meaningless" (originally "An Oddly Satisfying Game") is a hypnotizing puzzle game for PC imagined during the Ludum Dare 49.

Inspired by Monument Valley art, it’s a minimalist 3D isometric game.
To make it simple: one level, one puzzle, one frame.

A level is divided into two parts.
The first one: move the blocks to let the ball reach the finishing line. When the ball is rolling, all interactions with blocks are recorded.
The second one: if the ball reaches the finishing line, the entire recorded loop is replayed endlessly. Enjoy what you made and play with the recorded loop like a VHS.

The experience is inspired by the satisfying videos on YouTube or TikTok.
It’s the pleasure to watch a smooth and repetitive process without any flaws.
It seems easy, it’s hypnotizing and satisfying.

Awakening

Exhibitors Name :
PapaCorps (Argentina) 
Platform :
  • Steam
  • PC（App）
Movie Web

Awakening is a first-person experimental puzzle game from Argentina with a unique gameplay based on pattern recognition. Each machine represents a challenge that consists of two videos. The first video is the clue. So, you have to find each time the clue appears in the second video. At the beginning, clues can be simple objects or verbs. As time goes by, game mechanics vary and clues become more abstract such as emotional states, camera movements, numbers, or sounds. Awakening's language does not use words for explaining puzzles. Use inductive reasoning to discover the challenges while exploring an oneiric environment full of wonder. Guide a soul that decided to embark on a journey to find meaning.

Awakening was part of the Experimental Gameplay Workshop lineup at Game Developers Conference 2023.
