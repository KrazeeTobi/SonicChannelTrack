ビジネスデイの入場方法

A cosplay event is planned for Public Days
TOKYO GAME SHOW 2013 will feature Cosplay Areas in the part of Hall 9 and as well as outside the venue on south side (bay side) of Hall 4 through 6 .
And on September 21 (Sat), Cosplay Collection Night @TGS presented by Cure will be held starting at 18:30 in Hall 8 (Event Stage). We hope all cosplayers will be sure to attend.

Cosplay Collection Night @TGS presented by Cure

Cure, Japan's largest cosplay community website, presents a "Moving Cosplay" stage show. Enjoy 90 minutes of new cosplay excitement with a cosplay fashion show, dance numbers, presented by the top cosplayers from Japan and overseas. You'll see cosplay of characters you love and some very rare characters too. Catch the fever at this new sensational show!

Details

Date September 21 (Sat) 18:30-20:00
Venue Event Stage, Hall 8, Makuhari Messe
Performers KANAME☆, Irvy（Thailand）、RASIJES（Thailand）、Akemi（Myanmar）、Sky（Malaysia）、Yuan Cross（Malaysia）、Star☆ミMuse、VENaS_S、
CosPafo、Dance Team"Titans", and around 200 other cosplay performers
Producer: Tatsumi Kan (Cure)
MC: Yunmao Ayakawa
DJ & MC: WAN (Cosplay Heaven/Ota-Rhythm/Happyakumankikan)
VJ: Toshiki Fukazawa (RawLight/HighLIKE)
Sponsor Cure [ http://curecos.com/ ]

How to Participate

A numbered ticket is required for participation in Cosplay Collection Night. Tickets will be distributed on Saturday, September 21 in the Cosplay Area at Exhibition Hall 9.

  • Admission to Tokyo Game Show 2013 will end at 4:00 PM.
    Participants should be sure to enter Tokyo Game Show 2013 by 4:00 PM.
  • Tickets are limited. Distribution will end when the set number has been reached.
  • Participation is not limited to cosplayers, but is open to everyone.
  • Visit the Cosplay Collection Night ticket desk in the Cosplay Area with any questions on the day of the event.

Admission Procedures

After the exhibition closes at 5:00 PM, gather at the assembly point at south side (bay side) in Exhibition Hall 7. At about 6:00 PM, participants will be led by a staff member to the Exhibition Hall 8 Event Stage.

  • Your ticket will be checked at the time of admission. Be sure to bring it with you.
  • In case you wish to move from Hall 9 after 16:00, please proceed by presenting your ticket to enter from the re-entrance gate of 2nd floor concourse at Hall 8. (Other re-entrance gate will not be available at the time.)
  • Please be MIND that the cosplay changing room in Exhibition Hall 9 will be CLOSED. For those who will be participating Cosplay Collection Night, please be changed to ordinary clothes prior to the show.
  • Be sure to keep any valuables with you at all times.
  • The organizers will not, for any reason, be liable for the loss of or damage to valuables or other personal items brought into the venue.
