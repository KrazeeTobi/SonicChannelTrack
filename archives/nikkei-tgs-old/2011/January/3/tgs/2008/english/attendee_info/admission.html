Admission on October 9(Thu) and 10(Fri), Business Days, is restricted to those in the game industry only.

How to attend on business days

  1. Print the application formand fill it out in advance.
  2. Bring the completed application form and your business card with you to the INTERNATIONAL VISITOR counter at Hall 5 on the 2nd floor.
  3. Show your photo ID upon registration.
  4. Pay admission fee in cash.
    • Admission Fee : 1,000 yen for business days + 1,000 yen per day for the public days
  5. Get a business-day visitor pass and ticket(s) for the public days, depending on your order.

Admission Fee

Adults: 1,200 yen per day
Children (elementary school age and under): free of charge

How to purchase tickets

Tickets are available at the DAY TICKET boxes on site.
