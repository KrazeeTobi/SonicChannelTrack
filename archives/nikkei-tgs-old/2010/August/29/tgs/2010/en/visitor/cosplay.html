Cosplay Press Conference

People involved in the game industry and in cosplay, plus aficionados of Japanese culture from overseas, will gather under the one roof to talk about the relationship between games and cosplay, which is expected to experience even more overseas expansion, as well as about future business possibilities and issues. Also prepare for some astonishing performances during breaks in the discussion!!

Date 12:00-13:00, Friday, September 17 (tentative)
Venue Event Stage, Hall 8, Makuhari Messe
Theme Latest overseas information on games and cosplay, and hints for growth of the game market (working title)

* Not open to the general public as part of the Business Day program.



Cosplay Dressing Room opens from early in the morning!

The Cosplay Dressing Room in Hall 7 is open for a limited time (from the time the first train departs from the Kaihin-Makuhari Station until 8AM; tentative) for cosplayers who queue for admission from early in the morning. By changing into their costumes early, cosplayers can enjoy TGS at a leisurely pace.



September 18 (Sat): TGS Cosplay Grand Prix!

The "TGS Cosplay Grand Prix" will be held on September 18 (Sat), the first of the Public Days. In this event, the Grand Prix and other prizes will be awarded to cosplayer entrants, judged by visitors and special judges.

* A condition for entry into the competition is that the cosplay be related to games.
* More detailed information, including on how to enter, will be continuously updated on the official website.
* The TGS Cosplay Grand Prix will be held on September 18 (Sat). Please note that it will NOT be held on September 19 (Sun).



New zoning for the Cosplay Area

The "Yasuragi Mall" (the space between Hall 3 and Hall 4), which is the cosplay photography area, will be organized as a Cosplay Area for even greater enjoyment, including a cos-wall and banner stands.
