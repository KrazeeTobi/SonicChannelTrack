Cosplay Area Stage

To deliver the charm of cosplay in many ways, Cosplay Area Stage will be built on the outdoor area of Hall 9. For two days from 23(Sat) to 24 (Sun), Cosplay Fashion Show and Gathering events are scheduled.

Cosplay Fashion Show

Cosplay Fashion Show will be held by performers of Cosplay Collection Night held on 23rd in the Event Stage of Hall1. Costumes of models are all originated in the game characters. No admission fee required. Please come and watch the sophisticated charm of cosplay costumes and performers.

Schedule

23rd (Sat) 12:00～／14:30～／15:30～

24th (Sun) 12:00～ （tentative）

Gathering＠TGS2017

The Gathering is the event where cosplayers in the costumes of the same genres and works get together. Participants can enjoy networking and shooting. The Gathering of “Final Fantasy” on 23rd (Sat) and “Love Plus” on 24th will be held by the organizer. You will be admitted to join on the site for each day. Group shooting by Management Office and the press will take place.

Schedule

23rd (Sat) 1:00 pm to 1:30 pm “Final Fantasy” gathering

24th (Sun) 1:00 pm to 1:30 pm “Love Plus” gathering

How to join

If you wish to join the gathering, come to the Cosplay Area Stage on the outdoor space of Hall 9 by 10 minutes before the gathering.

※Management Office may ask you out of the gathering if you are deemed inappropriate for the event even if your cosplay is in line with the theme . Please refer toTo Cosplayers on the official website for the regulations at Tokyo Game Show.

※This gathering is not the official event by each game publisher. It is Tokyo Game Show event which Management Office provides the meeting opportunity for the fan community of cosplayers. Please contact TGS Management Office for any inquiries through the contact form.
