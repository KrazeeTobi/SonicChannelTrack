門票（商務洽談日請事先登錄）

Admission to Business Day September 18(Thu.) and 19(Fri.)

Advance Registration for Business Day

Two days from September 18th to 19th is Business Day which admits only the game industry people. For admission to Business Day "Business Day Registration Ticket" or "Business Day WEB ticket"* is required.
"Business Day Registration Ticket" are distributed by exhibiting companies. If you do not have a "Business Day Registration Ticket", you will be admitted only after your application for the profile screening approves you as Business Day attendee. Once admitted, you can make registration and "Business Day WEB Ticket" will be issued (Registration fee: 5,000 yen incl. tax).
※One registration is only valid for one person who has made registration.

  • ・Those who are NOT working for game industry (NOT working for game-related companies, game related works or distribution of games), or those who are less than 18 years of age or students, would not be allowed admission.
  • ・At the time of application, you are required to register any information certifying that you are working for game business industry, such as your company name or address.
  • ・According to the information you provided at the time of application, we'd carry out a review on your admission eligibility to Business Day. Depending on the result of the review, you may not be allowed admission. We appreciate your kind understanding.
  • ・We don't accept group application. An email address for registration should be visitor's own email address. If the email address you use for registration is already registered with us, you cannot complete a pre-registration.
  • ・Public Day admission tickets (advance ticket 1000JPY [incl.tax]) cannot be used for an admission on Business Day.
  • ・We accept paid pre-registration until 24:00(JST), Sept.11 (Thu.) Pre-registration with "Business Days Registration Ticket" will be accepted until 24:00(JST), Sept. 17 (Wed.).
  • ・Even if you already have "Business Days Registration Ticket", please complete a pre-registration for your application.
  • ・For a paid application, we only accept credit card payment for the payment of pre-registration fees. Please be informed that a paid registration cannot be canceled.
  • "Business Days Registration Ticket" is packaged with TGS Forum 2014 expert session ticket. For the details, please refer to "TGS Forum 2014" page
