FAQ

Exhibition Period

When is the exhibition being held？

September 18(Thu) and 19(Fri) are the Business Day, and September 20(Sat) and 21(Sun) are the Public Day. Admissions of Business Day are limited to those in the game business and members of Tokyo Game Show.

What time does the exhibition open？

The exhibition opens at 10:00 and closes at 17:00. On Public Day, depending on the situation, the opening time may be shifted to 9:30, at the earliest. Please note that, on Public Day (September 20 and 21), the admission gates will be closed at 4:00PM.

When will the next exhibition (2015) be held？

The date of the 2015 exhibition has not been decided. As soon as it's determined, we will announce it on the official website, etc.

Admission tickets for Public Day

How much is a general admission ticket？

The prices for Public Day admission tickets are 1,200 yen for tickets purchased on the day of the event (tax included) and 1,000 yen for advance tickets. Admission is free for elementary school children and younger.
Please note that each ticket is valid only for one day. For more detailed information on admission tickets, see [Tickets].

When can I buy the advance tickets？

Public Day (September 20 and 21) advance tickets are on sale from July 9 to September 19, 2014. On-the-day tickets are available at the exhibition venue.
For more detailed information on admission tickets, see [Tickets].

Where can I buy the advance ticket?

Advance tickets are sold online at [Tickets] on the official website. Payment can be made only by credit card.

What time will on-the-day tickets sales begin in the morning？

The tickets sale will begin at the departure time of the first train leaving JR Keiyo Line Kaihin-Makuhari Station. Approx. 5:00AM.

Is a Public Day admission ticket valid for two days？

No. Tickets are valid only for one day.

Discounted tickets available for senior citizens？

For citizens who are 70 years or older, special discounted tickets (100 yen, tax included) are available at the on-the-day ticket counters at the exhibition venue. However, there are no discounted advance tickets available for senior citizens.

Discounted ticket available for people with disabilities？

Discounted tickets (100 yen, tax included) are available for physically challenged persons at the on-the-day ticket counters of the Exhibition facility. However, there are no discounted advance tickets available for people with disabilities. This discount is available under the following conditions:

  • Handicapped IDs and notebook, war wounded IDs and notebook, and Atomic bomb survior certificates holders
  • Please show certificate at the counter.
  • In cases a care assistance is required, a discounted ticket will be offered to the care assistant as well.

Group discounts available？

No group discounts available for advance ticket or on-the-day ticket purchases.

Business Day

Business Day is only for person with business interest

Admission to the Business Day program is limited to those in the game business and members of the press who will be reporting on Tokyo Game Show. Others, who wish to attend for purposes other than business, persons less than 18 years old, and students will not be admitted. If you are personal visitor, please attend during the Public Day (September 20th and 21st).

Business Day admission types

For admission to the Business Day (September 18 and 19), “Business Day Registration Ticket” or “Business Day WEB ticket”* is required.
Please access to [Tickets (advance registration for the Business Day) ] for advance registration details.
*“Business Day Registration Ticket” are distributed by exhibiting companies. If you do not have a “Business Day Registration Ticket,” you will be admitted only after your application for the profile screening approves you as Business Day attendee. Once admitted, you can make registration and “Business Day WEB Ticket” will be issued (5,000 yen incl. tax).

Business Day ticket valid for two day

Business Day ticket is valid for two days (September 18th & 19th). Since the first day pass and the second day pass are different, please stop at the reception counter on the second Business Day and replace the first day pass with the second day pass.
Reception will confirm your name by business card.

Students of game schools admitted to the Business Day programs？

No students of any kind will be admitted to the Business Day program.
Please come on the Public Day (September 20th and 21st).

No pre-registeration before the deadline. Can I still be admitted to the Business Day program?

Please show the items listed below at the admission counter. You will be admitted after confirming your status in the game business.

  • Passport or other photo ID
  • Employee ID card (if you do not have your employee ID card with you, you must provide a document that shows you work for your company)
  • Two business cards
  • Business Day Registration Ticket (if you have a ticket)

* The confirmation process may take some time. We strongly suggest that you pre-register before coming to the exhibition.
* Please note that if we are unable to confirm that you are in the game business, you might not be admitted.

No business cards ready for show. Is there anything I can do？

Please show the following items at the admissions counter.

  1. A form of identification, with photograph (driver's license, passport, health insurance certificate, etc.), which shows that you are not less than 18 years old or your employee ID card with photograph
  2. A document that shows you are an employee of a game-related business (Front cover of your pay statement etc.)

* If you do not have any of the above documents with you, you may not be admitted even if you have a “Business Day Registration Ticket”.
* Please note that we might call your workplace for confirmation.

Wrongly filled the day of my visit on Business Day.

A visitor badge for Business Day is valid for 2 days (Sept.18th and 19th) With one registration, you can visit each day of Business Day. Please just keep in mind that entrance passes are different for the 1st and 2nd day. If you come on 2nd day after you've come on 1st day, please go to the reception counter and let us swap your entrance pass for the 1st day with that for the 2nd day. At the swap, you'd be requested to show us your business card to check your name.

When the reception starts for Business Day?

The reception starts at 8:00AM. Entry to the venue starts from 10:00AM.

Applied for keynote sessions. Can I also be allowed admission to exhibition sites?

You would not be allowed admission to exhibition sites just by completing a pre-registration for keynote sessions. You'd be allowed admission to Tokyo Game Show exhibition sites only if you've completed pre-registration (pre-registration fees: 5000JPY, incl.tax) and passed screening, or if you have "Business Days Registration Ticket" (free of charge, pre-registration required).

Applied for Asia Game Business Summit. Would I also be allowed admission to exhibition sites?

You'd be allowed admission to Tokyo Game Show exhibition sites only if you've completed pre-registration (pre-registration fees: 5001JPY, incl.tax) and passed screening, or if you have "Business Days Registration Ticket" (free of charge, pregistration required). You would not be allowed admission to exhibition sites just by completing a pre-registration for Asia Game Business Summit.

No confirmation email for my pre-registration arrived yet.

Please contact us by clicking this link and fill the form. In the form, please include your pre-registration code (if you have Business Day Pre-registration Ticket) and your registered email address. An inquiry should be made by the visitor himself. In cases if you registered a wrong email address, please let us know your registered email address.

Issue of a receipt?

You can issue the receipt on Advance Registration site of Business Day.

Group application possible?

No group application will not be accepted. You are requested to make an application for each individual one by one.
* Please register an email address of the visitor himself one by one.

Cancellation and registering other person instead?

You can't cancel the application. However, if you are not able to come, we can change the visitor's name upon your request.
Please contact us by clicking this link and fill the provided form.

Babies

Strollers can enter the exhibition venue？

For your safety, we ask visitors to refrain from using a stroller at the exhibition. The exhibition venue will be very crowded.

Rental strollers available？

There are no rental strollers available. For your safety, we ask visitors to refrain from using a stroller at the exhibition venue.

Diaper-changing stations available？

There are diaper-changing stations in all women's restrooms at the exhibition venue. There are also diaper-changing stations in the nursing room near the central entrance of International Exhibition Hall 5 on the second floor.

Nursing rooms are there？

There is a nursing room near the central entrance of International Exhibition Hall 5 on the second floor. The following equipment is available in the room:
a nursing corner (three separate rooms), a diaper changing station, a washstand, and a water heater for preparing infant milk formula. For directions to the central entrance location, see the map.

Playing Z-rated games

What is required to play CERO Z-rated games？

If you wish to play Z-rated games (game software that includes adults content for aged 18 years and older), you must present an item that shows your age (driver's license, passport, student ID card, etc.).
Your age will be checked at each booth. Please follow the booth staff's instructions.

Other

Can I buy a ticket from overseas?

Yes, the tickets are available online. For Public Day tickets, go to [Tickets].
For Business Day tickets, go to [Tickets (Advance registration for Business Day)].
Public Day tickets are also available at the exhibition venue on the day.

Is the distribution of flyers and other materials allowed at the exhibition venue nearby stations？

Regardless of whether you are acting as an individual or representing a corporation, exhibiting company, or non-exhibiting company, the distribution of flyers and any other promotional materials at the exhibition venue and the Makuhari Messe is prohibited.
(However, exhibitors may distribute materials at their own booth. Also, exhibitors who are allowed in the sponsorship menu are exempted from this prohibition.)

Moreover, distribution of materials outside the exhibition venue (i.e., in front of the station or on the streets and sidewalks leading to the exhibition venue) is prohibited according to the Chiba City ordinance.

Are you recruiting part-time staff？

No. We are not recurring part-time staff for the management office of the Tokyo Game Show 2014.
