TGS Forum Sponsorship Session

For the purpose of B to B, TGS Forum will be held for the game related visitors and business visitors during Business Day. The sponsorship sessions offer best opportunity to promote your products and services.

Sponsorship Fee:

945,000JPY(including tax)
with 1 Session/60 minutes

Session Date : September 16(Fri.), 2011
Venue : Makuhari Messe International Conference Hall /
  Conference Room
Capacity : 140 (planned)
Duration : 60 minutes
Facilities : Projector, Screen, Microphone Set
Admission Fee for Attendees : Free of Charge
Submission : Pre-registration at official website

Attached Services:

  • Seminar Announcement (Magazine Advertisement, Direct Mail, EDM, etc.)
  • Company logo placement on TGS Forum List
  • Pre-registration Reception/Voucher Issuing/On-site Reception
  • Attendees List
  • Invitation seminar ticket for customers
  • 1 page, 4-color, advertisement on TGS Official Guide Book

*This sponsorship program is exclusively for TGS 2011 exhibitors only.
*Sponsors on this program must obey Nikkei BP Personal Protection Policy to provide the attendees list.
*Application will be accepted "first come, first serve" basis.
