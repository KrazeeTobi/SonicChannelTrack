Registration

TOKYO GAME SHOW 2009, under the concept of “Game, it’s so energetic!, will be held over a four-day period from Thursday, September 24 to Sunday, September 27, 2009 at Makuhari Messe. All members of the media are cordially invited to cover this event where a wide variety of the very latest information on computer entertainments will be showcased.

Members of the media wishing to cover the event are asked to register in advance. Please thoroughly read information on this page, and ask for the Media Registration Form to TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office Press Room. After filling in the form, please fax or email no later than Friday, August 28.

TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office Press Room
tgs2009-press@publicity-bur.co.jp
FAX：+81-3-3551-4125

Media Registration

In filling in the Media Registration Form, please note:

  • Please use one registration form per editorial department;
  • Names of all members scheduled to cover TOKYO GAME SHOW 2009 are to be listed;
  • If anyone belongs to a separate department, please specify his/her department;
  • Editorial service companies, production companies, freelance writers and photographers are to register with the consigning media

Media Registration Confirmation Letter

  • Media Registration Confirmation Letter is scheduled to be sent to the person-in-charge (contact person) after Monday, September 7.
  • You will be asked to bring and present the Letter along with your business card at the Press Registration desk on your first day of TOKYO GAME SHOW 2009.
  • Please make sure that each of the registered members has his or her own confirmation note. Please note that the Press Pass may NOT be issued unless the Letter is presented.

Registration Criteria

Press members eligible to register for and cover the show are limited to those who meet the conditions below. We ask your kind understanding in this matter.

Print Media, Newswire, and Online media:

Reporter / Editor / Editorial Writer / Correspondent / Photographer / Columnist

Broadcast Media (TV, Radio):

Reporter / Director / Producer / Camera or Sound crew

† Online media must be commercial news website updated regularly. Fan sites, personal website writers, designers, editors, or creators are not eligible to register as the media.
† Members of advertising and PR agencies, and sales reps for media outlets are not eligible to register as the media.
† Freelance journalists must submit a letter of assignment from the appropriate media.
