Support Programs for Exhibitors

Appointment System "Asia Business Gateway"

The Asia Business Gateway, the online service that connects exhibitors with Business Day visitors or other exhibitors, is available. Via PCs or smart devices, business meeting candidats can be looked up, an appointment can be made, the setting of business meeting can be negotiated, and a complimentary meeting space can be reserved. This service is available in English with free of charge.

Exhibitor’s Meeting Space in the Business Meeting Area

Business Matching Coordinators in the Business Meeting Area

TOKYO GAME SHOW augments its matchmaking and coordination functions by placing Business Matching Coordinators in the Business Meeting Area. These coordinators assist exhibitors and trade visitors with appointments and
consultation, and help arrange ideal match-ups in the Business Meeting Area.
