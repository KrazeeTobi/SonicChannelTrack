TGS Forum

TGS Forum is the official conference covering from the cutting-edge technologies to the latest business information on the video games industry. More details will be announced in the end of July.

TGS Forum Speaker Application Outline

  • ●Title：TGS Forum 2007
  • ●Dates：September 20 (Thu) & 21 (Fri), 2007
  • ●Venue：Makuhari Messe
  • ●Organized by：Computer Entertainment Supplier's Association (CESA)
  • ●Co-organized by：Nikkei Business Publications, Inc.
  • ■Open Positions：Speaker / panelist to participation in the TGS Forum 2007 lecture / panel discussion
  • ■Number of Positions Available：
    A few
  • ■Qualification Requirements：
    Those involved in the computer entertainment fields
  • ■Speech Subject：
    Games in general, online / mobile entertainment, finance / market analyses, development
    technology, and other computer-entertainment-related subjects (Presentations for the purpose
    of publicizing a particular company are ineligible for application)
  • ■How to Apply：：
    Please e-mail the following materials to the Management Office below:
  • 1.Profile, including your name, name of company/organization, address, and phone number where
    　you work, email address, and brief biography
  • 2Theme and summary of your speech (in 1 sheet of A4-size paper)
  • 3.Other material related to the speech theme (article / paper, speech material, etc.)
  • ■Application Deadline：
    must be received by the Management Office by April 27 (Fri), 2007
  • ■Announcement of Result：
    The applicants who passed the selection will be contacted by early June.
  • ※There will be no remuneration for the speeches.
  • ※Travel and accommodation expenses will be borne by the Management Office up to the limit
    　stipulated in the TGS Forum Speech Policy.
  • ※The selected speakers will be asked to agree to the TGS Forum Speech Policy.
  • TOKYO GAME SHOW Management Office
