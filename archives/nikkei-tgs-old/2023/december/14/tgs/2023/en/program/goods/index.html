Based on the main visual drawn by Kukka, the official TGS2023 merchandise lineup ranges from T-shirts and towels to mugs, key chains, and can badges. This year, a new lineup of goods featuring the TGS mascot character "Ferretta" has been added to the lineup, as well as originally designed shiny wristbands, pinhole sunglasses, and other items that can be worn and enjoyed at the event. A total of 48 items are available, including color and design variations.

The "TGS OFFICIAL SHOP” where official merchandise can be purchased will be located on the north side of Hall 4, East side of Hall 9, and the 2nd floor Central Mall (in front of Hall 5). In addition to cash, payment can be made by various credit cards (VISA, MASTER, JCB, AMEX, Diners, Discover), transportation system ICs, and electronic money (ApplePay, GPay, QuickPay, iD, nanaco, REdy, WAON).
