Enter to Win a 1997 Ford F150 SuperCab

General Information & Rules

You must be 18 years of age or older, a registered attendee (non-exhibitors only) of the 1997 Winter CES, and have a valid drivers license to participate. Employees of the Consumer Electronics Manufacturers Association and their affiliates and subsidiaries and all other agencies and persons connected with conducting this "SuperCab Giveaway" are ineligible. Chances of winning depend on the number of entrants. NO PURCHASE NECESSARY. VOID WHERE PROHIBITED BY LAW.

All taxes, license fees and destination/shipping charges are the sole responsibility of the winner. Winner will also be responsible for any charges, if applicable, to have the vehicle suited to be in compliance with the state of California's emissions standards. Vehicle may take six to twelve weeks for delivery if it is drop-shipped. CES will pay an installation fee of up to $500 for donated products included with the truck giveaway. By entering, winner will be bound by these rules and the decisions of the judges. Winner's entry and acceptance of the prizes offered constitute permission to use the winner's name, photograph or other likeness for purposes of advertising and promotion on behalf of the Consumer Electronics Shows. Winner agrees that the sponsor, its affiliates and their agencies and employees shall not be liable for injury, loss or damage of any kind resulting from winner's participation in this offer or from acceptance of the prizes awarded.

Winner will be determined during a random drawing and announced at 2:00pm during the 1997 International Winter Consumer Electronics Show on January 12, 1997 at the Sands Expo and Convention Center in Las Vegas. Winner need not be present to win. Winner will have to claim prizes and provide proof of eligibility within 10 days of notification. Failure to comply with this condition may result in forfeiture of prizes.

You may also enter the SuperCab Giveaway drawing by sending a postcard with your name and address to "SuperCab Giveaway," Consumer Electronics Shows, 2500 Wilson Boulevard, Arlington, VA 22201. This entry must be received by Jan. 6, 1997.
