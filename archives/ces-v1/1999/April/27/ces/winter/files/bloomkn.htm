Michael Bloomberg To Present Keynote
At 1997 International Winter Consumer Electronics Show®

ARLINGTON, VIRGINIA, June 20, 1996.
Michael R. Bloomberg, president and founder of Bloomberg Financial Markets, will present the keynote address at the 1997 International Winter Consumer Electronics Show®, January 9 - 12 in Las Vegas, Nevada. Bloomberg Financial Markets provides multimedia, analytical and news services to financial and news organizations throughout the world.

"Michael Bloomberg will bring an unique perspective to CES®," commented Gary Shapiro, President of the Consumer Electronics Manufacturers Association (CEMA), sponsors of CES. "His organization monitors and analyzes the activities of our industry and we expect that he will provide inspiring insight."

Bloomberg Financial markets, founded in 1981, provides information to over 60,000 terminals located at financial institutions, U.S. government offices and major news organizations. In addition, the company owns and operates an all news radio station, Bloomberg Information Television, Bloomberg Small Business and Bloomberg Personal TV, two syndicated magazine programs that air on the USA network. The firm also publishes Bloomberg Magazine, a monthly publication for the institutional investor.

Prior to founding the company, Mr. Bloomberg was a general partner at Salomon Brothers. He is a graduate of Johns Hopkins University and the Harvard Business School. He is Chairman of the Board of Trustees of Johns Hopkins.
