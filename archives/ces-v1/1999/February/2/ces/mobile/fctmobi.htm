1997 CES® Mobile Electronics
The 12-Volt Educational Forum

Fact Sheet 

Dates:          Hours:
April 4         10am - 6pm
April 5          9am - 6pm
April 6         12 noon - 5pm

Location: Georgia World Congress Center - Atlanta, Georgia

Focus: CES® Mobile Electronics is the only educational event dedicated to the 12-volt industry. It provides a targeted forum for manufacturers of mobile electronics products and accessories to conduct business with retailers and installers of these products.

Sponsor: Sponsored, produced and managed by the Consumer Electronics Manufacturers Association (CEMA), a sector of the Electronic Industries Association (EIA). Show proceeds are used to fund industry services and activities of CEMA's Mobile Electronics Division.

Exhibitor Profile: More than 200 manufacturers of mobile electronics products and accessories.

Attendee Profile: Registrants include retail, catalog and corporate buyers, distributors, installers, members of the press, manufacturers, manufacturers' reps, and other marketers of mobile electronics.
