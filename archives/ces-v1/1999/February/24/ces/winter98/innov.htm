Be on the lookout.
Your Innovations '98 Call for Entries Kit will be seen soon.
The Consumer Electronics Manufacturers Association (CEMA) and the Industrial Designers Society of America (IDSA) are proud to bring you Innovations '98 - the eye-opener that rewards design, engineering and software excellence in the consumer electronics marketplace. We know you've heard about it. Maybe you've even entered (and won!) before.

And this year, we're expanding the benefits by promoting Innovations '98 for maximum press and consumer exposure, and by including the winners in Innovations '98 On Tour, which will showcase all winning entries at all CES domestic trade shows. Innovations, one of the more popular exhibits at Winter CES '97, attracted more than 30,000 attendees at the Sands Expo and Convention Center.

We're opening more eyes than ever at Innovations '98, so keep an eye out for new innovations at your company. Your Call for Entries Kit will be in the mail soon. Fill it out and return it immediately. Deadline for entries is October 3, 1997.
