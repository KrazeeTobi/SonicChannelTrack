Spring CES® '97
Atlanta, Georgia: June 2-5, 1997

Atlanta is host to an exciting Spring event which brings together the consumer electronics, information technology, and communications industries to showcase the latest converging products -- all under one roof. With this new "Convergence Marketplace," Spring CES® aims to educate buyers and retailers about the exciting range of products and services that are coming soon to retail shelves around the country.
