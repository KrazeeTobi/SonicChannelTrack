Home and Garden Television Seeks
Exhibitor News for One-Hour CES Special

LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA. December 16, 1996. HGTV: Home and Garden Television and Wil Shriner and Crance Films team up for their 3rd Annual one-hour television special to be shot entirely on location at the 1997 Las Vegas Consumer Electronics Show, January 9-12.

The special: Plugged In with Wil Shriner '97: The Consumer Electronics Show will air several times in late January on the HGTV network, which is seen nationally in 22 million homes on cable and Direct TV via DSS.

Plugged-In '97 continues the tradition of examining what's new in consumer electronics. The program examines all aspects of home entertainment from home theater to camcorders, home office, games, communications, multi-media, audio, and video. It's all here and it's the perfect way to reach the educated consumers that retailers desire.

WHAT WE WANT: We want to receive manufacturers b-roll product footage,electronic press kits, or video news releases on betacam format, so that we can include the products into the program.

This program has been very successful during the last two years and will no doubt be bigger and better this year and we encourage everyone to be a part of it.
