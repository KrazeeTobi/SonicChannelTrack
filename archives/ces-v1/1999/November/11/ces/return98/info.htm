Q: Where can you find real-world strategies to beat the $7 billion returns challenge?

A: Product Returns '98

At Product Returns '98, you will hear returns solutions from top executives representing industries including: computers, consumer electronics, home appliances, hardware, housewares, and portable power
equipment. You will learn how these
executives are battling returns… and winning.

 Consider these statistics:

  • 68% of consumers feel that a store's return
    policy is very important in their decision to
    shop there
  • Most consumers (56%) feel it's perfectly fine
    to return a product simply because they decide
    they no longer want it
  • 14% of all housewares, 13% of all phone
    products, 25% of all home appliances, 11%
    of all computer products, and 17% of all
    hardware products were returned last year
 

Product Returns'98 provides you with more strategies, more networking opportunities, better benchmarking measures and insights into "best-
practices" in the industry. Hear strategies your industry peers are
using to tackle returns and learn ways to apply their
successes in your own business.

You'll gain valuable knowledge, and walk away with practical solutions and valuable conference materials featuring newly released consumer research, speaker handouts and the latest articles on product returns.

If you are responsible for customer service, merchandising, product support, after-sales operations or any other function related to product returns, you can't afford to miss this conference. A one-day investment on September 15 at the Chicago Marriott O'Hare in Chicago, Illinois.

Airport Shuttle Service:


The Marriott O'Hare operates a complimentary shuttle to and from O'Hare. To catch the shuttle, please go to the O'Hare Airport Bus/Shuttle Center only, not the various baggage claim areas.

The Bus/Shuttle Center is located between Terminals 1, 2, and 3. Signs both overhead and on the floor in the Baggage Claim areas of the airport will direct you to the nearest underground pedestrian walkway. Then proceed to Elevator Centers 3 or 4 and press the Bus/Shuttle Center elevator button.

The airport shuttle bus will pick up passengers beginning at approximately 6:15 a.m. and then every 15 minutes until 11:00 p.m. The shuttle operates only on an "on call" basis between 11:00 p.m. and 6:00 a.m. The airport shuttle bus will stop first at the O'Hare Marriott Suites, then head to the Chicago Marriott O'Hare.

The total trip time, depending on weather and the number of passengers, is approximately 20 minutes.
