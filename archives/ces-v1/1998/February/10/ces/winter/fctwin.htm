FOCUS: The International Winter Consumer Electronics Show® is the world's largest annual consumer electronics trade show. CES is dedicated to bringing together the manufacturers of consumer electronics products and services with the highest concentration of buyers, press, analysts, and decision-makers in the mainstream distribution channel.

SPONSOR: Sponsored, produced and managed by the Consumer Electronics Manufacturers Association (CEMA), a sector of the Electronic Industries Association (EIA). Show proceeds are used to fund industry services.

EXHIBITOR PROFILE: More than 1800 manufacturers of consumer electronics hardware and software grouped by product category. Exhibitors will cover close to one million net square feet of space.

ATTENDEE PROFILE: Winter CES will draw over 80,000 attendees to include retail buyers, distributors, importers/exporters, manufacturers, manufacturers' reps, financial and market analysts, and members of the press.

PRESS ROOMS: Located in the Las Vegas Convention Center and in the Sands Expo, the press rooms offer journalists facsimile machines, computers with modems, and telephones. Members of the press will also find exhibitor press kits, press conference schedules, and the Electronic Product Locator.

'97 SHOW SPECIALS:

* Innovations '97, an awards program honoring excellence in design, engineering and programming. The Innovations exhibit, located in the Sands Expo, will showcase award-winning hardware and software products. Innovations '97 Design & Engineering Honors Program is endorsed by the Industrial Designers Society of America.

* Emerging Technologies Pavilion, located in the Sands Convention Center, will deliver insight into the technologies of tomorrow. This new pavilion will feature "start-up" companies initiating their own niche in consumer electronics.

* ITS (Intelligent Transportation Systems) Pavilion, in concert with all the Mobile exhibits, will be located at the Sands and will feature companies providing transportation systems and is designed to promote a better understanding of electronics advances in surface transportation.
