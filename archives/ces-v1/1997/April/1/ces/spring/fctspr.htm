Spring CES® '97

Fact Sheet

DATES:
June 2 - 5, 1997

HOURS:
June 2 - 4: 10:30am - 5pm
June 5: 10:30am - 4pm

LOCATION: Georgia World Congress Center, Atlanta, Georgia, USA

FOCUS: Spring CES is a trade only event dedicated to promoting the products that are shaping the home environment and sold through the consumer electronics market.

SPONSORS: Sponsored, produced and managed by the Consumer Electronics Manufacturers Association (CEMA), a sector of the Electronics Industries Association (EIA).

EXHIBITOR PROFILE: Companies who manufacture audio/video hardware and accessories, computer and multimedia, hardware and wireless or communications products that are sold through the consumer retail channel.

ATTENDEE PROFILE: Spring CES will deliver domestic and international retail buyers, distributors, manufacturers, manufacturer representatives, financial and market analysts and members of the print and broadcast media who market electronic products and services to the consumer.

CONCURRENT EVENTS: COMDEX/Spring is the the largest computer event in the world dedicated to promoting the latest advances and IT solutions, products and services for the computer and communications industry.
WINDOWS WORLD, held in conjunction with COMDEX/Spring, is the preeminent event for Windows-based computing and communications, produced in cooperation with Microsoft.
EXPOCOMM, featuring Wireless World Expo and Broadband World Expo, will have a dual focus on wide area communications infrastructure technologies and the services and applications the infrastructure enables.
