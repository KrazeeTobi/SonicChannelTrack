Focus: CES Habitech is the premier trade and training event for integrated home and small building systems in entertainment, energy management, communications, networking, security, and controls.

In its fifth year, CES Habitech is the only event designed to train and educate the broad scope of companies aligning themselves with the emerging market force of integrated home and building systems.

Sponsor: Sponsored, produced and managed by the Consumer Electronics Manufacturers Association (CEMA), a sector of the Electronic Industries Association (EIA).Show proceeds are used to fund industry services.
