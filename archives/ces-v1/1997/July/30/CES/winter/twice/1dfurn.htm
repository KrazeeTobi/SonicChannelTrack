Furniture Makers Display Their
Home Theater, Home Office Wares

As technology picks up pace with consumers buying more 35-inch TVs and upgraded computers so has the home theater and home office furniture to house it.

Trends in home office, for example, are designed to accommodate more computer hardware and software which translates to increased storage capability. New designs are also capitalizing on the consumer's desire for a larger workspace area. Additionally, some models now offer mobility with group components that can be arranged and rearranged easily. Other units maximize hard-to-fit corner spaces.

Fueling trends in home theater style are consumer demand for larger screen TVs in 32-inch and 35-inch sizes. And with more equipment to go with these TVs amps, speakers, VCRs and now web browsers storage capacity has been stepped up.

In look, ready-to-assemble furniture is relaxing its edges with softer curved constructions and lighter finishes like Scrubbed Pine, Light Sand Maple and Golden Oak.
