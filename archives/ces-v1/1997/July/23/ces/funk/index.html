USA Pavilion at the Internationale Funkausstellung
Berlin, Germany: August 30 - September 7, 1997

The USA Pavilion, sponsored by CEMA, provides an economical avenue for U.S. manufacturers to showcase their new products and to find new dealers in Europe, the Middle East and Asia. The USA Pavilion companies will be displaying everything in consumer electronics from loudspeakers and telephones to laser pointer and computer cables.
