Press Registration

Press registration for media representatives who wish to cover TGS2024 at the venue will be available on the dedicated website around early August.

Prior to the opening of the press registration, media representatives who wish to receive press releases and other information on TOKYO GAME SHOW are requested to contact the TOKYO GAME SHOW Press Room (tgs-pressvectorinc.co.jp) by e-mail, stating that you wish to cover TGS2024.

Please include your affiliation, media name, position, and other information that will help us determine whether you meet the following requirements for media coverage.

TGS Press Room will confirm the content of the request before distributing the press release and other necessary procedures.

  • If you have already received a press release from the Press Room, there is no need to contact us.
  • The contact information for TOKYO GAME SHOW Press Room has changed as of April 1, 2024.

Press Coverage Rules and Regulations

Press pre-registration for TOKYO GAME SHOW 2024 is limited to those who fall under any of the following categories ① through ⑥ below.

  • ①Editors, reporters, and photographers of newspapers and publishing companies
  • ②Editors, reporters, and staff members of Internet news or information website production companies established by corporations
  • ③Television program production companies, reporters, and filming staff, including BS, CS, and cable TV
  • ④Radio program production companies, reporters and staff
  • ⑤Writers and cameramen who have a contract with the above editing/production companies or editorial departments for news gathering
  • ⑥Filming models and performers involved in news gathering and program production
  • Advertising agencies, business and advertising sales departments of newspapers, publishing companies, TV stations, and radio stations, PR firms, research firms, securities analysts, and those who distribute or post on SNS or video channels are not eligible.
  • TGS Press Room reserves the right to refuse registration for coverage if it determines that the applicant does not meet the coverage rules.
  • In addition, influencer registration is available for those who distribute and post on social networking sites and video channels and meet the conditions set by TGS (fees may apply).
    The conditions for influencer registration and the registration start date will be announced on the official website around early August.
