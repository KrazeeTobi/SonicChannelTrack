Application (Worldwide) Application (TAIWAN)

TOKYO GAME SHOW VR 2023 (TGSVR2023)

This year's TGS VR will retain the immersive experience of VR technology, but will also be compatible with smartphones, allowing a wider range of visitors to participate.
In addition to the four-day event, which will be held concurrently with the real TGS 2023, the event will be extended by +1 week, upgrading the space for even more people to enjoy.
Please look forward to the various renewals of TGSVR2023.

Exhibit Plan (for game-related companies and VR platformers only)

Three plans are available: Gold, Silver, and Bronze.For any of the plans, you can choose an exhibition pattern according to the content of your exhibit.We will respond to customization upon request.

  • The following price are for basic exhibits that do not include customization.
  • For customization and embodiment of the game world, a separate production fee will be charged depending on the product to be created.

Gold Plan

  • Basic Exhibit

    22 million yen～ (Assumed 3 companies)

Placement Center of exhibit area
Design Based on selected exhibit pattern
(customization available)
(Additional fees will be determined based on content)
Size approx. 90m x 45m
PR materials 15 videos, 10 posters
Other items 3D models (with animation), WEB/EC links.

  • Basic Exhibit

    11 million yen～ (Assumed 15 companies)

Bronze Plan

  • Basic Exhibit

Placement In the exhibit area
Design Selected exhibit pattern base only
Size approx. 12m x 12m
PR materials 5 videos, 5 posters
Other items 3D models (static models only), web/EC links.
【NEW!】the「AR/VR Corner」Set Plan at the TGS Real Venue

If you exhibit at TGSVR2023 and also exhibit at the AR/VR Corner in the TGS Real Venue, you will receive a 10% discount on the basic exhibition fee for each of the TGSVR2023 exhibition plans!

In addition, the booth fee for the AR/VR Corner at TGS Real Venue will also be discounted by 10%!

Please take this opportunity to consider exhibiting with a set plan.

Sponsorship Plan (for general companies only)

Two plans are available: Premium and Standard.Both plans allow you to set up your own space within the TGS VR space to promote your brand and PR.

Premium Plan

  • 55 million yen～ (Assumed several companies)

Placement Near the center of the world
Design Full customization (Additional fees will be determined based on content)
Size approx. 90m x 45m
PR materials 15 videos, 10 posters
Other items 3D models (with animation), WEB/EC links.

Standard Plan

  • 16.5 million yen (Assumed 10 companies)

Placement Location Within World
Design Customized (Additional fees will be determined based on content)
Size approx. 12m x 12m
PR materials 10 videos, 8 posters
Other items 3D models (with animation), WEB/EC links.
