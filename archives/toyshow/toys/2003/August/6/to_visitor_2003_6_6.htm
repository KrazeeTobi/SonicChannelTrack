2003 Tokyo Toy Show

To visitors from overseas countries

1) Measures related to SARS

　　　In order to remove concerns of the people gathering in the exhibition hall, the
　　　Japan Toy Association has determined to ask the traders arriving from the areas of
　　　recent local transmission of SARS listed by World Health Organization (WHO) to
　　　refrain from visiting 2003 Tokyo Toy Show.
　　　Because of the seriousness of this issue, we would like to ask your kind
　　　understanding and cooperation in this matter.
