To Business Visitors from Abroad

International Tokyo Toy Show 2023 will be held with advance web registration.



Advance web registration is required for admission. (Free of charge)
(1) The visitor pass you will receive on the day of the event is valid for one day only.
The visitor's pass given at the time of admission is valid until the closing time of the day.
Visitors may leave and re-enter during the day, but cannot re-enter across dates, so two registrations are required to attend both days of the event.

(2) Pre-registration is allowed for only one visitor.
(Registration can be made by the visitor or delegate).
  • ・If you are coming as a group, all members need to be registered.
  • ・Even if a pre-registered person brings a companion, that person will not be admitted without registration.
  • *Please be sure to register in advance as we will not be accepting registrations at the venue on the day of the event.
  • *Note when registering
    In rare cases, we have noticed that users of Microsoft addresses (@outlook, @msn, @aol, @hotmail) have not been receiving emails from the Japan Toy Association. We apologize for the inconvenience, but please use an e-mail address other than those listed above when registering.
    Recommended browsers: Latest version of Google chrome, Microsoft Edge, safari
  • *Internet Explorer is no longer supported and is deprecated, so please use one of the above browsers.
