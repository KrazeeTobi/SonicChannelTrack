Japan Toy Award 2008

 

Dear foreign exhibitors,

"Tokyo Toy Show" has provided opportunities to introduce new products and develop business relationships, thus attracting many toy retailers, distributors, and other toy-related business people.
In order to strengthen the function of drawing attention from industries and the public, the Japan Toy Association, the JTA, will establish the "Japan Toy Award" this year.

The award is open to products in the following five categories.

Innovation toy toys with innovation in materials, technologies, etc.
Basic toy toys for childcare, learning etc.
High target toy toys intended for adults or the silver-aged
Trendy toy toys fitting the trends of the year
Universal-use toy toys with which deaf or blind children play at ease

The winners will be determined through three-stage selection procedures.

Only products put on the market after October 1, 2007, or those which are planned to release by September, 2008 are eligible for the award.
(Prototypes of the products are acceptable.
Drawings or specifications of products are admissible only for the 1st and 2nd selection stage.)

The application deadline is April 30, 2008.

The announcement of the winners will be made at the Press Conference of the Tokyo Toy Show 2008 at Imperial Hotel, Tokyo on June 17, 2008, two days before the show.

The winning products (as well as those nominated by the 2nd selection stage) will be exhibited at the corner of "Japan Toy Award" at Tokyo Big Sight during the show.

The Japan Toy Award is open to both domestic and overseas exhibitors.
The JTA is honored to present you here the "Application guidelines, Japan Toy Award 2008."
Please feel free to send us whatever query you may find.
