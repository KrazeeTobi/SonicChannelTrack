TOYNES Activities

Invitation to Toy Companies to Participate in TOYNES


The Japan Toy Association has created the VAN system called TOYNES.

This VAN system is domestic system and its main object is the exchange of the data about the placement and the receipt of orders and the offering of information on toys.

As regards VAN terminal, we have developped POS (Point-of-sale) for the retailer's use.

For the wholesaler's and the manufacturer's use, we have developped software package aimed at toy industry for their computers for general purpose and for personal use.

Why is industry VAN necessary

The construction of the industry VAN that can be employed by anyone with low cost and that is based on the collective will of the industry is especially needed for the realization of efficient live-and-let-live system of three layers of industry (namely, retailers, wholesalers and manufacturers) and for the purpose of improving efficiency in office labor and sharing information effectively.
The more one utilize the industry VAN, the more the costs of distribution and office labor will reduce. Also, customer's sevice will be improved by making good use of the various information.

Expected merits


For retailers

  1. ) Collection of goods complying the needs of consumers becomes possible.

  2. ) Office labor such as placing orders and checkup of demand and payment will reduce.

  3. ) Information on toys will be obtained with low cost.

For wholesalers
  1. ) Buying in of products will be made more efficiently with the up-to-date information on the selling products.

  2. ) Abundant collection of goods suited for the region or the business types becomes possible.


TOYNES is now preparing for the commencement of service of WWW SERVER - INFORMATION ON JAPANESE TOY -(tentative name) through INTERNET starting from August this year.
WWW SERVER will use INTERNET through which multi-media information in about 140 countries in the world can be exchanged, which enables the users interactive exchange of information with consumers. Through this service, we are planning to dispatch information on Japanese toy firms, their products and their related facilities.
