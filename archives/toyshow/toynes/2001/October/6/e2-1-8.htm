In 1992, we celebrated the 25th anniversary of Licca-chan's birth. (Yet, she has been 11 years of age all the time !)
How she has been loved by the girls !
This castle collected all the memories of Licca-chan and other dolls that you or your children once played. The castle also has an open factory where you can see how the dolls are made and informed with new life. Please recover your doll-loving heart by visiting this castle.
