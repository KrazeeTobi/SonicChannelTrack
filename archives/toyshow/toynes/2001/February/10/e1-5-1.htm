The Japan Toy Association developed the VAN system called "TOYNES"on 1989 in order to tie in the three layers of toy industry (retailers, wholesalers and manufacturers). Now, it has changed the EDI(electronic date interchange) system which main objects are the exchange of the data about the placement and the receipt of orders and the offering of information on toys. At the same time "TOYNES" serves the substantial EC(electronic commerce) and EDI(electronic date interchange) systems for the industry, it also helps to build the interactive relationship between the industry and consumers.

We provide the POS (point of sale) system for the retailers and offer the multilateral analysis, productive advice and information on toys from our research based on the POS data. Furthermore, the wholesalers and manufactures can get the latest information on the selling products and consumers' trend from us, then put them into use for the future management.

<Expected merits For retailers>
1.) Collection of goods complying the needs of consumers becomes possible.
2.) Office labor such as placing orders and checkup of demand and payment will reduce.
3.) Information on toys will be obtained with low cost.

<Expected merits For wholesalers>
1.) Buying in of products will be made more efficiently with the up-to-date information on the selling products.
2.) Abundant collection of goods suited for the region or the business types becomes possible.

For the consumers, we present various kinds of information related to toys using our web page or NIFTY-serve (a domestic electronic mail and computer conference system).

Our EC and EDI systems provide the plenty of convenient services for both retailers' and wholesalers / manufactures' sides on the internet.
