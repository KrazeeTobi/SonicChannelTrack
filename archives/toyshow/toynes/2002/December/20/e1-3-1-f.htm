Annual schedule of "small-bumps"


The Annual schedule for "small-bumps" proposals is fixed as follows:

Besides the following annual schedule, we receive many requests to participate in exhibitions held for the handicapped people from various welfare-related facilities and organizations, and have been doing our best to promote public relations activities by lending toys or dispatching staff-members to give explanations, if necessary.


January

The secretariat of the Small Bump Committee begin preparations for "Toy catalogue" to be published in the following fiscal year.

February

Issue news release on an explanatory meeting for small-bumps proposals for toy industry magazines.
Notify the cooperating manufacturers that subscription of advertisement of any products to be listed in "Toy catalogue" to be published in the next fiscal year begins to be accepted by the Secretariat

March

Carry out an explanatory meeting for small-bumps proposals and activities for the next fiscal year.
Start planning and printing "Toy catalogue" in the next fiscal year.

April

Start preparing for "Braille catalogue".
Finally decide the number of products to be listed in "Toy catalogue"

May

Complete both "Braille catalogue" and "Toy catalogue"

June

Exhibit toy products in the booth of Tokyo Toy Show and start distribution of new catalogues.

July

Send catalogues to various welfare-related organizations, schools for the blind, braille libraries, etc.

August

Participate in the Study Conference on sight-impaired children to promote public awareness of people and organizations related to the welfare

September

Exhibit products at the Toy Trade Fairs held at six different locations in Japan to promote public relations activities for people both inside and outside the toy industry

October

Participate in CM conference of department store section to promote public relations activities for people in the distribution industry

November

Participate in the Council of welfare facilities for the blind in Japan to promote public relations activities for employees working in welfare facilities for the blind

December

Participate in the Game Meeting of Japan Braille Library to offer prizes donated by cooperating manufacturers
