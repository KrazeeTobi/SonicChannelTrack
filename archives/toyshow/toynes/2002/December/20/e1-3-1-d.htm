Actual conditions of "small-bumps" proposals and activities



"Small-bumps" proposals and activities are promoted by the following various
subcommittees and staff-members:

(1)Management of activities:

"Small-bumps" campaign activities are managed by the Small Bump Committee established by The Japan Toy Association.


(2)Small Bump Committee:

The Small Bump Committee (hereinafter called "the Committee") began business activities after being established by representatives of many cooperating manufacturers, when the "small-bumps" campaign was started in 1990.
The committee is composed of four different subcommittees, namely, recommendation, advertisement, distribution, cooperating manufacturers, and is a central organization responsible for planning promotion of this "smallbumps" campaign.


(3)Cooperating manufacturers in "small-bamps" campaign

A cooperating manufacturer means a toy manufacturer who supports the "small-bumps" campaign, has registered itself with the secretariat as a cooperating manufacturer in the "small-bumps" campaign (hereinafter called "cooperating manufacturer") and has presented toys manufactured with special features with a certificate incorporating emblems in the form of a seeing-eye dog or a rabbit.
A cooperating manufacturer is requested to nominate someone within the company as the person-in-charge for "small-bumps" related business to aid the promotional efforts of this campaign.


(4)Cooperating stores in "small-bumps" campaign

A cooperating store means a toy sales agent who has registered with the secretariat as a cooperating store in the "small-bumps" campaign and cooperate this campaign regarding distribution of toys under campaign. (hereinafter called "cooperating store").
When the secretariat is inquired about "co-playable toys for both normal and handicapped people", they will introduce a cooperating store near an inquirer.


(5)Advertisement subcommittee for "small-bumps"

The "small-bumps" advertisement subcommittee (hereinafter called "Advertisement subcommittee") is responsible for the advertising and PR activities of this campaign.
This subcommittee makes it a rule to send news releases about "small-bumps" once a month to the four different toy industry magazines with a view to publicizing the concept to everyone in the industry and is in a responsible position to respond to the news-gathering activities of journalists.


(6) Distribution subcommittee of "small-bumps"

The "small-bumps" distribution subcommittee (hereinafter called "Distribution summittee") is responsible for responding to the people concerned with the distribution activities of this small-bumps campaign.The works of the subcommittee is to collect the cooperating stores of small-bumps products, who will help the consumers in purchasing small-bumps products and to promote the public relations activities at the CM meeting of the department stores section.


(7) "Small-bumps" cooperating manufacturers' subcommittee

The "small-bumps" cooperating manufacturers' subcommittee (hereinafter called "Cooperatin manufacturers' subcommittee") is responsible for responding to the to manufacturers participating in the activities of this "small-bumps" campaign.
An explanatory seminar about "small-bumps" to be held in March every year is managed mainly by this subcommittee.


(8) "Small-bumps" secretariat

The "small-bumps" secretariat (hereinafter called, "the secretariat") is responsible for carrying out matters adopted by the Small Bump Committee and responding to the production of toy catalogues and exhibitions at the Tokyo Toy Show and the Toy Trade Fairs in autumn, playing the role of mediating between the people concerned in welfare-related businesses and in the toy industry.


(9) "Small-bumps" recommendation subcommittee

The "small-bumps" recommendation subcommittee (hereinafter called "Recommendatio subcommittee") is organized by staffmembers and people concerned with various welfare-related organizations headed by Japan Braille Library.
This subcommittee has the function of carrying out monitoring inspections and giving advice on toys presented to the secretariat so that the hearing and sight-impaired may easily use them.

On December, 1994, the sight- "small-bumps" subcommittee and the hearing- "small-bumps" subcommittee were integrated into the "Small-bumps" recommendation subcommittee.
Since then, the subcommittee has broaden the range of its activities such as giving advice on toy catalogues which the Small Bump Committee publishes annually and establishing the " Committee for Guidelines on Rabit Emblem" in cooperation with the hearing-impaired and people surrounding them.
