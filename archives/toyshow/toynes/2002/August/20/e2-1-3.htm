Mibu is a Toy Town (Omocha-no-machi). Toy Town is the name of the station as well as the address. It was named so because there is a well-known toy industrial park in the town. This museum was established in order to further activate the town with the toy industry and also foster children's dreams.

The museum has three floors. The first floor is the toy wonderland, equipped with various devices with which children can actually play and experience.

The second floor is the display area of about 2,000 toy items classified by theme. It has also a touch-and-play corner. The third floor is an observatory with a 360 panoramic view, where you can take a rest. Mibu Toy MUseum gives dreams to children, and nostalgia to the grown-ups. All the staffs are looking foward to welcoming you at the museum.
