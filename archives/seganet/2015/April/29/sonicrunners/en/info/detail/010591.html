Event Ranking Calculations and Rewards
 
Thank you for playing Sonic Runners.
 
We have identified an issue where certain users in the Rankings have achieved high scores through cheating and prohibited methods.
 
In order to keep the Event Rankings fair, users who cheated will be removed from the Ranking at the event's end, with the correct users receiving the appropriate rewards.
 
Because of this, there may be a delay in the announcement of the final Event Ranking results as well as the delivery of the rewards.
 
Thank you for your understand, and we hope you continue to enjoy Sonic Runners.
