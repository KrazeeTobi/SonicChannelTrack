Sonic Runners

Run, Jump and Aim for the Top!

What is Sonic Runners?

The "Sonic" Action Game makes its advent! Tap to perform jumps and move Sonic chracters! Keep runnin' with Chao and radio control toys till you get the top with the most highest score!

Player Characters

Progressing the episode as "Sonic" will eventually unlock further Sonic series characters as playables! The 3 characters will each show a different action, and their levels can be raised using rings collected in stages! More characters will be joining the game shortly!

The Fastest Hedgehog in the World! Sonic is a free spirit with a strong sense of right and wrong. He will always rush to the aid of those in need.
Type: Speed/Hero
Tap the screen while in the middle of a jump to perform a special action.

Tails—real name Miles Prower—is one of Sonic's best pals. He's a super-friendly fox cub with two fluffy tails that he can spin at high speed to fly through the air.
Type: Fly/Hero
Tails is a Fly-type character Tap and hold mid-jump, and he'll float in the air.

Knuckles is a super-strong echidna, and one of Sonic's greatest rivals and best friends. A martial arts master, his punches can turn massive boulders into dust.
Type: Power/Hero
Knuckles is a Power-type character. Tap mid-jump, and he'll launch a dash attack.

Companions

Companions are buddies which player characters can take along. Helps players raise scores while some can enhance item effects! Just like player characters, they too have a certain attribute, and therefore their effects will be amplified when paired up with the "right" player character! There are currently 20 types of them and more will be joining in no time!
