Game Details

Contents

Sonic Runners is an easy-to-play endless running game—all you have to do is tap the screen to jump! Run alongside companions such as Chao and remote-control gadgets, save the cute animals kidnapped by Dr. Eggman, and aim to top the rankings with spectacular high scores!

Episode Map

Episode Map

This is the basic game screen. Scoring points as you play allows you to progress across the map and deeper into the story.
Various things happen at waypoints along the map—perhaps an event will take place, or maybe you'll earn some Rings or items.
Reach the end of the map, and you might even find a familiar foe waiting for a showdown!

Current Party

Current Party

This screen shows the characters and companions you can use during the game. You can choose a main character and a sub-character, as well as a main companion and a sub-companion.

Rankings

Rankings

This screen lets you take a look at the current rankings. There are three types of rankings: the Runners' League, in which you compete against other players to try and reach the top division; the Friends Rankings, where you can see how your friends are progressing; and the World Rankings, where players from all over the world duke it out for the top spot.
Furthermore, selecting "History" allows you to view historical rankings, and see who's been at the top of the tree in the past.
Each ranking is divided into two categories: High Score, which is based on the highest score accumulated in a single attempt, and Total Score, which is based on the total number of points acquired during a given period.

Roulette

Roulette

This screen allows you to win items and new companions by playing various types of Roulette. Playing Premium Roulette costs Red Star Rings, and allows you to acquire new companions.
Collect 10 Special Eggs, either while playing or via Premium Roulette, and you'll be given a chance to play Special Roulette.
Item Roulette is free to play, but you can only spin the wheel a certain number of times each day. You can win items and Rings by playing Item Roulette. Item Roulette has three stages, Normal, Big, and Super. You will proceed to the Big and Super stages when the Roulette wheel stops in the appropriate places. There's a Jackpot slot on the Super stage—if the wheel stops there, you'll win the biggest Ring prize out there!

Specifications

Characters

Characters

  • These are the playable characters you can use in-game.
  • When you first start playing the game, Sonic is the only playable character you can use, but as you progress through the story, other characters such as Tails and Knuckles will become available.
  • There are three different character types: Speed, Fly, and Power. Each type has its own special action.
  • Each character also has a “personality type”. Sonic, Tails and Knuckles are all “Hero” personalities, meaning you’ll be awarded a bonus on the results screen based on the distance you covered.
  • You can use up to two characters on a stage: a main character and a sub-character.
  • To use a sub-character while playing, you will need to use the “Sub-Character” booster item.
  • You can switch characters by touching the icon in the bottom-left of the screen. You will also switch characters if one of your characters comes a cropper. After you’ve touched the icon to switch characters, you cannot switch again for at least 5 seconds.

Characters

Companions

Companions

  • ”Companions” will accompany your characters on their adventures.
  • You can team up with two companions at a time: a main companion and a sub-companion.
  • Each companion has their own unique effect on your performance.
  • Companions effects include score boosts and item efficiency boosts. Some companions produce special effects when combos of certain lengths are performed, while others come into their own against bosses. The possibilities are endless!
  • Each companion has a rarity rating: Normal, Rare, or Super Rare. The rarer a companion, the more powerful the effect it produces.
  • Just like playable characters, companions have types: Speed, Fly, or Power. Companions are even more effective when they accompany characters of the same type.
  • New companions can be acquired by playing Premium Roulette, or by collecting 10 Special Eggs and playing Special Roulette.
  • Acquire more and more companions, and your total Companion Bonus will increase, based on their number and ability levels. This total Companion Bonus is applied to the scores you achieve in-game (up to a maximum of 100%).

Companions

Booster Items & Equippable Items

Booster Items & Equippable Items

  • Before you head out onto a stage, you’ll be taken to a screen where you can equip items or buy new ones using Rings.
  • Booster Items are bought using Rings, and will be used mid-stage.
  • Booster Items are active for the entire duration of your run.
  • If you already own Equippable Items, you can go ahead and equip these for use on the upcoming stage. If you don’t own any Equippable Items you’d like to use, you can buy more using Rings, then equip them before the stage begins.
  • Up to three Equippable Items can be equipped at any one time. Equippable items will be used, in the order they are equipped, at the following times: the start of a stage; when the game speeds up; when the game speeds up for the second time.
  • Equippable Items also appear on stages. These have the same effects as the items you equip before starting a stage, but last about half as long.

Booster Items & Equippable Items

Premium Roulette

  • Premium Roulette is a form of roulette that allows you to acquire new companions.
  • You can play Premium Roulette by spending Red Star Rings.
  • Companions have one of three rarity ratings: Normal, Rare, and Super Rare. The rarer a companion is, the harder it is to acquire via Premium Roulette.
  • If the wheel stops on a companion you already own, that companion’s level will increase by one.
  • If the wheel stops on a companion you already own who has already reached the maximum possible level, you will acquire a Special Egg instead.

Special Eggs

Special Eggs

  • Collecting 10 Special Eggs gives you a chance to play Special Roulette. You are guaranteed to acquire a Rare or Super Rare companion while playing Special Roulette.
  • Special Eggs are acquired by beating bosses the very first time you encounter them, and by winning companions you already own and who are already at their maximum level in Premium Roulette.

Special Eggs

Special Roulette

  • You are given a chance to play Special Roulette instead of Premium Roulette every time you collect 10 Special Eggs.
  • You are guaranteed to win a Rare or Super Rare companion while playing Special Roulette. The odds of winning prizes are also more favorable than when playing Premium Roulette.

Item (Login) Roulette

  • Item Roulette can be played a certain number of times each day.
  • There are three stages of Item Roulette: Normal, Big, and Super. If the wheel stops on the “Big” or “Super” sections, you will proceed to the corresponding stage.
  • A jackpot can be won on the Super stage. Get the wheel to stop in the right section, and score yourself a bumper crop of Rings!

Red Star Rings

Red Star Rings

  • Red Star Rings are used to play Premium Roulette. They can also be exchanged for Rings and Revive Tokens.
  • Red Star Rings can occasionally be acquired on stages and on the Episode Map, via Item Roulette, or via Daily Challenges. They can also be bought using real money.

Red Star Rings

Rings

  • Rings are used to level up your playable characters, and to buy items.
  • Rings are acquired on stages and on the Episode Map, via Item Roulette, via Daily Challenges, and by other means. Large amounts of Rings can be bought using Red Star Rings.

Revive Tokens

  • Revive Tokens are required in order to play the game. Each time you head out onto a stage, a Revive Token is consumed.
  • If you have five or fewer Revive Tokens, you will be given a new one every 15 minutes.
  • You can also receive Revive Tokens as gifts from friends.

Daily Challenges

  • Daily Challenges are specific missions that are issued to you on a daily basis. You will receive rewards for completing these challenges, such as items or Rings.
  • Daily Challenge rewards are given in the form of stamps on a seven-day sheet. Complete the sheet’s seventh and final mission, and you’ll earn a really spectacular reward!

Continuing

  • Fail a stage, and you’ll be offered a chance to continue your game.
  • Continuing your game will cost a certain number of Red Star Rings.

Crystals

  • Crystals are the main method of scoring points in Sonic Runners.
  • Large crystals are worth a lot of points, while small crystals are worth fewer points. A crystal’s color also affects its point value. Red crystals are worth the most points, followed by green crystals. Blue crystals are worth the least points.

Dr. Eggman's Robots

  • Robots are enemies that Dr. Eggman has deployed throughout the game’s stages to make life a misery for Sonic and co!
  • Defeat a robot, and the Animal trapped inside it will be freed.
  • Defeating golden robots scores you a lot more points than defeating normal robots.
  • Be aware that so-called “tough enemies” can only be defeated by Power-type characters.

Animals

Animals

  • Dr. Eggman is capturing the local Animals and trapping them inside his robots, where they are used as power sources!
  • Defeating a robot releases the Animal trapped inside it.
  • Touch a released Animal with your character to save it.

Animals

Dr. Eggman

Dr. Eggman

  • Dr. Eggman is the game’s main bad guy, and the leader of the robots who are out to get Sonic and co.
  • Sonic and his friends are chasing after Dr. Eggman in the hope of catching him and putting an end to his schemes. Dr. Eggman will sometimes appear on stages. Attack him, and he will drop a big pile of Rings. He might even drop a Red Star Ring every now and again.
  • Dr. Eggman will also confront Sonic and the gang at the end of certain Episode Maps. These stages are not ordinary endless running stages, but rather special boss battle showdowns. The stage will not end until Dr. Eggman has been defeated.
  • Dr. Eggman is more likely to drop Red Star Rings during these boss battles than he is during his appearances on ordinary stages.

Dr. Eggman

Checkpoints

Checkpoints

  • All the Rings you’re carrying are sent to the bank whenever you pass a Star Post.

Checkpoints

The Bank

  • In order for the Rings your characters have picked up on stages to be brought back home with them, they need to be banked.
  • Rings are safely banked whenever you pass a Star Post. Some companions also send Rings to the bank when you take damage.
  • Whenever you bank Rings, the number of Rings sent will be displayed beneath the number of Rings you’re currently carrying at the top of the screen.

Combos

  • Form combos by collecting crystals, Rings, or Animals in an unbroken chain.
  • Combo chains are broken when you take damage.
  • If you are using the "Combo Bonus" item, your combo will not be broken even if you take damage, or if you fail to keep collecting crystals, Rings, or Animals continuously.

Combo Bonuses

  • While you are performing a combo, a bonus is applied to the score you get for collecting crystals, Rings, and Animals.
  • The longer your combo, the bigger the bonus!

Rankings

High Score & Total Score

  • In the High Score rankings, players compete to see who can score the most points in a single attempt at the game.
  • High Scores are still valid even if a “Continue” is used during the game attempt.
  • In the Total Score rankings, players compete to see who can score the most points during a certain period of time.

Runners' League

  • The Runners' League is a ranking competition containing a number of divisions, each of which features up to 50 players who compete against each other.
  • Players compete to record the best High Score within a given period. Players who finish high towards the top of their division are promoted to the next division, whilst players who finish near the bottom of their division are relegated to the division below.
  • Rewards are given to those who finish with good results in the High Score and Total Score categories of the Runners' League. These rewards include Rings, items, and Red Star Rings.
  • The Runners’ League is comprised of 21 divisions, with F- being the lowest and S+ being the highest. All players start in the lowest division, F-.
  • The higher up the divisions you go, the better the rewards you can receive.

Friends

  • The Friends Rankings only contain people you’re friends with on Facebook.
  • You need to be logged in to Facebook in order to view the Friends Rankings.
  • Just like the other rankings, the Friends Rankings are divided into two categories: High Score and Total Score.

World

  • In the World Rankings, players from all over the world compete against each other.
  • Just like the other rankings, the World Rankings are divided into two categories: High Score and Total Score.
  • Rewards such as Rings, items, and Red Star Rings are awarded to players who do well in the World Rankings.

History

  • The Historical Rankings allow you to see who topped the rankings in all past ranking periods.
  • Historical Rankings are also divided into two categories: High Score and Total Score.

Resetting the Ranking Data

  • Rankings data is reset on a regular basis.
  • If you have recorded good results when the rankings are reset, you may get promoted to a higher division of the Runners’ League, or you may be awarded a World Rankings reward.
  • Any ranking rewards you receive, such as Rings, items, and Red Star Rings, will be sent to your Gift Box.
