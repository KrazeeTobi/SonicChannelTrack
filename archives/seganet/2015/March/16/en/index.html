Sonic Runners

Information

What is Sonic Runners?

The Sonic Action Game makes its debut! Tap to perform jumps and move Sonic and his friends! Keep running with Chao and Companions until you get to the top of the leader boards with the highest score!

Player Characters

Play through Sonic Runners and unlock playable Sonic's friends like Tails and Knuckles! The three characters will each show a different action, and their levels can be raised using rings collected in stages. Additional Sonic characters will be added over time.

The Fastest Hedgehog in the World! Sonic is a free spirit with a strong sense of right and wrong. He will always rush to the aid of those in need.
Type: Speed/Hero
Tap the screen while in the middle of a jump to perform a special action.

Tails—real name Miles Prower—is one of Sonic's best pals. He's a super-friendly fox cub with two fluffy tails that he can spin at high speed to fly through the air.
Type: Fly/Hero
Tails is a Fly-type character Tap and hold mid-jump, and he'll float in the air.

Knuckles is a super-strong echidna, and one of Sonic's greatest rivals and best friends. A martial arts master, his punches can turn massive boulders into dust.
Type: Power/Hero
Knuckles is a Power-type character. Tap mid-jump, and he'll launch a dash attack.

Companions

Companions are buddies which Sonic and his friends can take along for their runs. Each with different abilities, they can help raise scores or even enhance item effects! Just like Sonic and his friends, they also have attributes and when combined with the corresponding character, their effects will be amplified. There are currently 20 types of Companions with more coming!

Item

Equipping Items to characters will provide additional benefits. Players can equip up to three items for each run.

Equippable Items

Cyan Laser
High speed aerial movement available after transformation. Absorbs items when the player taps.
Yellow Drill
High speed underground movement available. Performs jumps when the player taps.
Indigo Asteroid
Touching it after the transformation will enable players to float. Destroys various obstacles.
Invincible
Protects players from all damages, including those from enemies.

Booster Items

Score Booster
Boosts the score gained as player bonus
Spring Platforms
