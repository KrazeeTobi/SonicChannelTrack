Sonic Runners

Run, Jump and Aim for the Top!

What is Sonic Runners?

An All-New Sonic Action Game is here! Simple controls – just tap to jump, dash, and fly! Equip items and buddies to improve your stats and run to the top of the leader boards!

Player Characters

Take control of Sonic and race through every level. BUT, you’ll quickly unlock more friends like Tails and Knuckles! Each character has special skills that are useful in different stages. Additional characters will be added in the future!

The Fastest Hedgehog in the World! Sonic is a free spirit with a strong sense of right and wrong. He will always rush to the aid of those in need.
Type: Speed/Hero
Tap the screen while in the middle of a jump to perform a double and triple jump.

Tails—real name Miles Prower—is one of Sonic's best pals. He's a super-friendly fox cub with two fluffy tails that he can spin at high speed to fly through the air.
Type: Fly/Hero
Tails is a Fly-type character Tap and hold mid-jump, and he'll float in the air.

Knuckles is a super-strong echidna, and one of Sonic's greatest rivals and best friends. A martial arts master, his punches can turn massive boulders into dust.
Type: Power/Hero
Knuckles is a Power-type character. Tap mid-jump, and he'll launch a dash attack.

Buddies

Items

Players can also equip up to 3 items each run, to help them run to a high score!
