Confirmed Bug for Ver. 1.1.0
 
Thank you for playing Sonic Runners.
We have confirmed the following bugs in Ver. 1.1.0 which was released on 6/25.
 
-Rappy Effect Adjustments
In order to balance the game, Rappy's effects were intended to be reduced by 30%, but we have confirmed they have been reduced by 60%. 
We will be giving out 50 Red Star Rings and 200,000 Rings to all Rappy owners as compensation.
 
-Event BGM Bug
Event BGM may be silent for some devices.
 
We are committed to correcting these issues as soon as possible.
We are sorry for any inconvenience this may have caused.
 
We hope you will continue to enjoy playing Sonic Runners!
