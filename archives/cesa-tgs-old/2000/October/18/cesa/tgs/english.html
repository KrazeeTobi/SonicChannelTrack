July 6, 2000 Overview of TOKYO GAME SHOW 2000 AUTUMN
　 Tokyo Game Show 2000 Autumn," will be held by CESA with "The Entertainment of Your Life" as its theme, in Makuhari Messe (Nippon Convention Center, Chiba City, Chiba prefecture) for three days from Sep. 22 (Fri.) to 24 (Sun.), 2000.
As of July 17, a total of 63 exhibitors are expected to attend the exhibition. A total of 1,169 booths are occupied.
Tokyo Game Show 2000 Autumn, the 9th time the show has been held, is expected to be the scene of unprecedented excitement as an event bringing everyone to the new future of computer entertainment.
