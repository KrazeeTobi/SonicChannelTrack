    Notes;
    *Adult Oriented Exhibits

    • Adult oriented exhibit refers to a merchandise with under 18 prohibited label, merchandise containing sexually explicit material or material judged inappropriate for exhibition by the Event Office.
    • To exhibit, display, sell and carry in adult oriented materials in the exhibition hall is strictly prohibited.
    *All publications, exhibits and sales should conform to the governing laws of Japan. Each exhibitor is responsible for any illegal action. In case of violation of Exhibition rules or governing laws of Japan, the Event Office may take appropriate measures. In addition, each exhibitor is responsible for injury or damage due to violation of Exhibition rules or governing laws of Japan.
    *Sales and Spot Sales
    • Any act of sales and spot sales including booking service is prohibited, with the exception of followings:
    • Publications
    • Merchandise such as premium products with the approval of the Event Office which do not deviate from the purpose of the "SHOW"
    *Approval of merchandise
    • All the details of the exhibits / merchandise must be submitted to the Event Office for approval by the designated application sheet.
