The Virtual Press Office will be automating the working press room at E3 by uploading exhibitor press materials (press releases, company and product backgrounders, photos, white papers, etc.) to a single homepage. The service has been designed to support the media in getting quick and efficient access to breaking news stories as well as comprehensive background information. The Virtual Press Office service is free of charge and is on-line year round.
