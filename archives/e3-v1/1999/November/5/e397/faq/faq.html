1. Is the show open to the public?
    No, E3 is a trade event and only professionals from the industry will be allowed to attend.

  2. I would like to bring my child to the show. Is there an age restriction?
    E3 is open to trade only. No one under the age of 18 (including infants) will be admitted.

  3. I am a member of the press. How do I register and get press credentials?
    The pre-registration deadline has passed. You must now register on-site, any day of the show. For more information please contact Bender, Goldman & Helper at (310) 473-4147.

  4. It's after hours. Is there any way I can get information by fax?
    Yes, simply call 800-315-1133, then press #2 for our fax-on-demand service.

  5. I would like to register. What is the process and when is the deadline?
    The pre-registration deadline has passed. To register you must do so on-site at the Registration Hall in the Georgia World Congress Center. See the How to Attend page for more details.

  6. I've already sent my registration by fax, web or mail. How do I know if I'm registered?
    Once processed, you should receive a confirmation card and badge information by U.S. mail. Your admission badge will be mailed to you on or about June 2, 1997.
