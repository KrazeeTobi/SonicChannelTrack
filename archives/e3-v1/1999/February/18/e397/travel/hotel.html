Hotel Accomodations

Accommodation Restrictions
Hotel availability is limited and is on a first-come, first-serve basis. A credit card is required to make any hotel reservations. Additionally, a non-refundable hotel deposit of two nights room rate plus 13% Atlanta state and local taxes will be collected at time of booking.

Airline/Hotel/Car Rental Cancellation Policy:
All changes or cancellations must be made through Event Travel Services. Airline, hotel and car rental policies vary by company. You will be advised policies and penalties at time of booking. Rates are subject to availability and change without notice.

Event Travel Services
Call Event Travel Services, the official travel agency for E3, to make all your travel and hotel reservations. ETS will make your trip to E3 easy and affordable. Just call 800.361.8191 (617.361.8111 outside of the U.S.) to secure your arrangements. Or fax them to ETS at 617.361.6936.
