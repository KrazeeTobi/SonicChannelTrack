Get the Publicity Your Company Deserves

More than 6,500 media are expected to attend CES 2017, generating unprecedented coverage via newspaper and magazine articles, television and radio reports, online stories and up to the second social media posts. Follow these guidelines, tips and samples to garner the publicity your company deserves.

CES Exhibitor Attire Policy

CES Show Management reserves the right to make determinations on appropriate exhibitor or presenter attire.

If for any reason an exhibit or its contents are deemed objectionable by Show Management, the exhibitor may be asked to alter the attire of its employees, exhibit staff or models. If necessary, the exhibitor may be asked to remove the individual(s) in question at the exhibitor’s sole expense.

Exhibitors with questions about compliance with these guidelines should consult with Show Management in advance of the show.

News Conference Dos & Don’ts

Do...

  • Submit your CES 2017 news conference request form by Dec. 16, 2016.
  • Establish clear and precise newsworthy objectives for your event.
  • Book the appropriate space in a setting that will enhance delivery.
  • Identify and invite targeted media to your event using our pre-registered media list, available Nov. 1, 2016 to exhibitors hosting news conferences.
  • Promote your event through targeted news releases, calls and pitches.
  • Send invitations to your targeted media one month before CES; follow up two weeks prior to the event.
  • Have staff on site to help with registration and seating.
  • Notify CTA staff if your event is invitation-only.
  • Have a lead retrieval unit or business card collector at the door to see who attends your event.
  • Distribute product spec sheets, news conference speaker bios and digital media kits.
  • Upload brochures, media kits, news releases and other materials along with your company logo to the CES 2017 App.
  • Allow for Q&A time at the end of your event.
  • Feel free to invite media to your booth for more information.

Don't...

  • Leak major product announcements or news before your news conference.
  • Start late.
  • End late.
  • Read straight from a PowerPoint presentation.
  • Schedule a news conference if you have no news.
  • Eliminate Q&A with media,
  • Hold a conference that lasts more than 45 minutes.
  • Expect media to attend without proactive work on your part.
  • Schedule an event that conflicts with any official CES media events, including keynotes.

Publicity Tips

Follow these tips to garner the publicity your company deserves:

1) Media Positioning — CES defines innovation. Develop media materials that capture this innovative spirit and reflect how your company is on the cutting-edge of forward thinking.

2) Announce New Products at CES — Debut new products at the show to generate global publicity. Consider hosting a CES exhibitor news conference to showcase your latest innovations.

3) Name Drop CES — Use your exhibitor status in your announcements and benefit from CES branding. Don’t forget to list your CES 2017 exhibit number and venue so that media can find you at the show.

4) Use Social Media — CES has an abundance of social media tools available to its exhibitors. Make sure you take advantage of our outlets, to peak interest and increase your outreach and coverage.

5) Use the Web — Post releases via our Online Media Center or at your own URL.

6) Media Materials — CES is the hotspot for the consumer electronics industry, with more than 20,000 anticipated product launches. Make sure your media kit materials stand out from the competition. A media kit tips PDF will be available soon. 

7) Exhibitor Profile — Update your exhibitor profile to identify PR reps who will be available for media interviews.

8) Think TV — Make your pitches visually appealing and be practiced and prepared to present to television journalists. CESTV coverage increases annually, with more programs reporting directly from the show floor every year. If you would like to be included in these promotions, fill out the CESTV On-site Coverage Consideration Form.

9) CES Promotions — We want to keep your company’s name in front of industry decision makers. CTA promotes the consumer technology industry and CES all year long. View all CES 2017 promotional opportunities.

Sample News Release Materials

News Release

  • Headline should be informative and grab the reader's attention.
  • The dateline should include date and location (Las Vegas, if being released during show).
  • Avoid clichés and jargon that people outside the industry will not understand.
  • Be concise and use proper grammar.

 A sample CES news release template will be available in the coming weeks.
 
Other tips:

  • Deal with the facts.
  • Do not make the release sound like a sale pitch.
  • Keep it simple.
  • Think and write like a reporter.
  • Make sure the piece can stand on its own and does not need further background.
  • Bullet points clearly display information.
  • Leave white space; clutter is not good.

Sample Social Media Posts

Sample Tweet
When writing a Tweet about CES from your company's Twitter account, we encourage the following:

  • When writing about CES either tag our handle (@CES) or use our official hashtag #CES2017.

  • Use only our official hashtag. #CES can mean many things in both English and other languages, using our official hashtag is the best way to ensure your tweets are seen by those searching for information about the show.

  • Include a photo or link to an article or video for readers to learn more about your tweet.

Check out CES Tweets.

Sample Facebook Post
When writing posts about CES in Facebook on your page and on ours, we encourage the following:

  • Writing posts that reflect a continuing positive dialogue with our community.

  • Tag our Facebook page in a post about CES.

  • Use a picture or video when possible to help further engagement.

  • Respond to questions and comments that your community has for the post.
