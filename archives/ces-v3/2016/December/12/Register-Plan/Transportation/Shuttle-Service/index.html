    CES Offers Complimentary Shuttle Service

    CES offers complimentary shuttle service as follows.

    Shuttle Schedule and Map

    View CES 2017 shuttle schedule and map. (PDF)

    ADA Shuttles

    We provide ADA-accessible buses for those with special needs. Please contact RPMs, the official transportation provider for CES, at 877-725-3398 or CESinfo@rp-ms.com.

    Airport Shuttle Service

    Shuttle service to McCarran International Airport (LAS) from the Las Vegas Convention Center (LVCC) and Sands/Venetian will be available every 25-30 minutes on Friday, Jan. 6 and Saturday, Jan. 7, 10 AM-7 PM, and Sunday, Jan. 8, 10 AM-5 PM. The ticket price for this service is $5 per person, one-way.

    C Space Shuttle

    The C Space Shuttle runs between the LVCC and Sands/Venetian to ARIA every 15-20 minutes on Thursday, Jan. 5, and Friday, Jan. 6, 8 AM-5:30 PM, and Saturday, Jan. 7, 8 AM-2:30 PM. Boarding locations are

    • LVCC, outside the Grand Lobby
    • Venetian, underground bus area 
    • ARIA, Level 1 Convention Space, adjacent to the Bristlecone Ballroom

    CES Unveiled Shuttle Service

    On Tuesday, Jan. 3, the shuttle service will run between the LVCC Central Hall 2 entrance, along the curb, and the Venetian underground shuttle area to Mandalay Bay every 15-20 minutes, 12:30-9 PM.

    Hotel Shuttle Buses

    CES offers complimentary shuttle service Wednesday, Jan. 4, through Sunday, Jan. 8, directly to and from the LVCC and all official CES hotels.

    Direct hotel service will also be provided to and from the Sands/Venetian. The full schedule with routes and operating hours is available in the PDF above.

    LVCC Golf Cart and Pedestrian Walkway

    Golf Cart Service will be provided every 10 minutes between the back of South Halls 1-2 and Central Hall 5, Thursday, Jan. 5, to Saturday, Jan. 7, 9 AM-6 PM, and Sunday, Jan. 8, 9 AM-1 PM.

    LVCC Shuttle Loop

    To help you get around the Las Vegas Convention Center, shuttle service will be provided every 10 minutes with boarding points at five key locations around the perimeter of the LVCC. Service is available Thursday, Jan. 5, to Saturday, Jan. 7, 8 AM-6:30 PM, and Sunday, Jan. 8, 8 AM-4:30 PM.

    Mandalay Bay Express

    On Wednesday, Jan. 4, the Mandalay Bay Express will run between the LVCC  Central Hall 2 entrance, along the curb, and the Mandalay Bay Convention Center/Shark Reef entrance every 25-30 minutes, 10 AM-4 PM.  Return service runs between the Mandalay Bay Convention Center/Shark Reef entrance and the LVCC Central Hall 5 Lobby entrance every 25-30 minutes, 4-5:30 PM.

    Morning Service to Mandalay Bay

    On Tuesday, Jan. 3, the morning shuttle service will run from select official CES hotels* to the Mandalay Bay Convention Center/Shark Reef entrance every 25-30 minutes, 10:30 AM-12:30 PM.

    On Wednesday, Jan. 4, the morning shuttle service will run from select official CES hotels* to the Mandalay Bay Convention Center/Shark Reef entrance every 25-30 minutes, 6:30 AM-10 AM.

    *Service not available to Excalibur, Luxor**, SLS and all Caesars’ properties.

    ** Use the free Mandalay Bay-Luxor-Excalibur tram. Trams run every 3-7 minutes from 6:30-10:30 AM, daily.

    Tech Express

    Express shuttle service will be provided every 10 minutes between the LVCC and the Sands/Venetian, Thursday, Jan. 5, to Saturday, Jan. 7, 7:30 AM-6:30 PM, and Sunday, Jan. 8, 7:30 AM-4:30 PM.

    Sands/Venetian boarding location: Curbside on Sands Avenue
    LVCC boarding location: C2 entrance
