    LVCC, Grand Lobby

    Located at the center of the action, CTA Center showcases the programs and initiatives of the organization behind CES, the Consumer Technology Association (CTA)™.

    Learn About CTA and Membership

    Visit the CTA Center at CES 2017 to learn about the year-round benefits of CTA membership, join CTA's Innovation Movement, see the award-winning Best of Innovations product display, and follow CES trends on social at the social media command center.

    As the owner and producer of the world’s most important technology event, CTA supports the industry through legislative advocacy, market research, technical training and education, industry promotion, standards development, and the fostering of business and strategic relationships.

    Learn more about CTA. >


    CES 2017 CTA Stage Programming

    Don't miss the CTA Stage featuring live interviews with key industry guests, authors from Gary's Book Club and much more.

    We will also be selling a limited supply of CES-branded items.

    Watch the live stream of CTA Stage programming. >


    CTA Stage Schedule

    Thursday, Jan. 5

    11-11:30 AM — Jason Kelly, author of Sweat Equity

    12-12:30 PM — CTA Foundation: Interview with Jeff Lewis, Chief Accessibility Officer, AT&T
    Lewis will be talking about AT&T's work in accessibility for both customers and their employees.

    12:30-1 PM — CTA Foundation: Interview with KR Liu, Director of Accessibility & Advocacy, Doppler Labs
    Liu will be talking about innovations in affordable hearing technologies. 

    1-1:30 PM — CTA Foundation: Interview with Eric Reed, Vice President Entertainment & Tech Policy, Verizon Communications
    Reed will be talking about Verizon’s philanthropic work, including their support of Music & Memory and their STEM programs.

    2-2:30 PM — Dan Ackerman, author of The Tetris Effect: The Game That Hypnotized the World

    3-3:30 PM — CES Media Round Table

    3:30-4 PM — Rick Smith, author of The Great Disruption: Competing and Surviving in the Second Wave of the Industrial Revolution

    Friday, Jan. 6

    11-11:30 AM — Seth Shapiro, author of Television: Innovation, Disruption, and the World's Most Powerful Medium: Volume 1

    12-1:30 PM — CES Media Round Table
