CES Asia Celebrates a Second Successful Event in Shanghai

CES Asia returned to Shanghai for its second year this past May, surpassing the inaugural event by all measures and reinforcing the show as the premier platform for launching technology in the Asian marketplace. From budding startups to established global brands, 439 companies from 23 countries, regions and territories unveiled their latest innovations across a show floor that spanned 42,614 gross square meters (21,307 net square meters) of exhibit space. Pre-audit attendance numbers confirm the event welcomed more than 33,000 attendees, including more than 1,100 members of the media. CES Asia will return to Shanghai June 7-9, 2017. Learn more and reserve your booth today.

About Consumer Technology Association

The Consumer Technology Association (CTA)™ is the trade association representing the $287 billion U.S. consumer technology industry. More than 2,200 companies — 80 percent are small businesses and startups; others are among the world's best known brands — enjoy the benefits of CTA membership including policy advocacy, market research, technical education, industry promotion, standards development and the fostering of business and strategic relationships. The Consumer Technology Association also owns and produces CES — the world’s gathering place for all who thrive on the business of consumer technologies. Profits from CES are reinvested into CTA's industry services.
