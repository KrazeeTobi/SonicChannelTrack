Bring Prestige and Value to Your Exhibit

As you prepare your exhibits for CES, don’t forget the prospect of awards and events.

CES awards programs bring extra prestige and value to your exhibit, which in turn increases media coverage and recognition.

Hosting a celebrity appearance or special event in your exhibit or meeting space also garners attention to your company and products.

Celebrity Appearances and Special Events

Investment: FREE

If your company is planning a special event with a popular chart-topper, Hollywood royalty or sports legend at CES 2017, let us know. We can assist you with special security arrangements, staging requirements, catering requests or anything else you need to make your event a success.

If it's something open to all CES attendees, we’ll include your event details on CES.tech, in a news release, and in all press rooms on-site.
