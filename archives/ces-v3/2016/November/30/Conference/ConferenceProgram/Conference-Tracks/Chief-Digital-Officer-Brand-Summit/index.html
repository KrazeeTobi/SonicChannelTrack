The annual CDX Brand Summit brings together Fortune 500 Chief Digital Officers, brand innovation executives, agency executives and leading technology platforms and disruptors to discuss best practices and how they are driving marketing innovation, digital transformation, and engaging with the increasingly connected consumer.

Top Three Topics:
  • Digital Transformation — Connecting consumers across multiple platforms, a solution for a complex digital ecosystem, brand distribution, OTT strategies and technologie
  • Marketing Innovation — The Sharing Economy, challenges of Artificial Intelligence, smarter homes for everyone and capitalizing on 5G
  • Disruptive Technologies — Experts look at best practices driving innovation, digital transformation and the connected consumer
