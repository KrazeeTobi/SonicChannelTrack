TransformingEDU highlights the impact technology is making on education worldwide. From Pre-K through higher education and lifelong learning, TransformingEDU showcases the innovative solutions from both public and private institutions. Join us as we discover nanodegrees, credentialing, augmented reality, robotics, OERs, machine learning and the newest in online platforms transforming education.

Top Three Topics:
  • Learn about the “unbundling” of higher education as well as competency-based online education and high-stakes digital credentials
  • Robots, maker spaces, and gamification go mainstream thanks to ed tech innovation and adoption. Get your game on.
  • Experience how virtual and augmented are becoming the new realities for education, engaging students like never before
