Include Exhibitor PR Contact Information in Map Your Show

Investment: FREE

Make sure that the media interested in covering your company and products can find you. List your company’s PR contact within your CES 2017 directory listing. We pull a PR contact list once per month from August through January and share this list with registered CES media before, during and after the show.

We also use the PR contact list to distribute Exhibitor PR News, which is designed to keep you in the know and up-to-date on all PR programs and opportunities, important deadlines and breaking show news.

Newsletters will be distributed bi-monthly starting Wednesday, Aug. 3. 

To add or update your company’s PR contact information, log into your Exhibitor Dashboard. 

Contact Map Your Show (MYS) at 1-888-527-8823 for log-in assistance. 

Preregistered Media List

Investment: FREE

The preregistered media list contains contact information of all the media that have preregistered for CES 2017. For your convenience, the list will be available starting Thursday, Dec. 1, 2016*.

The list will be updated daily as the number of registered media grows and can be filtered by product category. Take full advantage of this feature to pull targeted media lists for your news releases and press event pitches.

To download the preregistered media list:

  1. Log into your Exhibitor Dashboard.
  2. Select the “Media Lists” tile in the bottom right corner of your portal.
  3. Select "CES 2017 Preregistered Media.” 

REMINDER: Target the media you need! Filter the list by product category, region, distribution format, preapproved and more. 

PLEASE NOTE: Media choose whether or not to list their phone and/or email contacts. Titles, company names and addresses are automatically incorporated. If no phone number or email address is provided, CES cannot release that information due to our privacy policy.

*Exhibitors hosting an official news conference at CES 2017 will have access to the preregistered media list one month early. The list will be emailed to these exhibitors as an excel spreadsheet on Tuesday, Nov. 1.

Exhibitor Media Kits

Investment: FREE hosting in CES media rooms

CES 2017 will feature three media rooms on site at Mandalay Bay, the Sands and the LVCC, all with paperless exhibitor media kit areas.

Exhibitors are welcome to supply their media kits on USBs, CDs or host on their own URL with an accompanying one-page spec sheet (8.5" x 11" max) that lists on-site media contacts and the URL where the electronic press kit is located. Printed press materials greater than one page in length should be stored in the company’s exhibit.

Make sure your media kit is marked clearly with your company’s name, CES show venue and exhibit number. Media kits are a one-stop source for the media to get information about your company and products. Information included in kits should be kept simple and to the point — journalists are after hard news.

View our CES 2017 Media Kit PDF for more information about what to include in your media kit and where to mail it for on-site distribution.

Questions? Contact the CES PR team.

Join CTA's Communications Committee

The Consumer Technology Association (CTA)™ Communications Committee provides guidance to CTA's communications department on the design and implementation of the association’s public relations, media outreach, public affairs, consumer education, promotional campaigns, and related activities in support of the consumer technology industry, CTA, CES® and other CTA-events.

The Communications Committee consists of member company senior executives responsible for public relations, public policy, marketing and media relations within their respective companies.

If your company is interested in joining the Communications Committee or learning more, please fill out our Communications Committee Participation Form.
