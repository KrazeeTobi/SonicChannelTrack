    Request a group block for CES 2017 with a simple three-step process.

    1. If you require 20 rooms or more, use this form to submit your request to an official CES hotel. Note: Select hotels are accepting requests for 10** or more rooms as noted below.
    2. The hotel will respond to you within three business days regarding rates and availability.
    3. The hotel will give you a deadline date as to when you must commit to these rooms and sign a contract with them.
