    presented by Barnes & Noble

    • Gary's Book Club
    Share This Print This Page

    Location: LVCC, Grand Lobby

    Gary's Book Club showcases the works of prominent authors in the tech industry.

    Attendees have the opportunity to meet the authors, purchase books through Barnes & Noble and have their copies signed.

    Book signings at the Gary’s Book Club Booth will follow dynamic discussions by the authors on the Consumer Technology Association Stage.

    Check back soon to see the authors to be featured at CES 2017.

    Video: Watch Presentations from CES 2016

    Author Testimonials

    "The program is terrific. I'll be focused on extending the reach of that interview by adding it to social media"  

    — Aneesh Chopra, Author of Innovate State. Former (and first) U.S. Chief Technology Officer.
    Currently the cofounder and executive vice president of Hunch Analytics.


    "I had a great time at CES and really appreciate being selected for Gary’s Book Club. This was the perfect way to finish a 200+ event book tour!"  

    — Alexis Ohanian, Author of Without Their Permission.
    Cofounder of the social news website Reddit,
    helped launch travel search website Hipmuck and started social enterprise Breadpig.


    "I enjoyed getting to talk with lots of startup entrepreneurs about the things they were trying to accomplish. The exposure I got was to exactly the right audience."

    — Paul Paetz, Author of Disruption by Design.
    CEO of Innovative Disruption, a boutique consultancy that works with innovators
    to bring disruptive products to fruition and market success.
