    Meet Aisha Tyler

    Actor, director, author and comedian Aisha Tyler was named the CES Ambassador for 2017.

    As an avid gamer and tech enthusiast, Tyler leverages the power of technology and content.

    Aisha Tyler defines the term multi-hyphenate, by working simultaneously in multiple media formats. She is host of CBS' Emmy-winning The Talk, a series regular on CBS' Criminal Minds, the voice of superspy Lana Kane in FX's Emmy-winning Archer and host the CW's comedy improv show Whose Line Is It Anyway?
