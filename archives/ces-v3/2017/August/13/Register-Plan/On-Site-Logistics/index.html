    ADA Services

    The Consumer Technology Association is committed to ensuring that the show is fully accessible for persons with disabilities.

    Accessibility Services Desks

    • LVCC, Grand Lobby, upper level staircase near Room N250

    • Sands, Level 1, Lobby

    To arrange accessibility services on-site, indicate your needs during registration and a member of our team will be in touch.

    Scooters, Wheelchairs and Segways
    Scooters and wheelchairs are available for rent at the LVCC and Sands/Venetian. Segways are permitted at these venues for ADA use only. Segways are not permitted at ARIA, Mandalay Bay or Westgate.

    To rent motorized or non-motorized wheelchairs, contact Scootaround at 888-441-7575.


    Parking
    ADA parking is available on a first-come, first-served basis in the LVCC Diamond Lot and at all other official CES venues. See the shuttle section for ADA-accessible buses.

    Shuttles
    We provide ADA accessible buses for those with special needs from official CES hotels and parking lots during hours of operation.

    To arrange for your transportation on-site, please contact RPMs, the official CES transportation vendor, at 877-745-3398 or CESinfo@rp-ms.com.

    Please request service at least 20 minutes in advance of the desired pick-up time. Note this phone number is operational 30 minutes before and after the daily shuttle schedule.

    Business Centers

    Business Centers are located at the following locations:

    • ARIA: Business Center, Conference Center, Level 2, 702-590-9750
    • LVCC: FedEx Office, Grand Lobby Concourse and front of South Hall 1, 702-733-2898
    • Sands: Business Center, Level 1 Lobby, 702-733-5070
    • Venetian: FedEx Office, Level 2, across from Bellini 2006, 702-836-4400
    • Westgate: FedEx Office, 702-862-4104

    Coat and Bag Check

    Coat and bag check is available at the following locations, for a fee:

    • LVCC: South Hall 1 Lobby, next to Starbucks
    • LVCC: Central Plaza
    • LVCC: Central Concourse across from C3 entrance
    • LVCC: South Hall 2 Lobby (Cash only at this location)
    • Venetian: Foyer next to Venetian Ballroom D
    • Westgate: Bell Desk

    Cameras

    Attendees are permitted to bring camera equipment onto the show floor. Exhibitors retain the right to restrict photography of their products or displays and such decisions are within the discretion of the exhibitor and are not controlled by CES.

    Emergency Numbers at CES Show Venues - Please Do Not Dial 9-1-1 

    • ARIA: 702-590-7911 or dial 77911 in hotel
    • LVCC: 702-892-7400 or dial 7400 in LVCC
    • Mandalay Bay: 702-632-7989 or dial "0" in hotel
    • Renaissance: 702-784-5700 or dial "0" in hotel
    • Sands: 702-733-5195 or dial 75195 in Sands
    • Venetian: 702-414-9311 or dial 49311 in hotel
    • Westgate: 702-732-5414 or dial 5911 in hotel

    Personal Mobility

    Personal mobility devices (with or without motors) are not permitted at any CES venue. This includes hoverboards, skateboards, uniwheels and all similar products.

    Segways are permitted at the LVCC and Sands/Venetian for ADA use only. They are not permitted at any other show venue for any purpose. Please refer to ADA Services section if you require special assistance.

    Food


    LVCC: Check out Lucky’s Restaurant, Sbarro, Nathan's and Starbucks located in the Grand Lobby, or Aces Restaurant located in the South Hall 1 lobby. You can also find concessions areas throughout the exhibit halls and food trucks in Central Plaza. 

    Sands: ​Concessions areas are centrally located on the show floor in Halls A-D and Hall G.

    Venetian: Visit Café Presse or the Grand Canal Shops for a variety of options.

    Also check out options at Westgate, Renaissance and ARIA

    Mother's Suites

    Mother’s Suites (lactation pods) are available for the privacy and convenience of working mothers at CES. Each suite contains a deadbolt lock for privacy, a power outlet, a fold down table and two benches. Find Mother's Suites at these locations:

    • LVCC, N3 Lobby
    • South Hall Connector, near room S228
    • Venetian, Level 1, in the alcove outside the Casanova ballroom
