    Tuesday, Oct. 24, 2017
    2-7:30 PM*
    Palais Brongniart, Paris, France

    An expected 80 tech companies, including budding startups and global brands, from throughout France will showcase the latest transformative technologies at the 5th edition of CES Unveiled Paris.

    Learn trends, hear breaking CES news announcements and build relationships as you network with the more than 600 tech industry influencers and top-tier media.

    Event Schedule

    1-2 PM: Badge Pickup & On-site Registration

    2-3:30 PM: CTA Conference Programming

    • Speaker lineup to be announced late-September

    3:30-7:30 PM: Networking Reception & Tabletop Exhibition

    • Exhibitor list will be available late-September

    Interested in Attending?

    Calling all technology influencers, media, buyers and members of the financial community. Join us at CES Unveiled Paris.

    Build your global business network, form game-changing partnerships and witness tech innovation firsthand.

    Interested in Exhibiting and Branding Opportunities?

    Get your products and services in front of the tech industry elite. Whether you're a fledgling startup or recognized brand, CES Unveiled Paris is a must if you're looking to gain access to more than 175 media, 600 industry professionals and kick-start global buzz before CES.

    Contact us today to learn more or to secure your space.
    Ask us about exclusive sponsorship and branding opportunities!
