    ON-SITE BRANDING AND SPONSORSHIPS

    Banners (Exterior and Interior)

    Gain guaranteed exposure to 165K+ attendees and from influential media capturing footage of key locations.

    To learn more, call Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.

    Outdoor Promotions

    Space is available for promotional displays outside and in the lobby areas for static promotional products, such as custom inflatables, cars, interactive kiosks and more.

    For more information, contact Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.

    Sponsorships

    Choose from a variety of on-site, tangible attendee items and services to get your brand front-and-center: badge lanyards, badge coaster, show bags and charging stations.

    There are also brand opportunities for industry events, programs and show locations.

    Lounges & Meeting Spaces

    Sponsorship opportunities range from Consumer Technology Association (CTA)™ Member Lounges to the International Business Center.

    To learn more, call Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.

    Registration Greeters

    Corporate greeters welcome attendees in the LVCC's main registration area and distribute giveaways, product information or incentives to visit your exhibit.

    To learn more, call Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.

    Transportation Sponsorships

    Complimentary shuttle service between CES venues and more than 30 official hotels provides you with customized marketing opportunities.

    For more information, contact Jeff LoGioco at 401-294-0040.

    EXPORT OPPORTUNITIES

    Meet International Trade Specialists

    International Business Center
    LVCC, South Hall Connector, S224

    CES 2018 is a proud participant in the U.S. Department of Commerce’s International Buyer Program.

    Through the International Buyer Program, the U.S. Commercial Service is available at CES to assist qualified U.S. exhibitors and/or CTA Members interested in expanding their international sales. The program is recruiting international buyers to attend CES 2018 and will be connecting them to the U.S. exhibitors.

    Visit the International Business Center to meet with International Trade Specialists at CES 2018.

    Global Market Insight Program

    Thinking about exporting to a certain overseas market? Sign up for the Global Market Insight Program at CES 2018 for the opportunity to present your company's product and/or services in front of 25+ U.S. Embassy Commercial Specialists specializing in the information and communication technology sector in select overseas markets. One-on-One appointments for qualified U.S. exhibitors are available upon request.

    Exhibit at the International Matchmaking Reception

    The International Matchmaking Reception provides U.S. exhibitors the platform to showcase innovative products and services in front of hundreds of global tech professionals, including buyers and media.

    Increase your brand exposure and meet new customers by purchasing a tabletop display. Don't delay, space is limited.

    B2B Appointments with International Buyers

    Want to meet pre-screened international buyers attending CES 2018 as part of the delegations recruited by the International Buyer Program? Appointments are available at CES 2018 to qualified U.S. exhibitors or CTA Members.

    Official Directory Listing

    Complete your CES directory listing to be included in the online exhibitor directory and the print CES Official Show Directory.

    More information coming soon!

    Customer Invitations

    Give your valued clients and prospects up to $100,000 worth of free registration. Registration is free for up to 1,000 recipients, per exhibitor, during the free registration window and thereafter are valid for $100 off the $300 registration.

    More information coming in the fall!

    Registration Link on Your Site

    Promote your presence at CES by placing a link on your corporate website to registration for CES 2018 using this address: CES.tech/register

    Exhibitor Profile Distribution

    Receive free electronic distribution via Business Wire of a 100-word exhibitor profile to registered media and related audiences worldwide.

    CES Logo

    Leverage the power of your participation in CES 201 by including the official CES logo on your website, advertisements or promotional materials.

    CES Speaking Opportunities

    Share your knowledge and build your reputation as a leader in the consumer technology industry by speaking at a conference session.
