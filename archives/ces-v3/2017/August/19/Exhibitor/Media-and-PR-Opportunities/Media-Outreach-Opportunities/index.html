CONTACT INFORMATION

List Your PR Contact Information

Include your company’s PR contact within your CES 2018 directory listing, which is shared with registered CES media.

PR contacts also receive the Exhibitor PR News e-newsletter to stay on top of all PR programs and opportunities, important deadlines, and breaking show news.

Add or update your company’s PR contact information. >

For log-in assistance, contact Map Your Show at 1-888-527-8823. 

See a List of Registered Media

Available Dec. 1, 2018
Pull targeted media lists for your news releases and media event pitches. The lists will be updated daily, and you can filter by product category. 

If you are hosting an official news conference at CES, the media list will be emailed to you one month in advance. 

NOTE: Media choose whether to list a phone number or email. If none is listed, CES cannot release that information.
