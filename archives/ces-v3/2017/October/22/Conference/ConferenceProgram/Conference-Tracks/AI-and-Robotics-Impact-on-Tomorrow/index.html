AI and Robotics Impact on Tomorrow

presented by Consumer Technology Association

Artificial intelligence will soon equal human intelligence and businesses are banking on it as a profitable investment. Robots are part of our lives. What’s the future and what are the challenges?

Top Three Topics:

  • Companies are investing in AI research and development with an eye on profits
  • Robots — What are the challenges as they integrate into society?
  • How will AI and robotics impact life in the year 2020?

10:15-11:15 AM January 11

LVCC, North Hall, N258

Turning AI into Dollars

Companies are investing in AI research and development, looking at new ways to leverage these multi-purpose technologies and, ultimately, turn them into profit centers. Find out what’s being done in the labs and what’s on tap for the future.

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 11

LVCC, North Hall, N258

The Future of Robots at Work and Home

A look at the future of robotics and their role as companions and co-workers. What are the challenges as robots become integrated into society? Learn about advancements in AI, machine learning and the potential when paired with increasingly sophisticated robotics.

1-2 PM January 11

LVCC, North Hall, N258

Augmented Reality and Artificial Intelligence: A Disruptive Duo

Virtual reality, augmented reality and mixed reality. Tech evangelist Robert Scoble and Shel Israel, author of The Fourth Transformation: How Augmented Reality & Artificial Intelligence Will Change Everything, talk about moving from handsets to headsets and disruptive changes.

2:15-3:15 PM January 11

LVCC, North Hall, N258

Rise of Machines – Future of AI

Futurists have predicted that machines will match human intelligence in as few as 12 years. There will need to be many points of understanding to trust in AI systems and the decisions they make. Hear about the future of artificial intelligence and the universal standards that are needed to usher humans into this brave new world.
