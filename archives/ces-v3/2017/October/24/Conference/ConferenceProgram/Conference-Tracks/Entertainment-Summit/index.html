Entertainment Summit

presented by Variety

No Additional Fee Required

Technology is transforming the entertainment industry at an astonishing clip, creating huge choices in how to produce, monetize, market and distribute content for today’s increasingly digitally savvy audiences. Pioneers and innovators of the entertainment industry will share how they are taking risks and finding opportunities to succeed.

Top Three Topics: 

  • The future of TV
  • Stealing the show – Best in brand storytelling 
  • Let’s get personal – Power of custom engagement

9-9:40 AM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

The Future of TV

Chief multiplatform executives explore best business models and audience engagement strategies for various TV services, spanning pay TV, ad-supported OTT and subscription-based models.

9:40-10:10 AM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

Headliner Conversation

An interview with a top entertainment executive and/or celebrity personality.

10:10-10:30 AM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

Nissan CMO Conversation

An interview with a top entertainment executive and/or celebrity personality.

10:30-11:10 AM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

Stealing the Show – Best in Brand Storytelling

What entertainment and consumer brands are the most progressive and creative across new marketing opportunities in social, digital publishing, OTT and linear TV? Marketers creating the ultimate in storytelling around their brands converge to share their strategies.

11:10 AM-12 PM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

The New Age of Hitmaking

Brilliance can come from any source in today’s entertainment industry – podcast, social media-shared video, or a plain old TV show or film. Today’s hitmakers of the expanding platform universe will share their stories.
 

1-1:45 PM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

Let’s Get Personal: Power of Custom Engagement

Learn the latest strategies connecting personalized messaging to audiences. Can you create personalization at scale? What ad formats are breaking through on premium TV, short-form video and multiplatform brand integrations? Advancing analytics helps advertisers fine-tune and target desired audience segments.

1:45-2:15 PM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

Headliner Conversation

An interview with a top entertainment executive and/or celebrity personality.

2:15-3 PM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

The State of the Stream

Entertainment-on-demand has built a complex streaming economy, with a new group of media power players calling the shots. Business strategies vary wildly. User-generated content mixes with premium offerings across platforms. In a crowded field, the streaming elite debate what’s next.

3-3:45 PM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

We’re Going Live! Thrills with Experiential Tech

Advancing technology is enabling sports, concert and live events to offer truly spectacular experiences for fans. Learn how companies might introduce VR, extending social media engagement and ramping up in-venue interactivity during the game, concert or live production.

3:45-4:15 PM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

Headliner Conversation

An interview with a top entertainment executive and/or celebrity personality.

4:15-5 PM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Bristlecone 4

Entertainment Top Gear

Leading entertainment executive and creative talent give their take on hot trends coming out of CES.
