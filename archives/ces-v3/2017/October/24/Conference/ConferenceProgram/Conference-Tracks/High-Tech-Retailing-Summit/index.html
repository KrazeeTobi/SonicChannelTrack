presented by Living in Digital Times

Fear of becoming an endangered species looms large in the world of retail. Whether it’s online, in store, or a mixed channel experience, retailers are struggling with technology that will let them reach their customers. It’s a survival imperative that retailers find technology that will create long-term customer engagement.

Top Three Topics:

  • Experiential shopping meets high- tech
  • Big data = Big insights into a shopper’s brain
  • The life cycle of “smart” merchandise

1-1:30 PM January 11

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

Arming Yourself With the Facts

Debra Weinswig, Managing Director of Fung Global is joined by other industry analysts to offer a candid picture of the retail landscape and where technology can help.

  • 1-1:10PM -Welcome: Shopping is Personal
  • 1:10-1:30PM -Armed with Data

1:30-2 PM January 11

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

Shopping that Feels Like Magic

Check-out counters that know what’s in your cart, body scanners that reveal what flatters you, or test new skis on the slope without leaving the store. Now that’s magic.

2:15-3:15 PM January 11

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

Case Studies: Rewiring Retail for the Digital World

We’ll examine case studies and exercises for approaching the new retail paradigm. Whether you’re a mom and pop shop, a big box store, or an online delivery service, we’ll show you how to make “Going Digital" part of your DNA.

  • 2:15-2:40PM -Try Before You Buy
  • 2:40-3:15PM -Selling High Tech with High Tech

3:30-4:15 PM January 11

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

Pathways to Retail

Retail is thriving for those willing to experiment and test assumptions. Retailers and product creators need to be comfortable working with startups, technology, experimentation, and speed.

4:15-5 PM January 11

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

Scaling Retail Tech Vertically and Horizontally

Behind the curtain there’s some very powerful tech informing everything from loyalty programs to inventory and tracking. Learn how  data analytics, artificial intelligence, and IoT are being infused into the retail business and every part of a product and customer’s lifecycle.

  • 4:15-4:40PM -Adding Emotion to the Retail Equation
  • 4:40-5PM -Creating Your Personalized Retail Plan
