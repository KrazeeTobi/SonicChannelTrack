ICCE (International Conference on Consumer Electronics)

presented by Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE)

IEEE International Conference on Consumer Electronics (ICCE) 2018 is the flagship conference of IEEE CE society. ICCE is where the underlying research that will appear in tomorrow's consumer electronics technologies is first presented. The theme of ICCE 2018 is "Cybersecurity".
