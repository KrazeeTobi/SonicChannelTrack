VR|AR|MR: Digital Reality Delivers ROI

sponsored by Deloitte

No Registration Fee Required

Today, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and Mixed Reality implementations are delivering billion dollar efficiencies for industries including technology, retail, travel, and automotive. Hear groundbreaking results from our clients, market leaders, and luminaries around actual case studies where Digital Reality transformed corporate processes and yielded high value return on investment.

​Top Three Topics:

  • VR|AR|MR market enablers and accelerators disrupting the enterprise and driving consumer adoption
  • Hardware, software, platform, apps: VR|AR|MR advancements pushing the boundaries on what is possible for the enterprise
  • Industry leaders speak to their own Digital Reality innovations and associated value or ROI

1-2 PM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N260

Trendsetters for 2018

A look back at 2017 expectations: which ones were met and which ones fell short for Digital Reality (VR/AR/MR)? Looking ahead to 2018, what accelerators and enablers in the marketplace need to be developed to allow even greater success?
 

2:15-3:15 PM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N260

Breakout Technologies for 2018

Hear from leading hardware, software, platform and app companies that are taking Digital Reality to a higher echelon. Digital Reality form factors will push the boundaries on what is possible; these technologies are reshaping how work gets done across enterprise.

3:30-4:30 PM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N260

Delivering ROI with Digital Reality

Virtual, Augmented, and Mixed Reality implementations are delivering billion dollar efficiencies for industries including all industries.  Deloitte’s clients will speak to metrics achieved in actual case studies where Digital Reality transformed their processes and yielded high value return on investment.
