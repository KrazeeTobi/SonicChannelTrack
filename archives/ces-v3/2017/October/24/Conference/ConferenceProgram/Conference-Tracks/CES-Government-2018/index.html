CES Government 2018

presented by the Government Business Executive Forum (GBEF)

Join us for “The Future: How We Govern & Live,” an exclusive government technology summit. Hear top government, critical infrastructure and tech leaders discuss machine learning, advanced analytics, artificial intelligence and security — focusing on transportation, national security, defense, law enforcement, homeland security and healthcare. By invitation only. Visit CESgovernment.com for more information.

Top Three Topics:

  • Technology and innovation
  • Advanced analytics 
  • Artificial Intelligence and security
