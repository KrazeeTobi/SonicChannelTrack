Learn the status of 5G as it moves from the lab to the market with an expected roll out date of 2020. With 5G comes new services across the technology world. Hear predictions on the implementation and difficulties. IoT is the great connector and opportunities abound. Experts discuss the realities.

Top Three Topics: 

  • Moving 5G out of the lab — Lessons and predictions
  • Optimizing the 5G network and watching out for roadblocks 
  • IoT connects everything providing vital information. Hear about the reality and the possibilities.

10:15-11:15 AM January 8

LVCC, North Hall, N261

Status Report: Where Will 5G be in 2020?

5G technology is moving out of the lab and into the marketplace with trials being conducted across the country. Hear the latest on lessons learned and predictions on when we will see full implementation.

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 8

LVCC, North Hall, N261

Connected Devices Connecting Everything

IOT is at the center of connectivity, and the devices give us control over many aspects of our lives, help quantify energy savings and provide intelligent insights for business.  Hear from industry leaders who will discuss the value of connected devices.

1-2 PM January 8

LVCC, North Hall, N261

Three Industries Transformed by 5G

5G technology is moving out of the lab and into the marketplace, innovating industries from healthcare to education to sports. Join experts leading the charge to harness the potential of 5G, and delve into cutting edge case studies of how 5G implementation is changing the game.

2:15-3:15 PM January 8

LVCC, North Hall, N261

The New Normal in Smartphones

Bendable displays, face recognition, bezel-less and button-less screens:  each new smartphone launch promises a user interface to outdo the last. Experts will delve into the latest smartphone trends and lend vision to future phones.
