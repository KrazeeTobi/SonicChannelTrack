Sports Business Innovation

presented by Turner Sports

Sports Business Innovation (SBI) presented by Turner Sports is a convening of leading executives and influencers with a focus on ways technology is disrupting and accelerating the business of sport. The multi-day program will emphasis on areas such as diversity and empowerment, sport for social change and community development, along with opportunities and challenges facing content owners and distributors.

Top Three Topics:

  • Accelerating opportunities for members of underrepresented communities and identifying the next generation of stars
  • Fragmented content landscape and the shift in distribution and content platforms
  • Player-generated content and the consumer need for behind-the-scenes access

10-11 AM January 10

Sands, Level 2, Booth #45222

Impact of Diversity: Reshaping the Future

Moving the needle on diversity in tech and sports – behavioral change, transparency, accountability, recruitment and growth

11:15 AM-12:15 PM January 10

Sands, Level 2, Booth #45222

Social Impact of Sports

How athletes, teams, and leagues are best leveraging their assets with technology to drive social and community change

10-10:45 AM January 11

Sands, Level 2, Booth #45222

Evolving the Content Ecosystem

As content consumption and distribution continues to rapidly evolve, industry leaders will discuss the opportunities and challenges across new forms of media, rights buyers and player-generated content.

11-11:45 AM January 11

Sands, Level 2, Booth #45222

Alternative Platforms, Innovation & Monetization

A dynamic look at next generation content creation, the immediacy factor associated with it, along with the flexibility to extend it across an ever-growing number of platforms

12-12:45 PM January 11

Sands, Level 2, Booth #45222

Unprecedented Access: View of the Fan

With an insatiable appetite for more content delivered in real time, sports fans have greater access to athletes than ever before.  Our Players Only crew will discuss access and what today’s athletes can provide fans as they engage with them in new and unique ways.
