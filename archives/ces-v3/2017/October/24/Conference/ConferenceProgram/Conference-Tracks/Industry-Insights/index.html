Industry Insights

presented by Consumer Technology Association

No Additional Fee Required

Help your company create new business and drive innovation by reimagining your business model. Technology is changing the workplace, the work we do and creating opportunities. Get an in-depth look at the upheaval.  Discover why Hong Kong is the gateway to disruptive innovative technology. Connectivity demands and opportunities.

Top Three Topics:

  • Use new business models to drive innovation, business and profits
  • Technology is changing the landscape of the workplace
  • Hong Kong — gateway to innovative disruptive technology

9-10 AM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N253

Let's Go Humans: Celebrating Life-Changing Tech

Let’s Go Humans celebrates the power of human ingenuity. This session highlights the best and brightest and their vital "tech for good" missions. Hear first-hand from innovators creating life-changing tech, and those helped by it.

10:15-11:15 AM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N253

Connected Ecosystem of the Future

Our lives are more connected than ever and demands on the networks continue to increase. How about the future? How will the roles of ecosystem players - consumers, product developers and service providers - evolve? Join Deloitte to explore the possibilities.

10:15-11:15 AM January 10

LVCC, North Hall, N253

Views from the Workplace

Technology advancements are bringing upheaval to the workplace as we know it. While creating new opportunities, there are also implications of what the jobs of the future will be. Technology is augmenting today’s workplace and reshaping the work we do.

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 10

LVCC, North Hall, N253

Fly High in Asia

Asia is advancing on global innovation with China as a key driver and Hong Kong as the gateway to Asia.  It’s a pivotal innovation hub for disruptive technologies such as AR/VR, drones, blockchain technologies and humanlike robots.  Learn how technology pioneers can exploit Hong Kong to succeed in Asia.
