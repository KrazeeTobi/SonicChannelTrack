presented by Living in Digital Times

Monetary transactions are based on trust, and digital currency is no different. We’ll have to put trust in mobile payments, the Internet of Paying Things, robo-advisors, cryptocurrencies, and peer-to-peer exchange as we democratize currency and go cashless. Fintech’s challenges are harder than ever, but the rewards are just as great.

Top Three Topics:

  • New Links in the blockchain
  • IoT and commerce: humans optional
  • The rise of robo-advisors

9-11:15 AM January 9

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

The New World of Digital Payments

Keep up with the latest trends in fintech, how automation and artificial intelligence are changing the game, and what you can do to keep funds and customer data safe while you grow and experiment.

  • 9:00-9:15AM -In Money We Trust, Michael Casey
  • 9:15-9:45AM -Payments Deconstructed
  • 9:45-10:30AM -Never Talk to a Human Again
  • 10:30-11:15AM -Cybersecurity and Privacy: Keeping Commerce Safe

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 9

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

Who’s on Top: Leaders in the Movement

A look at the companies, nations, and minds at the forefront of innovation and adoption in digital payments.

1-2:15 PM January 9

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

Personalizing Transactions

From the person who’s never banked to the one who’s saved for decades, from the first home buyer to the in-debt college graduate, how can tech help expand the ranks of the financially stable?

  • 1:00-1:45PM -Personalizing Transaction
  • 1:45-2:15PM -Governor Tim Pawlenty on Fintech and Tradition

2:15-3:15 PM January 9

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

Cryptocurrency: "Game of Coins”

After years of doubt and derision from the mainstream finance community, cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum, and hundreds more, have become fodder for cocktail party chatter. Get an insider’s look at how to evaluate them all.

  • 2:15-2:45PM -Clash of Coins
  • 2:45-3:15PM -Ready to Invest?

3:30-5 PM January 9

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

The Future of Blockchain

It’s obvious that blockchain technology is here to stay, and we’re just beginning to see its potential to transform the world. Explore the possibilities with true blockchain innovators, and hear from some companies hoping to be the next big thing.

  • 3:30-4:15PM -100 Uses for the Blockchain
  • 4:15-5:00PM -ICO SlugFest: Startup Competition
