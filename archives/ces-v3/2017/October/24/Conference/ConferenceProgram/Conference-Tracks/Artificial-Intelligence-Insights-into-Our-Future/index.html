Artificial Intelligence: Insights into Our Future

presented by Robotic Trends

$ Additional Registration Required

$ Additional Registration Required

$ Additional Registration Required

Track purchases are handled within the registration process.

This track examines recent technology developments, current applications, and concerns about artificial intelligence. Hear leading AI experts discuss the reality behind the hype and how AI will impact our everyday lives.

Top Three Topics:

  • Case studies on how AI will be applied in different industries in 2018
  • Understanding the promise and limitations of AI technologies and applications
  • Analyzing the economic, ethical, and societal concerns around AI

9-10 AM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N260

The State of Artificial Intelligence

This session will examine the state of artificial intelligence in 2018. We’ll discuss leading AI breakthroughs from various industries, where the technology is headed, and challenges that still need to be overcome.

10:15-11:15 AM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N260

Designing AI-Powered Robots

Social and service robots that understand and interact more naturally with humans rely on advanced artificial intelligence. This session will demonstrate how artificial intelligence is helping build smarter robots that will be more user-friendly.

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N260

How AI is Changing the World

Attempts to commercialize AI are proceeding worldwide. Hear from global players on their efforts, regionalized approaches and applications, the impact on global economies, and how to prepare for the biggest transformation in human history.
