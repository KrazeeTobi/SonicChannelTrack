Smart Home

presented by Consumer Technology Association

Consumer behavior has had a driving impact on smart home trends. Learn which consumers have the most influence in the smart home technology, how voice activiation, smart speakers and other applications are changing the way we live, and as our homes become more connected,  learn what is being done in network security to keep us safe.

Top Three Topics: 

  • Smart home trends and purchase patterns 
  • Consumer behavior and smart speakers 
  • Smart home network security 

10:15-11:15 AM January 10

LVCC, North Hall, N256

Smart Homes: Bringing Tech to the Cul-de-sac

Good Housekeeping will lead a discussion and share insights on smart home trends, purchase and behavioral habits. Panelists will probe the evolving connected home space including the influence of women and the increasing benefits that smart home products offer.

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 10

LVCC, North Hall, N256

The Appeal of Voice Activated and Smart Speakers

Smart speakers are dramatically affecting consumers’ daily lives. A new study provides an in-depth look at consumer behavior and attitudes in this fast- growing market.

1-2 PM January 10

LVCC, North Hall, N256

Living Independently in Smart Homes

The growing populations of older adults and people with disabilities represent markets that benefit from smart home technologies. Learn the benefits and challenges of accessible smart home technologies which keep people living independently at home.

2:15-3:15 PM January 10

LVCC, North Hall, N256

Securing the Smart Home

Industry experts will explore and analyze the latest trends in smart home technology ranging from devices to infrastructure. Discover the innovations helping keep the network secure.
