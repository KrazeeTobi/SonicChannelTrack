    Request a group block for CES 2018 with a simple three-step process.

    1. If you require 20 rooms or more, use this form to submit your request to an official CES hotel. 

    2. The hotel will respond to you within three business days regarding rates and availability.

    3. The hotel will give you a deadline date as to when you must commit to these rooms and sign a contract with them.

    This form enables you to check rates and availability at the properties you select. You are under no obligation to contract room blocks based on this request.
