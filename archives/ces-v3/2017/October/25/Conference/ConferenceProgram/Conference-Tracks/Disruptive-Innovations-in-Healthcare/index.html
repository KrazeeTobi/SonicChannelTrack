Healthcare is a trillion-dollar business and a critical issue for consumers. In these sessions, you’ll learn how technology is playing a critical role in today’s healthcare access and delivery and how high-tech is transforming preventative and personal healthcare and playing a role in limiting the use of pharmaceuticals.

Top Three Topics:

  • Global healthcare technology. Access and delivery and how it’s changing health and well-being worldwide
  • Personal health and the role of technology  
  • Pharmaceuticals. Can technology help replace them?

9-9:45 AM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N261

Digital Therapeutics Research Presentation

Presentation of key findings from the CTA-PCHA digital therapeutics research report.

10:15-11:15 AM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N261

Predictive Analytics and Software as a Medical Device

Predictive analytics and software are improving patient care, driving positive behavior change and leading to better management of chronic diseases. Explore the positive implications of predictive analytics and software as a medical device (SaMD) as well as the regulatory challenges associated with implementation into the healthcare system.

11:30 AM-12:15 PM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N261

The Positive Future of Value Based Healthcare

Insurance providers address how technology is driving value based healthcare, what technology payers are willing to cover and how this impacts the overall healthcare insurance ecosystem. How do payers evaluate the effectiveness of technology programs and roll them out?

1-2 PM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N261

The Digital Rx – How Technology is Changing Pharmaceuticals

Technology companies will discuss how digital therapeutics can replace pharmaceuticals,  drive greater patient engagement, and lead to healthier behaviors, all while bending the healthcare cost curve in America.

2:15-2:45 PM January 9

LVCC, North Hall, N261

Please Disrupt the Doctor

Healthcare providers will discuss how digital therapeutics are disrupting traditional chronic care management and disease prevention. Speakers will share clinical case studies and other real world examples of digital therapeutics in practice.
