Wearable Technology Summit

presented by Living in Digital Times

$ Additional Registration Required

$ Additional Registration Required

$ Additional Registration Required

If it’s strapped, inserted or attached to your body, uses Bluetooth, the cloud, and the internet there’s a good chance it’s a wearable. Glasses, smart watches, smart jewelry, fitness trackers, wearable cameras, on skin sensors, sleep caps and “chipping.” We’ll show what’s new, what’s working and what’s on the horizon.

Top Three Topics:

  • Everybody’s talking wearables, but who’s buying?
  • Wearables in the workforce
  • Skin, ears, implants: Where will they put wearables next?

9-10:15 AM January 11

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

The State of the Wearable

On your wrist, in your hair, on your clothing, in your skin, on your shoes, in your hat - everyday things are turning into a high-tech wearable.

  • 9:00-9:15AM -Welcome
  • 9:15-9:45AM -The Wearable as a Fashion Statement
  • 9:45-10:15AM -Functional Wearables

10:15-11:15 AM January 11

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

The Wearable Workforce

The workplace may be the golden goose for wearables. ABI research forecasts enterprise wearable device revenue will top $60 billion in 2022. We’ll look at safety, training, tracking, and more.

  • 10:15-10:45AM -Wearable Safeguards
  • 10:45-11:15AM -The Wearable Assistant

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 11

Venetian, Level 4, Lando, 4302

The Smartest Wearables in the Room

Just around the corner are wearables so intuitive that they learn over time and devices so smart they can save a life. Bio and e-textiles, wearables with bionic power,  we’ll showcase the future stars of this life-altering revolution.

  • 11:30-11:45AM -2018’s Top Five Wearable Trends
  • 11:45AM-12:10PM -Textiles of the Future
  • 12:10-12:30PM -From “Me” to “We”: The Community of Wearables
