One Club, Endless Connections

The Executive Club is an invitation-only program offering high-level industry insiders unique access to CES. The club allows you to be among the first to discover disruptive new technologies while connecting with peers and other influencers who share the same interests in products and companies, content and innovation.

From buyers in key retail organizations, to entertainment executives, CMOs and beyond, the Executive Club provides an opportunity to make new contacts and discover new international distribution platforms and channels.

Executive Club Levels

The Executive Club is one robust program encompassing what were previously known as CE Platinum Club, Red Carpet Club, CMO Club and VIP Buyer Program — Gold and Silver levels.

These programs have merged into the Executive Club program, with each group represented by a specific level of membership within the Club.

Platinum Preferred Level (previously known as CE Platinum Club): For questions or membership consideration contact PlatinumPreferred@CTA.tech or 703-907-5246.

Platinum Level (previously known as the Red Carpet and CMO Clubs): For questions or membership consideration contact ExecutiveClub@CTA.tech or 703-907-5206. Learn more about Executive Club Platinum. >

Gold Level (previously known as VIP Buyer Gold): For questions or membership consideration contact Gold@CTA.tech or 703-907-5286.

Silver Level (previously known as VIP Buyer Silver): For questions or membership consideration contact Silver@CTA.tech or 703-907-5286.

Executive Club Benefits

All members of the Executive Club receive the following benefits at CES:

  • 25 percent discount toward the purchase of any conference pass
  • Keynote VIP seating tickets
  • Access to exclusive hotel blocks
  • Plus additional benefits unique to each level of membership

Access Your Executive Club Benefits and Register

Executive Club membership is invitation-only and nontransferable. Members received notification of their status earlier this fall.

To accept your Executive Club Membership and register for CES in one simple step, access your registration using the unique, personalized URL sent to you previously via email.

Or if you’ve already registered for CES, simply log back into your account, select the benefits tab and accept your Executive Club benefits.

New Membership Consideration

Executive Club membership is invitation-only. New membership requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

To be considered for Executive Club membership, please reach out to the appropriate level contact listed above. Please include your full name, title, company and a link to your corporate website with your request.
