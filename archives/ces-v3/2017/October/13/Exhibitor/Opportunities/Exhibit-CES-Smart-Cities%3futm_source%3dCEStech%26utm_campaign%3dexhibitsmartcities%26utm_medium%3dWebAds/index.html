Global spending on smart cities is projected to reach $34.35 billion by 2020.* B2B, B2C and B2G players investing in smart cities have an unprecedented market opportunity.

CES® already showcases leading-edge smart city innovation and IoT adoption. And with the launch of CES Smart Cities, it’s the only show that brings together all of the technologies, solutions and audiences in the smart city sector.

Contact Us to Exhibit or Sponsor


Audience Insights

At CES 2017, almost 25 percent of attendees were representatives from buying organizations, and more than 67,000 attendees were senior-level executives.

To attract local and state municipalities and federal agencies with a vested interest in smart cities, the exhibit area will host a visionary conference program.

The three-day conference program, presented by the Consumer Technology Association (CTA)™,  is aimed at drawing key decision makers, influencers, buyers and investors. Conference topics, with content and speakers from Bosch, FirstNet, Ford, Nissan and Qualcomm, will include:

  • Mayors and state governors
  • Federal government representatives for key agencies (e.g., HUD and DOE)
  • Law enforcement and first responders
  • Utility and energy companies
  • Transportation companies
  • Financial institutions
  • City planners and city managers

Expose your leading-edge technologies to these core prospects and customers.


Exhibiting and Sponsorship Opportunities

Become a Premium Partner

Leading players in the Smart Cities arena can be among four premiere partners with CES Smart Cities.
Partners will create a showcase centered on a leading Smart Cities initiatives:

  • Smart transportation
  • Smart cities and venues
  • Smart energy and the smart grid
  • Public safety, utilities and health care
  • Data, analytics and security


Partners will receive:

  • Premium location in the CES Smart Cities exhibition space.
  • Extensive branding
    • On-site including highly-visible column wraps, banners and signage
    • In preshow promotions through CTA’s digital properties and social media channels
    • In key attendee tools and guides, including the CES App, CES Show Guide and CES Show Daily 
  • Exposure to global media
    • Before the show at CES Unveiled events in Amsterdam, Paris and New York
    • Before and during the show via media tours, press releases and media briefings
