    Registration for CES 2018 opens Sept. 6, 2017.

    We are committed to a secure and safe CES experience, and the following guidelines can help you have a smooth registration process.

    A Few Special Registration Requirements

    • Date of birth: In line with guidance from federal law enforcement and industry best practices, we are requiring date of birth (DOB) for all CES registrants.
    • Unique email address: New for CES 2018, we will be requiring a unique email address for each registrant.
    • Badge photo: ​CES badges will now feature a photo ID.


    Exhibitor Registration Guidelines

    All exhibitor personnel will be required to provide a unique email address.

    Unique email addresses will allow your exhibitor personnel to access their own record and provide their date of birth and other secure personal details.

    You (or your exhibitor personnel coordinator) can continue to manage the exhibitor personnel badge allocation, and you will simply enter the full name and unique email address for each individual.

    Individual exhibitor personnel will then receive an email notification to log in to complete their record.

    Badge photos are an optional add-on for exhibitors.
