    CES. One show, three exhibit experiences, countless opportunities. Where do you fit in?

    Organized by market-specific product categories and Marketplaces, our official show venues host companies spanning the entire consumer technology ecosystem. Secure an opportunity in one or more of these official locations.


    Tech East

    Las Vegas Convention and World Trade Center (LVCC), Westgate Las Vegas (Westgate), Renaissance Las Vegas (Renaissance)

    Where innovations in audio, automotive electronics, gaming, video, wireless devices, wireless services, digital imaging and photography, or anything "i" comes to market.

    Join the powerhouse brands and technologies of today come as they come to life on the show floor.


    Tech West

    Sands Expo/The Venetian (Sands/Venetian), The Palazzo, Wynn Las Vegas and Encore at Wynn (Wynn/Encore)

    Featuring the innovative power behind the industry’s emerging technology, Tech West encompasses revolutions in fitness and health, the Internet of Things, sensors and other high-growth technologies changing the way we live, work and play.

    You’ll exhibit alongside the titans of tech, promising startups and everything in between.


    Tech South

    ARIA, Park MGM and Vdara

    This location hosts a variety of C Space activities, including conference sessions, networking events, exhibits and meeting rooms.

    See a map of locations. >


    CES Product Categories and Locations

    To help the more than 160,000 expected attendees find you, the show floor groups like-technologies by product categories and Marketplaces.

    Your exhibit location is based on the products your company exhibits.

    Product categories represent the ever-changing ecosystem of consumer technology. Each category is centered in a specific hall or area — with a handful having primary and secondary locations — and others receiving more recognition as one of our 20 Marketplaces.

    Marketplaces focus on up-and-coming technologies considered high-growth areas, receiving amplified promotion and garnering wide-ranging attention from our more than 160,000 attendees and 6,500 world-wide media.
