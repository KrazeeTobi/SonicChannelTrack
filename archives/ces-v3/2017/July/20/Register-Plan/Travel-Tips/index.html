Planning Your Stay

  • CES features more than 2.4 million net sq. ft. of exhibit space. You will be walking a lot, so make sure to wear comfortable shoes.
  • Las Vegas can be cool and rainy in the winter, so pack a warm jacket and umbrella.
  • Despite the potential for rain, the Las Vegas air is very dry. Drink plenty of water and think about requesting a humidifier at your hotel.
  • Review your health plan in case you need to see a doctor during your stay. Think about packing cough and flu medicine as a precaution.
