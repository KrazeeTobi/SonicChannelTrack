    The CES 2018 Innovation Awards Call for Entries is now CLOSED.

    Entry Fees

      CTA Members Nonmembers
    Exhibiting $475 $800
    Non-Exhibiting $950 $1,300

    If you are not a CES exhibitor and/or CTA member at the time that you submit your application, you can become an official exhibitor/member and receive a partial refund up until Oct. 6, 2017. After Oct. 6, we are unable to issue partial refunds.

    Contact us at InnovationAwards@CTA.tech for more information on receiving a refund.

    You may submit the same product in multiple categories, but there is a separate entry fee for each category.

    Honoree Selection Process

    A three-member judging team evaluates each product on a number of criteria.

    Learn more about the award criteria. >

    Honorees Notified: Oct. 6, 2017

    You will be notified by email of your award status.

    Honorees Announced: Oct. 25 (French only) and Nov. 9, 2017 

    French Honoree companies will have their awards announced at CES Unveiled Paris on Oct. 25, 2017.

    Unless you indicate otherwise on your application, all other Honorees will be announced at CES Unveiled New York, Nov. 9, 2017, and listed on the Innovation Awards Honoree page.
