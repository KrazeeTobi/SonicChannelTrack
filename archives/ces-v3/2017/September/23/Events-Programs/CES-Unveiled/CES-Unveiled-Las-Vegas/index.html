    Sunday, Jan. 7, 2018
    Las Vegas, Nev.

    CES Unveiled Las Vegas is the official media event of CES, welcoming press and industry analysts from more 150 countries, and it takes place before the start of CES.

    Whether you're an innovative startup or an established global brand, this event is your chance to get ahead of the game, stand out from the show buzz and break your news early to journalists there specifically to tout the best that CES has to offer.

    This annual press event draws more than 1,500 influential media from around the world and will feature the CES 2018 Best of Innovation Awards Honorees and tabletop displays from more than 100 local and global tech companies.
