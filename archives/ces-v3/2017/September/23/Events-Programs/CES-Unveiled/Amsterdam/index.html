    Thursday, Oct. 26, 2017
    1:30 PM-5:30 PM*
    Beurs van Berlage, Amsterdam, Netherlands

    The best technology from Netherlands and the surrounding region will be on display at the inaugural CES Unveiled Amsterdam.

    Feel the future and see what's next along with more than 300 prominent European media and tech industry influencers.

    The half-day industry event will kick off with global tech trends and breaking CES news from CTA executives and industry experts. Up to 25 local and global tech companies from the Netherlands and surrounding countries will be showcasing their groundbreaking technology during the tabletop exhibition and networking reception.

    Event Schedule

    12:30-1:30 PM: Badge Pickup & On-site Registration

    1:30-3:30 PM: CTA Conference Programming

    • Speaker lineup to be announced late-September

    3:30-5:30 PM: Networking Reception & Tabletop Exhibition

    • Exhibitor list will be available late-September

    Interested in Attending?

    Whether you're a member of the media covering the latest and greatest, a venture capitalist or finance/investment professional hoping to spot the next gizmo poised to revolutionize the industry, or a part of the tech community looking to expand your global business network and see innovation from dozens of companies, CES Unveiled Amsterdam is the event for you.

    Interested in Exhibiting and Branding Opportunities?

    From new-to-market startups to established brands, you can stand out among the CES elite at CES Unveiled Amsterdam. Meet your business needs, and gain access to top influential media from the Netherlands and nearby countries, including Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Sweden and the United Kingdom. Be seen— display your product or service to an estimated 300 industry professionals.
