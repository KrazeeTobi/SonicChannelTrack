    Call for Speakers Is Now CLOSED

    Thank you for your interest in the CES conference program! Applicants will receive a speaking status update by Oct. 12, 2017, via email to the address provided on the submission form. 

    For inquiries related to our Call for Speakers, please contact the CES Conferences Team.

    Qualities of a Good Speaker Submission

    • Knew the CES audience. Our audience comprises qualified consumer technology industry attendees looking for real B2B applications. 
    • Reviewed the form carefully before submitting. 
    • Did not pitch products, companies or services. Submissions contain clear takeaways for the audience. 
    • Linked to the speaker bio on the contact information page. Make sure the speaker bio is clear on why this person is an authority on subject matter. 
    • Provided case studies or research that will assist the audience in their own business applications. 

    Important Dates

    July 14: CES Call for Speakers closes
    Oct. 12: Applicants notified of speaking status
    Jan. 9-12: CES 2018
