About the Innovation Awards Program 

The Innovation Awards program recognizes two levels of honorees among 28 award categories:

Honoree: A product or technology that scores above the threshold set for a specific category.

Best of Innovation: Given to only the highest-rated product or technology in each category — or to multiple, in the event of a tie.
