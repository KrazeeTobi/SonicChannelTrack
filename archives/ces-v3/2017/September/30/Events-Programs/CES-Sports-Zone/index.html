    As the global event for technology innovation, CES provides the perfect arena for sports technology advancements.

    From smart products that enhance athletic performance to state-of-the-art venues and next-gen sponsorships, the new CES Sports Zone incorporates exhibit space with four days of conference programming for a customized experience.

    Explore the themes that will impact the business of sports — both on and off the field — disrupt our industry, influence industry executives, and entertain us as sports fans. Conference themes include:
     
    • Quantified Athlete
    • Immersive Media
    • Next-Gen Sponsorship
