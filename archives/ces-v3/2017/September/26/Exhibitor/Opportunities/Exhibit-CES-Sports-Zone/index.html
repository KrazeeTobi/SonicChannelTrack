Audience Insights

CES is the only place for those with a vested interest to fully explore the future of sports technology and better understand the implications for athletes.

By delivering compelling content and showcasing leading-edge technologies, CES Sports Zones will attract key players in the sports technology industry.

At CES 2017, our sports technology programming attracted thought leaders, national sport leagues, athletes and sport fitness experts. CES 2017 hosted all major sports leagues (Major League Soccer, MLB, NASCAR, NBA, NCAA, NFL, NHL, US Olympic Committee, WWE) and celebrity professional athletes (Shaquille O’Neal, Charles Barkley, Joe Montana, Landon Donovan, Bryce Harper, Michael Phelps) were represented.
