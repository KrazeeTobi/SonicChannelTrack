    • Media Room Locations & Hours
    Share This Print This Page

    Media rooms will have the tools and assistance journalists need to conduct interviews, break news and share the CES story.

    Media rooms or workspaces will be located in

    • The Las Vegas Convention Center (LVCC)
    • The Venetian
    • ARIA C Space
    • Mandalay Bay (Sunday and Monday only)

    These services will also be available in the Satellite Media Room at the LVCC and in the new C Space Media Workroom in ARIA.

    Convention Center Media Rooms

    LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S229

    • Sunday, Jan. 7: Noon to 7 PM
    • Monday, Jan. 8: 7:30 AM to 7 PM
    • Tuesday, Jan. 9:  8 AM to 6:30 PM
    • Wednesday, Jan. 10: 8 AM to 6:30 PM
    • Thursday, Jan. 11: 8:30 AM to 6:30 PM
    • Friday, Jan. 12: 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM
