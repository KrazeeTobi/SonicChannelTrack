    Get to and Around CES

    With more than 170,000 attendees expected at CES 2018, consider the various transportation options in the Las Vegas region to get to and around the show.

    Take advantage of the many free shuttles, ride the Las Vegas Monorail, or join a pool from your favorite ridesharing service.

    C Space Shuttle Boarding Locations

    • ARIA: Level 1 Convention Space, adjacent to the Bristlecone Ballroom
    • LVCC: Outside the Grand Lobby
    • Venetian: Underground shuttle area

    Las Vegas Monorail

    Travel along the Strip and to the LVCC without waiting in traffic. Convenient station locations at: MGM Grand, Bally’s/Paris, Flamingo/Caesars Palace, Harrah’s/The LINQ, LVCC, Westgate and SLS. Trains run every 4-7 minutes. 

    Discounts are available for CES attendees.

    Ridesharing in Las Vegas

    Uber, Lyft and other transportation network companies are available in Las Vegas. During the show, go to the designated pickup locations to meet your driver. 

    Pickup Locations

    • LVCC: Gold parking lot or on Sierra Vista Drive
    • Sands: Main porte-cochere
    • Venetian: the valet area in the parking garage

    Taxis

    Hail a taxi at the front of the LVCC (North or South Halls) and all other show venues.

    Looking to jump the long taxi lines? Download the Bandwagon app before you arrive in Las Vegas to decrease taxi line wait times at the North and South taxi stands at the LVCC. Matched parties get to jump the line for priority access to departing taxis, saving you time and money.
