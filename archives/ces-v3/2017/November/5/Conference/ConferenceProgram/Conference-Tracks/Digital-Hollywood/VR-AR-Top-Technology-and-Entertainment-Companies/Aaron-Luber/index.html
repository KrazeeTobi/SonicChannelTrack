Aaron Luber

Head of AR/VR Content
Google

Aaron leads content partnerships for Google's AR and VR efforts. Aaron's journey began with launching YouTube360 first as a 20% project and then to a live product in March 2015. Throughout that time, Aaron transitioned from YouTube to the Google VR team and has been working to bring their VR products to the masses. Probably the most impressive surprise has been the incredible brand engagement and eagerness for companies (such as Mattel, GoPro, Disney/Star Wars and, of course, The New York Times) to work with Cardboard, Daydream and Tango. Aaron has been core in leading each of these partnerships for Google. These partnerships have been able to deliver on great content, while building towards the mission of delivering innovative AR and VR experiences to the masses.
