Matt Jones

Senior Vice President of Software
Hyperloop-One

Matt Jones leads the Hyperloop One Software Engineering team; tasked with providing all software needed to manage, control, and operate a Hyperloop One system. This includes software for everything from closing the loop on a mechanism, managing Pod traffic, analyzing backend data to powering all of the customer-facing applications.

Matt joins was previously with moovel Group where he was Chief Product Officer in charge of integrating personal, shared and public transportation options into single-view mobility products. Prior to that he spent 14 years at Jaguar Land Rover as the Director of Future Technology, leading development of electronic and software systems including autonomous vehicles. He served as a board member at the Linux Foundation and the GENIVI Alliance, pushing the Linux platform across IoT devices.
