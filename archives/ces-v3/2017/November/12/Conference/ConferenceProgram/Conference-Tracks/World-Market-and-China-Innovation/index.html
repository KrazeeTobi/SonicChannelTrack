World Market and China Innovation

sponsored by Suning

The 2018 China Innovation North America Forum’s mission is to introduce innovative products and ideas from China into the global market. In these sessions, Chinese manufacturers and retailers will share their creative process and experience and reveal the latest technology and design trends coming out of China. 

Top Three Topics:

  • China Innovation Forum. Delivering brilliant design and innovation to the world market 
  • Technology trends in China this year and the smart retail experience
  • Future innovative technology and design from China revealed. What’s the creative process?

9:30-10:30 AM January 10

LVCC, South Plaza, Booth #62000

Introduction of China Innovation Platform

The committee will introduce the China Innovation Forum platform and analyze creative trends in China this year. Our co-organizer, Suning Commerce, will also discuss the distributor’s creative purchase and smart retail experience.

10:30-12:30 AM January 10

LVCC, South Plaza, Booth #62000

Creative Design Sharing

Experts from well-known Chinese manufacturers will present innovative technology and creative design. They will enlighten the audience on how they create products that will be embraced in the future market.
