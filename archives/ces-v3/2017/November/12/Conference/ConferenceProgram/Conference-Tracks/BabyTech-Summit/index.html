BabyTech Summit

presented by Living in Digital Times

The BabyTech Showcase at CES 2017 was an enormous hit and you can expect more for BabyTech @ CES 2018! From smart breast pumps to at-home medical diagnostics tools and connected nursery devices–we’ll continue to bring the best of the best to this one-of-a-kind marketplace.

Top Three Topics:

  • Modern Retailers Add the Baby Tech Aisle: Discover how big-box and online retailers are making baby tech accessible
  • The Modern Connected Nursery: How are smart devices and platforms helping parents raise healthy, safe and happy babies?
  • Millennials: Powering the Baby Tech Craze: Connected millennials are adopting tech to solve fertility, pregnancy and parenting challenges

10:30-11:15 AM January 9

Sands, Level 2, Booth #43731

Flipping the Switch on Your Connected Nursery

We use our phones to monitor everything- our fitness, our schedules, and even our baby’s habits. Let’s explore the many ways a connected nursery can enhance your parenting skills and support you in raising a safe, healthy and happy baby.

1:30-2:15 PM January 9

Sands, Level 2, Booth #43731

The Internet of Baby Things (IoBT)

You diligently track your exercise with wearables. What about your baby’s sleep patterns or milk consumption? This data is part of the Internet of Baby Things and today’s devices and wearables are smarter, more fashionable and more insightful than ever. 

3:30-4:15 PM January 9

Sands, Level 2, Booth #43731

Best of Baby Tech Awards

Join us for the third annual Best of Baby Tech Awards where we will be recognizing outstanding achievements in fertility, pregnancy and baby technology.  Grab a seat for this highly anticipated awards show.

11-11:45 AM January 10

Sands, Level 2, Booth #43731

Modern Retailers Add the Baby Tech Aisle

With a 48% funding increase in baby and kids from 2013 to 2016, baby tech has become a legitimate industry. As a result, big box and online retailers are making baby tech accessible. Discover how they’re approaching this rising market.

3:30-4 PM January 10

Sands, Level 2, Booth #43731

A Feeding Frenzy: 21st Century Breast Pumps

The breast pump has been virtually unchanged for the last 50 years, but today’s FemTech market is transforming this technology into a hyper-smart, high-performing, durable and, most of all, comfortable device. Join today’s cutting-edge innovators radically breaking new ground.
