C Space Storytellers

presented by Consumer Technology Association

No Additional Fee Required

Influencers and newsmakers share stories and case studies about the unique relationships between brands, entertainment and technology.  Brand executives highlight best practices leveraging artificial intelligence, data and analytics and digital content to enhance the brand narrative. 

Top Three Topics: 

  • Artificial intelligence: the must-have tool for marketers
  • When to embrace original content creation
  • Providing transparency in data and analytics 

10-10:30 AM January 9

ARIA, Level 1, Pinyon Ballroom

C Space Storyteller: Facebook

A fireside chat with Fidji Simo, VP of Product for Video at Facebook. The conversation will explore the evolution of video on Facebook and how the company thinks about creating great video experiences for people, publishers, and advertisers. Fidji will share the latest on Watch, Facebook’s platform for shows, and how she sees video consumption evolving in the future.

1:30-2 PM January 9

ARIA, Level 1, Pinyon Ballroom

C Space Storyteller: TBWA\Worldwide

Machine Intelligence Meets Human Creativity. Join TBWA's Troy Ruhanen for a frank conversation on how organizations can leverage AI technologies to unlock creative business solutions, disrupt the status quo and create a future of growth.

2:30-3 PM January 9

ARIA, Level 1, Pinyon Ballroom

C Space Storyteller: IBM

Cognitive technology is reshaping the M&E industry, providing ample opportunities for brands and streaming services to drive impact and for consumers to benefit. These panelists will provide unique insights into how businesses can leverage the latest advancements in AI and prepare for the future of the industry.  

9:30-10 AM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Pinyon Ballroom

C Space Storyteller: Google

The age of assistance has created empowered consumers who are more curious, demanding, and impatient than ever. Join Google’s Allan Thygesen on how brands can win today’s consumers with more relevant, assistive and instant experiences.

Speaker
Allan Thygesen
President, Americas,
Google

10:15-10:45 AM January 10

ARIA, Level 1, Pinyon Ballroom

C Space Storyteller: Knotch

Hear from Anda Gansca, Co-Founder & CEO of Knotch together with industry leaders who are running some of the biggest media budgets globally, on how brands and data companies can work together to create an efficient and effective marketing ecosystem. They will discuss the successes, hurdles and fears that marketers are faced with on pushing the standards of our industry on transparency.
