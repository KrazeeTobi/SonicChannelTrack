    Intel Keynote Address: How Data Is Shaping Innovation of the Future

    • Home
    • |
    • Conference
    • |
    • Keynote Addresses
    Share This Print This Page

    6:30-7:30 PM January 8
    Monte Carlo, , Park Theater

    We are entering an artificial intelligence revolution. To power the technology of the future and create amazing new experiences, we need to unlock the power of data. Its collection, storage and analysis continue to change and grow, having more of an impact on our everyday lives than ever before.

    With advances in artificial intelligence, 5G connectivity, autonomous driving and virtual reality, Intel is taking the next steps to reimagine how data will create amazing new experiences that will transform our daily lives.


    Keynote
    Brian Krzanich
    Chief Executive Officer,
    Intel
