    Location: LVCC, South Halls 1 and 2

    Discover where digital information and the real world collide in the Augmented Reality Marketplace.

    Through specially designed hardware and software full of cameras, sensors, algorithms and more, your perception of reality can be instantaneously altered in context with your environment. Applications include athlete statistics showing on TV during a match, the trajectory of an object overlaying an image, gaming, construction plans and more.
