    Gain insight, expertise and advice from the influencers and major decision makers themselves.

    C Space conference programming included a major CES keynote presentation, headliners from the industry's movers and shakers, and a few dedicated tracks that drilled down into the specifics that you care about most. 

    Watch the CES 2017 C Space Keynote

    The C Space Keynote included an exclusive interview with Barry Diller, chairman and chief executive of Expedia, moderated by Michael E. Kassan, chairman and CEO of MediaLink, immediately followed by a panel discussion with top media and marketing leaders.

    C Space Keynote, presented by MediaLink

    These conference tracks explored the relationship among brands, agencies, media and the influence of technology with industry thought leaders:

    • Chief Digital Officer Brand Summit
    • Content and Entertainment, presented by Consumer Technology Association
    • Digital Hollywood
    • Entertainment Summit, presented by Variety

    • ShellyPalmer Innovation Series Breakfast, presented by The Palmer Group

    • StoryTech Show Floor AM Briefings, sponsored by CANOE
