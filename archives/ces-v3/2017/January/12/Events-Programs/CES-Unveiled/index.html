The CES Unveiled event series brings innovative technology companies, top press, industry analysts, global buyers and key tech influencers together to showcase new products and trends leading up to CES.

Hosted in technology hotspots around the world, we added CES Unveiled Prague to the established and successful lineup of pre-CES events in Paris, New York and Las Vegas. Will your city be next?

Grow your global brand, increase your international presence, mingle with tech innovators, build your network and see the latest technologies that might just change the world.
