CMO

Johnson & Johnson Consumer Companies

10:30-11:30 AM January 5

Westgate, Westgate Theater

Alison Lewis is Johnson & Johnson Consumer Companies' first ever Chief Marketing Officer, responsible for iconic brands including NEUTROGENA®, JOHNSON'S®, LISTERINE®, and TYLENOL®. A creative visionary with over 25 years of marketing experience, she has accelerated growth by building a new global operating model and delivering dynamic marketing and product innovations.  

Her industry accolades include the Clio Brand Icon Award, Cannes' Creative Marketer of the Year and New York Advertising Woman of the Year in 2016.

Prior to joining Johnson & Johnson, Lewis held various senior roles at The Coca-Cola Company and at Kraft Foods in Canada.
