Prepare yourself for the best work week of 2017 with our guide to navigating the CES 2017 conference program.

Build Your Schedule

With exhibits, sessions, networking events and the show floor to choose from, we recommend building your own personal game plan for CES.

Craft your own CES conference schedule by using these tools:

  • Create your own personalized show plan with Map Your Show. Start planning your daily schedule for each day of CES. Create a Map Your Show account and add conference sessions to your schedule via the "Conferences & Events" tab.
  • Add your must-see sessions to your personal Calendar. Simply choose "Add to Calendar", located under the time and date on any session page on the CES website, to add to your Outlook, Google or iCal Calendar. 
  • Download the CES Mobile App. Your top comprehensive onsite tool from scheduling, planning, exploring and more.

Additional Planning Resources

  • ​Search by Partner or Sponsors or by Conference Tracks. CES partners with a variety of industry thought leaders that are slated to bring the best topics and research to the conference program. 
  • ​Find your favorite speakers in the CES 2017 Speaker Directory. Search by name or by company to find the sessions that are a must-attend for your business.
  • ​Explore all CES events and programming. Find what speaks to you. Search CES conferences and events by conference pass type, topic or track.

Talk the Talk

Don't get lost in the jargon. Navigate 2017 with confidence with a quick vocab refresher.

Keynote Address: Keynote addresses showcase global industry leaders on a main stage.

SuperSession: SuperSessions feature emerging, up-and-coming companies and senior executives on a premier stage.

Track: A track is comprised of sessions that narrow in on a topic or emerging trend.

Session: Sessions are the individual panels, presentations or fireside chats within a track. Remember, when you buy a track, you have access to all of the sessions included within that track.

C Space: C Space is the focused program for advertising, marketing, content creators and more.
