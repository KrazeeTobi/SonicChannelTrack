Purchase Your CES 2017 Conference Programs

Tailor your CES experience by purchasing individual conference tracks or the conference pass that's right for you.

As you browse the CES 2017 conference tracks, select programs to add to your cart. If the tracks you select total more than the cost of a conference pass, based on your selections, the system will automatically process the purchase option that is the best value.

Friendly reminder: Consumer Technology Association (CTA)™ members and Executive Club members save 25 percent on the Deluxe Conference Pass and the Starter Conference Pass. The discount will automatically apply when you register with your company email. Learn about CTA membership. >

Conference Passes



Deluxe Conference Pass (Advanced Price: $1,400 | Regular Price: $1,700): This pass offers access to four days of conference programming at CES. It's perfect for those planning on attending a variety of tracks or looking for flexible options to fit their busy CES schedule. Note: THX and ICCE tracks not included.

Starter Conference Pass (Advanced Price: $700 | Regular Price: $900): The producers of CES have curated a sample of the latest emerging trends by combining seven tracks at the LVCC. This pass is ideal for anyone at CES looking to catch a few sessions throughout the show on the latest topics.
