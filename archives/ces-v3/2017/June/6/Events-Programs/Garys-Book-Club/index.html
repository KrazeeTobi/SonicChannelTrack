    Apply Now to Present as Part of Gary's Book Club at CES 2018

    If you've written a book — that will be published January 2017-January 2018 — about technology, innovation or tech policy, you can be a featured author at the CTA Stage as part of Gary's Book Club.

    Authors can also meet with attendees, sell books on-site through Barnes & Noble, and sign their works.
