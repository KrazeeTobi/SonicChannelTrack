Gary's Book Club: Amy Webb, The Signals Are Talking

  • Home
  • |
  • News
  • |
  • Videos
Share This Print This Page

"The Signals Are Talking: Why Today's Fringe Is Tomorrow's Mainstream" reveals a systemic way of evaluating new ideas bubbling up on the horizon — distinguishing what is a real trend from the merely trendy. Signals helps us hear which signals are talking sense and which are simply nonsense so that we might know today what developments have long-term consequence for tomorrow.
