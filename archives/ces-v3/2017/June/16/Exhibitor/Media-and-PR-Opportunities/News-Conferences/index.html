    CES Exhibitor News Conference Opportunities

    All media day events and CES news conferences, unless otherwise requested, will be included in the official CES Media Events Schedule and distributed on site at CES.

    CES Media Days

    Media Day 1: Noon-2:45 PM, Jan. 7, 2018
    Media Day 2: 8 AM-5:45 PM, Jan. 8, 2018
    Location: Mandalay Bay

    Make your major product or company news announcement exclusively to registered CES media and industry analysts with a Media Day news conference before the show floor opens.

    Selection in August 2017
    You should receive your Media Day selection number in an email in early July.

    If you do not receive a Media Day selection number, you can apply to be on the waitlist for any open slots, which will be filled on a first-come, first-served basis.


    CES Media Days Power Sessions

    Date: Jan. 8, 2018
    Time: 9 AM-12:45 PM
    Location: Mandalay Bay, Palm A Ballroom
    Cost: Free

    Break news quickly and directly to CES media with a Media Day Power Session.

    You will have 15 minutes for setup, 30 minutes for the event and 15 minutes for tear down. See what’s included in this space. (PDF)

    Sessions are available on a first-come, first-served basis.


    Show Day News Conferences

    Date: Jan. 9-10, 2018
    Time: Show floor hours
    Location: LVCC and The Venetian
    Cost: Free
     
    Hold news conference during the show in a CES-provided news conference room. 

    See what’s included in the space (PDF). Exhibitors are responsible for ordering and paying for additional services.

    Sessions are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

    News Conferences in Your Exhibitor Booths or Meeting Rooms

    Date: Jan. 9-12, 2017
    Time: Any*
    Location: CES-approved venue
    Cost: Free

    Hold a news conference in your exhibit space or paid meeting space during the show. Notify us of your news conference for inclusion in promotional materials.

    Interested in booking a meeting room? Contact your sales representative today.

    *Events held on the show floor before or after show hours require advanced security forms and hired security guards. If your form indicates that you’d like to host your event before or after show hours, the CES PR team will send you the appropriate paperwork.

    The CES Stance on Outboarding

    Outboarding is any exhibit, demonstration, event or branded activation at a non-official CES venue, and it is a serious violation of CES policy and the International Association of Exhibitions and Events (IAEE) guidelines with consequences.

    Please read the official CES Stance on Outboarding for more information.
