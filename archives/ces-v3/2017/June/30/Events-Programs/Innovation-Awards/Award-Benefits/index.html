    • CES Innovation Award Benefits
    Share This Print This Page

    Earning the coveted CES Innovation Awards Honoree title provides engaging benefits:

    • Innovation Awards Honoree logo to include on your product packaging and advertising materials.

    • The opportunity to display your product in the Innovation Awards Showcase at CES, one of the most popular destinations on the show floor (for official CES exhibitors only). 

    • The opportunity to be featured on the live radio broadcast of "Into Tomorrow" with Dave Graveline. This nationally syndicated consumer electronics radio program features interviews with CEOs, presidents and senior executives from the hottest companies exhibiting at CES live from the show floor.

    • Innovation Awards Honoree trophy to display in your exhibit at CES and in your company's trophy case.

    • Industry-wide recognition and media attention through a CTA press release announcing the honorees.

    • Listing in the January/February issue of It Is Innovation (i3), CTA’s flagship publication distributed to more than 51,000 and bonus distribution at CES 2018 (for official CES Exhibitors only).

    • Exposure on the official CES website.

    • Innovation Awards icon published next to your company listing in the Official CES Show Directory (for official CES exhibitors only).

    • Substantial discounts on advertising opportunities in CTA's It Is Innovation (i3) magazine.

    • The opportunity to display your product at additional CTA events throughout the year, including CES Unveiled New York and CES Unveiled Las Vegas.
