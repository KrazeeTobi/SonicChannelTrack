ON-SITE BRANDING AND SPONSORSHIPS


Banners (Exterior and Interior)

Gain guaranteed exposure to 165K+ attendees and from influential media capturing footage of key locations.

To learn more, call Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.


Outdoor Promotions

Space is available for promotional displays outside and in the lobby areas for static promotional products, such as custom inflatables, cars, interactive kiosks and more.

For more information, contact Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.


Sponsorships

Choose from a variety of on-site, tangible attendee items and services to get your brand front-and-center: badge lanyards, badge coaster, show bags and charging stations.

There are also brand opportunities for industry events, programs and show locations.


Lounges & Meeting Spaces

Sponsorship opportunities range from Consumer Technology Association (CTA)™ Member Lounges to the International Business Center.

To learn more, call Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.


Registration Greeters

Corporate greeters welcome attendees in the LVCC's main registration area and distribute giveaways, product information or incentives to visit your exhibit.

To learn more, call Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.


Transportation Sponsorships

Complimentary shuttle service between CES venues and more than 30 official hotels provides you with customized marketing opportunities.

For more information, contact Jeff LoGioco at 401-294-0040.


DIRECT MAIL AND ONLINE ADVERTISING


Social Media Sponsorships

From unique data insights to sponsored posts, you can collect market intelligence and place your brand in the pockets of tech gurus, consumers and CEOs from around the world.


Attendee Postal Mailing List

Available only to official CES exhibitors, reach last year's attendees and the coming year's registrants with lists targeted the specific demographics you want at your exhibit.


CES App

With several enhanced placement opportunities, the official CES App can highlight your brand. Packages range from enhanced Exhibitor Directory listings to a presenting sponsor opportunity.

To learn more, contact Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.


Online Directory Sponsorships

All CES exhibitors receive a free directory listing, but you can choose from a variety of packages to help boost your bottom line and connect to the attendees you want to meet.

To learn more, contact Brett Glatfelter or call 908-399-6245.


PRINT ADVERTISING


It Is Innovation (i3)

The official publication of CTA, i3 has exclusive publications distribution areas, including all exhibitor booths, publication bins, CTA Member Lounges, executive suites, media rooms, the VIP lunchroom, CES Unveiled and the Innovation Awards Showcase.

To learn more, contact Liz Tardif at 703-907-7681.


Official CES Daily

Fresh, daily editorial content, produced by TWICE, covers all the show floor excitement. You’ll receive additional exposure through distribution in hotel rooms, publication bins and by brand ambassadors throughout the show floor.

To learn more, 

  • Email Ed Hecht or call 917-281-4725
  • Email John LaMarca or call 917-281-4775
  • Email Pete Haeffner or call 917-281-4742


Official Show Directory, Guide and Maps

The Official Show Directory is the only year-round reference source, with exhibiting company profiles, brand listings and product information.

The Official Show Guide & Maps is the primary on-site resource, with listings of all events and awards; exhibitors; show floor maps; Marketplaces; show services; and hotel, travel and transportation information.

To learn more, contact Benjamin Felix at 215-238-5310.


EXPORT OPPORTUNITIES


Global Market Insight Program

Meet one-on-one with U.S. embassy commercial specialists in the information and communication technology sector in select overseas markets. You can also meet with experts in export finance. Offered to qualified U.S. exhibitors and CTA members.

More information coming soon!


International Matchmaking Reception

Showcase innovate products and services in front of hundreds of global tech professionals, including buyers and media. Increase your brand exposure and meet new customers by purchasing a tabletop display. 

More information coming soon!


B2B Appointments with International Buyers

Meet pre-screened international buyers recruited by the International Buyer Program. Appointments are available to qualified U.S. exhibitors or CTA members at CES 2018.

More information coming soon!

Exhibitor Exporting Program

Grow your international sales database with the Exhibitor Exporting Program. Participation is complimentary for CES exhibitors, and benefits include

  • Post-show list pull with contact information for attendees from up to 10 countries you select.
  • Complimentary tickets to the International Matchmaking Reception.

Indicate that you are interested in exporting opportunities when you complete your online exhibitor directory listing.


COMPLIMENTARY OPPORTUNITIES


Official Directory Listing

Complete your CES directory listing to be included in the online exhibitor directory and the print CES Official Show Directory.

More information coming soon!


Customer Invitations

Give your valued clients and prospects up to $100,000 worth of free registration. Registration is free for up to 1,000 recipients, per exhibitor, during the free registration window and thereafter are valid for $100 off the $300 registration.

More information coming in the fall!


Registration Link on Your Site

Exhibitor Profile Distribution

CES Logo

CES Speaking Opportunities
