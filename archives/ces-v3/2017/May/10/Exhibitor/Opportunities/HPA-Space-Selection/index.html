High-performance audio space selection for CES 2018 closed on April 26, 2017.

Selection Appointment Details

You'll find selection appointment details in the email that was sent to you. This email includes your company's, selection number, selection date and time. 

If you have any questions or did not receive your time, please reach out to the appropriate CES Sales Representative.

See sales representative contact information. (PDF)

Exhibit Space Selection Process

We urge you to be available for your call at least 5 minutes prior to your scheduled selection date and time.

Your Exhibit Space Contract will be electronically generated by a CES representative for your review. You will then be asked to submit your approval (electronically). A copy of the contract will be sent to the contact information provided. Please note that we no longer accept paper contracts.

A sample of the CES 2018 Exhibit Space Contract is available for your reference, but you do not need to complete this form prior to space selection.

See the sample CES 2018 Exhibitor Space Contract. (PDF)
See the CES 2018 High-Performance Audio exhibit pricing. (PDF)

CES 2018 Exhibit Space Addendums

The Venetian has a few locations that require a specific addendum in addition to the Exhibit Space Contract. The Venetian Exhibit Suite Addendum and the Venetian Meeting Room Addendum must be reviewed and followed, as part of your space contract, by all exhibitors in these locations.

See the Venetian Exhibit Suite Addendum (PDF).
