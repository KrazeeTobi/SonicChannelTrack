WHO IS COMING TO CES

CES 2022 will be in Las Vegas in January providing a global audience access to major brands and startups, as well as the world’s most-influential leaders. Over 2100 companies have committed to exhibiting in Las Vegas and many more are being added each week. Key industry audiences are also showing strong commitment: 200 of the Fortune Global 500, 77 of the Interbrand 100, 66 of the top retailers and leading US and global media are already registered and joining us in Las Vegas.
