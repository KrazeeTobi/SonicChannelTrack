Monday, October 10, 2022

Palais Brongniart 
Place de la Bourse 75002 Paris

CES® Unveiled returns to Paris to showcase how the country’s entrepreneurship and dedication to innovation creates tech for a better tomorrow.

From promising startups to established brands, the CES Unveiled series provides the ultimate sneak peek of what will be launched to the world at CES 2023 in Las Vegas.

Tech for a Better Tomorrow

Check back here as we make more announcements about CES Unveiled Paris 2022.

CES Unveiled Paris 2021 featured Brian Aggeler, Chargé D’affaires a.i., U.S. Embassy in Paris, France; Ayumi Moore Aoki, Founder and CEO, Women in Tech; Cédric O, France’s Secretary of State for Digital Transition and Electronic Communications, and leading companies to discuss how innovation builds greater sustainability.

Conference programming explored key areas such as blockchain and fashion technology, sustainability, resilience and climate change.

Who Will You Meet?

CES Unveiled welcomed the entire spectrum of the tech industry including:

  • Media
  • Investors and financial community members
  • C-Level and senior executives
  • Government officials and policy decision makers
  • Industry influencers
  • Other groundbreaking exhibitors
