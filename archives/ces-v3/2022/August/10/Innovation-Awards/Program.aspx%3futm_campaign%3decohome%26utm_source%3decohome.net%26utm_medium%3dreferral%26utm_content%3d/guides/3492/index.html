CES 2023 Innovation Awards Program

The CES Innovation Awards program is an annual competition honoring outstanding design and engineering in consumer technology products. The program recognizes honorees in a multitude of consumer technology product categories and distinguishes the highest rated in each.

About the Innovation Awards Program

The program recognizes two levels of honorees among the product categories:

  • Honoree: The top scoring products in each category.
  • Best of Innovation: The best of the best – the highest-rated products across all categories.


The program is open to any individual, organization or authorized agent who manufactures, designs, engineers or promotes consumer technology products. Product submitted must be available in the market for the first time after Apr. 1, 2022 or must be projected to be available in the market by Apr. 1, 2023. A panel of industry expert judges review applications in each product category based on specific criteria.

CTA does not test or endorse the Innovation award-winning products.
