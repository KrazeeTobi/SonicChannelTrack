Indy Autonomous Challenge to Return to CES® 2023 With Boosted Engine Package on All Cars

The Consumer Technology Association (CTA)® announced that after making history at CES 2022 with the world’s first-ever, head-to-head autonomous racecar competition, the Indy Autonomous Challenge (IAC) will return to CES 2023.

The Autonomous Challenge @ CES will take place at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway on Saturday, January 7, 2023. The event will be limited to CES attendees and transportation will be available from the Las Vegas Convention Center (LVCC) to the Las Vegas Motor Speedway.
