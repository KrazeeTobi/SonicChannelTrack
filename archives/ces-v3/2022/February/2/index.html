CES 2022 AWARD

Global Women’s Health Tech Awards

Through a joint global challenge, CES, owned and produced by the Consumer Technology Association (CTA)®, and the World Bank Group, aim to identify and celebrate innovative technologies that address gaps in women’s healthcare and safety in emerging markets.

The Global Women's HealthTech Awards recognizes innovative startups that leverage tech to improve women's health and safety in emerging markets. 

Learn more

over 35

countries applied

Historically, women’s health and well-being has suffered due to socio-economic factors, discrimination, and lack of adequate infrastructure and services. Technology is opening new opportunities to knock down these barriers, providing more inclusive, affordable solutions for girls and women around the world.
