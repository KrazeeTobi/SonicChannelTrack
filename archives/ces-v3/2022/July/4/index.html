CES® 2023 Will Focus on How Innovation is Addressing Global Challenges

CES 2023, the world's most influential technology event, will partner with the World Academy of Art and Science (WAAS) to showcase the critical role of technology in support of the United Nations efforts to advance human security for all.

We are also pleased to announce that John Deere, the iconic manufacturer of agricultural machinery, will take the keynote stage this January in Las Vegas. Attendees will hear from Chairman and CEO John May, who will share how purpose-driven sustainability and advancements in technology are addressing one of the world’s greatest challenges — food security and feeding a growing global population.
