    Location: LVCC, South Hall 3

    New at CES, High-Tech Retailing Marketplace will include the Retail Innovation Lounge, where you’ll experience the cutting edge of retail tech in an intimate setting with fireside chats, demos and seminars.

    From augmented reality makeup mirrors and robotic store clerks, to line-less checkout and next generation window displays, innovation is helping a new generation of customers experience new ways to shop, both on and offline.
