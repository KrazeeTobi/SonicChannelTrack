CES is the world’s gathering place for all who thrive on the business of consumer technologies. It has served as the proving ground for innovators and breakthrough technologies for 50 years — the global stage where next-generation innovations are introduced to the marketplace.

As the largest hands-on event of its kind, CES features all aspects of the industry. Owned and produced by the Consumer Technology Association (CTA)™, it attracts the world’s business leaders and pioneering thinkers. CES 2018 is expecting more than 55,000 international attendees. Make the most of your CES experience by learning more about the services provided.

Register Now for CES 2018

The basic CES attendee registration is an Exhibits Plus Pass, which includes access to:

  • CES 2018 exhibitors
  • Keynote addresses
  • SuperSessions
  • Any free conference track or session

To attend additional conference programming, you'll need to purchase an individual conference pass or — for the best value — upgrade a Starter Conference Pass or Deluxe Conference Pass.


Learn more about registration.

Bring a Group of Industry Professionals to CES

If you are bringing a group of at least 15 business professionals to CES, become a Delegation Leader. As a Delegation Leader, you will assist help your clients in maximizing the value of their CES trip.

Featured Event

International Matchmaking Reception
Tuesday, Jan. 9, 2018, 6-7:30 PM
LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S222
View the Invitation

All international attendees, exhibitors and media are invited to the International Matchmaking Reception. Don’t miss this opportunity to network, build global partnerships and enjoy light refreshments after an exciting day on the CES show floor. U.S companies seeking to export will have their products and services on display during the reception. This event is open to all international registrants and their invited guests.
