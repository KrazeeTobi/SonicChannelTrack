We are committed to ensuring that CES is fully accessible for persons with disabilities.

Accessibility Services Desks

  • LVCC, Grand Lobby, upper level staircase near Room N250
  • Sands, Level 1, Lobby

To arrange accessibility services on-site, indicate your needs during registration and a member of our team will be in touch.

Scooters, Wheelchairs and Segways

Scooters and wheelchairs are available for rent at the LVCC and Sands/Venetian. Segways are permitted at these venues for ADA use only. Segways are not permitted at ARIA, Mandalay Bay or Westgate.

To rent motorized or non-motorized wheelchairs, contact Scootaround at 888-441-7575.

 

Transportation

ADA Parking

ADA parking is available on a first-come, first-served basis in the LVCC Diamond Lot and at all other official CES venues.

ADA Shuttle Service

We provide ADA-accessible buses for those with special needs from official CES hotels and parking lots during hours of operation.

To arrange for your transportation on-site, please contact RPMs, the official CES transportation vendor, at 877-725-3398 or CESinfo@rp-ms.com.

Please request service at least 20 minutes in advance of the desired pickup time. Note this phone number is operational 30 minutes before and after the daily shuttle schedule.
