ADA Services

See our accessibility services for CES attendees.

Business Centers

Business Centers are located at the following locations:

  • ARIA: Business Center, Conference Center, Level 2, 702-590-9750
  • LVCC: FedEx Office, Grand Lobby Concourse and front of South Hall 1, 702-733-2898
  • Sands: Business Center, Level 1 Lobby, 702-733-5070
  • Venetian: FedEx Office, Level 2, across from Bellini 2006, 702-836-4400
  • Westgate: FedEx Office, 702-862-4104

Coat and Bag Check

Coat and bag check is available at these locations, for a fee:

  • LVCC: South Hall 1 Lobby, next to Starbucks
  • LVCC: Central Plaza
  • LVCC: Central Concourse across from C3 entrance
  • LVCC: South Hall 2 Lobby (Cash only at this location)
  • Venetian: Foyer next to Venetian Ballroom D
  • Westgate: Bell Desk

Cameras

Attendees are permitted to bring camera equipment onto the show floor. Exhibitors retain the right to restrict photography of their products or displays and such decisions are within the discretion of the exhibitor and are not controlled by CES.

Emergency Numbers at CES Show Venues

If you are in a show venue, always contact venue security first.

  • ARIA: 702-590-7911 or dial 77911 in hotel
  • LVCC: 702-892-7400 or dial 7400 in LVCC
  • Mandalay Bay: 702-632-7989 or dial "0" in hotel
  • Monte Carlo/Park Theater: 702-730-7190 or dial 37190 in hotel
  • Renaissance: 702-784-5700 or dial "0" in hotel
  • Sands: 702-733-5195 or dial 75195 in Sands
  • Venetian: 702-414-9311 or dial 49311 in hotel
  • Westgate: 702-732-5414 or dial 5911 in hotel
  • Wynn/Encore: 702-770-2820 or dial 2820 in hotel
Report tips and communicate directly with CES and venue security with the LiveSafe app. Learn more.

Personal Mobility

Personal mobility devices (with or without motors) are not permitted at any CES venue. This includes hoverboards, skateboards, uniwheels and all similar products.

Segways are permitted at the LVCC and Sands/Venetian for ADA use only. They are not permitted at any other show venue for any purpose. Please see our accessability services if you require special assistance.

Hotel Quick Guide

Find maps and information about remote badge pickup locations and hours, shuttle bus stops, and business center hours in all official CES hotels in the CES Hotel Quick Guide.

The CES 2018 quick guide will be available in the fall.

Security

See our CES 2018 security measures.

Lost and Found

Lost and Found is located in the CES Security Offices at the following locations:

  • LVCC, Central Hall, Room C105, 702-943-4080
  • LVCC, South Hall Connector, Room S219, 702-943-3532
  • Venetian, Level 3, Murano 3206, 702-691-8602

Food

  • LVCC: Check out Lucky’s Restaurant, Prima Stampa, Honey Salt and Starbucks located in the Grand Lobby, or Aces Restaurant located in the South Hall 1 lobby. You can also find concessions areas throughout the exhibit halls and food trucks in Central Plaza. 
  • Sands: ​Concessions areas are centrally located on the show floor in Halls A-D and Hall G.
  • Venetian: Visit Starbucks or the Grand Canal Shops for a variety of options.
  • Also check out options at Westgate, Renaissance and ARIA.

Mother's Suites

Mother’s Suites (lactation pods) are available for the privacy and convenience of working mothers at CES. Each Mamava suite contains a deadbolt lock for privacy, a power outlet, a fold down table and two benches. Find Mother's Suites at these locations:

  • LVCC, N3 Lobby

  • Venetian, Level 1, in the alcove outside the Casanova ballroom

The LVCC also provides private mother's rooms at the following locations:

  • LVCC, South Hall Connector near room S228

  • LVCC, North Hall across from N253
