9-9:45 AM January 8
Mandalay Bay, Mandalay Bay, Level 3, Palm A

BrainCo was incubated in the Harvard Innovation Lab, and develops Brain Machine Interface (BMI) wearables and platform technologies. BrainCo translates brain signals to digital signals, and develops various applications to improve users' attention level based on neurofeedback principles.
