2018 Tech Trends to Watch

  • Home
  • |
Share This Print This Page

4-4:45 PM January 7
Mandalay Bay, Mandalay Bay, Level 2, Oceanside B

Backed by research, learn more about the trends that will be on display at CES 2018 before the show floor opens. Presented by Steve Koenig, senior director of market research, and Lesley Rohrbaugh, senior manager of market research, Consumer Technology Association (CTA), this session is designed to preview key industry highlights to look for during CES and throughout 2018.
 
They will also give an exclusive first preview of projections from CTA’s highly-anticipated, semi-annual U.S. Consumer Technology Sales and Forecast report, lending data and context to the industry’s categories to watch.

Session attendees will receive direct, early access to CES Unveiled Las Vegas taking place at 5PM after the presentation concludes.


CES is owned and produced by the Consumer Technology Association, which provides the ultimate platform for technology leaders to connect, collaborate, and propel consumer technology forward.
