A CES Speaker is a thought leader, an innovative thinker and an industry influencer.

Complete Your Speaker Registration

  • Be sure to register for CES with the same email address that you used in the speaker portal to be recognized as a speaker. Please note: You will need to complete the registration application in one sitting.
  • Check out the step by step CES speaker registration video below to help you ensure that you’re ready to speak at CES.
 
  • Your speaker credentials and complimentary Deluxe Conference Pass will be automatically added to your registration.
  • You will receive a confirmation email with your registration confirmation information upon completion.
  • Please direct any questions to the CES Conferences Team.

Complete Your Speaker Portal

  • Speakers will not be listed on CES.tech until they have accepted our speaker agreement. Make sure to accept yours as soon as possible!
  • Your speaker portal will allow you to upload a headshot and a biography. This will allow CES attendee to learn more about you and your awesome ideas!
  • Let us guide you through our speaker portal in this video. We’ll show you how to accept your speaker agreement, upload a headshot and enter in your bio.


Navigate CES

  • Download the CES 2018 locations maps.
  • Be familiar with where your session is located, and please arrive at your session room 15 minutes before the start time to make sure equipment is ready and working properly.
  • Utilize the Speaker Ready Room as a pre-session meeting spot for business needs.
  • Badges can be picked up at any of the three Speaker Ready Rooms. Speaker access and benefits are linked to your badge no matter where it is printed; however, speaker gifts are exclusively provided in the Speaker Ready Rooms.
 

CES offers a variety of services for its conference speakers, including complimentary show registration.

Speaker Benefits

  • Complimentary Deluxe Conference Pass, which includes entry to the Show Floor, SuperSessions, keynote addresses, as well as nearly every conference track.
  • Speakers will receive three individual guest tickets that will grant access to the speaker’s conference session. Speaker handlers/guests must be registered for CES with a valid CES badge to use the speaker guest tickets.
  • Exclusive access to the Speaker Ready Rooms, lounge areas where speakers can fine tune presentations and network with peers.

Speaker Ready Rooms

 
  • Tech East, Level 1, Westgate, Pavillion 9
  • Tech East, LVCC, North Hall, N263
  • Tech East, LVCC, South Plaza, 62000
  • Tech West, Venetian, Level 4, Lando 4301
  • Tech South, Aria, Level 1, Bristlecone 5

Speaker Ready Room Hours:
  • Monday, January 8 (8AM- 5PM, LVCC SRR only)
  • Tuesday, January 9 (8AM - 5PM)
  • Wednesday, January 10 (8AM - 5PM)
  • Thursday, January 11 (8AM - 5PM)


Speaker Ready Room Amenities include:
  • Speaker work area/lounge space with light refreshments
  • Computers with hardwired internet connection
  • Wireless internet access for personal computers
  • Printer and copier access
  • CTA staff available to answer questions about CES and the conference program

Speaker Guidelines

  • Speakers cover their own travel expenses and hotel accommodations.
  • Press and analysts have full access to conference sessions. There are no specific press services offered for speakers.
  • Standard CES conference program includes: interview, fireside chats, presentations, and moderated panel discussions.
  • Speakers are responsible for providing their own laptops for presentations with connections for VGA, DVI or HDMI. Equipment cannot be ordered on-site. If using a Mac, tablet or smartphone, notify the CES Conferences Team with the type of device so we can coordinate the appropriate adapters.
  • Speakers are recommended to use our CES PowerPoint template for presentations. Please submit your presentation to CESconference@cta.tech by December 20 if you would like the presentation to be included on your session in our official CES mobile app.
