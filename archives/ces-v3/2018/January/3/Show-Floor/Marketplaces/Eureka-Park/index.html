    • Eureka Park™
    Share This Print This Page

    Location: Sands, Level 1

    With access to more than 3,500 financial professionals, 7,000 members of the media, key investors and suppliers, Eureka Park provides startups with a unique opportunity to showcase their ingenuity.

    Be here to uncover 800 of the world's most promising tech pioneers — and maybe a few unicorns.

    • More than 20 country groups will be showcasing the newest ideas and innovation from countries and regions like France, Quebec, Ukraine, Luxembourg, Singapore and more.
    • More than 1,100 startups have used the CES brand to showcase their products and have been funded at more than $1.5 billion since 2012.
    • Case Western Reserve University, University of Nevada, Las Vegas (UNLV) and more will join University Innovations at CES to showcase game-changing tech developments from some of the country’s leading education institutions.

    Eureka Park Marketplace Exhibitors

    See the budding entrepreneurs who will be participating at CES 2018.

    Arlington Economic Development: Arlington Economic Development is dedicated to preserving and enhancing a competitive and sustainable community and creating exciting, diverse places with top services for business, arts, real estate and tourism. We provide innovative programs that value business needs and enrich entrepreneurs, technology companies and large corporations.

    Avnet: Kickstarter, Avnet and Dragon Innovation are bringing Hardware Studio to CES this year. We'll be talking to designers, engineers, and makers about how the innovative hardware products that CES celebrates get made, and sharing tips for how to make the manufacturing process less daunting. We'll be hosting livestreamed chats with hardware creators about how they approached manufacturing to bring their projects to life, and experts from Avnet and Dragon Innovation will hold office hours for hardware startups.

    Samsung NEXT: Samsung NEXT is one of the core drivers of Samsung's transformation into an integrated hardware and software company. In 2017, NEXT accelerated its work with early stage startups and entrepreneurs in areas including IoT, AI and AR to help them build, grow and scale their technologies. In 2017, Samsung NEXT invested in 29 companies, acquired 4 companies, ushered 2 successful exits and expanded into Europe with the launch of Samsung NEXT Berlin. Expect more great partnerships, investments and product development to come in 2018.
