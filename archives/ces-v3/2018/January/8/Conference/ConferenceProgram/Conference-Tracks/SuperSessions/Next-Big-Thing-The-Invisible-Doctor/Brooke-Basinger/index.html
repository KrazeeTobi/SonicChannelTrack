Brooke Basinger

Technical Lead, Ophthalmic Hardware
Verily

Brooke Basinger has spent her career as a medical device engineer and engineering lead. She has driven multiple Class II and Class III products from concept to launch at companies including Avalon Labs, Abbott Medical Optics and Verily Life Sciences. She currently oversees innovation in ophthalmic implants as a Tech Lead at Verily. Brooke received a B.S. in Engineering from Harvey Mudd, M.S. degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Medical Device & Diagnostic Engineering from USC, and a Ph.D in Biomedical Engineering from USC.

CES is owned and produced by the Consumer Technology Association, which provides the ultimate platform for technology leaders to connect, collaborate, and propel consumer technology forward.
