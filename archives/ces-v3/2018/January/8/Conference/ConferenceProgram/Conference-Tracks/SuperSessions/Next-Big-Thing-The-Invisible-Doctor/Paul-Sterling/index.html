Paul Sterling

Vice President of Emerging Products, UnitedHealthcare

Paul Sterling is Vice President of Emerging Products for UnitedHealthcare. In this role, Paul is focused on the integration of new and emerging technologies (e.g. internet of things, artificial intelligence, and data analytics) with contemporary health benefit plan designs.

Paul is dedicated to using technology to help solve the long-standing and important challenges facing the health care system. He brings more than 15 years of experience in health care and management consulting, holding various executive positions in product and business development at Optum, AccentCare, and Accenture.

Paul holds an MBA from the Carlson School of Management at the University of Minnesota and a bachelor of sciences degree in Management Information Systems from Minnesota State University, Mankato.
