Next Big Thing: The Invisible Doctor

  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Program
Share This Print This Page

3-4 PM January 9
Tech East, LVCC, North Hall, N257

Join CNET and a panel of luminaries rethinking the way we manage our health. Futurists discuss the fantastical future while realists are using today’s technology to improve lives. We’ll explore how the digital health industry will take signals from all parts of our lives and integrate them into personalized care.
