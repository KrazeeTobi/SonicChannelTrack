2018 Preview: FCC and FTC Commissioner Roundtable

  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Program
Share This Print This Page

3:30-4:30 PM January 9
Tech East, LVCC, North Hall, N253

FCC and FTC Commissioners discuss critical regulatory and policy issues, including: infrastructure, spectrum allocation, 5G, privacy, accessibility, the Internet of Things, regulatory reform, disruptive innovation, and technological convergence, among other hot topics.

CONFERENCE TRACK: Innovation Policy


Moderator
Julie Kearney
VP, Regulatory Affairs,
Consumer Technology Association

Speaker
Brendan Carr
Commissioner,
Federal Communications Commission

Speaker

FCC

Speaker

National Telecommunications and Information Administration
