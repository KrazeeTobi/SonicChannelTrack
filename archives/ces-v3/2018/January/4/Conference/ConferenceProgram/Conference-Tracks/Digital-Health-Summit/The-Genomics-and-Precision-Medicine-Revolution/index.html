The Genomics and Personalized Healthcare Revolution

  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Program
Share This Print This Page

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 10
Tech West, Venetian, Level 4, Lando 4304

The “one-size-fits-all” medical approach is a thing of the past. Advances in genomics, precision medicine and machine learning have led to powerful new discoveries. Learn how doctors use genetics, behavior, and lifestyle to match patients with the most effective treatments.

  • 11:30 AM-12 PM Unlocking, Decoding and Unleashing the Powers of DNA
  • 12-12:15 PM Nano-Diagnostics and Micro-Wearables With Mega Powers
  • 12:20-1 PM LUNCH & LEARN: Bots Will Run Your Health Using Mixed Reality and AI 

CONFERENCE TRACK: Digital Health Summit
$ Additional Registration Required
