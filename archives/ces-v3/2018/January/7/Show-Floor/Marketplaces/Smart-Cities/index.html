    Location: Westgate

    CES Smart Cities represents the entire connected ecosystem that brings together the technologies, solutions, players and audiences in the smart city sector including: IoT, 5G Connectivity, Transportation and Smart Automotive, Energy and Utilities, Health and Public Safety, Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics.

    If you’re a key decision maker, influencer, buyer or investor in the smart city arena, join us to see how smart cities will influence policies, transportation, cities and towns, our industry and our planet.

    IoT Infrastructure Pavilion

    The IoT Infrastructure Pavilion highlights many of the most influential technology providers in the IoT sector. This special pavilion and conference area is a must-see -- from markets including healthcare, automotive, energy, home appliances, and building management -- that are looking to deploy connected devices in the field. Exhibitors will offer answers on the toughest issues, including end-to-end security, life-cycle management, backbone integration, and speed-to-market. Nuts-and-bolts products and services on display include:

    • Software -- device/connection mgmt, analytics, security, devel kits
    • Hardware -- modules, gateways, components
    • Connectivity -- cellular, LP-WAN, satellite, near-range
    • Integration -- IT stack tie-ins on large or small scale
