Protecting the Internet Economy

  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Program
Share This Print This Page

2:15-3:15 PM January 10
Tech East, LVCC, North Hall, N253

American internet leadership is based on wise policy decisions, like not holding social media platforms liable for the actions of their users.  Now, some claim these protections go too far. Experts discuss the rights and responsibilities of internet companies regarding user-generated content.

CONFERENCE TRACK: Innovation Policy


Moderator
Michael Petricone
SVP, Government Affairs,
CTA

Speaker
Steven Marks
Chief, Digital Business & General Counsel,
Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA)

Technology Policy Federal Affairs Manager,
R Street Institute

Speaker

President & CEO,
