C Space Storyteller: Disney

  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Program
Share This Print This Page

1:15-1:45 PM January 10
Tech South, ARIA, Level 1, Pinyon Ballroom

In this Q&A discussion, Disney will share how it is changing the way it creates new content and experiences on emerging platforms. Today’s fans and families interact differently with their favorite Disney stories and characters than ever before, and Disney will share how it is storytelling for a new generation on social media platforms, via short-form video, and through augmented reality.

CONFERENCE TRACK: C Space Storytellers


Speaker
Kyle Laughlin
Senior Vice President, Games & Interactive Experiences,
Disney Consumer Products & Interactive Media

Speaker
Andrew Sugerman
Executive VP, Publishing and Digital Media,
Disney Consumer Products and Interactive Media
