Incorporating Drones and Self-Driving Vehicles into Communities

  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Program
Share This Print This Page

1-2 PM January 10
Tech East, LVCC, North Hall, N253

As new technologies promise increased safety, efficiency, and mobility, communities across the country consider how to treat drones and self-driving cars.  Consistent policies governing their use are necessary if we are to fully realize the benefits they offer society.

CONFERENCE TRACK: Innovation Policy


Moderator
Jamie Boone
Sr. Director, Government Affairs,
Consumer Technology Association

Speaker
Secretary Elaine L. Chao
U.S. Secretary of Transportation,
Department of Transportation

Speaker
Marjorie Dickman
Global Director / Associate General Counsel, IoT & Automated Driving Policy,
Intel Corporation

Speaker
Cory Hunt
Deputy Director,
Nevada Governor's Office of Economic Development

Speaker

White House Office of Science and Technology Policy

Speaker
Cherilyn Pascoe
Senior Professional Staff Member and Investigator,
US Senate Committee on Commerce, Science, and Transportation
