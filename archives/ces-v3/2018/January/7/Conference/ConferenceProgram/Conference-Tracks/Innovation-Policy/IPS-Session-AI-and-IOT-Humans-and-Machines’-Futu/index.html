2:30-3:15 PM January 9
Tech East, LVCC, North Hall, N253

Technology, from artificial intelligence to the internet of things, is rapidly changing the way we work and live. Learn about the big changes facing the American workforce, and how new tech will create new opportunities for the next generation of workers.
