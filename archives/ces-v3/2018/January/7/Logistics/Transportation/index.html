Get to and Around CES

With more than 170,000 attendees expected at CES 2018, consider the various transportation options in the Las Vegas region to get to and around the show.

Take advantage of the many free shuttles, ride the Las Vegas Monorail, or join a pool from your favorite ridesharing service.

Full Shuttle Schedule and Map

See the shuttle schedule and map for exact stops, shuttle routes, and all boarding locations. 

Download the shuttle schedule and map. (PDF)

See real-time transportation status. For current wait times for shuttles, the monorail, taxis and more, visit transitscreen.com/CES.

Look for TransitScreen monitors in the following locations: 

  • LVCC, South 1 Lobby
  • LVCC, Grand Lobby
  • Venetian, Level 1 Transportation Lobby
  • Venetian, Level 2, Venetian Ballroom Foyer
  • Sands, Level 2, Hall C Entrance
     

 

Hotel Shuttle Service

Take advantage of complimentary shuttle service to the LVCC and Sands/Venetian from most official CES hotels.

Tech Express Boarding Locations

  • LVCC: Central Hall 2 entrance
  • Sands/Venetian: Curbside on Sands Avenue


C Space Shuttle Boarding Locations

  • ARIA: Level 1 Convention Space, adjacent to the Bristlecone Ballroom
  • LVCC: Outside the Grand Lobby
  • Venetian: Underground shuttle area

*New this year, take the C Space shuttle to CES keynotes taking place at the Park Theater at the Monte Carlo on Wednesday. Service to this location will begin one hour prior to the keynote start time.

Las Vegas Monorail

Travel along the Strip and to the LVCC without waiting in traffic. Convenient station locations at: MGM Grand, Bally’s/Paris, Flamingo/Caesars Palace, Harrah’s/The LINQ, LVCC, Westgate and SLS. Trains run every 4-7 minutes. 

Discounts are available for CES attendees.

Ridesharing in Las Vegas

Uber, Lyft and other transportation network companies are available in Las Vegas. During the show, go to the designated pickup locations to meet your driver. 

Pickup Locations

  • LVCC: Gold or Bronze Lots
  • Sands: Main porte-cochere
  • Venetian: the valet area in the parking garage

Taxis

Hail a taxi at the front of the LVCC (North or South Halls) and all other show venues.

Looking to jump the taxi lines? Download the Bandwagon app (www.bandwagon.io) to decrease taxi line wait times and congestion at the LVCC. CES attendees can use Bandwagon to connect with other conference attendees headed the same way, skip the taxi line while saving cash and carbon dioxide. For more information about Bandwagon at CES tweet us @HiBandwagon.
