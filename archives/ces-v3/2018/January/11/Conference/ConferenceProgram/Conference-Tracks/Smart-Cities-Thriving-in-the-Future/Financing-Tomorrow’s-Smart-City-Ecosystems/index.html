2:30-3:30 PM January 9
Tech East, Westgate, Level 1, Pavilion 9

Funding tomorrow’s smart city ecosystems will be challenging as city budgets are constrained. Listen to this group of experts discuss the latest innovations and trends in smart city financing and how they are working together to fund their initiatives.
