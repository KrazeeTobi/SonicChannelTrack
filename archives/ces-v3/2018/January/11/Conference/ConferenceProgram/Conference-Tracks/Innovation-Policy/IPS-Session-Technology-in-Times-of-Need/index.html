Sharing Economy: Tech to the Rescue

  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Program
Share This Print This Page

1-2 PM January 11
Tech East, LVCC, North Hall, N253

The sharing-economy has revolutionized life for people of all backgrounds. From providing transportation in underserved communities to sharing homes during hurricanes, companies are enhancing services for a diverse set of users and circumstances. Experts and policymakers discuss the future of these innovations.
