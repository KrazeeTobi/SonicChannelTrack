11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 11
Tech East, LVCC, North Hall, N253

An urgent labor shortage of qualified, high-skilled workers exists in the US today. Companies have high demand for employees fluent in STEM skills. Discover how we must educate our existing and future workforce to succeed in the ever-changing global and digital economy.
