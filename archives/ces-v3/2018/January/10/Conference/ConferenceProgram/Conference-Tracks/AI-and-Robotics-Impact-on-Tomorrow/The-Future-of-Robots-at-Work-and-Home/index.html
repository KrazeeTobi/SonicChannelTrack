The Future of Robots at Work and Home

  • Home
  • |
  • Conference
  • |
  • Conference Program
Share This Print This Page

11:30 AM-12:30 PM January 11
Tech East, LVCC, North Hall, N258

A look at the future of robotics and their role as companions and co-workers. What are the challenges as robots become integrated into society? Learn about advancements in AI, machine learning and the potential when paired with increasingly sophisticated robotics.

CONFERENCE TRACK: AI and Robotics Impact on Tomorrow
