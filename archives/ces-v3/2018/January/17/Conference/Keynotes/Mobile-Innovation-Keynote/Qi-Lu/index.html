Vice Chairman, Group President and COO

Baidu

10-11 AM January 10

Venetian, Palazzo Ballroom

Dr. Lu holds both bachelor and master degrees in computer science from Fudan University in Shanghai and a PhD in computer science from Carnegie Mellon University. He also holds over 40 US patents in his name.

At Baidu, Dr. Lu is in charge of products, technology, sales, marketing and operations.

Prior to joining Baidu, Dr. Lu previously was the Global Executive Vice President of Microsoft, leading one of Microsoft’s three business units, and before that was the Executive Vice President of Yahoo!
