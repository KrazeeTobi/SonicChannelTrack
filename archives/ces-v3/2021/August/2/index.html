CES 2022 Will Take Place in Las Vegas and Digitally

CES®, the world's most influential technology event, will return to Las Vegas, Nevada — and also digitally — for CES 2022, giving a global audience access to major brands and startups, as well as the industry's most influential leaders and advocates.

For those launching a product, looking for media coverage, garnering leads, building a brand or meeting new partners, CES is where business gets done — fuel the future of your brand by exhibiting at CES 2022. Contact our sales team to learn more about participation opportunities currently available, including open exhibit space, meeting rooms, hotel suites and more.
