Dates and Hours

The all-digital CES® 2021 will take place January 11-14, 2021.

JANUARY 11 — Exclusive media-only access

JANUARY 12 — Exhibitor showcase and conference programming

JANUARY 13 — Exhibitor showcase and conference programming

JANUARY 14 — CES partner programming

Exhibitor Showcase Hours

  • January 12: 7 AM-8 PM EST
  • January 13: 7 AM-8 PM EST


Outside these hours, exhibitors may not respond to questions immediately.

Programming Hours

  • January 12: 7:15 AM-6:15 PM EST
  • January 13: 8 AM-7 PM EST


These hours are inclusive of the live anchor desk.

Scheduling and Holding Meetings

Connect, collaborate and do business with CES 2021 exhibitors, media and attendees with meetings held on Microsoft Teams. 

  • Schedule meetings: December 8-January 15 
  • Hold meetings: January 10, 12 AM-January 15, 11:59 PM EST

Digital Venue Available Through Feb. 15

Registered CES 2021 attendees can continue to engage with the CES 2021 digital venue until February 15. Features and content that will remain available include

  • Exhibitor showcases.
  • Programming to watch on demand.
  • One-to-one attendee messaging.

CES®, owned and produced by the Consumer Technology Association (CTA)®, is the most influential tech event in the world — the proving ground for breakthrough technologies and global innovators.
