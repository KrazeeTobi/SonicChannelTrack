CES 2022 Will Take Place in Las Vegas and Digitally

CES®, the world's most influential technology event, will return to Las Vegas, Nevada — and also digitally — for CES 2022.

The global stage for innovation, CES convenes the tech industry, giving a global audience access to major brands and startups, as well as the industry's most influential leaders and advocates.

CES is where the world's biggest brands do business and meet new partners, and the sharpest innovators hit the stage. Owned and produced by the Consumer Technology Association (CTA)®, CES features every aspect of the tech sector.
