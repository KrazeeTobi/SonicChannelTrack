Charging Stone Xylo and Charging Stone Lamp Nox

Einova by Eggtronic
  • 2023 Honoree in Mobile Devices & Accessories
  • Mobile Devices & Accessories
Engineered out of carefully selected marble, the Charging Stone Xylo and Charging Stone Lamp Nox wireless charging pads blend the elegance of marble with Einova’s revolutionary E2Watt wireless charging technology to offer the fastest, safest, most energy-efficient wireless charging solution in the industry, with a 15W fast-charge output. Both come in a selection of multiple marble variations; however, the Charging Stone Lamp Nox comes with a built-in warm-white lamp, thus enhancing the design and functionality of the stone.
