[275] Sega of America are ruining Sonic!

From：  Rozie

http://www.sonicanime.net/forums/index.php?showtopic=8575
http://www.sonicstadium.org/board/index.php?showtopic=10278

Fans are disgusted that the entire Sonic cast for the video games was replaced by the childish sounding 4Kids cast. No disrespect to the 4Kids people, some of their VAs are all right but they do it very much for the children.

Ryan and the others were not contacted regarding this - until recording had already began! This is suicide for Sega of America as most people prefer the game VAs by far, it will damage sales and lead to more importing of Japanaese versions.

Most western Sonic fans these days are in their late teens and twenties! That doesn't mean they want guns and violence, just that they want to be treated with respect with mature plotlines and voice actors. Just because Sonic is an animal character doesn't mean he has to be immature; I don't remember Sonic 3 being so.

So if anyone from Sonic Team is reading this, or anyone who knows Sonic Team, please get them to do something about this Tragedy. It was a great mark of disrespect, especially to the late Deem Bristow who would have been deeply saddened to be replaced like this, and plain out disgusts me.

At the very least, make sure to include english subtitles with the japanese versions, and the japanese dub with the english versions(also with english subtitles).

And why is Shadow the Hedgehog an E all of a sudden? A lot of people were upset that Shadow got a gun, but that doesn't mean it should be kiddied down like Sonic Heroes was.

Please make more games on the par of Sonic CD and Sonic 3 & Knuckles! And why weren't Chaotix and Arcade on Gems Collection..? Is it something to do with Mighty? Bring him back please! If you're ever stuck for ideas, check out the British Fleetway Sonic the Sonic, it's the best alternate Sonic Universe around.
