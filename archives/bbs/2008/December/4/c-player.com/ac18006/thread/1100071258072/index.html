[945] Re:Please Rehire Ryan Drummond as Sonic's English Voice

From：  HeavensBounty

>
> Dear Sonic Team:
>
> Yesterday, I, along with some of my colleagues, called the main Sonic Team office and were told to post our comments here on the BBS. We would be most appreciative if you could read them.
>
> I am writing on behalf of literally thousands of English-speaking Sonic fans. Ever since 2005 when the game "Shadow the Hedgehog" came out, the original English video game voice cast for the Sonic series was completely replaced by the 4Kids Entertainment dub cast of Sonic X. Fans were very upset at the news due to the fact that now many of the characters did not seem the same anymore. Most notable and unfortunate was Sonic the Hedgehog himself. His new voice actor, Jason Griffith, has often been criticized as sounding dull, dorky, and overall not fitting for a speedy teen hedgehog like Sonic. While we have nothing against Mr. Griffith personally, we simply feel that he does not fit the iconic role of Sonic.
>
> While we would appreciate it if you could rehire all of the original voice actors (aside from the late Deem Bristow as Dr. Eggman), we would be most appreciative if you could at least rehire Ryan Drummond to voice the blue hedgehog himself. Ryan put his heart and soul into the role, and he truly brought Sonic to life. He captured Sonic's personality perfectly too - energetic, impatient at times, yet always willing to help a friend in need.
>
> In case you are not aware of the magnitude of this issue, I will present to you a few pieces of evidence that support the fact that the majority of English speaking Sonic fans want Ryan Drummond to return.
>
> First is a petition that has been around since the voice cast switch first took place. If everyone who signed the petition were to stop buying Sonic games, Sega would lose roughly a quarter of a million dollars on each game.
> http://www.petitiononline.com/a4kvaisg/petition.html
> What is also cool about this is that signature number #1970 is signed by Ryan Drummond himself, in which he wrote:
> "Wow. Can I even sign this? Is it LEGAL?!? I hope every single one of you that signs this petition reads this:
> THANK YOU, I LOVE YOU ALL. Sonic fans are the BEST, MOST SUPPORTIVE people in the whole world!!! And you can quote me on that.
> Long Live Sonic!!!! (www.RyanDrummond.com)"
>
>
> Secondly is one of the many online polls regarding the voice issue:
> http://sbg6.deviantart.com/journal/poll/155680/
> The poll shows over 480 fans preferring Ryan Drummond (and the rest of the original cast), while only 35 (as of posting this) prefer Jason Griffith & the rest of the new 4Kids cast.
>
> Perhaps the biggest campaign that Ryan Drummond fans did was known as the "Ryan for Brawl" Campaign. Just a few days after Nintendo announced Sonic's inclusion in Super Smash Brothers Brawl, fans began calling Nintendo of America, asking if they could try to negotiate with Sega to get Ryan Drummond to voice Sonic in the game. Nintendo has reported that over 2,000 calls were made requesting Ryan's return, and you can feel free to contact them for further confirmation of this. Fans continued to call up until a few weeks until Brawl's American release. Unfortunately, despite the huge amount of calls, those requests were left unanswered.
>
> Fans have also been calling up Sega of America and Sega Europe as well, although the two divisions cannot directly do anything about the situation. Sega of America has reported getting tons of calls on the issue, and you can even talk to them about it and they will undoubtably confirm it. Not only that, but some of us fans have gone far enough as to call Sega of Japan all the way from America on the issue, and finally you, Sonic Team, as we know that you are the ones who are in control of the voice cast.
>
> We fans love Sonic so much that we want him to be the best he possibly can be. With future games appearing to be getting
> back on the right track, the only thing now that is missing is Sonic's voice. Please rehire Ryan Drummond as soon as you can. Ryan himself has stated numerous times that he absolutely loved voicing Sonic and would do it again in a heartbeat if given the opportunity. He is someone who treats voice acting for the series as much more than just a job - he truthfully loves Sonic as a character, and that shows in his magnificent portrayal of the blue hedgehog. You can contact Ryan at http://ryandrummond.com. He would be absolutely thrilled to hear from you.
>
> Thank you very much for your time, and we all hope we can see Sonic as he should be in the English games soon.
>
> Sincerely,
> Thousands of Loyal Sonic Fans
>
>

[148] Re:Please Rehire Ryan Drummond as Sonic's English Voice

From：  TrueBlueJ7

Dear SONIC TEAM,

I've been a fan of Sonic the Hedgehog for a long time now. Of all the voices Sonic had over the years, the ones that really suited his attitude and voice we would picture in him, were Jaleel White's and Ryan Drummond's voices. Jaleel White voiced Sonic in the old DiC cartoon shows.

I love how Sonic sounded with Ryan Drummond's voice. He suited him. Ryan could give Sonic the right voice tone in the right situation. He's not perfect, but he didn't have the same problems Jason deals with.
Jason Griffith seems to have some trouble voicing Sonic. When he wants to sound energetic, he sounds like he's a teenager entering puberty. When he tries to solve that, he ends up sounding unemotional. When he gets the tone or emotion right, he sounds too deep for Sonic. In my opinion he did well in Sonic and the Secret Rings, but still, not as good as Ryan. In Super Smash Bros. Brawl, he also did well. Except for the "Too easy! Piece of cake!" line. Ryan said that in Sonic Adventure 2, and someone told Jason to say it too. But he sounded horrible. It's clearly something he can't pull out: sound more like Sonic. Lots of people say that the way Jason makes Sonic sound is the directors' fault. To me, that's another reason why he shouldn't voice Sonic. He has bad directors, while Ryan didn't have those, and voiced Sonic just fine.

Jason Griffith is OK, but not good enough. In several games, I've seen him sound either well or plain horribly. He hasn't been capable of sounding energetic and cool, without making us wish he could have made a better job with them. Ryan has his share of bad lines, but they've dropped down in Heroes. He has shown more signs of improving in 4 games than Jason in lots.

Please hire back Ryan Drummond. We, the fans, think he's the right voice for Sonic. Jason doesn't give Sonic the same energy we fans grew up hearing.

The image of a character is something you need to look out for. A character's voice is part of his/her image. Sonic's image is being threatened because of Jason's voice. It's not all Jason's voice's fault, it's also because of the poor stories. But going that way, I can only tell you to find better writers, or tell yours to find inspiraton elsewhere. About Sonic's personality, Jason's voice makes him look bad, and it's too late to change his image, the image the older fans have for such an iconic character. We all know what Sonic means to us, how we feel when we look at him, or play one of his games. Jason Griffith can't do this job as well as the previous actors. Sometimes he sounds really well, there are some lines that I enjoy. But the list of lines I don't like is much larger. I've heard enough to know Ryan Drummond is better fit for the job. His enthusiasm became part of Sonic's character, it made him sound like the hedgehog we grew up with. Ryan is probably not as good as Jaleel White, but as time flew by, Ryan became the new Jaleel. And Jaleel suits better the short MegaDrive Sonic, while Ryan suits the taller and green-eyes Sonic that first appeared in Sonic Adventure.

So we all ask you to hire Ryan back.
It'll mean a lot to us if you hire him for Sonic Chronicles and Unleashed. If you don't, make sure these are the last games Jason voices Sonic. But if you fire him, give him notice. You fired the old voice actors without notice, and that wasn't right. Some people see that as business. Well, firing someone because of any kind of need is normal, it's business. Not giving notice is disrespectful, and that's probably the reason why so many people want you to give the old voices their job back. Because of the way you fired them, because of the poor reasons that made you do that, because they were right for the characters, and because, since you acted wrong, we want to see you fixing that mistake, so that we can give you another chance. We, the fans need to see you acting like real people, so that we're less hesitating on buying one of your products. Believe it or not, people are sensitive to that.

Some people hate you, some of them, for the wrong reasons. Some people are angry, and there are people who were angry before, but not anymore, like me. Now I'm hoping you fix your mistakes. Sonic Unleashed shows me that you're fixing lots of them. I say, keep up the good work, get better voices than the current ones, and keep using the Hedgehog Engine in future games. But the way you're dealing with Sonic's image is still a mistake that needs to be fixed as quickly as possible. Sonic's and most of the characters' images, for that matter.

P.S. By the way, about the 4Kids voices, they came after the old voices, which had given us a certain image of the characters. After the 4Kids voices came, the characters didn't give us the same feeling. It's either because of the dialogs, that make them say weird things (it happened in Sonic Heroes too, though), or because of their voices, that sound a lot differently. I understand if it's better if you hire a woman to make a young boy's voice, like what's happening with Tails. But Amy Palant makes him sound too much like a girl, and she doesn't give him the young boy charm he had when he was voiced by actual boys. You need to hire a better woman for the job, or go back to the young boys. I hate to say this, but Amy Palant is totally wrong for Tails. Dan Green makes Knuckles sound too dumb. Jason Griffith makes Shadow sound like he's whispering and always serious. Shadow is serious, that's how he is, but not the boring type of serious. David Humphrey made him sound cool, energetic, not boring at all, and serious. Mike Pollock is probably the best voice actor in the recent games. He makes Eggman sound propperly. I have nothing against Pete Capella's voice, which suits Silver's personality. Bella Hudson makes a good job voicing Blaze. About the other characters she voices, the ones that will appear in future games should get a different voice. I think at least Mike Pollock, Pete Capella and Bella Hudson should continue voicing the mentioned characters. All the rest should be replaced.

If you'll hire Ryan Drummond back, try and call whoever voiced each character in Sonic Heroes. I'm convinced most of them will take the job back.
