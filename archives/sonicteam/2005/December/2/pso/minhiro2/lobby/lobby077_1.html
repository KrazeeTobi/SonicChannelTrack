Information on Quest released in October !

The long-waited sequel to the Phantasmal World series was released, where your goal is to wipe out enemies that emerged in large quantity across Gal Da Val Island!
The place of duty this time is the Control Tower area where a multitude of giant enemies are crammed. Break through this challenge with the benefit of new help!

Gal Da Val Island, the whole area of which turned into "Phantasmal World", as massive enemies suddenly appeared in each place of the island.
Hunters who go forth carrying new equipment called "AMULET" which Lab prepared in order for them to accomplish their commission.



The request of this time is to exterminate enemies in Control Tower where a great number of huge and powerful ones are roaming. This can be described as the most dangerous, without doubt.



Therefore, this time, it became possible to get the supply of recovery items in the facilities established in Control Tower.
Challenge a series of difficult fights as you make sufficient preparations here!



It is inevitable that even experienced soldiers will face uphill battles against a host of enemies that appear in various locations inside Tower in combinations of types you have never seen before!



However, the effect of "BLESSING" protects Hunters that is cast to the equipped of AMULET!
Overcome difficulties, making the best use of this effect!



Can you utilize new gimmicks effectively, fulfill your mission safely and return?
Please look forward to the fourth edition of the popular series, "Phantasmal World #4"!
