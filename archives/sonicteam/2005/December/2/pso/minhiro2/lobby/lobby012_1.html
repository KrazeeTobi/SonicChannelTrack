"The West Tower" will be released on 24 June !
 With "Seat of Heart" and "The East Tower", the research of Control Tower has finally begun on a large scale. However, all you players, haven't you noticed anything strange while you explore there ? ...Yes, through the glasses of Control Tower, there can be seen another Control Tower, for some reason !!!?

"The existence of an entry point to the West Tower was discover"
The order of this time is to go into this "West Tower" and secure the route to the uppermost floor.



The approach gate to the West Tower lies in Seabed Plant.
Furthermore, it is said that the entrance is in the area where no one has ever stepped into... !?
It is possible for you to locate the entrance without difficulty ?



The West Tower is of the high difficulty level that largely surpass that of the "East Tower".
All those ferocious enemies will pounce upon players from the beginning without mercy !!



However the team is in harmony, unless you brace your nerves, you will never reach the uppermost floor.
Can you clear this "Quest for the advanced players" ??



"You helped my father before right?"
