Here's much-awaited information on Quests released in October.

Gallon: "I'd like you guys to appear in a movie I will shoot !"

The client is of course this man !
Surprisingly, he says he would like you to appear in a 'movie' he himself will direct.



AMY: "Which one ?"

We will get you to aim at rare, hidden treasure, following the scenario prepared beforehand.
Overcome various trials waiting for you coolly, which is a proper manner for movie stars !



???: "... We are the ones who used to hold the same aspirations as thine..."

If you think this is a mere fun Quest..., that's totally wrong !
This is a contemplation of that particular Gallon, so it cannot be an easy job.
After you went through unexpected accidents, what on earth will be waiting for you...?

Quest title: Gallon's Treachery
Genre: Online Quest/ Retrieval
Number of players: 2- 4 players





AMY: "Let's hurry !"

We will have you experience VR that the principal government newly developed, commonly called "RES".
"RES" will evaluate your fighting ability comprehensively, based on time taken to clear this Quest or reactions to your mates' deaths, as well as the number of defeated enemies.
Aim to make a high score as you use all your power worked up through daily effort !




"I am the programmer of 'RES'...
But I included a little trick in the program behind the back of the principal.
Try typing in this secret code I will give you now into the terminal placed on Level 1."

Of course, also this Quest is full of secret elements !
Try and play it together with various friends !

Quest title: Towards the Future
Genre: Online Quest/ VR
Number of players: 1- 4 players
