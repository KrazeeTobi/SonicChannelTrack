This is my boyfriend's character...I illustrated her with the Elenor Mag to give the effect of angel's wings, but a bit more robotic than the actual Angel Wing mag. (Besides, androids can't use the actual winged mags)
You see, I met my boyfriend on PSO about 3 years ago or so, back when it was on the Dreamcast. Back then, we were just friends. But recently, we became more than that. But if we'd never met on PSO, we'd never be in love today, and I owe a huge debt of graditude to Sonic Team for PSO. You gave me the love of my life! Many thanks from Shotie and Ping!

私と彼は3年ほど前にPSOで出会いました。その頃はドリームキャストでプレイをしていました。
当時は単なる友達でしたが、最近になってそれ以上の存在になりました。
でも、もしPSOで出会わなかったなら、現在お互いを好きになることは無かったでしょう。
