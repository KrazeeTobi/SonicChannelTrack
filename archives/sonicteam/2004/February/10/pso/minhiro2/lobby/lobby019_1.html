2003.08.22

Information on new Quests released on August 21 !

On 21 August, distribution of new Quests was started. The ones that became available this time are 6 Quests including Challenges and Battles.

　

Challenge: Stage 5 and Stage 6
Challenge (for 2 - 4 players)
Just a single mistake can be fatal to you ! The chapter of Challenge Mode enters Mines ! Lots of powerful enemies will appear such as Sinow Beat or Garanz. Can you survive this !?



Battle 4
Stage: Temple/10 minutes. With players' Levels equalized, they will scramble for Meseta scattered all over Map.

Battle 5
Stage: Temple/10 minutes. With no Level restriction. Serious battle without recovery. Defeat other players and seize Meseta !

Battle 6
Stage: Spaceship/10 minutes. Players will attack each other through the wall in one room and battle it out.



Lost ICE SPINNER
The second edition of the familiar "Recover lost (weapon)" series.
Again a request came from Hunter Hopkins.
He asks you to regain his precious Ice Spinner that was swallowed by De Rol Le.
Let's team up all together and defeat De Rol Le !
