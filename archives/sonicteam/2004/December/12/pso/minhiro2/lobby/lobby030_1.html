2003.10.30

New Quests have been released for the EU version!
The two Quests have been newly released that includes a new one for Episode I & II!

　

Irene: "Test of this time is what measures your 'force of unity'".

Principal Government decided to hold a test to measure 'force of unity' among Hunters, as it took notice of the power that is generated by Hunters active in teams cooperating with and giving influence to each other.



AMY: "Have you found capsules ?"

In this Quest, you will retrieve as many as possible capsules that were previously set up in the area, as all team members work together.

The number of collected capsules are straightly reflected in the team's evaluation and it will be marked on Hunters' grade report, "Hunters Report".



AMY: "What is this ???"

Not only enemies but unforeknown gimmicks will forestall you all.
Develop a satisfactory strategy within the team and enter the test !

Quest title: Labyrinthine Trial
Genre: Online Quest/ VR
Number of players: 2- 4 players




"Today's Rate", which was talked about with its tricky gimmicks, finally appears on the GC version !
　
Contents of the request is "to sneak into the highly secret facility of Lab and erase research data".
Can you safely destroy host computer ?

Quest title: Today's Rate
