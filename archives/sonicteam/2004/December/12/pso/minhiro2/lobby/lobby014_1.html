Tails' Volunteer Story

 Contributer:Azrael

This month, Sonic and his friends appeared on Lobbies !
Azrael sent us screenshots of them, who were hiding in various spots, along with a story.


In Celebration of Sonic's Birthday, Tails decided to help out the Medics of Pioneer 2 by volunteering his services.



His work was so good that Principal Tyrell spoke to the General, Natasha Milarose, and they gave Tails access to the lab to help out the Medics there.



Tails was tired so he decided to hide and rest. I found him and told him that he needs to get back to work.




Tails then decided to find Sonic. Sonic tried to help him hide, but as I said before, nobody gets the better of Azrael.

We much appreciate your work-up that effectively uses as many as four images.
The story is very Tails-like, in which while he pushes himself to the max for Sonic, he gets tired and hides away !
Thanks for the truly cute story.
