STRENGTH

HOD2


Strength is a huge, chainsaw-wielding zombie. Its weak point
is its head, but because it blocks your shots with its
chainsaw, it’s extremely difficult to hit. You can slowly
wear away at Strength’s health by shooting its body, too,
but that won’t stop Strength's forward movement, so it’s
probably best just to aim for its head.


Strength’s attack patterns can be broadly divided into three types.


[1]Chainsaw Slash
Strength will rush at you with tremendous force and try to
slash you with its chainsaw. There’s no other way to stop
it except to aim at its head and fire repeatedly. When shooting,
it’s best to aim slightly to one side rather than at the center
of its head,

and you should try to avoid hitting its chainsaw.


[2]Hatchet Throw

Strength will attack you by throwing hatchets that’ve been
embedded in its body. The hatches will fly at you in an arc.
Shoot them down and then go back to firing at Strength’s head.


[3]Jump Down and Slash
Strength will jump down from the tops of walls and try to slash
you. Just before it lands, you’ll have a chance to shoot its head,
but the window of opportunity is extremely narrow. The key is to
aim for its chin area as soon as it jumps and to fire repeatedly.



At times, Strength will threaten you with its chainsaw raised
menacingly. It leaves itself wide open when it does this, so fire at
its head relentlessly.



Depending on what route you took, your battleground will either
be the east side or the west side of the coliseum, but the basic
strategy will be the same.
