IN CLOSING

Now I’ve told you everything I have to tell.


In these battles where a split-second’s hesitation could mean
the difference between life and death, I’m sure the things you’ve
learned from this file will help keep you alive.


But just because you’ve learned a thing or two here, don’t think
you can go in cocky. There are some things you can only learn on
the actual battleground.


And remember that the strategies I’ve written here are only one
way to go about things. There are lots of other strategies that might
fit your skills and style better.


Finding the strategies that are right for you is the only way you’ll
ever become a true AMS agent.


Good luck, and continue to hone those skills.
