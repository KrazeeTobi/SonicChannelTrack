What is CHAO ?
What is Chao? What kind of creature is it?
CHAO is a strange little living things which has a pudding-like body but it's life force is very weak. CHAO lives at the place called CHAO GARDEN. You can bring up your pretty CHAOs at this GARDEN.

What is CHAO GARDEN ?
What is Chao garden? What kind of place it is?
CHAO GARDEN is a paradise for CHAOs. There is a nice and clean pond so CHAO loves to stay here. But I have heard that GARDEN is not one kind. Try many things to make your way to the another GARDEN.

How do I get close with CHAOs ?
What's the best way to get along Chao? How do I get close to Chao?
You should give them their favorite food. CHAOs are much like a human baby. So if you pat or embrace them gently, they will be delighted.

How can I go to the Chao kindergarten?
What's Chao kindergarten? How do I get there?
The Chao kindergarten is a very important place for raising your Chao. You can name your Chao there and take them to the health center to get them checked out. Your Chao can learn a variety of things from the lessons found there. You can also go shopping! By the way, I, Professor Chao, am the principal of the Chao kindergarten. If you have any questions about Chao, visit the principal's office anytime.

You can get to the Chao kindergarten through the Chao lobby. The entrance has a red roof. Just go through the entrance to get to the Chao kindergarten. However, in the story mode, when you exit out of the Chao garden, the story will begin. Once the story begins, you cannot take your Chao to the Chao kindergarten. Please keep this in mind. If you want to go anywhere in the Chao world without any restrictions, select the stage select screen to go to the Chao world. This is useful. Don't forget!

My Chao don't grow up...
My Chao don't grow up even if I give them a lot of small animals, Chaos drives and tree fruits. Their statistics went up, but why they don't grow up?
Chao grow up depending on the length of time that you spend in the Chao garden. You don't grow up right away even if you eat a lot, right? In order to raise your Chao, you need to spend more time in the Chao garden with your Chao. Keep giving them small animals and tree fruits and take good of them!

Is it true that Chao become adults?
Is it true that Chao become adults?
What will change when they become adults?
Yes, that's true. Chao who were born from an egg will eventually become adults. Their body size doesn't change much, though.

Chao also have a life span and will die when their time comes. Every creature dies eventually, and it's very sad to say good-bye. If you become a very good friend of your Chao, Chao will remember you and will be reborn from an egg after they die. This is called reincarnation. Chao is a truly mysterious creature. You can see the chart of the Chao's life cycle below. This may give you more information about a Chao's life when you raise them.

How do I get more Chao?
I only have 2 Chao in my garden...
How do I get more?
When Chao grow up and are ready to matea flower will bloom around them. Once this Chao is ready, you must find another Chao ready to mate. They will perform a short dance, after which an egg will be created. This is how Chao are born. In the case you want to increase the chance of your Chao to mate, feed them "Heart" fruits.

How do I plant the seeds?
When I buy seeds for my Chao, he just throws them.
How do I plant the seeds?
So you bought some seeds, huh?!
You need a special tool to plant the seeds. I guess you can call it a tool to dig the ground with... Well, it called a "Scoop." To get the scoop, you have to win the third level of the Crab Pool in Beginner's Race of Chao Race.
Remember, if the Chao is still crawling and has the scoop, he will not be able to plant the seed so be careful and raise him well!
