Going for the Gold part1

Advanced Tactics

Gold Medals help you unlock items in the Special Book as well as certain skills.
You need to clear missions within a certain time to earn Gold Medals, so speed becomes key. The information below describes how to customize Sonic for speed, as well as tips for each mission type.


Recommended Skills
Charger, All-Rounder, Quick High Step, Super Quick Air, Warp Boost, Super Speed-Up, Landing Boost, Cancel Boost, Splash Jump, Rocket Jump, H-Mega Expand, Homing Recovery, Quick Jump, Warp Dash
Default skills (8)

Skill point total: 288 (363 including defaults)
Equippable level for skills: Level 57 – 66


If you do not yet have enough skill points or levels, you can adjust by using less powerful versions of the skills listed above.
