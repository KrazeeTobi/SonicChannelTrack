Information on Quest released in September !

The much-waited sequel to the Phantasmal World series, where your goal is to wipe out enemies that emerged in large quantity across Gal Da Val Island, was released on September 28!
Utilize new gimmicks to get over fierce battles in the Seabed areas!!
Å@

Gal Da Val Island, the whole area of which turned into "Phantasmal World", as massive enemies suddenly appeared in each place of the island.
And the inside of the Seabed areas which Lab had been conducting research on was no exception... ...



The purpose of this Quest is exterminate enemies in the facilities and to secure the route to the computer in their innermost recesses.
As stopping it, a herd of powerful monsters launch into attacks on Hunters who go into there!



Unlike the open places on the earth's surface, you will be forced to fight in tight space particular to artificial buildings in some scenes.
Under this situation, you have no other choice but approach enemies, and it is not too much to say that it is impossible to avoid their attacks!



The machine newly developed in order to support Hunters who undertake this difficult mission, is "BARRIER FIELD".
This equipment that nullifies all sorts of attacks, although it cannot be described as perfect because it is still at the experimental stage, it would be definitely of your great help!



Can you successfully accomplish mission and return safely, utilizing "BARRIER FIELD" tactfully?
Please look forward to the third edition of the popular series, "Phantasmal World #3"!
