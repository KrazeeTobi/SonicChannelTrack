This is a challenge I've posed for a long time. It's come a long way from its inception, when I thought the answer was just 106.

The challenge is to start a new game save file, and get as many emblems as you can on that file. The only restriction is that you can't pick up any of the six Mystic Melody character upgrades. It may look like that restriction robs you of a seventh upgrade, but that isn't in fact the case (though with what's been discovered so far, that upgrade doesn't help anything--it might eventually be found to make the difference between 170 and 172).

Right now, on my dedicated MM-less file, I'm up to 164 emblems. Because one of the lost Chao procedures is incredibly random, I've done the trick that allows me to get to 165 and therefore 166 only on my main file. Right now I can ascertain that at least 168 emblems are possible this way.

The ones I'm missing in my 164 are:
-Aquatic Mine 3, 4, 5, and Knuckles All-A (100% sure these are possible)
-Egg Quarters 3, 4, 5, and Rouge All-A (85% sure these are possible)
-Eternal Engine 3, 4, 5, and Tails All-A (55% sure these are possible)
-Iron Gate 3, 4, 5, and Eggman All-A (3% sure these are possible)

Everything else has been done, at least for me. Does anyone want to try and see if they can solve one of the 3 remaining unsolved levels (or find an easy way to do Aquatic Mine so that the all-A actually has a realistic chance of happening and causing 168)?
