Lost World? That's one of my favorite stages! A couple tips are:

1) When you're activating the colored switches, after you hit the one on the upper level, run backwards along the wooden snake, and hit the red switch, it saves time.

2) Don't use the mirrors in the 2nd part of the stage.

3) When you're activating the switches in the final room, after you activate the 2nd one or something like that, run along the top of the lighted panels next to the ones leading up(don't walk on them, walk on the top of them). If you did that one the right one, you will get to the switch making the trail of rings to light dash on very early.
