How to score lots of bonus points with different characters.


Sonic and Shadow
In stages such as City Escape and Radical Highway, hit as many ramps as you can going down them as fast as you can to pull off a neat trick and score bonus points with the A button at the top ramp before you run right off it.
Use light dash whenever you see a nice set of lined up rings to get some points and go fast, this can be practiced at Metal Harbor.
Finally, about Rails, in some stages like Metal Harbor and Green Forest, you'll see hidden rails in the stages on the sides when you're running through loops you can take to get some points off there.
You can also pull neat tricks off of the rails to score points as well in the stage Final Rush. At the end of most
rails, you'll see signs of checks floating above at end of a rail. Keep grinding until you reach the very end of it, then press A to do a neat trick off and score points for it.
Also in Final Rush, if you're very skilled at the stage, you could also try grinding for extra points on the really short rails you see when you're on the ridiciously long rail that takes you down in a spirial(sp?) pattern.
That's all I can say about Sonic/Shadow's stages overall.


Tails and Eggman
Easier than it sounds, you get big points depending on how many lock-ons you got on enemies, so get as many lock-ons as you can on enemies before firing to score huge amounts of points. The only other to do is to beat the stage at a very short amount of time.

Knuckles and Rouge
You get points based on how quickly you get the pieces of the emerald and how many computer hints you used.
