In Radical Highway, don't run down the ramps, use Spindash! Go for all the boosts you can, and make the Light Dash your best friend!

In Green Forest, the ancient shrine is by where you land after you use one of the vines that spins around, just go forward until you reach the slope down with the checkpoint at the bottom. Instead of going there, go left and fall while hugging the side of the cliff. Use the Mystic Melody and bounce your way up using the Bounce Bracelet. If you charge up the Ancient Light, miss one of the Beetles, and land on the platform, you will miss doing the 'Yes!' speech!

In White Jungle, instead of using the vine right after the spring near the second Chao crate and the 3 5 ring jars, go back until you see the shrine and use it. Follow the path until you see the Spark Beetle. Homing attack it and use the invisible beetles as a spring. The Chao should be right in front of you.

For the other, I'm not sure.
