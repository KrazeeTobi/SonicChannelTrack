You won't notice at first, but a higher levels you can see a significant boost in stats, usually in the range of 50-100 points for a S rank stat at level 99. However, it costs an incredible amount to raise it that far on the GBA, so you really want to make your rings count. I recommend raising it three levels with the purple triangle fruit, and switching to the orange and repeating. That gets you the most bang for your buck.
