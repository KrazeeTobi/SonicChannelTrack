No I didn't. It was too hard. But if you don't know how, you have to go to challenge mode (to do it easier than story mode) and select a level that has a picture of a grey emerald below your grade. Play the level and find the golden key. Get to the end of the level without losing it by getting hit and losing your rings (or dying). You will then play the EMERALD CHALLENGE. Beat the emerald to the finish by getting spheres (especially the ones that are orange with stars on them) and pressing B repeatedly to boost foward. If you beat it, you keep the emerald and it will be colored instead of gray in the challenge mode list. It is easier to get to the EMERALD CHALLENGE as Team Rose, but Team Sonic and Team Dark can have an easier time beating the EMERALD CHALLENGE because they are faster.
