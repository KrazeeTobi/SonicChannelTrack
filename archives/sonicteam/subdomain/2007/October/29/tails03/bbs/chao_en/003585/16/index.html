Gigazubyte ID:015441 2005/05/04 08:19:16

and half some other color. They look a lot like chao of the solid color variety (ie: a two tone blue will be mostly blue)but they have tips that are darker or lighter than the normal variety, and their colors change as they go through evolutions, though not as much as normal colored chao. You can get two tones by breeding normal chao with solid color chao. Translucent chao are partly see through, as their name would suggest. Those you get by breding shiny chao with jewel chao (the special chao only avalible in the tiny chao garden).
