Some of your pokémon in your PC box may turn in Bad Eggs when you use GameShark codes, and worst of all, they never hatch, and you can't even get rid of the egg. I really hate it when that happens.

As for PKRS, it means Pokérus. It means your pokemon is infected with pokérus. Nurse Joy says it's a good thing because it helps boost slightly the stats of your pokémon when you are raising it. It only last for some time and when your pokémon recovers from pokérus, it will never become infected again. However, you can conserve the pokérus if you leave the infected pokémon stored in the PC box. If you want to infect other of your pokémon, all you have to do is keep your infected pokémon in the party with other pokémons and walk around a bit, then check the other pokémon's stats, if it doesn't get infected, keep walking around for a bit until it does.
