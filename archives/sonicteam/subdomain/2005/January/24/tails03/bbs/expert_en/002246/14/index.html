PSO Episode I&II Plus:
I hate to say it but I didn't play this game untill about three weeks ago and I wish I had bought it sooner. I truly regret not being able to play it online yet. If you haven't bought is yet, just take my word for it and go and buy it now!
Aside from being a great game period, PSO 1&2 plus is a great game offline for multi player and single player with the different modes and play forms. Although the multiplayer mode lacks a real story to play along with, nothing has been more fun than playing with my friends (PSO aliases will be used) Urameshi1956, DARTH VADER, GOVANATOR, and somone whose name I for got. With split-screen action play late into the night, the game has hundreds of chances for complete hilarity from playing tag to upclose and personal camera stealing to the hundreds of instances of item stealing and of course the goofy character models.
