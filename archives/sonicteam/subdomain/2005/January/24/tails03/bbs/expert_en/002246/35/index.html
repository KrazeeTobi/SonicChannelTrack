The sunglasses are in meteor herd. They are at the bottom of the lava pit. You should find a floating platform a bit above the ground with a meteor on it. Punch the meteor towards the nearest metal door next to the pit. It takes a few tries to get it right. That should reveal a metal box. Break that to activate a switch which will open the door to the pit. Then go down the pit to get to a platform with the sunglasses.
