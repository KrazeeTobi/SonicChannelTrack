1. PERFECT!(previous post)

If you get every ring in a level, it will say perfect on your ring total. You will automatically get an A rank in the level regardless of total points and time. When I discovered this and posted it, people would not believe me. So in June I posted a walkthrough of getting the rings in Metal Harbor Mission 1(the easiest, no shooting enemies). I have gone through and made the post shorter for number two.

2. Metal Harbor PERFECT rings(previous post)

Simply go through the level, almost none of the rings are hidden, here are the problem areas:

a. After the aircraft carrier, you will be taken up two pulleys to a high area. After you ride the second pulley, jump off. Bounce up to the platform the pulley is hanging from, this should be your 100th ring, if you passed each checkpoint.

b. This is possibly the hardest part to accomplish. Run up the walkways to where you would normally hit the spring and ride from a bar on the rocket. Run past it. A line of enemies will appear. Jump attack them to cross the "pit". Next will be a line of rings, light speed dash them(this is why we took this route). You will see a set of three springs in front of you. Hit them. Position yourself so that when you land, you land on the next spring that takes you to the next highest switch. You only have seconds to pull this off, so be fast! Practice makes perfect!(argh, another pun...)

c. You will proceed to board down a long room, when you're almost at the jump pads, jump over them. You will dislodge from the board. Run back up the tube getting all the rings you missed.

To help on b, try this: Before you trigger the countdown, jump onto the base of the rocket, you will see two springs. Hit the right one. This will let you skip three walkways.

You should end up at the end with 174/174 rings, you may have less if you didn't hit all the checkpoints or rings boxes. Don't worry, if you have the same number of rings as the total at the end you will have PERFECT!
