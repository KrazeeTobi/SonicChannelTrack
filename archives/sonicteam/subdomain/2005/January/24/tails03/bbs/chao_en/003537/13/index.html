but I have a few tips to add. First of all, when you're doing this it's easier if the chao you are raising is in the neutral garden, not the hero garden. Second, petting it is the most efficient way to turn it dark, but attacking it with hero characters can work as well. However, if you want the chao to reincarnate that isn't the way to go. The most important thing you should know is, it takes a long time for the chao to mature and transform (three hours); so it might be best to leave the game on and go do something productive while you wait. If you're going to do that, stand in the water, otherwise the chao gets annoyed when you don't feed or pet it for long strecthes of time.
