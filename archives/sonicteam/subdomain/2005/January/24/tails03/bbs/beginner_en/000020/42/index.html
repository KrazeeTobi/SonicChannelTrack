1. Knuckles/Rouge Faceoff

Go to the scene before Knuckles and Rouge fight in the center of Meteor Herd. You will notice that in the subtitles, each time "Lady" is shown it is capitalized. The meaning of this im not sure of, it could be some kind of text play by the ST(Sonic Team). It could perhaps be that way because it is important, such as some words were highlighted in Sonic Shuffle.

2. Japanese PSO Demo

Ok, for this one you have to bust out that demo you got with PSO V1. In the helicopter scene, you will notice that the pilot says "What the hell!?". In the english release however, he only says "What!?". It is possible that Japan can have more swearing in their games. In the US however, to get an E rating they had to exclude the swearing. Interesting, it seems that the Japanese can get away with more vulgarity than the US can...
