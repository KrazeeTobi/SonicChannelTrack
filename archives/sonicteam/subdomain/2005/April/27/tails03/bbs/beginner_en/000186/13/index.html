Claws the Wolverine: part three

Claws looked around. At first, nothing seemed different. "I wonder how far in time I went..." said Claws. Suddenly, there was a crashing sound. Huge robots came crashing through the trees. Claws had never seen robots, so he had no idea what was going on. I think that we all know who those robots belonged to. That's right, Dr.Eggman! Claws was terrified. He tried to run, but was to scared to move. The robots closed in on him. All that could be heard was the sound of screams and lasers being fired.

later, at a hospital:

Claws woke up on a soft bed. He didn't know where he was, or what had happened, but he did know one thing; he was safe from those robots.... for now. Suddenly, Sonic walked into the room. "About time you woke up." He said to Claws. "Are you okay?" "Who are you?" asked Claws. "I'm Sonic! Sonic the hedgehog!" replied Sonic. "How did I get here?" asked Claws. "Well," Said Sonic. "I was walking through the forest, and I saw a bunch of robots all standing in a circle. I decided to check it out. They were beating you up! So I beat them up, and brought you here." Claws looked confused. "Robots?" He asked. "You mean those weird metal things?" "Wow." He thought to himself. "The world's changed a lot......."
