The treasure scope and sunglasses allow Knuckles and Rouge to see things they couldn't before. First for Knuckles sunglasses go to Meteor herd. Then look for a platform that has a blue boulder on it. Make sure it faces a door on the base of the big tower where him and Rouge fight. Smash the door by punching the boulder into it and press the switch. Climb into the tower and jump down the newly open floor. On the lowest platform you'll find the sunglasses. Now for Rouge. You'll need the iron boots and mystic melody. Mystic melody is on first Rouge level. On ledge to the right dig through the wall above pond and presto. Iron boots in Mad Space. Take rocket to gravity meteorite and they're in room in tower on top. Go to Security Hall, (by the wall this is the treasure scope now) set it to "collect 100 rings" for infinate time. When you land turn around and take crane to yellow floor turn left and go along floor till you rech the Chaos ruin. Play mystic melody and jump across new platforms. Kick steel crates and go through hall to get the tresure scope. Hope this helps.
