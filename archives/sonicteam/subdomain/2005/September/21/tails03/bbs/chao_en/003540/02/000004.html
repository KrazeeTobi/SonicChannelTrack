Two Tone Chao are simply Chao that are half normal.
When a monotone colour Chao breeds with a normal Chao, the baby Chao may come out as a "two tone".
We buy a Green Chao from the black market, and then it mated with a normal Chao, the possibilities of the baby Chao would be:
a) A Normal Chao
b) A Green Chao
c) A Green Half-Normal (Two Tone) Chao
