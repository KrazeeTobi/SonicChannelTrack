Hold on, Mr. Chao Breeder. You don't have to give your Chao ONLY one color of Chao drive to make it that type; for example, if you want your Chao to be a Flying type, he could recieve other Chaos Drives or animals, but he should have an outstanding Fly stat before evolving. It should be much bigger than any other stat.

And who said a Chao must only eat fruit to become a normal Chao? A normal Chao is simply a Chao that doesn't have one stat that doesn't stand out above the rest; a well-rounded Chao, you could say. Or a Chao who's Stamina stands out the most.
