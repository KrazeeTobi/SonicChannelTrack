Well...The fastest way to train a Chao is to stock up on chaos drives and buy some heart fruit at the blackmarket. Then feed a heart fruit to your chao, but if your chao is newborn it might not work!! If your Chao is grown up and has gone through its cocoon form, this is the best time to do it. After you give it the heart fruit, it will sit down and flowers will circle it. Then standing STRAIGHT infront of it, put the drive really close to it, but don't actually give the Chao the drive. Let it take it. If you put the drive TOO close to the Chao, it will take it, but it won't work!! If it does work correctly, the drive will go out of his hands, then Chao will have the power from the drive. The drive will still be reusable. (You could go days using only one drive)! This will take practice though. This hint is for the Nentindo Gamecube version Sonic Adventure 2 Battle.
