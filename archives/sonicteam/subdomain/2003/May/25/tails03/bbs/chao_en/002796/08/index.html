From IGN:
Sega and McDonald's Join Forces
Happy Meals to get a little bit happier.

February 18, 2003 - McDonald's and SEGA have entered into an agreement that will allow McDonald's to offer SEGA themed happy meal toys. Neat!
Due in stores this summer, the toys will feature characters from both Sonic the Hedgehog and Super Monkey Ball.

"Sonic the Hedgehog and Super Monkey Ball are two of SEGA's most popular video game franchises," said Mike Fischer, SEGA of America's vice president of marketing "SEGA's affiliation with McDonald's affords us the opportunity to generate increased exposure for these properties among a growing universe of gamers."

The folks at McDonald's also put down their Big Macs long enough to express their delight at the deal.

"We believe consumers will have a great reaction to the innovative Sonic the Hedgehog and Super Monkey Ball premiums found only in McDonald's Happy Meals this summer," said I mean Karlin Linhardt, senior director of U.S. marketing for the company, "SEGA's properties are extremely popular and we look forward to bringing them to McDonald's consumers through this unique happy meal offering."

Further details will be released at a mysterious later date. Summer's coming pretty quick, though, so you'd better start building up your tolerance to delicious saturated fats now.
