The Forest Spirit

Not so long ago, in a not so far away forest, there was a deer. But he was not just any deer, he was the spirit of the forest. He looked like a normal deer, but he was MUCH bigger, and had huge antlers, and a bright red face. His family had abandond him, but they left him a small dagger made of crystal, which he wore around his neck. This deer had no name, and he was very frightend, in the forest all alone. "How will I survive?" He thought to himself. Suddenly, a HUGE wolf bounded out of the forest. She was obviesly very old. She approched the young deer, and he was scared. "Do not fear me." The wolf said. She continued. "I saw you, and I know that you are the chosen one." The deer was suprised. "Chosen one?" he asked. "Chosen for what?" The wolf smiled. "Chosen to be the next forest spirit. I am the old forest spirit, and I'm getting ready to retire. You'd be a perfect replacment, I can tell." The deer wasn't scared anymore. "What do I do?" he asked. "Simple." said the wolf. "you must make sure that no-one destroyes this forest, and you also control life and death." "Life and death? Wow... That sounds hard." Said the deer. This made the wolf laugh. "HAHAHAHAHA!!" She said. "You don't have to worry about that. When you become the forest spirit, yoy get all sorts of powers." The deer was getting exited. "Powers? What kinds of powers?" He asked. "Oh, all kinds. Don't worry, you'll know how to use them." Said the wolf. "Okay, I'll do it!" Cried the deer. "Okay." said the wolf. "Don't move." She started mumbling weird words, and a cloud of purple smoke appeared around the deer! When the smoke cleared, the deer had beutiful golden fur instead of dull brown fur. Also, his antlers got HUGE!!!!!!! "You are now the forest spirit." said the wolf. "Tell me, what is your name?" That's when the deer remembered. He didn't have a name! "I don't have one..." he said. "No name? Well, we'll have to think of one for you..... How about.... Shishi Gami?" Asked the wolf. The deer liked the sound of it. "Okay." He said. "Shishi Gami it is." So, the new forest spirit walked off into the forest.

The End
