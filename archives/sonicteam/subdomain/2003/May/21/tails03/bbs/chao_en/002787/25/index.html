The wolf said. She continued. "I saw you, and I know that you are the chosen one." The deer was suprised. "Chosen one?" he asked. "Chosen for what?" The wolf smiled. "Chosen to be the next forest spirit. I am the old forest spirit, and I'm getting ready to retire. You'd be a perfect replacment, I can tell." The deer wasn't scared anymore. "What do I do?" he asked. "Simple." said the wolf. "you must make sure that no-one destroyes this forest, and you also control life and death." "Life and death? Wow... That sounds hard." "Powers? What kinds of powers?" He asked. "Oh, all kinds. Don't worry, you'll know how to use them." Said the wolf. "Okay, I'll do it!" Cried the deer. "Okay." said the wolf. "Don't move." She started mumbling weird words, and a cloud of purple smoke appeared around the deer! When the smoke cleared, the deer had beutiful golden fur instead of dull brown fur. Also, his antlers got HUGE!!!!!!! "You are now the forest spirit." said the wolf. "Tell me, what is your name?" That's when the deer remembered. He didn't have a name! "I don't have one..." he said. "No name? Well, we'll have to think of one for you..... How about.... Shishi Gami?" Asked the wolf. The deer liked the sound of it. "Okay." He said. "Shishi Gami it is." So, the new forest spirit walked off into the forest.

The End
