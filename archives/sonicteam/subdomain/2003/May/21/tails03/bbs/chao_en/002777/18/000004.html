I don't know how to get a REAL Amy chao but I know how to get something that's pretty close. You know how to get a Sonic chao, right? Well, just hatch a pink chao and make it a Sonic chao. Here's some other great chao recipies:

Eggman chao:
Make a sumo chao (I don't know how to do this) use a red egg. When it becomes a sumo chao, give it penguins untill it has the eyebrows. Give it skeleton dogs untill it has ONLY the eyebrows. It looks a lot like Eggman!

Cocroach chao:
Get a black egg OR a grey egg. When the chao hatches, give it dragons untill it has the antennae. Give it skeleton dogs untill it has ONLY the antennae. It looks a little bit like a cocroach!

Bolder chao:
Get a grey egg and make the chao that hatches a sumo chao. Give it a bat so that it loses its feet. It looks like a bolder!

Black and White chao:
Get a gold chao and make sure it likes the character that hatches it. DON'T LEAVE THE GARDEN!!!!!! Don't leave to collect rings, get animals, don't even go to the kindergarten! Just stay in the same garden untill the chao evolves, and keep petting it and feeding it. When it evolves, it should be black and white! It's worth the effort! This chao is so COOL!!!!

Shiny regular chao:
Get a shiny chao and when it evolves, mate it with a regular chao. (not colored, just a regular, normal, nothing special chao) When the egg hatches, it should look like a regular chao, but shiny!

Strawberries and cream chao:
Get a chao. It has to be either regular or white. Give it skunks untill it has all the skunk parts. If you used a regular chao, then you have to make it hero. If you used a white chao, it can be anything you like. When it evolves, the skunk parts turn reddish-pink, and it's the strawberries and cream chao!
