(Sorry Knower of Chaos, I'll have to write in Eggman's lines for this one scene)

Rouge is about to set off to find some more emeralds, when Eggman rushes to her side.

"Rouge...wait..." he says breathlessly. He had obviously ran quite a ways, to tell her information.

"What is it, Dr. Eggman?" Rouge asked curiously. "Is everything...okay?"

"Perfect," he grins.

Rouge looks at him sceptically. "...Huh?"

"You know that knucklehead who happens to be your rival?"

Rouge nodds. "Yeah..."

Eggman looks at her with excitement. "It turns out he's been kidnapped by someone!"

Rouge looks worried for a brief moment, but quickly shakes it off. "By who?"

"I don't know," Eggman says. "But who cares?" he starts to laugh evilly. "You have no competition now! Getting the emeralds will be one hunderd times easier!" he resumes laughing.

Rouge fakes an evil laugh. "Perfect," she says a little hesitantly.

Eggman stops his laughing, and glares at her curiously. "Is everything alright, bat girl?"

Rouge nodds. "Yeah."

Eggman grins, before walking away. "Just thought you'd love to know!"

Rouge frowns, the minute he is out of sight and hearing range. "What am I going to do?" she says with a worried tone. "I don't know if Knuckles is alright..." she catches herself. "Not that I care, or anything. But...I guess I should go tell his little friends."

Rouge runs off to find Sonic, Tails and Amy.

--End of Prologue--

Oh, and we don't have to use this...I just wrote this prologue for fun. ^^;;
