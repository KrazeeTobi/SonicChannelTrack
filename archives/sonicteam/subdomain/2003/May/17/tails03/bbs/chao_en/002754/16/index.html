Aquatic mine...Right, here we go...

Lower the water first. At the start of the level, go up the central structure and grab the lift to go up to the highest level. Find the level three switch and hit it.
Go back down and look for a long pipe type thing with two cautions signs. Go down it. If theres a spring, thats the right one. If there is no spring, go to the other side of the level and you should find it.
When you are at the bottom of the right pipe, then you have to swim through the water to reach a small room with wooden planks (still underwater) and quickley swim through them. Then you have to swim upwards as fast as yo can to reach the air necklace.

I hope that helps you. If you need any more help, just ask, I'll be willing to help^.^
