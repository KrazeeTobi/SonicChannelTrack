When he starts to chase you, run, but not too fast or he'll turn around. Jump over the wood dividers when you see them. He'll get tired soon, and the pipe on his neck will start flashing. Jump onto it, and when you get there do a homing attack on his red life-support system (kinda like a hemisphere). Repeat.
Then he'll use energy balls - somersault under the high ones, jump over the low ones. Grind the pipe again, homing attack again (once he's tired). Repeat.
He then spawns the pink eggs - which attack you if you're not quick enough. Homing attack them until you get high enough to attack his life-support. Repeat.
He'll then levitate you into the air - dodge the eggs (I'm not sure how to move around), move towards his life-support and when you get there, just press A and he's beaten. It may not be exactly correct.
