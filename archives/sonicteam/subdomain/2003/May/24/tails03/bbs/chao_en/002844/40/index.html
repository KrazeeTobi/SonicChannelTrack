Pages 1 and 1/2 through 5-Expert users flame and flood users who aren't doing the same to the Experts. I was one of the users away attacking Expert, but I saw this when I got back.

Page 5- The war is over. We signed some kind of peace treaty, but grudges were held. Eventually, the treaty was broken. Many later wars, (too many to count) broke out because of this.

Pages 5-469- Times are prosperous. Pages are non-flooded, imformative about chao, and fill up slow because we make *wise* (hint to flooding n00bs) use of each topic. I coudn't get enough of those days...

Page 470- The flooders come in. There's about 4 of them. Chao users also started pestering me to stop them, so I made a new account, called Enzo (Reboot), ID 4000 something. The flooders swere banned, and times returned to rosperity. This is also when the famous "Bunny Chao" started posting.
