Crystal Chao (Rare)--Get a Sapphire Chao and reincarnate it at least once, and set it aside. Then, get a Shiny Blue Chao and reincarnate it at least once, and set that one aside. Next, get a Shiny White or Shiny Regular Chao, and reincarnate it once. Now, get the Shiny White or Shiny Regular Chao and the Sapphire Chao, and have them mate. Next, get the Shiny White or Shiny Regular Chao to mate with the Shiny Blue Chao. Now, reincarnate the two newborn Chao once. Then, let hem mate twice. Now, let those two reincarnate once. Now, let them mate once, and get the Shiny White or Shiny Regular Chao, and have the newborn to become an adult, and have the Shiny White or Shiny Regular Chao to mate. Last but not least, have the newborn to grow into an adult, and have it mate with the Shiny White or Shiny Regular Chao. Now, make that Chao mate at least twelve times before it reincarnates, and have it repeat that phase again. After it has reincarnated twice, get the Shiny White or Shiny Regular Chao and have the Chao that reincarnated twice to mate with it. Get that newborn Chao to mate with a Transparent Chao, and then, have it mate with a Green Transparent Chao. Have the two newborns mate, and have the newborn mate with the Shiny White or Shiny regular Chao. When that egg hatches, it should be a see-through blue Chao, and it should have a little Crystal on it's stomach when it shines in the sun. This trick might not work, and I'm sorry if it doesn't!
