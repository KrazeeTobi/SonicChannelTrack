Eggman chao:
Get a red egg. Make it a sumo chao. (I don't know how to do this) Give the chao penguins untill it has the eyebrows. Give it skeleton dogs untill it has ONLY the eyebrows. It looks a lot like Eggman!

Boulder chao:
Get a grey egg. Make it a sumo chao. Give it a bat so that it loses its feet. It looks like a boulder! This chao ROCKS!!! (pun intended)

Black and White chao:
Get a gold egg. Make sure the chao that hatches like the character that hatches it. DO NOT LEAVE THE GARDEN!!!! Don't go to get rings, fruits, animals, chaos drives, or anything else!!!!! Just stay in the same garden untill the chao eveolves, and keep petting and feeding it. When the chao comes out of it's cacoon, it should be black and white! It's so COOL!!!

Strawberries and Cream chao:
Get either a regular or a white egg. Give the chao that hatches skunks untill it has all the skunk parts. If you used a regular chao, than make it hero. If you used a white chao, make it anything you like. When it evolves, the skunk parts turn reddish-pink, and it's the strawberries and cream chao!
