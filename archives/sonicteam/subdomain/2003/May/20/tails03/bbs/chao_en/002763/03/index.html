Continued from the story line of Sonic Adventure 2 Battle..

Amy: Whats the matter Sonic?
Sonic: Oh its nothing..come on lets go home! Keep the planet has blue as me!......Sayonara..Shadow The Hedgehog..


*Eggman walks up to Knuckles and sees him looking out the window of the Ark Colony*

Eggman: hey knuckles...you ok?
Knuckles: Not 100%
Eggman: What happened
Knuckles: Its about Sonic
Eggman: and..
Knuckles: Why does he always has to be the hero..no one likes me...amy tails loves him..but not me..
Eggman: Thats not true...
Knuckles: Oh yes it is eggman, he's even stronger than me..
Eggman: he is?
Knuckles: Yes, remember you tricked me to let you get the Master Emerald?...(Sonic and Knuckles)
Eggman: yeah...
Knuckles: well, one day he was at my shrine..and we faught
Eggman: so..he won
Knuckles: Believe it or not..he did

*Sonic comes up to knuckles*
Sonic: hey...
*Sonic sees knuckles not happy*
Sonic: Whats the matter Knuckles..let me guess..its about shadow right..yeah im sad about that too.
Knuckles: its not about that...We barely knew eachother..theres no need for me to be sad over him..
Sonic: Well that is true..but you should be said about him..and if your not sad about him then why are you sad?
Knuckles: 1st of all..i wasnt sad..im just thinkling all the things you have done to me.
Sonic: what do you mean?
Knuckles: you prooved to everyone that your stronger than me, and much popular than me..
Sonic: I did?
*Knuckles chokes Sonic*
Knuckles: yes you idiot, it was about 8 years ago
*Tails runs over..*
Knuckles: see we have Tails..he was there..
Tails: Hey Knuckles!..why are you choking sonic?!
*Knuckles Lets go of sonic's neck*
*Sonic caughs*
Knuckles: Yo tails, remember when Me and Sonic faught each other
Eggman: Im still confused about this
Tails: well yea
Knuckles: 100%
Tails: uh huh
Knuckles: you remember who won?
Tails: yea..Sonic
Knuckles: SEE YOU DISGUSTING DIRTY HEDGEHOG!
*Knuckles said it so loud that rouge and amy heard it*
*Amy and rouge comes*
Rouge: Hey, whats all the emotion about?
Amy: Yea?
