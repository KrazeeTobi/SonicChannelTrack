OK, it takes a long time to get an all "S" rank Chao. What you do is say it has a "B" in Swimming. You would make it a Swimming type (doesn't matter if it's Neutral, Hero, or Dark) and its "B" rank would go up to an "A" rank. Then you have to reincarnate your Chao and do it again so the "A" turns to an "S" rank.

Note: to raise the Stamina rank you have to make it normal.

It will become a Chaos Chao when it would usually evolve to become an adult (only if it really liked you and you gave it all 21 animals).

Yes, you can. You can either get it to 99 before it evolves or after. You can still raise attributes on a Chaos Chao.

BTW: Try making an all "S" rank Chaos Chao, it takes a long time.
