Shadow lives on Part 2

(Outside Space Colony Ark:Shadows Atmosphere entery 47 Minutes & 34 Seconds)
(Sonic reaches the atmosphere coordinants)
Tails over intercom:Sonic,you only have about 46 Minutes to find shadow,hurry!
(Sonic eagle dives closer to the atmosphere looking left and right for shadow)
Sonic:where is he,I'd swear he fell over here
(Sonic sees Shadow pretty close to the atmosphere)
Sonic:guys,I found him,and tails.......I think your calculations were alittle off
Tails over intercom:Hold on,oops!!!Sonic,you need to hurry,you only have 8 Minutes
(sonic dives as fast as he can to shadow and grabs him)
Sonic:Luckely hes still in his super form
Sonic:I got him,hes still alive
(sonic starts back to the colony when he sees a beam fly past him,only afew inches away)
Sonic:!!!
Tails over intercom:Sonic!!Watch out,the colonies defense weapons have come back online
Sonic:I thought they were destroyed
Tails over intercom:I don't get it either,you have to be carful and avoid the weapons
Sonic:This is not going to be pleasant
(sonic starts to the colony again and avoids several beams but gets nailed by a turret)
Sonic:ouch!
Tails over intercom:Sonic,the security doors are closing,you need to find another way
(sonic thinks)
Sonic:Chaos Control!
(Inside Space colony ark)
Rouge:Do you think hes going to make it?
Tails:I don't know
(Knuckles sits up)
Knuckles:that energy,he used chaos control
(Sonic and Shadow appear and fall to the floor,still in their super forms)
(Sonic gets up and dusts himself off)
Sonic:hes still not awake
Tails:I wonder how he maneged to stay in his super form for that long
(Sonic changes back,right when he does this Shadow changes back too,though still asleap)
Knuckles:thats it,he maneged to stay alive using the enrgy given off by all 7 emeralds,including the master,but he didn't do it intentionaly,someone wanted to keep him alive
Sonic:hm
Amy:Strange
Sonic:I wonder who
(The colony vibrates)
Knuckles:wait a minute,I recognize that
(Sonic looks out the window)
Sonic:the prototypes still alive!
Amy:That big lizard never gives up,does he
Tails:well,I think that was the source to our escape pod problem
(The colony starts shaking again)
Gerald:All of you ungrateful humans,who took everything away from me,will feel my loss,and despare
Knuckles:here we go again
(out side space colony Ark)
Man:On my command,that prototype will launch to earth,dragging the colony behind
Other person:You allready tried that
Man:Ah,yes,but this time it was unexpected
Other person:are you sure they will not have a victory
Man:oh yes,they will pay with their lives
Other person:what about Shadow?
Man:I will regain control of him,then I will go to countless planets and soler systems,and destroy them all
Other person:I must go now,I have buissness to attend to

To be continued
