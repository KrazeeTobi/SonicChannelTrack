Amy, Tails, Sonic and Tikal all ran back to Eggman's base.

"What are we doing here?" Amy asked Sonic, curiously.

"You guys wait out here...I'll go get Rouge."

"Rouge? Why Rouge?" Amy asked.

"Because she'll be able to help us."

"How?" Amy asked.

Sonic looked a little frusterated. "I'll explain later, okay?"

Amy frowned a little. "You don't need to get all personal about it," she said angrily.

Sonic ignored her last comment, before he turned to the others. "You guys wait out here. I'll go get bat girl, and then we can go save Knuckles."

"Okay, but how are you going to get in?" Tails asked Sonic. "The place is swarming with guards!"

"Guards, Smards," Sonic waved his hand lazily, before running in the front door.

"Sonic!" Amy called after Sonic in a worried tone, but Sonic couldn't hear her because he was already in the building.

"I still don't see why we have to get Rouge," Amy said, confused.

"Because Rouge can help us find Knuckles. They have a lot in common, Amy. They're both treasure hunters, and she might know some more information."

Amy frowned. "I guess..." she said hesitantly.

"Plus, Rouge has the Chaos Emerald power, from all of her collected emeralds," Tikal added with a smile. "So maybe she can use her Chaos powers to transport us to where Knuckles is."

"Yeah," Tails nodded in agreement.

Suddenly Sonic came bursting out of the doors, with Rouge in tow.

"You continue to amaze me, Sonic," Tails said with a grin. He still didn't know how Sonic managed to get through all of the guards.

"Sonic!" Amy hugged him, and Sonic blushed a bit, as he shook her off.

"Oo," Rouge teased. "Looks like Mr. Hero has a little girlfriend!"

"She's not my girlfriend!" Sonic retorted with a blush. Amy giggled.

"Sure," Rouge said slyly, with her eyes half open in suspicion.

"Well you like Knuckles," Sonic said. Rouge's eyes widened a bit, but she shook it off.

"Why would I like the knucklehead?" she asked hautily.

"Oh puh-lease," Sonic said slyly. "You've always likes Knuckles!"

"Enough!" Tikal said. "We can sort out our feelings later, guys, Knuckles needs us!"

"Right!" Tails agreed. Sonic and Rouge exchanged angry glances of 'I'll keep my eye on you.'

"Rouge," Tails turned to her. "Use your Chaos power to transport us to him, okay?"

"My Chaos power?" she questioned.

"Yeah, you don't collect emeralds because you feel like it," Sonic snorted, and Rouge frowned.

"Alright, alright," she said, "the things I do for you, Knuckles," she muttered to herself, before she closed her eyes and a large shimering ball (looks similar to the "Mystic Melody" ball) engulfed them, and when they opened their eyes, they were in a dark, cold place.

"Where are we?" Amy asked, clutching onto Sonic's hand.

"I don't know," Sonic said in confusion.

--End of Chapter Two--
