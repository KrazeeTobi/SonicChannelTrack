Incase you didn't know, the tails chao is available through Phantasy Star Online for Gamecube, instructions below.


Go to the quest 'The Fake in Yellow'. After you find the fake rag rappy, he will create a telepipe. Go through the telepipe, then go back through it. There should be 3 rag rappys staring at the wall. shoot the boxes underneath where they're staring and a chao should come out and give you 'Tiny Chao's Garden'. Bring it to Game Boy Advance mnachie next to the Hunter's Guild place(it'll be there.) Now transfer the 'Tiny Chao's Garden' to the GBA (without sonic advance in it) and the Tails Chao is in it! Then just upload it to the GCN!
