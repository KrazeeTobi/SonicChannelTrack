Please take note that thsi list assumes that you are just now going through the game and that you have not already beat the game and are going back to get all of the Items.

Sector 1 Tanks

Item: Energy Tank
Requirements: Morph Ball, Beam/Missile
Location: Horizontal shaft connecting Stabilizer Rooms 1 and 2.
After destroying your first Stabilizer head to the room connected to the bottom-right corner of the first Stabilizer Room. About halfway through the room, the floor inclines. Crouch down and shoot the left section of the inclined ground. Roll through the pipe to find another precious Energy Tank.

Item: Missile Tank
Requirements: Nothing
Location: 3x2 room in west Sector 1
If you look on the map at the very left of Sector 1, under the main room, is a 3 block by 2-block rectangular, lava-filled room. As soon as you enter jump up and grab the rugged ceiling from which you can hang. Go left to drop on a Missile Tank.

Item: Missile Tank
Requirements: Missiles
Location: Room connecting to upper-left corner of 4th Stabilizer room.
After destroying the 4th stabilizer go through the door in the upper-left corner to find a Missile Tank being guarded by a ball-spewing X-Crab.

Item: Missile Tank
Requirements: Missiles, Morph Ball
Location: Room above Sector 1 boss.
After beating the Sector 1 boss, head through the door in the upper-right corner to find your self trapped. But in a Metroid game, there’s (almost) always a way out. Carefully observe the ceiling to find that one section of it is separated from the rest. Fire a round up at the loose block to disintegrate it. Jump through the newly made hole, then jump again and hang from the ceiling. Aim to the left, fire a few missiles, then go through the hole via Morph Ball. The next room is infested with a crap load of those X-Crabs. In the bottom of the room is a Missile Tank.

Item: Missile Tank
Requirements: Beam, Morph Ball
Location: Secret room connecting to eastern-most Save Room.
Start by getting to the Save Room all the way on the right of the map. If you look on the right wall you will notice that the 3rd block up is cracked. Shoot it, and then jump up in the air while holding right against the wall. At the height of your jump quickly press DOWN DOWN then hold right again. You will morph and land in the hole. Roll right to find a semi-tall room with metal walls and stuff. This is the tricky part. You have to wall jump up to the top where a Missile Tank awaits your arrival.
