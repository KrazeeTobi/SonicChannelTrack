I've noticed that a lot of people think that you need to buy a certain kind of fruit to make a Chaos Chao.

Just so everyone knows, you DO NOT have to give a Chao ANY fruits to make it a Chaos Chao. Buying Dark Fruits to make a Devil, Hero Fruits to make an Angel, or Chao Fruits to make a Neutral, is all a WASTE OF RINGS.

In short: You DO NOT have to buy any fruits to make a Chaos Chao. Just raise it with Dark char. for a Devil, Hero char. for an Angel, or both types of char. for a Neutral.

I have NEVER, in this whole time I've owned the game, made one of my 26 Chaos Chao by bing a fruit. So don't waste your rings on fruits to make a Chaos Chao.
