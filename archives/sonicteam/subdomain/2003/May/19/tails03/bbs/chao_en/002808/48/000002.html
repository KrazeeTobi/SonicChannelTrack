Please take note that thsi list assumes that you are just now going through the game and that you have not already beat the game and are going back to get all of the Items.

Main Deck Tanks

Item: Missile Tank
Requirements: Missiles
Location: Vent after getting missiles.
As you advance through the vents after receiving Missile Download, you will fall down a hole. On a platform to the right you will find a Missile Tank.

Item: Missile Tank
Requirements: Any Weapon
Location: In room left of previous Missile Tank
From the platform the previous Missile Tank was on, go left, fall down, and then go through the passage on the left. Shoot the top of the second step to reveal a Missile Tank.

Item: Energy Tank
Requirements: Missiles
Location: First boss room.
As you navigate your way through the narrow passageways in the first boss room you will come across an Energy Tank lying there in plain sight. After you get that STOP RIGHT WHERE YOU ARE!!! DO NOT FALL DOWN!!! WHATEVER YOU DO!!! READ THE NEXT ITEM LOCATION BEFORE FALLING DOWN!!!

Item: Energy Tank (This is your only chance to get it!)
Requirements: Missiles, Beam
Location: Right next to the last Energy Tank
Remember that this is one of those “One time only” items. If you read what I said last time then you should still be where the last Energy Tank was. What you want to do is SLOWLY walk to the hole. Stop 1 unit/block to the left of the hole. Aim up and fire a Missile. If you are 1 unit from the hole you will destroy a Missile block. Then fire a few beam shots up into the hole, jump up and hold right. You will grab a ledge. Go to the right and you will fall down to the Energy Tank.

Item: Missile Tank
Requirements: Morph Ball
Location: Vertical shaft connecting to Quarantine Bay.
As you are heading up the shaft you will notice a narrow hole in the right wall. Roll through via Morph Ball to find a Missile Tank.
