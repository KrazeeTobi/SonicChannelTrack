There are 2 places I know of.
1.)White Jungle: When you get to the part with the hollow tree cave tunnel thing, to the right (not actually in the cave), is a wooden crate that contains one. Not one of the long entirely enclosed caves, but the liitle short one on the left side of a platform. You'll know it when you see it.
2.)Egg Quarters: From where you start, go left to the room with the huge platform surrounded by torches and pillars.
On the far side of the room from where you entered, is a cage with one of those "things" in it. This part is tricky. Have the monkey on the nearby pillar trow a at you. Quickly pick it up. Walk it over to the cage and set it down. Get back and it should break the cage.

Hope that helps.
