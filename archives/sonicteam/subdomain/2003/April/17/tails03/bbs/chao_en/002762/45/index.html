Chaos Chao: Reincarnate a Chao 2 times then give it 1 of each of the 21 animals and make it either Hero, Neutral, and Dark. When it goes into a cocoon you will hear very strange organ music, and out will come a Chaos Chao (the Chao must like you, too).

Shadow Chao: Just make a Dark Running/Running Chao.

Sonic Chao: Just make a Neutral Running/Running Chao.
