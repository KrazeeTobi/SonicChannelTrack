Ok,for one thing,the mystic melody isnt found in the ruins. Instead, the mystic melody is the same as a level-up item. They are hidden in a certain level,and each character has their own. Once you hve found it for sonic for example,you can go to the ruins in any level,and your action button will change to 'mystic melody' when u stand near them.If it makes a portal,jump into it.If it makes a line of rings,light dash through em.Jump on the spring or platform if it makes one... well,you will know when u see it.
Here is where u can find the melodies for each char

Sonic: Somewhere in final rush,ill get back to u on this

Shadow: In final chase;You know those gravity cylinder things? one of them has a full electric fence going around the circumference.go above the fence,and i think there is a spring on top.hop on the spring and it will take u to the melody

Tails:In tails egypt level, just look for 4 metal boxes,break through them and BAM! you found it

Jelly Belly:In eggmans egypt level; i forgot exactly where,but near the beginning of the stage there is a rotating plaform. jump off into the direction where you see a platform in the distance;you need hover to get there

Knuckles: In wild canyon. On the top of the beggining area (dont go up through the air thing,just climb up the walls) there will be 4 metal boxes.break em and u will find it.

Rouge: Almost the same as knuckles(but in dry lagoon of course).in the area where u start,climb up to the sandy area and look for the 4 metal boxes

Hope this helps,i gotta play sonic again to see exactly where it is
