If you wanna take fruits, eggs and items in the Kindergarten the easy way, do the following:
Go to the "Stage Select" and then go to an easy stage with many rings, like "City Escape". Then choose the first or the second mission to play (I suggest the first) and collect as many rings as you can.Beat the mission as many times as you want. Then go to the Chao World (also from "Stage Select") and go buy whatever you want from the "Black Market" in the Kindergarten with the rings you've
gathered. Smart huh?!
