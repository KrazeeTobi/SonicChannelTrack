Sonic Advance: The latest chao news
As you know, Sonic Advance, Sega's first game on a Nintendo system, was delayed untill January 23. It has now unfortunetly been delayed again untill February 5. As for chaos, it has been confirmed that there WILL be chaos in SA. Nintendo Magazine says there will be a "Tiny Chao Garden". They also showed a picture. The chaos will have the power of textual speech, and you will be able to hatch a fresh baby chao and raise its stats on your GBA and earn it exotic fruits from mini games. You will also be able to transfer your chao from SA2 Battle to and from your GBA (though as you will read, SA2B may not me all its cracked up to be). Sonic Advance will also have a single and multti pack link for multi player play and four playable characters, including Sonic, Tails, Knuckles, and Amy.

SA2 Battle: The debate of DC/Xbox/GCN/PS2
I have seen many pictures of the upcoming game "Sonic Adventure 2 Battle", and I have to say, I'm not impressed. The pictures I saw looked dull and flat and even having noticible pixles, compared to the DC version of the game. And those were the story scenes and game play scenes!!! I know it's not just the photos, because I have seen many photos of other games from the same source and they were all very clear. It is a PROVEN FACT that Xbox has better garphics due to its built in harddrive, which also eliminates the need for memory cards!!! That saves you about $60 or more on memory cards right there, thinking that your probably going to need to buy 3 or more eventually. But enough about MY opinion, lets hear your opinion! The debate between GCN, PS2, and DC is still raging! Just please don't forget about your Dreamcast and its SA2!!! Trust me, you may get tired of playing SA2 Battle when you start having to look at Gamecube's... less-then perfect... controller while your playing :).....

Sonic Team's developement for other systems
Aside from Sonic Advance and SA2 Battle, Sega and Sonic Team are releasing several new titles for varios systems. Sega has already released Chu Chu Rocket for the GBA. And Sonic Team has definate plans to release Phantasy Star Online for the GCN and Xbox!!! Thats gonna be great, but to tell you the truth, I've never played it before! Looking forward to play it on my Xbox, though.
