Last episode level (as Rogue): Go to the top, and when you press the button, fly the same way that you got there. Climb up on the wood thing and you should see Big the Cat. You cannot go there -- he is just turning his back at you.
Information in this section was contributed by ThugN4Now2.
Last? story level (as Dr. Eggman): As you fall down a ravine with lasers, Big is sitting on the far left ledge near the three water enemies.
Information in this section was contributed by Bonthra.
Last? story level (as Shadow): Before the biolizard fight (called the Ultimate Battle in scene select), repeatedly press A through the beginning of the sequence to see Big the Cat walk by you twice.
Information in this section was contributed by Bobby Gaines.
Lost Colony: When you reach the part with a high platform that has a lot beetles, about three quarters of the way on the platform, Big is on the wall to the left. Try shooting a lot of beetles to get light to see him.
Information in this section was contributed by superiour sonic.
Metal Harbor level (as Sonic): Big can be seen when you reach the rocket that takes you to the big rocket. Look around to see him sitting on a platform off to the side
Information in this section was contributed by The Insane One.
Meteor Herd level: Big can been seen below the light screen window that is close to the lava. He is hanging on the middle of the ledge. Note: It is located close to the huge platform that has a sign that reads "0-2 0-3". He is difficult to spot.
Information in this section was contributed by superiour sonic.
Prison Lane level (as Tails): Go to the room with the "elevator". In one of the cells you will see something sticking out on the left side. Move the camera so its pointing to that cell to see Big the Cat.
Information in this section was contributed by Kpetty45fan.
Pumpkin Hill level (as Knuckles): Go to the one of the train gates. You can see Big the Cat hiding behind the gate, waving to you.
Information in this section was contributed by Leukerbad and zealot123456.
Pumpkin Hill/Mountain level (as Knuckles): Go to the Ghost Train Turntable and drop down to the tracks. In one of the barred tunnels, Big the Cat will be waving at you.
Information in this section was contributed by Cassis90.
Pyramid Cave level (as Sonic): You can see Big the Cat on the far left side of the screen, behind the fence on the first rail you grind on.
Information in this section was contributed by Firesnake101.
Radical Highway level (as Shadow): Go to the rocket that takes you over the "Got Ring?" blimp. Big the Cat will be looking down. When playing under the hard difficulty setting, you will see him hanging from the blimp instead of looking down.
Information in this section was contributed by Timothy O'Brien and Ice Hedgehog.
Sand Ocean level (as Eggman): Big can be seen immediately near the end ring. Turn the camera to see Big sleeping on a platform off to the side.
Information in this section was contributed by The Insane One.
Security Hall level (as Rouge): Immediately when you start the level, go to your right or left and reach the ledge directly next to the hole with the A/C tubes in it. Move the camera so you can see under Rouge, and you should see Big hanging onto a wall. Note: You can use Drill Drive to attack him and pass through him like a ghost.
Information in this section was contributed by The Insane One and Bobby Gaines.
Sky Rail level (as Shadow): Immediately at the start of the level, when Shadow is grinding down a pole, look at the girder behind him. You will see Big sitting on a girder in the background. Note: You will only see his head unless you jump.
Information in this section was contributed by The Insane One.

Information in this section was contributed by The Insane One.
