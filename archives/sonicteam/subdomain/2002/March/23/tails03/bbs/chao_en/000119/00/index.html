You must attend your chao with nothing but hero characters... Sonic, Tails, or Knuckles. I like Knuckles... All you must do with one of these characters is rub their head until the heart shows... hold down on "X" 'tll sqiggle marks are gone... now you can rapidly pump away at making the heart reapear(rubbing head) and watch as the chao turns alabaster white. Now you can feed or collect animals to bring about first evolution; becoming a hero chao. If you give all one kind of animal he may change color when he evolves. You must collect drives and animals with heros until first evolution or he may become a normal neutral chao. Collect with all heros but choose one to feed chao... they don't always like all heros... most can come to love Knuckles... even in the dark garden. If you want to make dark chao use all dark characters and do the same thing. I like Eggman... You need to make at least one dark chao to open the dark garden.
