Weekly Chao Recipies
Every week I will be putting special and original chao recipies here. If you have any suggestions, tell me and I will post them in next weeks issue!!!

The most real-looking Super Sonic Chao yet:
First, get a Gold chao either from the Black Market or by importing a SA1 Gold chao and mating it. Its baby will remain gold. Keep it neutral (pet it equally with Hero and Dark), and give it all green animals and chao drives (run element) untill it evolves. It must have had at least 15 before it evolves. Next give it only RED animals and chao drives(power element). Give it about 50 or 60 and its spikes should have turned upward like Super Sonic's. Give it Skeleton dogs (I think) to get rid of the animal parts. You now have the ultimate Super Sonic Chao! Follow the same recipe but with a Dark Silver chao to get the Ultimate Hyper Shadow Chao.

Eggman Chao (maybe):
Make a Dark chao into a first evolution swim chao and with yellow animals and chao drives and then give it all power 9red) animals and chao drives. Follow the same numbesr as in the recipe above. The chao might come out looking like Eggman. I am still working on this chao myself, turning my main chao in to it. It's just a theory, but I hope it works!!! I know the chao will at least get fat. HINT: In Shadow's Sky Rail stage, a purple bear (red power element) can be found after the first rail in the shadow of a pipe. Get it and restart the level. Get it again. Repeat untill you have ten. If you get Game Over, don't worry, you still have the animals. Just go back into the stage. REMINDER: to give a chao animals, you must go into the garden with a character from the SIDE(Hero or Dark) of the character you got the animals with, not that specific character nessescarily.
