> where do i find all the mystic melodies in the stages? ive never seen any anywhere.
Sonic's: Find the ruins in Final Rush. Get the checkpoint nearby, then jump forward on2 the idle platforms near the rail. Ride the rocket on it. The rest is selfexplanatory.
Tails': Only 1 wall in Hidden Base is composed of steel crates. Break it & the green glow is it.
Knuckles': Find Lonely Statue in Wild Canyon, check Omochao if stuck at here. Now climb upward until u c a painting. Dig under the hawk, on darker mud. The Melody is in front of u.
Shadow's: In Final Chase, skip areas b4 the 2 still green tubes. Skip areas afterwards until u c the 5th tube. Go 2 its top, jump 2 the far tube, with full circle of electric barrier. U find it on the platform near its top.
Rouge's: When start Dry Lagoon, go on2 the right side of the plaza. Find a picture in the shade of the lowest ledge on the side. The digging refers 2 Knuckles'.
Eggman's: At the beginning of Sand Ocean, ride on the rotating platform. On left of where u come, there's a bomb dropping robot. Destroy it, then hover across the quick sand so that u can land on the far away platform. Otherwise u will always fall in2 the sand. Get the Melody at there.
