White Jungle level: Big can be seen again at the end of the level. Before you hit the ring, turn the camera to the right to see Big sitting on a tree to the left.
Information in this section was contributed by The Insane One.
Wild Canyon level (as Knuckles): Go forward and up the wind. Go over to the wall in between the two paths you can go, and climb up. Big the Cat will be hanging on for life.
Information in this section was contributed by Timothy O'Brien.
You can also see him in the battle against Sonic while playing as Shadow.
Information in this section was contributed by ViPeR98Zz.
In the Tails level where you are running from the police and you first get your hover ability, Big the Cat can be seen again. There is a part in the level where you need to knock a column over in order to use it as a step to reach a higher level, from which you hover over to a platform with a police barricade and two robots. If you miss the platform and fall in the hole, you will see Big the Cat sitting on the girders underneath the platform.
Information in this section was contributed by jkr2.
In the intermission when Sonic beats up the Egg Golem (Dark storyline) look against the wall to see Big behind one of the doors, jumping up and down,
Information in this section was contributed by The Insane One.
After you complete Final Hazard, and Shadow and Sonic get ready to use the Chaos Control, Big will sometimes be seen floating around in the background.
Information in this section was contributed by The Insane One.
Play as Rouge and do the battle against Knuckles. At the end when you and Knuckles are saying "Whew", Big will walk across the beam behind you.
