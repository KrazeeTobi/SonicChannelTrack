I lost many chao this way until I discovered that you can stop the dying process. Leave the garden your in immediatly.
Then you must post your chao in this sites daycare before you shut the game down. Leave the SA2 daycare and "bluebutton" to the main menu. Choose HOME and go on the internet. Post chao then download chao. You now have it safely stored in a MC or VMU. You actually have 2 of that chao now and if your lucky you can find one sleeping in the garden you left him in. If he start to cocoon again before you can put him in a VMU... LEAVE IMMEDIATLY... If you go to the races you will find he's still there and can race against himself (from your download). The downloaded chao is also dying so its best to leave him in a memory card but if you have something (fruit, drive, animal, etc.) in your hand as you let him out of the transporter and run (carefully) to him he will take it. Then you can pet... pick up and return to VMU. There will come a time he won't accept your offering and starts to cocoon. Do it all over again to keep your modified chao. I have filled my Dark Garden by modifying a chao "SLEEPER" like this. SLEEPER is a neutral chao I was able to max out SWIM, FLY, RUN, PWR. He looks like a fat baby chao but is a winner in racing. All the modified were also winners TRY IT!
