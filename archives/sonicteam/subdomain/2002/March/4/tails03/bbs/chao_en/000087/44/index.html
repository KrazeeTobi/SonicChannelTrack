> Does anyone have any pointers on how to win at the slots in CA2?

When you play the 'slots' you will see 100 credits( aka: your score) and the 'bet' at 10. You can change the bet from 10 (the max) to zero (the minimum). I suggest that you place the 'bet' at one (since it wouldn't spin for free). Right after it starts spinning, press "A" button repeatedly. This setup will give you a higher chance at winning more credits or snacks.

NOTE: You can only carry up to four snacks per CA2. Unlike CA1, where you can take out the fruits you won and raise some of the stats, the snacks stay in the VMU and they don't raise anything on any of the stats.
