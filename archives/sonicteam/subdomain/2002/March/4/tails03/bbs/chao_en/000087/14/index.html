HOPE ID:000663 2001/09/06 08:45:09
Reply Edit post Homepage
back the first few days after sonic 2 came out- i posted a link and how to make your vmu run on 4 AA batteries. Basically all you need is a 4 slot battery pack from radio shack ($1.99) make sure it is the ones with a red and a black wire. then get a pack of alligator clips- the ones with rubber stoppers are the best ($0.99). open up the back of ur vmu- take out the batteries. There are 2 metal prongs in there. take the red wire wrap it around the alligator clip- hook it up to one prong. Take the black and do the same, only on the other prong. then turn on your vmu and u have unlimited power. and if you get new vmu batteries- simply unclip the alligator clips from the prongs and put in new batteries. All in all it takes 3 min to do tops and cost at most $4 with the 4 AA batteries included. Yes the vmu will still fit in the controller too. If you dont like it u can always unclip it. GIVE IT A TRY! around here they dont carry vmu batteries- so this is what we were forced to come up with =) HAVE FUN
