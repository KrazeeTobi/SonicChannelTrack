We all know about the chaos' ability to
copy the physical apearance of anything
they touch. And it is a known fact that
chao can pull objects, of any size, out of
thin air. But only a few handfull of people
know the truth about the chao and
their sole purpose. Read on.

The govenment has been experimenting
with BETA chao technology since the
Cold War ended. These advanced
Light Chaos chao, have been carefully
altered and cloned to the point that
they not only can transform into animal
forms, but can now take the physical
traits of a human child! The difference
between the real and the fake are almost
indistinguishable...

More startling is the fact that the
government is using them as espionage
agents and assassins. These chao
lack the gentle pacifist nature of thier
kin. These chao play with a different
set of "toys."

In a recent interveiw with one of the
retired chao agents, the chao agreed
to show us its' entire collection of
weapons. It consisted of knives of all
sizes, semi/fully automatic weapons,
hundreds of pounds of explosive material,
and thousands of amunitions rounds. Just
one chao had enough fire-power to take
down a small millitary complex. And that
was just one of many chao involved in
the program.

Right now as we speak, there are chao
running around, seemingly harmless,
plotting world domination. One minute,
you pick up your beloved and trusted
chao for a quick hug. The next minute,
you lay dead on the floor with a steak
knife jutting out the back of your face.
It has happened, it will continue to
happen as long as chao have free reign.
