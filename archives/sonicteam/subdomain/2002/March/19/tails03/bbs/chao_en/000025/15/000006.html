1. Sign up for yahoo mail
2. Sign in and click on options
3. Click on POP Access & Forwarding
4. Click Web and POP Access
5. Click Text messages only
6. Click Submit
7. Enter this info in mail settings
Incoming mail (POP) Server - pop.mail.yahoo.com
Outgoing Mail (SMTP) Server - smtp.mail.yahoo.com
Login Name - Your Yahoo ID
E-mail address - Your Yahoo Address
Password - Your Yahoo password

Now you should be able to check your yahoo mail from the DC.
Again some ISPs will not let you send mail.
