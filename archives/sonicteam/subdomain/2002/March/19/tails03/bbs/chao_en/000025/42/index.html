Many of you are confusing one type of Chao with other types of Chao. So read this and learn all about Chao.

Chao Basics.

When you hatch an egg you get a NEUTRAL Chao. You can do one of three things with it.

1. Make it a HERO Chao by using the three HERO caracters.
2. Make it a DARK Chao by using the three DARK characters.
3. Keep it a NEUTRAL Chao by using both HERO and DARK caracters.

Now we have three types of Chao.
You can do one of five things to these Chao.
As an example I will use a NEUTRAL Chao.

1. Give it SWIM class animals and YELLOW chaos drives until it cocoons.
This will give you a NEUTRAL WATER Chao.

2. Give it FLY class animals and PURPLE chaos drives until it cocoons.
This will give you a NEUTRAL AIR Chao.

3. Give it SPEED class animals and GREEN chaos drives until it cocoons.
This will give you a NEUTRAL SPEED Chao.

4. Give it POWER class animals and RED chaos drives until it cocoons.
This will give you a NEUTRAL POWER Chao.

5. Give it GARDEN FRUITS until it cocoons.
This will give you a NEUTRAL NORMAL Chao.

So now we have fifteen different Chao.
