Hello.

My screen name is Ultroid Blue. And, well
I have a confession to make. It isn't
pretty, so all you out there in bbs-land
who can't stomach this sort of thing
should use your back button now...




Ok. Here goes.

Today I intentionally and willingfully
beat a chao, injuring it severely. I
continued to attack the defenseless
chao as it screamed and ran from me,
and ended my assault by placing it into
a vmu and deleteing it forever.

Gruesome as it is, even more sickening
is what provoked such a grizzly fate.
It all began in the normal garden. I was
tending to my various chao, petting,
hugging, feeding, and most importantly
raising their stats with chaos drives.

Everything was going as usual except
for this one chao who kept following me.
I would pet it and think nothing more.
But it's affection towards me drove it
to continually shadow my every movement
in hopes of another snuggle. It seemed
so, so...dirty.

I let it tag along for what seemed to be
hours. I had duties to complete. But this
chao's demanding nature was treading on
my very thin patience. It kept grinning
this uncomfortable grin in my direction.
I was very uneasy at this point. I had
downloaded that particular chao, and
its' motives were not yet clear to me.

Then, in an instant, A-life in the garden
changed for the worse.

I had just harvested a new fruit from
a tree planted earlier, and was about to
feed it to one of my best chao, when
in runs my stalker who assumes the
fruit was for it. The chao snatches the
fruit out from under my nose and sits
down with a smile for it's last meal.

In a frantic act, I grab the fruit from
the chao. But it grabs it right back
and continues it's feeding. Desperate
to save the fruit, I...

...I...hit, the chao. I must have hit it
too hard because it went flying into the
pond. I dropped the fruit and went to
aid my struggling friend. But as I
grabbed for it, the chao bucked and
twisted from my grasp.
I was shocked. My once biggest fan
had turned my enemy in one angry swipe.
It scoweled at me and I could tell it was
holding back tears. I tried to cuddle it
and say I was sorry...but...it, it wouldn't
listen.,,IT WOULDN'T LISTEN TO ME!

I then noticed something else: in my
initial attempt to secure my fruit,
I had wounded not only the new chao
but also the chao I spent hours
raising from an egg myself; the chao
whom the fruit had been intended for in
the first place.

Enraged, I flung it with all my might
into the marble stone wall of the
garden. It landed in a heap. What
started as a sniffle led to a full
blown wail as my chao victim burst
into tears. It kept crying and WOULDN'T
SHUT UP! I hit it some more. I kicked it,
stomped it, and rebounded it off of
walls a few times untill it stopped its'
crying. It looked right at me. Right into
my soul. It then turned about face and
ran full tilt away from me, screaming
all the way.

I chased it down and realized there was
no turning back now. This was going to
be the ultimate punishment. I held the
dying chao fast against me. Despite it's
struggles, I managed to hurl it into the
transporter. I uploaded it into the vmu
and in one instant, I pushed the memory
manager's button and erased the chao
from existance. The other chao saw the
whole incident go down, and I couldn't
have any witnesses to pin me to the
scene of the murder. T-they...met with
a similar fate.

And that dear friends is my story. I have
come to terms with my actions and am
continuing on with my life. If you have
a similar tale, then by all means share
it with the group. Let us learn and grow
from each others' mistakes. Although...
sometimes, I feel that, that those chao
aren't gone forever and may be
born again.

I can still hear...the screams...


-Ultroid Blue
