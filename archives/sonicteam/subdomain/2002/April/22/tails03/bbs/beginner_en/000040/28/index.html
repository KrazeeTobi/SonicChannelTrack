sonic: hay guys wazzzzz up?
knux: nothin much just eatin my easter candy.
sonic: ya i got a basket of candy about the same size as your basket.
knux: cool. hay, do u belive in the easter bunny.
rouge: ya he does cause hes a baby. i dont belive in the easter bunny.
sonic: shut up u bastard the easter bunny RULES!!!!!!!!
knux: ya the easter bunny RULES!!!!!!
rouge: oh cmon, u guys are babies.
sonic: come here and say that bat girl.
rouge: im gonna kill u. *attacks sonic but sonic does a "Light Freeze" and rouge freezes*
sonic: ok rouge i did it so u freeze but u can still talk. lets settle this in a positive manner. now do u belive in the easter bunny?
rouge: no u fuckin bitch.
sonic: say yes or ill use an extra strength sonic wind.
rouge: watcha gonna do, dry my hair??? ha. that wimpy sonic wind couldnt lift a leaf.
sonic: but it can lift a building.
rouge: right.
knux: just do it i wanna see some action here.
*tails comes over*
tails: whats happening here.
knux: sonic and rouge are fighting about what kind of damage his sonic wind can do.
tails: oh ok. fight fight fight.
*shadow comes over*
shadow: hay guys wazzzzzzz......
knux: shut the fuck up i wanna listen.
shadow: to what????
knux: jesus, do i have to tell this to u too?
shadow: YES!!!!!!!!
knux: fine u baby. sonic and rouge are fighting about what kind of damage his sonic wind can do.
shadow: oh ok. fight fight fight.
tails: hay dont copy me i just said that like 3 minutes ago.
sonic: will u guys shut the fuck up.
everyone but rouge: ok *they get quiet*
sonic: ok now where were we?
rouge: we were agrueing about your sonic wind.
sonic: oh yes. do u wanna see what it can do to that shed over there?
rouge: i already know what its gonna do. its gonna fly away and im supposed to be impressed right?? sonic, i could kick that thing once and it would go down.
sonic: fine ill do it to u then.
rouge: fine its not gonna do anything u bastard. its just gonna lift my dress up.
shadow: oh ya i gotta see this.
rouge: oh shit everyone is gonna be stareing at my ass.
shadow: u know it bat girl and im gonna squeeze that ass of yours.
rouge: oh no u wont.
sonic: oh yes he will. ready
rouge: oh fine.
sonic: "Sonic Wind"
rouge: that so power...... ahhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhh
sonic: bye bye rouge.
shadow: oh damn i didnt get to squeeze her ass.
sonic: dont worry youll squeeze it later.
shadow: ok
sonic: shadow??
shadow: what??
sonic: do u belive in the easter bunny??
shadow: hell ya he rocks!!!!!!
sonic: ok then lets go home and eat our candy.
everyone: ok. mmmmmmmmmmmm candy.

*mean while rouge lays in the pacific ocean*
rouge: can someone help me???
people: shut up bastard.


The End
