> who do you find the lost chao on the first stage

you need the mystic melody. When you have it go all the way to the part where Sonic runs down the building wall and bounces off. There should be a recycle bin anda mystic shrine behind. Use the mystic melody and bounce up all the springs and pulleys. When you get to the part where there is a pole and you fly really high, float towards the wall and the little guy should be prancing around in an opening. Your welcome:-)
