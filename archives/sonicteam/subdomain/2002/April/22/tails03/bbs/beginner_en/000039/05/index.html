> i dont know where are knuckles sunglasses exactly
Well, it's rough, but it's in Meteor Herd. Locate a tower at ground 1st. Next, locate a meteorite on same level yet another platform. Bash it towards the tower, remember aim it on2 the flashing red mark. If hit, a metal crate should appear. Otherwise try once again. Now destroy the crate & the switch under it. Enter the tower & dive down the pit newly opened. At the bottommost platform lies the Sunglasses.
