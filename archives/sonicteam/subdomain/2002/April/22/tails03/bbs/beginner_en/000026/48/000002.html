> I've noticed that some animals are VERY rare so I thought I'd make a small list to help.
> The best place to find the following is
>
> Skeleton Dog (reverts chao to normal): Pumpkin Hills
> Dragon: Iron Gate, you can get two in one run. Looks good on any chao.
Only when u count the 1 from the 3rd chao box. Also avalible in Wild Canyon & Aquatic Mine.
> Bats (makes a floating Chao, looks evil): Pumpkin Hills, good for Evil Dark chaos.
A better loot (& commoner at there) is avalible in Pyramid Cave.
> Tigers: They're everywhere in Sky Rail, these are great for red chaos.
> Green Bears: Not exactly special, but you can get them easy in Prison Lane, the pipe to the left of Tail's starting room. Good for green chaos, which you may have to breed.
> Phoenix: The only ones I've found are in sky rail and metal harber, get all the boxes. Good for red or yellow chaos.
Yes there r 4(yes, FOUR) in Metal Harbor. 1 in Meteor Herd & Final Chase.
> Half Fish: Pumpkin hill, get all chao boxes.
Get 1 in Egg Quarters.
> Unicorn: Get all the chao boxes in City Escape, or fly across to the rocket in Prison Lane, it goes good on purple chaos.
These r the rarest, I believe.
> Purple Bear: Not too hard to find, but most common in Sky Rail. Looks best on a dark purple colored chao.
> Raven: Easy to find in Sky Rail, looks best on a purple Chao
> Tropical Bird: Not too rare, but found in Sand Ocean after busting the boxes near the start at first turn table, there's a free life at the start so you can keep restarting. They go good on just about any Chao.
> Seal: These guys are in metal harbor mostly, kind of rare but not too special, If you can breed a sky blue chao it may look cool.
>
> These were the only ones I found rare enough to mention, I hope it's helpful to someone.
