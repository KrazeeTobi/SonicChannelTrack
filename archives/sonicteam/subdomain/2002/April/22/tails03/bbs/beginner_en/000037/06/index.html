> could anyone tell me where to fing mystic melody for knuckles and tails?

Knuckles' Mystic Melody is hidden in a room, in Wild Canyon, behind a painting above the Lonely Statue. You need the Shovel Claw to dig through, however.

In Hidden Base, take a pulley to a small room with Kiki's in them. After you blow them up, lower the nearby platfrom and use it to reach the narrow ledge on your right. Walk down the ledge, then drop from the ledge onto the red pillar just below and then hover to the other narrow ledge just ahead. Now carefully drop down toward the screen and land on the path below. The room ahead is blocked off by four iron containers. Destroy the containers, and then walk inside to grab the Mystic Melody.
