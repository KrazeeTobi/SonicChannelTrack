> do u know how 2 have a chao with a ball of fire on d head??????

Go to XXXX XXXXXX(I meant Pumpkin Mountain) with Knuckles, if you get all chao boxes the last one has an "Alien Fish" , that's what your looking for.
Easy way:
From start glide toward right cannon... dont take it but fly to JackOlantern tower in front of you. Climb and go left around tower to darker surface. You should see a platform with first chao box just above. Glide from platform to pumpkn MT. It's to the left of chao box and way in the distance, hold glide until your safe on the MT.
climb almost straight up to ledge with second chao box. If your collecting nothing but alien fish break box and turn and run to edge of cliff, take off and glide out into space, circle to the left and look for suspended platform with last chao box. Once you get it... Exit game and do it over and over (10 times max) until you have as many as you want. You will have same number of lives as you started with.
You can go to Egg Quarters with Rouge and find one in a Cage. Make a Tiki throw a bomb, pick it up and break cage. Exit stage, come in again...etc.
