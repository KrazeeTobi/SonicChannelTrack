It's in Final Rush... when you have passed(killed) the only 2 sleeping artificial chaos in your way on floating platforms, reached the highest point and have been flung into space to a long platform be very alert there are many ways to get there but there is a rail at a very sharp downgrade just after going through a chek point on another long platform.
As you ride down the steep rail you will see a, "TWO platform floating island " off a ways to your left, jump to it from a high point on rail, don't GRIND. If you miss have a few lives and you can try again from the chek point. Take the cannon and it will jet you where you want to go. Ta Taaa!
