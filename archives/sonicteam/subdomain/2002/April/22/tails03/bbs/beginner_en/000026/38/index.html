for sonic's mystic melody i believe when you reach a platform inside an almost fully enclosed area you have a choice between 2 rails

i believe its the right orange one, grind all the way off into midair till you get to a rocket

take the rocket and you will land on a square platform, jump off and hit the spring on a platform far away from you

from here keep going up platforms to the right, you should find it

if you take the purple rail, you will land next to a ruin, this will take you to the chao

tails: its in his egypt zone

its on the bottom floor of the level, beyond 4 metal boxes

knuckles: wild canyon, beyond 4 metal boxes and a wall on the catwalk above the first floor(in a corner close to the ceiling)

shadow: final chase, go all the way up each of the gravity tubes

after going up one you will see a tube ahead of you with a electric gate fully surronding half of it, jump on the top part, on the platform above is the melody

eggman: in his egypt zone

at the start of the level you see a rotating platform, jump on it and stay on one of the walkways until you face all the way to the left

the camera will turn around to show an open room, hover to it, but watch out for the bombing robot(try homing in while hovering)

rouge: dry lagoon, same deal, behind a 4 metal box wall
