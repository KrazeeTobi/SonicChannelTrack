Eggman:
Mystic Melody is at "Sand Ocean". Before you go on the very first rotating platform jump up on the low railing on the left side near the end. Rotate your camera left to look at a small lighthouse protected by a Beetle. Fire all guns in that direction. Your $%#^& camera will not look as you shoot you must turn it again to check the result of your shooting. You should also jump and hover while shooting. Careful don't touch analog stick while shooting. When the Beetle's gone it's safe to go to the end of one of the arms on the rotating platform and hover over to the prize. Aim at landing point very carefully and push analog HARD forward, don't relax on A button until you'r there.
