U may wonder y there is water in Death Chamber, but yes there is a waterway, which leads u 2 the chao! Anyway, pick Knuckles' Mystic Melody & Air Necklace up 1st. Find a ruin in Pyramid Core. (the ROUND room) Play it & the waterway opens! But there is NO air supply along. Once underwater, hit hourglass, swim clockwise 2 pass the door. Thereafter swim further across 2 more rooms. Float out, & u arrive in2 a room with NO doors. Find the hourglass on the ceiling rooms (1 of them is sealed) & hit it! The sealed room should open, & it's ur chance 2 retrive the lost chao, inside this room!
