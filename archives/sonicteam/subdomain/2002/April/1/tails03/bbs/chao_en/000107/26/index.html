> Can someone tell me where are hidden mistic melodies of Eggman,Rouge and Shadow?
Ignore other people that responded u.
4 Eggman: In Sand Ocean
Go a little forward. Stand on the rotating platform. Hover 2 ur left. But u MUST destroy the enemy that drops bombs when hovering. On the far shore lays the Melody.
4 Shadow: In Final Chase
When u reach where there r 2 stuck drums, (they don't spin) it's near. Go further forward until u reach the 4th spinning drum stack, counting starts from the stuck drums. On the top, jump 2 the 5th stack (far away) but beware of the electricity. The Melody is on the top.
4 Rouge: In Dry Lagoon
Go 2 the right wall. There's a ledge. Under it is a wall painting, in the shade. Dig in2 it & that's it!
