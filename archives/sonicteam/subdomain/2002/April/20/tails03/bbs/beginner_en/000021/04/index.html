(Smell that? These are fresh from today!)

1. Outside camera angle/Big the Cat

Float up with the air current. Turn around and climb up to the platform with the two wooden boxes. Climb up as far as you can above the platform, and move a bit to the right. You should see Big above you hanging on for his dear life. Go left a bit till you are in the middle of a darker area of rock, then you can go up a bit more. Rotate the camera to the right using the R Trigger. The camera will go right through the wall! You will get a view of the outside. You can see the other side of Big. But it doesnt end here! You can dig and see Knuckles go through the other side of the wall! You may have though that the items actually appeared on the other side of the walls, but now you know better. You will notice that pressing right will make you go left and vice versa. Go all the way to the right by climbing. If you let go the camera will go back around to your side. You will notice that some objects go through the wall, including one side of a chao box. Go back to where Big is. Go down without hitting or landing on any ledges(this is easy to do, you can see the objects clearly to avoid. When you are almost to the bottom, you can see the inside of the bottom floor of the level, the roof is removed and you can see the rocks floating.

2. Play to a wall

Go to the ledge at the top of the bottom level. Go to the corner with the metal boxes. Bust through and
climb through to the other side. Climb on the right side of the ruins door. Rotate the camera to the right again. You will see the Mystic Ruins object on the other side. Jump down. Stand next to the wall and you will notice you can play through the wall and activate the other mystic ruin without even opening the door! This could save time in certain situations.

DC: Making the rational irrational to your advantage. Enjoy.
