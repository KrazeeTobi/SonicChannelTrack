1. Eggman/Golem(previous post)

After you defeat the golem, hover over the edge of the ring. When it shows eggman jumping for joy, you can control the top of his mech, including rotating and shooting.

2. Eggman/Big in Weapons Bed

On the first aircraft carrier, you may see Big looking over the edge of one of the buildings near the edge. Defeat the enemy before the black boxes. Hit all the metal boxes(3). Jump to the top box. Jump off and land on the fence to the left. You can walk on the fence, so go all the way to the left. Jump onto the nearest building. Either you can hover around the 2 floor building, or jump inside it then jump to the next one(you go back in the direction of the beginning of the level, except on top of buildings). When you get to the building right next to the control tower, you are actually on the same one as Big the Cat! Go over to him, you will notice that he is not solid. This explains a lot. Um, there is nothing else to do, but at least you get a close up of him.
