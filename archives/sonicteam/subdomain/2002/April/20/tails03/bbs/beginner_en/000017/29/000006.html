The first emerald is on the ventilators on your right. Use the Drill Drive to get it. The second one is on the upper floor. Go to the upper floor, and go to the left side to open the red safes. Then go up to the middle platform. The emerald is in the middle. Th best way to get it is using the spiral upper. Now go out of the upper floor and look for a ventilator with metal & steel containers and a G.U.N. robot with a laser. Destroy the G.U.N. robot and the steel container that's right in the middle of the ventilator. Use the Treasure Scope and there will be a spring. Use the spring. That'll take you to a switch in the ceiling. Acivating that switch will make the weight in front of the red safes stop. Now go to that safe and dig in the carter to open it. That's where the last emerald is.
