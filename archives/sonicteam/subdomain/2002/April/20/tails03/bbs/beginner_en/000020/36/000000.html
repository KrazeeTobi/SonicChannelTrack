1. Sonic the Comet Part 1

In hard mode on 2 different walls you have to scale the wall using the black boxes. Go to one of these areas and stay on the [floor/roof]. Go over to a box that you can bounce attack onto, but don't. Instead go under the box. Stay against the wall. Jump into the box and proceed to use the bounce attack. You will go through the wall. If you are still in the bounce attack "position" you will appear to fly forever as a comet.

2. Big Part 1

Go to the area with the different colored shapes in hard mode. Pull the switch to end up on the purple one upside-down. There are 4 ways to see Big, I will list them in order from easiest to hardest. The harder ones have a bigger payoff(no pun intended).

a. Go through normally until you get to the side of the blue shape. Go down to the metal rail and look over the edge at the bottom of the blue shape.

b. Continue until you get to the side of the yellow shape with the spring on it. Go to the area above and to the right of the spring. Stand in the corner and look at the bottom of the blue shape.

c. Go to the area with the Mystic Ruin. Jump and dash to the top of the blue shape. Also try speeding up before you jump and dash. Go to the end of the blue shape, nearest to the goal ring. Jump down to the top of the yellow shape. You can barely see it so try landing on the edge of the catwalk of the blue shape before jumping to the yellow shape. Go under the blue shape on the top of the yellow shape.

d. While you are still upside down after you pull the switch to get on the purple shape, go to the right. You need to do C, except upside down. Jump to the blue shape. Go all the way to the end, by the goal ring.

In each situation you will see Big jumping upsidedown. However, you can only stand inside him if you do D.
