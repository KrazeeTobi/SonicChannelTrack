You don't truncate or pull out their tongues... even in a snowstorm! Treat them nice, send them to classes and observe them when they are happy. They will play with you... walk up with a wiggle and tease to be picked up, yank at your coattail(eggman) for attention. You can sometimes get 2 or 3 chao rubbing up against you and each other as you pet only one and the others show a heart also. Some times two will continue to kiss and snuggle after you go away. They will watch TV in a half circle at neutral garden and play the radio in dark garden. If they learn an instrument I have heard you can form them in a group around another who is playing(flute,trumpet,etc.) and a band will start up. Never did that my self. The real answer is spent a lot of time in the gardens and observe...
