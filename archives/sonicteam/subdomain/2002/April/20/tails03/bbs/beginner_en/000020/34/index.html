1. X-Cube(previous post)

Go to the room with a pulley above a chaos robot. Destroy the robot. Pick up one of the items near the far wall. Go backwards until the camera "zooms" in. This object is what i call an X-Cube. It has a design similar to the X Box, but is the shape of a GCN. Sonic Team seems to like putting future plans as conspicuous objects in their games.

2. The 3 Switches

You may know that you should never pull the center switch in the long room, you will fall to your death. You were mislead by incompetent people. I use the center switch as a shortcut. Pull it. Swing the analog pad all the way to the right. You will land on a thin path. Walk over to the rail and jump on it. You will grind to the next area. Watch out though! You will seem to be pushed back into the "hole". Keep cool, just keep moving forward. You will land in a sec. Sometimes, especially in hard mode, you will end up running backwards very fast.

DC: Making the rational irrational to your advantage. Enjoy.
