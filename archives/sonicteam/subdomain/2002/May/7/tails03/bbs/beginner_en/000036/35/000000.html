Nobody asked but I sure had trouble with this site... was I the only one? The #$%^#camera is zeroD in a 3D world.

Short cut; after finding third chao box and collecting a Pheonix (the're rare in SA2B) turn back and hop up on the fence facing the wrong way. Tails will be looking right at you. Make a leap of faith and hover, pulling the stick toward yourself. As tails glides toward you the #$@^%% camera will make a reference shift, don't let it throw you. Stay centered on the yellow bridge center and don't relax your stick pressure until you see the ancient ruin. Don't relax and turn until your able to land on or near the ancient ruin. Play the melody and jump on the booster tracks, don't push "A" untill you've collected the XtraLIFE. When you reach the Weight platform you will find on your left a line of tile platforms leadin up to the Pheonix in a cage.
Before you get there you will hop from one bobbing vertical column to the next highest.
If you're after the lost chao go from the lowest to the next highest on your left. (If you feel shaky you can go left then right) You will see a red surprise ballon around the other side of the girders. Leap and hover around (don't bump into girders) collect ballon prize and land on platform. The ancient ruin gives you a portal to the very top so you can hover along the path of vertical columns leading to the lost chao. Watch out for a lone Robot.

If you want the Pheonix instead, just jump down to get him with the cannon behind metal boxes. I've collected 10 Pheonix this way many times for my chao "PEACHES". Most times you can avoid the drives. Peaches is from a bright red egg, she's a bright red flying dark chao. Very different then ones I've made with Dreamcast SA1 or SA2. The gamecube chao do not lose stats when they mate and I've been able to maxout(all 99's) three and have 4 or 5 others on the way. I find raising chao much easier and I haven't used the gamboy advance yet... Chao adventure still boreing!!
