At the part where you run down the wall into the spring which launch you onto a bar, stay off the bar. Turn around, back towards the springs and the wall you ran down. Go forward and towards the right (facing the springs, I don't remember if the camera will stay turned) and there will be an Ancient Ruins. Play Mystic Melody and a spring will appear. This part can be tough. Go up to the first lift. Jump to the next. Use the homing attack on that beetle and hook onto the next lift. The beetle after that, when you use the homing attack, you need to hold the jump button when you do it. (So you bounce higher off the beetle, onto the next lift) Homing attack on the spring beetle to the next lift. This last beetle has an electric shield. You need to hold A again afterwords and move forward a bit to make it on the lift. Jump on the bar. When you jump off, go towards the wall, there's a hole in it, and there's the chao.
