To find the lost chao you need the hammer gloves, mystic melody, and shovel claw. At the beginning, climb up the wall until you reach a ledge. Look around until you find some iron containers. Break them. You should see a picture. Dig into the picture. When you come out on the other side, you will see a door and a shrine. Play the mystic melody. The door will open. Go inside and you will see another shrine. Play the mystic melody again and a blue swirl will appear. Jump into the swirl. when you come out, you will see the lost chao.
