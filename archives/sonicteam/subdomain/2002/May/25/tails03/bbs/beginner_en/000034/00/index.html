Here are the basic rules of seeds just so everyone knows.
1. Chao needs to know how to walk properly.
2. The Chao who you want to plant the seeds must have the spade and the watering can.
3. Trees can only be planted in the Normal Garden.
4. You can have up to 3-4 trees at a time.
5. Buying fruit is more expensive than growing it so you can get a good deal.
WARNING: If you exit and then enter the garden, there should be 1-3 unripe fruits growing on the tree. If you leave the garden without picking the ripe fruit on the special tree, it will count towards used fruit but you don't get the benefits! If you wait for the fruit to grow fully ripe EVERY time you go in there, you can get at least 12 fruits out of the tree before it dies. That quite a Ring Saver!
