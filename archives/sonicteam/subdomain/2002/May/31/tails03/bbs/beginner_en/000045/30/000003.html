Knuckles' Mystic melody is very easy to get. Above the lonley statue on level 2, (note that you need to dig) you will find a strange picture with a circle-like shape on it. Dig into the shape. It is but a few pixels in front of you after that. Now Sonic's is much harder. It is on the final Hero story level. Along the way on a rail, you will find a rocket. It will take you to an alternative route. The Mystic Melody is at the end of this route, although I'm not so sure where to find the rocket. Now, you need Rouge's Mystic melody for the treasure scope. (If you don't have it, it is in the starting area of the first level) Climb up to the second floor on the safe level, where somewhere you'll find the Altar. Once the platforms appear break the crates (You MIGHT need the iron boots) It is now in plain view. The air necklace is hard to find. Lower the water to either level 1 or 2, then go past a sign (I don't know which one) warning about ghosts. There will be a vast pool of water, so swim VERY fast or you'll drown (I didn't even have 1 second left!) At the end of the tunnel, there will be a way up to your new air necklace. Shadow's Ancient Light ability is easy to overlook. When you get to Sky Rail, go to the first Chao container. Break it, then go behind where it used to be to find a military enemy appear out of nowhere. Keep Homing Attacking the enemies until you hit a spring. There is the Ancient Light on top of the cliff. Now Sonic's Ancient Light is easier. Go to the part in Green Forest where you break open wooden crates. Turn right and you'll find floating enemies nearby. Destroy them using Homing Attack, and then there is the Ancient Light on the other side of the pit. Hope this helps :)
