Ok boy now I have to tell you somthing really important. Do NOT try this without the air necklace! You can get it at Aquatic mine. 1st kill the Bio-bot gaurding the laser door. Then get under the liquid and hit the time switch. Its the same one Rouge had to hit. Now get back to where the laser once was as well some air. Watch out for the sentenail and drill dive on to the steel crates which cover a spring board that will take you to the 1st door switch. Hit the switch and a scene will show one of the blocked doorways under the liquid opening. Dive under and swim through it while avoiding the Biobot lasers. Theres a bomb behind the green vat to your right. It will KO all 3 enemies. Also there is another door switch. Hit it and the blocked door will open. There is a time switch behind you. Swim up a bit and hit it. While time is frozen swim to the next room and avoid the lasers. You will find a sheild, rings, and several enemies in here. Swim down and hit the next time switch. Go through the next door way like you mean it and hit the second time switch. Swim on through and avoid the cages. You will find a small animal on the way. In the last room you will find the core switch. Hit it and get ready to play as Sonic...
