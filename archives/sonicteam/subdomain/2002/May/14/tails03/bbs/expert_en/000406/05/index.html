> k so all i need now is nuckles, tails, eggman, and shadow. . .i think

Knuckles-Go to Wild Canyon as Knuckles. Go straight into the windy gust throwing you upward. Look to the left and you should see three small buildings containing four boxes in each one. Ignore them. Go straight past them to find the Lonely Statue. Climb above the statue's head straight up and you should see a strange painting. Dig through it to find the Mystic Melody

tails- in hidden base after you ride the pulley and there are 4 tikis (the monkeys) throwing bombs at you in the next area there is some floor down where the sand is and 4 metal boxes blocking a doorway break the boxes and inside is the mystic melody

eggman- in sand ocean ride the spinning thinguntil it faces left you will have to hover to the platform to find the mystic melody

Shadow- in shadows last level there is one part of where you can keep spiraling up on magnetic tubes. Eventually you will get to the Mystic Melody. The Mystic Melody is a tool every character can get. To use it, you must find those strange boxes that are in each level.
