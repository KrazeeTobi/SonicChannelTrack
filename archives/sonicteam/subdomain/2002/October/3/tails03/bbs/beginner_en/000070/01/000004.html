technically, the biolizard has two forms; the biollizard and the finalhazard. the bioliz. will try and attack you by biting you.. run slowly away from it until it slows to a stop. you will notice it trying to breath through its life support system. jump and grind the tube thing- it waill lead to the support system- and attack the life support system thing with a homing attack. he'll again try and attack you.. run until he stops, and it will shoot out these orb things- he will shoot them up AND down so do a somersault to avoid the higher ones and, obviously, jump over the lower ones.. he will nudge his head to the side and start breathing through his life support system.. procede to grind on the thing and attack. eventually he will make this earthquake thing, and dozens of bubble things will shoot up. use a homing shot and HOLD A after each shot... when youre close enough, hit the support system and get ready for his next move- he will again make the bubbles fly up, but this time, you'll fly up, too. the bubbles are moving in a clockwise pattern, so you need to do the same, slightly edging yourself closer to the support system until you can jump up and hit it.. that should be the final hit and you should see a wonderful cutscene afterwards.. have fun
