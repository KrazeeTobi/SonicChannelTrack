Ugh... I don't really like the wording of that. Here's an easier explanation:

You have been let go of the rocket to land on the immediate rail below you. Press and hold B, then hit A at the right time at the end of the rail to jump and do a flip (trick). You'll score 1,000 bonus-trick points if you land on the platform ahead. This trick works with any rail that has a GREEN X at the end of it. This trick is also good for nagging extra points on other rails (with green X's). Just be sure to land on platforms and NOT other rails or you won't get the bonus points.
