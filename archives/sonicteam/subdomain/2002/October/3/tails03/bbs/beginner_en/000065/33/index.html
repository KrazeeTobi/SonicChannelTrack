Shadow: Final Chase
When you see a gravity tube that elongates beyond the platform, climb up. You'll eventually see a gravity tube with an electricity collar. Jump onto it, climb up and find the Melody.

Eggman: Sand Ocean (Booster needed)
Near the beginning of the level, hop on to the first turn-table. Look to the left and you'll see a platform. Wait for the proper moment, then jump and hover over. There's the Mystic Melody (and a nice shortcut)

Rouge: Dry Lagoon (Pick Nails required)
Near the beginning, look right. Go over that way to find a wall carving (as seen in Knuckles' Death Chamber episode) Dig in (don't eat it) and find the Mystic Melody. Easy.
