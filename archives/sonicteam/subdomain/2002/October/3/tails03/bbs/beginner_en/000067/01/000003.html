Dr. Eggman can find the Mystic Melody in Stage 03: Sand Ocean. Just jump on to the first of thouse turning platform thingies and stay on. To the left of where you entered the stage there is a building with a robot flying above it (NOT the one with the metal boxes). Destroy the robot from afar then hover over to the building the next time that it comes around and jump up to the glowing circle to get the upgrade.
