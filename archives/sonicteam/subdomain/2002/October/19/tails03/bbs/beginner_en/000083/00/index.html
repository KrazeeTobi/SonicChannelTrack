If you mean Sonic/Shadow, wait for Sonic Wind/Chaos Spear and do a Homing Attack IMMEDIATELY so you don't get hurt!
Or, the first 3 hits, just do a Spin Dash and he won't see it coming!
If you lose a lot of lives just use the Light Dash to collect as many rings as you need to make them back up...you won't be attacked while you're doing it!

If you mean the Biolizard, do the following:
First 2 hits, grind up the life support tubes & Homing Attack the shiny red part.
After that, homing attack the bubbles when they appear until you reach the top.
Last hit, you MUST have rings b/c you WILL get hit the first few attempts. Float toward the life support pack while dodging bubbles (the hard part). When you reach the pack just press A (the easy part). Then comes Finalhazard.
