This is divided in steps, ok?

1- Go to the Chao Black Market, and buy the most expensive Item there is. Eggs/fruits don't count.

2-Put the item in the Neutral Chao Garden, save the game.

3- Go back to The chao garden, and sell the Item to the Black Market.

4- Reset the game without saving anything.

5- When you go back to the Chao Garden, The Item will still be there, and the rings you got will still be there, too.

6- Repeat steps 3-4 repeatedly until you can buy The item you want.
