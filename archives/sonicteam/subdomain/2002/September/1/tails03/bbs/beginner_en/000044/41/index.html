You have to wait about 6 hrs for it to turn back into a baby Chao. Than you have to start all over again until it has transformed a total of 3 times. Then feed it one ( and Only one ) of every animial.
To get a Dark Chaos feed it 1 dark fruit
To get a Light Chaos feed it 1 Hero fruit
To get a Nuetral Chaos feed it 1 Chao fruit
You can check you Age status at the Doctors.. it has to say atleast 2 under times transformed. Good luck.
