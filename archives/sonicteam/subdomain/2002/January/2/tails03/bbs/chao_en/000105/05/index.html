Hi folks! I've been away a while - creating an online chao club!! There's a chat site, message board, news, photo album and more - all under one roof!!

The club is dedicated to chao breeders everywhere! And is a place where they can come to ask questions, meet other chao parents, join in with a chao debate, flick through the photo album, and chat about their (adoribal) chao.

The club is also a place where regulars from this board can keep in touch with each other. The minute you enter the club room you can see which of your buddies are online at that moment, click on them, have a chat. The club is called "Chao Express", here's the address:

http://clubs.yahoo.com/clubs/chaoexpress

Why not drop by and join - it's absalootly free!!

See you there! ;oD
