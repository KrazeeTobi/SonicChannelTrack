I have a Sonic Chao and a Knuckles Chao. I have here how I made them both.

For the Sonic Chao, take a baby and give GREEN animals and chaos drives. When it evolves, it will be GREEN. Just keep giving it GREEN animals and chaos drives and it will turn blue. (I still have a Chao that is green, but I have seen other sites on what to do.)

For the Knuckles Chao, I do the same thing only with a RUBY Chao. It will keep it's red color, too. I stick with giving it just Chaos Drives because if we give it other animals, it would get its looks (e.g. rabbit ear).
