I'll give you your answers right here. ^_^

OK, once you give a Chao an animal and it get's one of the animal's body parts, it can't be taken off.

And for a Sonic Chao, give a chao that just hatched GREEN Chaos drives and animals. If you give it anything else, it won't work. One more thing, you must raise the Chao with good and bad characters (Sonic and Shadow), and when it evolves it will look green. Just keep giving it GREEN chaos drives and animals. Don't forget to feed it! :)

I have a special way to make a Knuckles Chao. I think it's easier and fun. Download a RUBY Chao from the Black market. Then make it a Sonic Chao. I like to use Chaos drives only, so it doesn't get animal parts. When it evolves, it won't turn geen or blue, it will stay red. I like the shiny effect. Have fun!
