The best way to thin out chao is to use a chao editor to delete them. You can pick one up at <franken.de/users/deco> I think. It's no good for making and posting chao on the net as they will be glitchy and illegal but it makes an excellent trash bin.
1 load game in slot 1, fire up!
2 load editor in slot 2
3 throw unwanted chao in VMU with editor( if it has a baby already, must remove VMU from slot and delete)
4 pause game and remove VMU2... run editor and delete chao. replace VMU in slot 2.
5 Continue game and throw next unwanted chao in VMU2... etc.
