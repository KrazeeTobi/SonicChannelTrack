ok, heres a few things to start you off

1. try to hit all the ramp at the beginning, and when you get to a corner go to the outside edge and turn sharply

2. after you run down the wall and hit the springs, aim so that you can hit the bar in the middle, letting you reach the top of the ledge

3. use the light dash whenever you can, for example, after a pulley when you first enter a park area, go to the left to find a ramp and jump off it, this will lead you into a speed square that will then lead you to a curve of rings before a loop, after you light dash those, light dash the small line of rings right after, you may get a bonus

4. at the end, if you hold all the way right when sonic goes down the hill, you may jump right into the ring

5. at the top of the large set of stairs, after you hit the robot, jump dash to propell yourself and jump off the orange pad (to the right)into a speed up item

6. after the boarding scene, and a robot, you may see a metal box to the right, break it and a line of rings will appear, light dash em

7. the gold beetle is on a ledge right after the first set of curving stairs

i also suggest getting all the permanent upgrade items(one in every level!)

these arent in any particular order, and if you need more hints, just post me, im on everyday
