After you begin raising a chao you must be extra nice to it. They were programmed to be unpredictable creatures with their own personality flaws and of course ailments. When a Chao coccoons (please ignore spelling if it is not correct) itself for the second time it wil either fade away or reencharnate itself. We all can get attatched to the little guys (or girls, just for the sake of arguement),but death is unavoidable. There is no specific time limit on a chao lifespan, so don't feel bad. If you really want to keep your favorite chao alive just backup your save file after you form it.
