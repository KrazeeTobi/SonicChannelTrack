In Neo Green Hill, act 1, play as Tails and collect as many rings as you can on your way, then hit the bonus spring and complete the bonus stage. Fly back to the beggining of the stage and collect as many more rings as possible before finishing the stage. With this trick, I can usually get upwards of 600 rings at a time.
