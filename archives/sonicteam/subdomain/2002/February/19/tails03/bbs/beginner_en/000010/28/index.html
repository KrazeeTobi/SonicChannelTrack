First, go to the Aquatic Mine and get the air necklace.

Then, There is a freeze time swith on one of the pillars. Press it and quickly swim to the surface. Jump out of the water and head past the water creature and through the lazers. Grab the handle and head up. Press the switch and head down the other side.

This is where you need the air necklace. You will have to quickly swim through the tunnels to get to the switch. You will have to freeze time in order to pass through tunnels of rushing water also.
