Take one of the dark characters, I like Eggman, and love each one until the squiggle goes away then pet by rapidly pumping on the "X" button. When the heart show for a second or two quickly pick up and love more. Now feed and pet... keep the heart showing. When he doesn't want anymore to eat, goes to sleep... leave him alone and go to the next one. Occupy yourself collecting fruit in a pile while they sleep. Do not leave until they have all woke up.
