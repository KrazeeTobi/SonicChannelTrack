> I need the locations of a skeleton dog, and any imaginary animals for dark characters. Also, if anyone knows a unicorn location for light or dark I would realy appreciate it. Any help is really apreciated

Go to "Pumpkin Hill". Glide to the cannon on your left, take the cannon to its highest point then glide to the bonedog in Church Mountain graveyard. Exit and run again, repeat until you have 10 animals(or less). You will regain all you lives next time you start Dreamcast.

Dragon... Got to "Wild Canyon", glide ahead and slightly to left, get chao Garden Key from column and flip backward into air stream . Rise to the top of air column then glide left down alley with enemy craft to the end. Land on sand break Blue Box and jump quickly to large statue head, avoid released animals. Jump and glide all the way to the end of the other alley across the way. Break the Blue Box and you have 1. If you got your digging claws climb the lonely statue, jump to wall, climb to picture, drill through wall, jump and climb wall on left pick #2 Dragon in the corner, EXIT and repeat until you get 10 or less.

Phoenix... Go to "Metal Harbor" Push all the way to the spot where you have to use Homing Attact on a bunch of beetles. Don't try to pick up the extra life. If you are careful and you must be quick, you'll endup on the platform with planes divebombing you. IF you have avoided animals and drives so far, GOOD!, hang left but avoid penguin. Use magic hands on beetles and you will find two yellow hardhat. Both have a pheonix. Further along on same side you will see two more hardhats. One has another Pheonix. EXIT...etc.

Unicorn... go to "Dry Lagoon". Stand behind turtle guards and take them out without picking up drives. The trick of getting 10 is to get all three Blue Boxes without getting anything but the Unicorn in the 3'rd box. I use this route... Don't open the visible blue box but climb the wall at the end of that platform. A beetle is protecting the blue box, don't mess with him, rush to the corner behind blue box. Watch his fire and rush out, break box , collect key, and charge down to the box you saw below. Make sure you got an angle when you break the box so you can sail on over to the turtle quickly, avoiding any junk animals. Go to next level jump to Pylon and climb to to top. look to left and you should see the Blue Box containing Unicorn.

For dark character... go to "Sand Oceans"... got bats, bonedog if you get all 3 Blue Boxes.
