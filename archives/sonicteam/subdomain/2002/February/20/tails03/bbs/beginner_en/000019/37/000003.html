You must play "Final rush" to get the mystic melody. If you can choose "Stage Select" move cursor right until the end of stages. "final rush" is the next to last Sonic icon. Otherwise you must wait 'til you get there in the story(Hero Side) If you are there you must go through 4 point markers. you will have gone way down then way up to a very high level using rails and springs. A spring after the 4th point marker will throw you into an intense situation you must not help it but steer to the left and try to stop on long platform... look around carefully... I forget exactly but it's wise to look around every chance you get from now on, don't be forced into jumping before your ready or u will miss it. This was my most difficult to find... hang in -Good luck.
