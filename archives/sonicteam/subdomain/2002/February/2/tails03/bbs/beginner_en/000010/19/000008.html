The magic gloves are found in city escape; do you know the part with the two swinging bars, right after the mystic melody ruin?
Well, don't go on the bars. Proceed and you will see some iron crates below you. Use the bounce attack on them and voila, the magic gloves are yours.
Just push Y until "Magic hands" shows in the B function, top-right corner of the screen, and use it nearby an enemy.
Pretty useless if you ask me, but it's a quick way to take out that chaos-like monster.
