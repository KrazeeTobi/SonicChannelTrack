I gather you want the Air Necklace for Cannon's Core? It really does help out a lot!

Anyway, you can find it in the 'Aquatic Mine' Zone.

Look for the shafts at each corner of the area (they're probably underwater at this point). One shaft will have a Spring/Jump Pad

Once you find the right shaft, lower the water level so that you can get down there without wasting any oxeygen, (skipping this step will ultimately cost Knuckles a life!).

Go down into the shaft that you found. Then dive into the water at the bottom of it, follow the tunnels until you come to the point that only hasone way... UP. Swim up as quickly as you can, at this point, Knuckles' oxeygen countdown will begin, when you surface, jump straight up to grab he Air Necklace!

Now Knuckles can breathe under-water! This is really useful in Cannon's Core. Just be careful, Knuckles' portion of Cannon's Core can be really hard, even with this upgrade!

Good Luck!
