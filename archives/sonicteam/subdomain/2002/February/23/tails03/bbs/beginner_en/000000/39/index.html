I had a similar problem getting online with SA2, hopefully my solution will help you too. I had Unreal Tournament and entered in a fake account for my 2nd internet connection in order to get some locked levels that require Seganet to access. If you've used a game that has the option to put in a 2nd internet account and you entered a 2nd account, apparently this web browser tries connecting with the 2nd account, not the primary account. If the 2nd account is no longer working you won't be able to connect. You will have to use whatever disc you have that allows you to configure your 2nd account and enter your working internet account info into the 2nd account setup. This solved the problem for me, hopefully it will help you too. Good luck, let me know if this helped!
