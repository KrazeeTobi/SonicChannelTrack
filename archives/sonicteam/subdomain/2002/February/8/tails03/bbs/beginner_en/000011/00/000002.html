chao legend, i went through the same
problem. sorry, but i can only tell you
2 things:

1. certain ISP's may be incompatible
with any DC, especially AOL, Prodigy,
CompuServe, Juno, and NetZero,
probably because those are
not PPP-compatible.
(PPP means point-to-point protocol;
AT&T andMSN are 2 examples.)

2. check your phone number options,
like your area code, long distance
number, and outside number. my advice
is to only fill in the dial-up number
(you don't need a backup number) and
the area code, and the DMP numbers.
if you don't know the DMP numbers,
write to your ISP about them.

one last thing: when your PC's connected
to the Internet, look for the connection
icon (2 green monitors hooked together),
right-click on it, and choose Properties.
this should tell you whether your ISP
is PPP compatible or not.

well, thats all i know. i hope you can
solve your problem soon!
