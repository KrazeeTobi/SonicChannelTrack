> I have looked everywhere in the zone's after the hidden chao, but I can't find'em anywhere?!? Where are they? Is it like the animals that they are in a container or something?

First of all, you have to choose Mission 3 of the stage you want from the Stage Selection Screen. When you first start the level, the box should say, "Mission 3: Find the lost Chao!" If you choose a different mission, a life will be there instead. Second of all, you must find the Mystic Melody level-up for that character, or you can't get to the Chao.
Here's the levels for Mystic Melody:
Sonic--Final Rush(near Ruins)
Shadow--Final Chase(near Ruins)
Tails--Hidden Base
Eggman--Sand Ocean (must have Hover from Lost Colony, and I think the Volkan Cannon power-up from Weapons Bed)
Knuckles--Wild Canyon (must have Shovel Claw)
Rouge--Dry Lagoon (near Ruins, must have Pick Nails from Egg Quarters and Iron Boots from Mad Space)

Here's a few of the Chao that I found (for some of these you need level-ups from other levels!)

City Escape(Sonic)
At the part where you're walking down the building and hit the bumpers at the bottom, DO NOT GO FORWARD! Instead, turn LEFT and head towards the buildings behind you. The Ancient Ruins (where you play Mystic Melody using B button) are next to the recycling bin. After you play the Melody, you'll know what to do next.

Pumpkin Hill(Knuckles)
Ancient Ruins are at the top of Pumpkin Mountain. Grey platforms should appear. Jump on them and fly to the floating platforms (you can't reach them any other way cuz they're too high). The Chao is on the farther platform.

Wild Canyon(Knuckles again)
On the top floor of the Wind Temple (ie, DONT USE THE WIND ALTAR TO LEAVE THE BUILDING YOU START IN!) in one of the corners, behind some crates. You may have to play Mystic Melody more than once.

Dry Lagoon(Rouge)
Don't save the turtle, you don't need him. Climb all the way up to the top level and head towards a part where you see a tank that shoots bombs near the middle of a wall. Destroy the tank, then break the iron containers. You should see a painting with a cartouche (Egyptian-type amulet) on the wall. Dig into the cartouche to pass through the wall. The Ruins are on the other side, the rest is self-explanatory.

Radical Highway(Shadow)
This is at the middle of the level. Near the area where you float, there are some suspension cables. Grind on them, crouching until you've gone BACKWARDS all the way up the rail. The Ruins are on the platform.

Check the Game Hints page to find out where the rest are, I don't have them yet.
