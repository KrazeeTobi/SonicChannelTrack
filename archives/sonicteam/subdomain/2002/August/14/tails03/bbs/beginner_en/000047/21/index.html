> O.k., your lucky because I beat this part! first, flip the switch in front of you.this should bring you to a purple building. now go to the switch behind the gun beetle(the floating robot). this should bring you to a green building. follow the path until you reach two boxes. jump onto them, then bounce attack to another part of the building. flip the switch, and then you should be on a yellow building. follow the path, homing attack the beetle, then homing attack again to reach the blue building. to the right there is a switch. you know what to do!(if you don't, FLIP THE SWITCH!!!) attack the artificial Chaos, then flip another switch. bounce on the bouncer and go down hit another bouncer onto a rail onto the green building again. hit the shield beetles( but not when their shield is up!) and bounce to another building. after this you should be able to figure it out!
