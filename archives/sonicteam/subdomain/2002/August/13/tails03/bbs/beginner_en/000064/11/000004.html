Well it takes a couple of hours in SAME GARDEN as your chao for them to grow. If you want the one who is crawling to walk just make sure he has 50 point in the running section.
Once they formed a blue cocoon and become adult, you can give them heart fruit to have them go into their mating season and have babies or you can wait until they are around 2-3 years old.
If you chao likes one type of fruit buy the right seed and they will be happier, but to plant the seed you need a chao who as the shovel(scoop or wathever you call it;)) it's won after you beaten the 3 levels of the first begginer race(the swimming race)
At Kindergarden you can give them a name and see the medical chart, also the courses are cool because after they start doing what they learn in the course in the chao garden.
My Chao draw Shadow on the grass in the Chao garden, that was soooooo cool.
