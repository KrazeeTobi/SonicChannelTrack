If you are having troubles raising your chao's stats, do this:
Fisrt, buy 5 chao fruits and put them in a remote area. Next shake all the fruits off all the trees and bring them to that spot. Finally, bring your chao over and have it feast on it all. When it's done, it's stats should be alot higher. Do this often and you'll have a level 99 in his stats in no time.
