Ancient Light: Green Forest: Before the second checkpoint you'll hear a Bomber Beetle. Walk back twords a ring capsule and you'll see it. Homing Attack it, the capsule next to it, and the 1up capsule after it. the Ancient Light is right next to the capsule.

Mystic Melody: Final Rush: At the sixth checkpoint, walk back to the Anicent Pillar. Run on the left side of the platform and JUMP of at the end. HOLD FORWARD OR YOU WON'T MAK IT!! You should land on a platform with a Rocket. Grab it and you'll land on another platform with a 1up. You'll see another platform with a spring on it. Jump to it and hit the spring. Walk forward from where you land and you'll see a Vertical Rail. Grind up it and the Mystic Melody is right up there.

Useage of Magic Gloves: Walk up to an enemy and press Y. The action window will pop up saying "Magic Hands". Press B and you'll grab the enemy. It will also turn into a little ball you can throw at other enemies.
