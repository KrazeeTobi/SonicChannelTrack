> WHere do you find the breathing app. in aquatic mine for knuckles?
>
Form the begining of the level run up to the water powered lift in the middle(It has big ghost symbols on it) and climb up. Grap one of the pullies that are on top of it to get to the top floor. Run around until you find a star swich(The eyeball thingy) with a sign in back of with a big '3' on it. Hit the switch and drop to the bottom of the now drained main room. Run around the edge of the bottom(Where the little amount of water is) until you find a caged in area. Climb the cage an go in through the top. It should have a spring and a small brick passageway in it. Go in the passage and dive down through the water until you hit the bottom. There shold be a tube that pops out of the side of the bottom of the well. Go through it(There's also a booster tred on the bottom of the pipe) and swin around all the wood AS QUICKLY AS POSIBLE!! You should be at about half air by now. There is another pipe at the bottom of the wood shaft, go through it. When you get out swim up as quickly as you can. The timer music should have started by now. You should hit the srface of the water quickly and when you do, the camera will shift revealing the Air Necklace right above you.
