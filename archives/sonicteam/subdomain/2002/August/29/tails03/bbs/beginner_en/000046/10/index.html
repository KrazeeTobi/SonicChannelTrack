Go to 1P mode and select Stage Select. Select Chao World as your stage and pick one of the characters who has already been in the garden. Go to the garden the Chao you want to move is in and pick him up. Leave the garden and you'll be back in the Lobby. Walk accross the Lobby to the other end to the door with blue pillars on either side of it. That's the kintergarden.
