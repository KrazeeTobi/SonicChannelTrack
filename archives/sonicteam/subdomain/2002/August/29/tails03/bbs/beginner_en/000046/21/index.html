> Were is the chao in radical High way


I shouldn't musle in on someone elses help but...
After the double Loop-de-loop, Hit one of the ramps and get up on top wher the G.U.N. Hunter is. It has a swing pole behind it and a Omaochao. Either use the pole, climb the elevators, and Homing attack the three G.U.N. Bettles or just SLOWLY walk along the steel beam to the left and right of all this, to got to another G.U.N. Hunter with a spring board behind it. Hit the spring board and you'll be at the point were you grind down the cable supports. Stand were you would normaly like you would grind down it, BUT DON'T!! Instead turn around and grind down the other support. Remember to hold 'B' or you won't make it. When you come up you'll go flying off and miss the spot you want to land on unless you steer yourself onto it. This spot will look just like the other spot with the gold Beetle, but instead of another GB there will be an Ancient Pillar where you play the Mystic Melody. This will make two Chao platforms appear right in front of you which are easy to jump accross. The lost Chao is on the other side of them on a large metal platform.
