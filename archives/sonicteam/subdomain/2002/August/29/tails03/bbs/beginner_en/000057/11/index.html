I can help a little. from what i've heard, you need to pet ur chao a whole lot. mine has on level 2 on stamina and it's on the vedge of becoming a Hero/Angle chao.
If you pet it alot with a hero character(sonic/tails/knuckles) it will be a hero chao.
If you pet it alot with a Dark character (Shadow, Eggman/Rouge) it will become a dark chao.

AND LISTEN TO THIS. IF YOUR CHAO EVOLVES INTO A HERO CHAO, GO INTO THEY LOBBY AND THERE WILL BE STAIRS LEADING UPWARDS TO HERO GARDEN! SAME THING WITH DARK CHAO EXCEPT THAT THEY WILL BE GOING DOWN INTO THE DARK CLOUDS.

O ya, I forgot to mention, you need to have a regular chao to do this.
