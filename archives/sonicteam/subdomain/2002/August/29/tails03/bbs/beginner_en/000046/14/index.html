> > > rouge final stage

Ok, when you start you'll notice four pillars with white blocks on them. Climb the shortest one(To your right) and glide at a diagianle to the second shotest one. Make sure you land above or on the white block. Now glide to the pillar to the right of you and climb on top of it. One final glide diaganaly over to the tallest pillar. The caemra angle will change showing a waterfall when you hit the Time Stopper on top of the pillar. Drop down to the floor and pass through the waterfall into a tunnel with a Shield Hunter in it. Go past where the SH was and drop down onto a platform(If you don't hit it, you'll land in a vat of acid). This room has the flying dice blocks like in Eggman's section. Cling
to the wall with the Time Stopper under the tunnel with the blocks coming out of it. As soon as one leaves the tunnel hit the TS and enter the tunnel. There are three Artfical Chaos(Blue Blobs) that you should kill. There is a Star Switch where one of AC's were. This will open the casule that contains the button to finsh the level. Head back out to the black dice room and jump onto the wall with the Time Stopper again. As soon as a block blocks the passage you just came through, hit the TS and jump back on the platform in the middle of the room. There is a spring you can jump on to get you back to the tunnel where the Shield Hunter and the waterfall was. Run through the waterfall with the Time Stopper switch on and hit the button in the now open capsule. I really hope you've gotten the Air Necklace form Aquatic Mine for Knuckels. You'll have a really, REALLY hard time in the next part of the level without it.
