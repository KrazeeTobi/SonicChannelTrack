Sonic:Hey, whats the big idea?
Tails:I wanted to live with those guys, but, they kicked me out. (they look down, and pull down a branch.)
Sonic:Why would you want to live with them? You're not a bird.
Tails:I'm not?
Sonic:No.
Tails:But, I can fly, maybe I'm a bug.
Sonic:You're a fox, and foxes don't fly!
Tails:I do. (starts flying around Sonic)
Sonic:You've got two tails!
Tails:Doesn't everybody?(He lands in Sonic's hands)Can I live with you?
Sonic:Well, don't you have a family?
Tails:Nope, I've been lost for a long time, and I need somebody to live with.
Sonic:I can't slow down for stuff like that, gotta juice kid! (Dashes off)
Tails:(following Sonic) You wanna see me cry?(starts crying)
Sonic:(Slips on the wet trail, and crashes into stuff)
Tails:(Picks him up with his teeth, and carries him around)
Sonic:Ok, ok, you can tag along for awhile, and I'll think about it.
Tails:(lets go of Sonic)Great! You can be my big brother!
Sonic:(drops into more stuff)
Tails:(drops down and gets him in a net)
Sonic:What's your name, squirt?

Sonic:Miles?
Tails:Yeah, I hate it.
Sonic:Ok, I'll call you something else, I'll call you Tails.
Tails:Wow, what a day, I find out that I'm not a bird, not a bug, get a brother, and get a new name, I'm Tails!
Sonic:(barely holding on to the net as Tails is flying around really quickly)
