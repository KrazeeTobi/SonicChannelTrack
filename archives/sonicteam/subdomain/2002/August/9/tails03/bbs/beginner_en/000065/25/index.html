> Instructions to Air Necklace

Ok. Start at AQUATIC MINE stage. Flip the egg trigger to level 3 (lowest setting) and go to where to the water would normally rise to level 2. Walk around and you'll see a "Caution" sign with ghosts. Go down there and follow the shaft. It'll be close, and you might lose a couple lives, but you'll eventually get to the air pocket and find the Air Necklace level-up item. This item allows you to stay underwater forever. Useful in: Death Chamber, Aquatic Mine, and Cannon's Core (Knuckles period)
