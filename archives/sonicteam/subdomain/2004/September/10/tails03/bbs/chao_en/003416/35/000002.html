Unlock Game Gear Sonic 1
Get 20 emblems to unlock the Game Gear version of Sonic 1

Unlock Game Gear Sonic 2
Get 110 emblems to unlock Game Gear Sonic 2

Unlock Mission Mode
Complete Adventure mode with each character.
Then, Mission Mode will be unlocked and playable for that character via
the Main Menu.

Unlock Sonic Chaos (Game Gear)
Collect 60 emblems to unlock Sonic Chaos.

Unlock Sonic Drift
Get 40 emblems to get the Game Gear Game Sonic Drift

Unlock Sonic Drift 2
Collect 130 emblems and have beaten at least 20 missions to get Sonic Drift 2.

Unlock Sonic Labyrinth
Either get 100 Emblems or 80 Emblems and Complete 20 missions to unlock Sonic
Labyrinth

Unlock Sonic Spinball
To get Sonic Spinball, get 80 emblems.

Unlock Sonic Triple Trouble
Collect all 130 emblems to unlock Sonic Triple Trouble.

Unlock Tails Adventure
Collect all 130 emblems and complete all 60 missions.

Unlock Tails's Sky Patrol
Get 130 emblems and complete 40 missions to unlock Tails' Skypatrol.

Sonic Chaos: Instant Chaos Emeralds:
Press Up, Down, Up, Down, B, A, B, A, B, A at the Sonic Chaos character selection
screen.

Sonic Chaos: Sound Test:
Press Down(2), Up(2), Left, Right, Left, Right, B, A at the Sonic Chaos title screen.

Sonic Chaos: Extra continues:
In the first Turquoise Hill Zone mission, press Up + A to make Tails fly to the top
of the screen. Stay there until the CPU makes you get down. When your points are
getting added up, you should get three to four continues.
