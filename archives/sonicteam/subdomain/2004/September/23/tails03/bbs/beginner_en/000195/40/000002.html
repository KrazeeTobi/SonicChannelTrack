To get a sonic chao, make your chao a neutral running chao. For a shadow chao, do the same thing, but make it a dark chao. Here's some other cool chao!
Metal Sonic chao: get a shiny blue chao and make it a sonic chao. If you want it to REALLY look like metal Sonic, make it win a yellow metal in the chao races!
Eggman Chao: Get a red chao and make it a sumo chao. (I think u make a swimming/power chao, but I'm not sure) Then, give it penguins untill it has the eyebrows. Then give it skelaton dogs untill only the eyebrows remain. (this might take a few tries because the skelaton dogs might take away the eyebrows) it looks a lot like eggman!
Simba chao: Ya I know, the lion king sucked, but this chao is so cool! Get either an orange chao or a yellow chao and give it all the cheetah parts. It looks like Simba as a cub!
Black and white chao: Get a gold chao. When it hatches, DON'T leave the garden!!!!! Don't go to collect rings, to the kindergarten, don't even go to the transporter!!!! Just stay in the garden untill the chao evolves, and keep petting it and giving it fruits. When it evolves, it should be black and white! This chao's lots of work to get, but it's worth it!
boulder chao: get either a gray or a black chao. (I recommend the gray one. It looks more like a rock) then make it a sumo chao. Give it a bat so it loses it's feet. It looks like a giant boulder! This chao ROCKS! (pun intended)
