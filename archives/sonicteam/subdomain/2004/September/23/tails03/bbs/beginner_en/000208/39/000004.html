Sonic- Final Rush. Go to the part where you stand on a long platform and there's a much higher one to your right. You know, the part after the inclined rail where you don't need the homing attack? Get onto the higher platform by using the rail I just told you about.

Tails- Hidden Base. Go to the part with the doorway blocked by four steel crates. Break the crates and go through the door. Be careful not fo fall!

Knuckles- Wild Canyon. You know that part in the valley with a wall underneath two platforms held up by 2 pillars? One of those walls has a wall painting. Inside that painting, you'll see a series of OTHER paintings. go in the one in the middle on the wall to your right. If you go in any other painting in the series, you'll end up back in the underground.
