> How Do You Get Gold And Silver Chao. I Have Found A Shiney White Egg But It Keeps Dissapearing In The Shop When I Have Enough Rings. Can Somebody Help.

You get the gold and silver chao eggs from the Tiny Garden. The egg you get there will be choosen at random, so you may have to restart the game a few times to get what you want. If you don't a chao in the garden, the egg will always be the normal one. Once you get the egg, you can then bring to the GC via the link cable and the chao transporter.
