The three draiks carried me away. They were all bigger and older than me. The female, the grey one with the black stripes, said, "Atri is glad Atri and her brothers found Atri's sister!" (she always refers to herself in the third person) The white draik with the blue stripes nodded. "I'm glad too, Atri. Dante, isn't it great that we finally found her?" He asked the purple draik. The purple draik nodded and smiled. "It sure is great, Gonu." I was very confused.

Later, they stopped flying and set me down. The draik who had called herself Atri spoke first. "Atri thinks Atri and her brothers should tell Atri's sister who Atri and her brothers are." "Good idea Atri!" Said the draik who had been called Dante. "We're your brothers and sister. My name's Danteno, Dante for short." The draik that had been called Gonu spoke next. "I'm Doragonu, but you can call me Gonu." Then, the rather irritating grey draik spoke. "Atri is named Atriiyu, (a-tree-a-you) But Atri's sister can call Atri Atri. What is Atri's sister's name?"

I thought for moment, and realized that I didn't have a name yet! "I... I don't have one." I said. "Oh," Said Dante. "Well we'll just have to give you a name, then!" Said Gonu. "Atri wants to name Atri's sister!" Cried Atri. I rolled my eyes, then thought for a minute. "Zephyr Windstorm." I said. "What???" Said Gonu. "My name is Zephyr Windstorm." I said.
