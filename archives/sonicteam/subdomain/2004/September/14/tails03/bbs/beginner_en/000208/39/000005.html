Sonic- Final Rush. Go to the platform that comes after the slightly-inclined rail where the homing attack is not needed. If you look to your right you'll see a higher platform. Use the same rail to get there.

Tails- Hidden Base. Go to the area with the door blocked by 4 metal crates. Break the crates and go through. Try not to fall!

Knuckles- Wild Canyon. Go up the wind thingy and go down. You'll either land on the ground, on the wall, or a platform. Go in the wall painting underneath the platform. You'll see a series of other wall paintings. Go in the painting in the middle of the wall to your right. Otherwise, you'll end up back in the underground area.
