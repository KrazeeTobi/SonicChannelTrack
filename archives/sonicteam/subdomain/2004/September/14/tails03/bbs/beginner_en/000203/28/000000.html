The Chronicles of Snowfrost and Dark: part 1

I am Snowfrost. I am a white Chinese dragon with green fur running down my back. My eyes are an icy blue. I have a sister named Dark Snowfrost. She is a soul sister. That means she was once part of me. I shall tell how we were seperated....

I was once quite normal. I lived with my family and was very happy. But I always felt as though there was a presence deep inside of me... a darkness. "You have a soul sister." my mother would say. I didn't know what a sould sister was. But one day I found out...

I was playing with my favorite ball, when an older dragon came. "Lemme see that ball." he said. I handed it to him. "Thanks!" he said. And he ran off with the ball. I was, of course, very upset. That ball was my favorite toy. Then suddenly, a very scary, black dragon seemed to slither out of me. She flew in front of the bully. "Give that back." she said. The bully was so frightened that he threw the ball down on the ground and ran away. Then the black dragon flew back into me. I knew now what a soul sister was. I picked up my ball and continued playing.

end of chapter one
