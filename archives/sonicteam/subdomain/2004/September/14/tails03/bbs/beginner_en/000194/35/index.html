*a fox, a wolverine, a bear, and a raven rush over to me*
Let the meeting of the forest spirits now come to order!
*everyone sits down on tree stumps* We are gathered here today to talk about Shishi Gami's dissapearance! But first, we must make sure everyone is here! Aniyu! (an-eye-oo)
Fox: Here!
Me: Nedju! (nee-joo)
Wolverine: Here!
Me: Nava! (sounds like java)
Bear: Here!
Me: Daiben! (day-ban)
Raven: Here!
Me: And I'm here, of course..... But, Shishi Gami is not. And that is why we're all here!
Nedju: Mononoke! You know that you are supposed to be the master of the forest when Shishi Gami is gone!
Me: Yeah? What's your point?
Nedju: Why don't you take advantage of this opportunity and take Shishi Gami's place?
Me: Well, I-
Aniyu: Nedju! You know she can't do that!
Nava: That's right! We need proof that Shishi Gami is dead!
Daiben: Or at least proof that he's decided to step down!
Me: That's just what I was gonna say!
Nedju: Well, we need a leader!
Aniyu: Hmmm... Nedju's right!
Nava: Yes! If we don't have a leader, then what shall we do?!
Daiben: Mononoke! You have to do SOMEthing!
Me: Hmmmmmmmm.... Then here's what we'll do! I will be the leader until we find Shishi Gami!
Nava: Brilliant!
Aniyu: But how can we find him?
Me: I'm trying to figure that out!
Everyone: Hmmmmmmmmmmmmmmm.....
Me: I've got it!
Everyone but me: What? What?!
Me: We'll combine our skills to find Shishi Gami! Aniyu! You are a master when it comes to trickery! And Nava! You are extremely smart! You can help figure stuff out! Daiben! You are excellent at finding things! You will be the scout! And you, Nedju! You are the master of fear! You can scare litterally anything! If we use our skills wisely, we will be victorius!
Everyone: YAY!
Me: Then it's settled! Let's go!
