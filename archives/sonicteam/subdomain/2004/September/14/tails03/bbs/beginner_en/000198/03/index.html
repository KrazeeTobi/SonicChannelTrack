1. The Doctor and Principal are just idiots and doesn't know how themselves. You have to get the watering can and scoop from the Beginner Chao Races. Once this is completed, you need to retrieve a seed from the Black Market. Give the seed to your Chao and it will walk around. Wait for it to choose a spot and it will dig (using the scoop) and water it (using the watering can). It will do a little dance. The sproutling should be visible after that and will grow periodically after that. Be warned, these trees will not last forever.

2. Try nurturing the Chao with Dark characters like Shadow, Eggman, and Rouge. If you keep petting a Chao with either of these characters, the Chao's color should tint to gray and then to black. Then just wait for the evolution and you will have a Dark Chao.

3. They are the symbols that prove that you've beaten a level. I guess you could call them the 'proof of purchase' of that level. The more that you acquire, the rarer the items will be at the Black Market. Also, once you get all 180, you unlock Green Hill Zone (3D).
