*getting off boat* Okay Ashitaka. I don't think I need to be a seeing eye dog anymore....

in the gift shop.....

*the store is owned by a man and a female red chao*

Man: Hello there! Welcome!
Chao: *walks over to Wolfsbane* Hi.
Wolfsbane: *sweating* Uhh.... Hi. My name's Wolfsbane. What's yours?
Chao: Wolfsbane, huh? I'm Ruby... You're quite the unusual looking chao, aren't you?
Wolfsbane: Uhhh... yeah. That's how I got my name... *wolf tail swishes around* Nice to meet you, Ruby.
*Rouge comes rushing into shop*
Rouge: Did somebody mention JEWELS?!?!
Mononoke: RAAAAAARRG!!! *leaps on Rouge, knocking her out onto the street* GET OUT YOU FREAK OF NATURE!!!! *savagly tears Rouge apart*
Wolfsbane: *points to Mononoke* Uhh... that's my adopted Mom....
Ruby: *stares at Mononoke* Heheheheh.... That's kinda funny....
Mononoke: *steps in Rouge's remaining bits* Eww.
Ashitaka: Well, I think we should go now....
