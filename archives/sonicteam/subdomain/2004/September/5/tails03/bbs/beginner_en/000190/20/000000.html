Princess Mononoke

chapter 2: Ashitaka's journey

Ashitaka, the wise woman, and some others were sitting in a small hut. Ashitaka had a piece of cloth wrapped around his arm, and the wise woman was flicking some stones around on the floor (I'm still scratching my head over that one!). After a few minutes, she said "I'm afraid this is very bad. Prince Ashitaka..." "Yes?" Said Ashitaka. "Show everyone your right arm." ansered the wise woman. Ashitaka unwound the piece of cloth wrapped around his arm, and held it up for everyone to see. Where the demon had touched him, he had a twisted, black scar. "What's it mean?" asked someone. "Ashitaka, are you prepared to learn what fate the stones have foretold you?" asked the wise woman. "Yes." replied Ashitaka. "I was prepared the very moment I let my arrow fly." "The infection will spread through your whole body, bone and flesh alike." said the wise woman. "It will cause you great pain, then kill you." For a moment, no one spoke. Then, somebody said "Is there no way we can stop it?" another person said "The prince got that wound by defending the village and saving our lives!" "Do we just sit here and watch him die?" asked another. "You cannot alter your fate, my prince." said the wise woman. "However, you can rise to meet it if you choose." She handed him a little ball of iron. "This iron ball was inside the boar." she said. "It shattered his bones and bored its way deep inside of him, causing him to become a demon monster. There is evil at work in the east. It's your fate to go there, Ashitaka, and see with eyes unclouded by hate." Ashitaka prepared to leave. "Our laws forbid us from watching you go." said the wise woman. "Farewell." She said as he walked out the door.

Outside, Ashitaka climbed onto Yakkul. He was about to leave, when Kaya came out and stopped him. "Kaya?" Ashitaka said, suprised. "What are you doing out here? You know it's forbidden!" "Do you think I care about that?!" asked Kaya. "I came to give you this so you won't forget your little sister!" she said, handing him a piece of crystal on a string. "Your crystal dagger!" said Ashitaka. "Kaya, I can't take this." "Please keep it with you, brother." said Kaya, tears welling up in her eyes. "Please! I want you to have it... so you won't... forget!" "Kaya, you know I could never forget you." said Ashitaka. He took the dagger, and rode away.

end of chapter 2. Stay tuned for the next chapter, "Irontown"!
