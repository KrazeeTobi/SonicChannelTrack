"How do you get your Chaos' to plant seeds? Do they have to learn it in school or something?"

No, they don't learn it in Chao Kindergarten. Beat all the beginner races, and your Chao will obtain the shovel, and the water sprout (forgot the actual name).

"Also, I hatched my first two Chao but how do I get more eggs?"

When they both turn into adults, buy a heart fruit in the Black Market, and they will reproduce a new egg.

Or, you can just earn the Hero and Chao garden by getting the Chao.
