Sonic's Mystic Melody is in Green Forest. When u do a loop and then shoot off a ramp and into a giant tree, get to the top where u would exit and keep going, change the camera to look behind u. U'll c a robot and a platform where the Mystic Melody is waiting.

Shadow's is in Final Chase. There's a gravity cylinder thing that has electric fences near its top. Find another cylinder and jump from there to bypass the fence.

Knuckles' Oxygen Necklace is in Aquatic Mine. Change the water level to 3. There's a thing just like the "Beware of Ghosts" mine shaft but lower down in the mine. Go in it, swim as fast as you can down, forward, and up and the item is at the top. There's also a lost animal-dragon-near the ceiling too.
