Yes, Rouge's Mystic Melody is in the Dry Lagoon level. Here's a quick walkthrough:
In the starting room, look around for a picture to crawl through. It should look like the ones you ran across in Egg Quarters and Knuckles' Death Chamber levels (They seem to have Egyptian-carvings on them). Anyway, there should be 2 pictures in the room you start in. One is uncovered while the other is hidden behind some boxes way up on the top. Find the uncovered one which is a little bit lower than the covered one. Once you find it, crawl in and grab the Mystic Melody. Play it on the shrine to create a warp and continue on through the level.
