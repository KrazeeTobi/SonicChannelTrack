We had been following the migrating dragons for some time now. The sun was setting. The black dragon, who was obviously the leader, stopped walking. The green dragon spoke. "Kerchak," He said. "There is a more protected spot further down the-" the black dragon interupted him. "We'll rest here for the night. Go ahead, Zimbarii." The green dragon grunted, then roared loudly. The other dragons all started finding spots to sleep, literally climbing over each other in order to find the best spots.

The elderly dull green dragon said to the old dull blue one, "Oh, Aniyu, I wish we were at the pastures now. All this pushing and shoving about, just for a place to sleep." The blue one replied, "Kaya, you've got big feet, just give 'em a kick!" A small dragon tried to climb over the blue one. She kicked it off. "Hi there!" shouted Atri when she was next to the blue dragon. The blue dragon jumped up in suprise. "Oh, sorry about that." Said Dante. "She gets a little excited sometimes." "I understand." Said the blue dragon. She held out her hand. "Name's Aniyu. This," She pointed to the dull green dragon. "Is Kaya." We looked up at Kaya. She was very tall and had a very long neck! Kaya looked down at us. "Hello there. Who are you?" "I'm Danteno." Said Dante, flying up to see Kaya. "But you can call me Dante." "I'm Doragonu." Said Gonu, also flying up to see Kaya. "But everyone calls me Gonu." "I be Atriiyu!" Said Atri, zooming around in cilcles. "But everyone calling me Atri!" "And I'm Zephyr." I said. "Zephyr Windstorm." A small bright green dragon walked up to me, smiling, but he didn't say anything. "Well I'll be." Said Aniyu. "Ren doesn't normally warm up to strangers so fast." By now, the sun had almost completely disapeared behind the mountains. "Well, tme for bed." Said Kaya, curling up to sleep. We all did the same.
