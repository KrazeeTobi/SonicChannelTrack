Start off a new game and get Tails and Knuckles. Once they're unlocked, go and
select Knuckles and get the burger man. Take him to the Train Station. The
doors are locked. so what you need to do is place him under the sign that say SS
Central Station.

Then jump up on the ledge above you and slowly drop down. You should land behind
him. Now you can walk through the wall. Make your way to the train station and
once you're in, go behind the computer next to the door leading to the casino area.
See the bricks on the wall? Notice that there are 2 darker bricks. You need to
glide into the top dark brick. You should be pushed through the wall. Glide to
the street and beat Casinopolis. Tikal will come like normal and transport you to
the past... permanetly. You're stuck there. So exit and select Tails. Play and
complete Windy Valley and Casinopolis. Then exit and go to trial mode. Select
Knuckles. Notice that he has no levels to select... or does he? Press A. A
confirmation box will appear saying Knuckles in Windy Valley!
