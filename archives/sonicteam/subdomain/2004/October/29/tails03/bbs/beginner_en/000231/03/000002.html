As Quack shouted his lungs out, not literally, over the escapee's obscurity, Swazi was struck with a sudden apprehension. Out of his frustration, this particular man had shouted the words "godforsaken long-neckedness of those infernal giraffes" in the same sentence- which was pretty rare in those days- due to the radio signal-broadcasting insane asylum escapee grappling his ankle. And it is a well known fact that when someone shouts something out of frustration due to a radio signal-broadcasting insane asylum escapee grappling their ankle, it can only have come from the deepest confines of their heart and soul. Or gallbladder. How perfect. Why couldn't he have come three years earlier? Because it was just that perfect.
