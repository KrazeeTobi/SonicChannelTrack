The New World 2: part 1

Mark the neutral power/flying chao sat down in the chao garden. Ever since Emily had left, Mark had been very lonely. You see, Mark was not like other chao. As you know, most chao only care about eating, sleeping and being petted. (really, that's all. o.o;) But Mark was different. He cared about the other chao. Because of this, everyone thought he was weird.

Anyway, Mark really missed Emily. It seemed like she was the only one who understood his feelings. He decided to find out where she was.

Mark knew that Tails had something to do with Emily's disapearence. the next time Tails came into the chao garden, Mark ran over to him and said, "Tails? I need to talk to you." "Alright," Said Tails. "About what?"

"About Emily." Said Mark. "Where is she?"
"She's in another garden."
"I want to see it!"
"Why?"
Mark paused. "Because... because this place is boring." You see, Mark never told anyone about how he felt towards other chao. He was afraid of getting teased.

"Well, okay." said Tails. "Come with me." He walked out of the garden, and Mark followed. 'This is it!' thought Mark. 'I'm finally gonna see Emily again!'

Just like he did to transport Emily, Tails took out a remote and pressed some buttons. "Goodbye, Mark." Said Tails. "Say hi to Emily for me."

Mark appeared in the new chao garden. but this one was strange. It appeared to be inside of a giant volcano. But what was really amazing as the buildings. There were huge buildings, carved out of volcanic rock! "Wow..." Said Mark. "Now, to find Emily..."
