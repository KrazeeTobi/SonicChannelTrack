I know how to get some pretty cool chao!

Eggman chao: Get a red chao egg from the black market. Then make it a sumo chao (I'm not sure how to do this, but I think you make your chao a power/swimming chao) Then, give your chao penquins untill it has the eyebrows. Then give it skeleton dogs untill only the eyebrows remain. (This may take a few tries, as the eyebrows might get removed) It looks a lot like Eggman!

Boulder chao: get a gray chao egg and make the chao that hatches a sumo chao. Then give it a bat so it looses its feet. It looks like a big boulder! This chao ROCKS! (pun intended)

Black and white chao: Get a gold chao egg. When it hatches, DO NOT LEAVE THE GARDEN!!!! Don't go to collect rings, buy fruit, don't even go to the transporter! Just stay in the same garden untill the chao is an adult, and keep feeding it and petting it. When it evolves, the chao should be black and white! This chao is VERY hard to get, but it's worth it!

Evil bunny-footed chao: Get any dark color of chao egg. (black, brown, etc.) Then make it a dark chao. Then give the chao those little pink bunnies untill it has the feet. Then give it skeleton dogs untill only the feet remain. Then get a skull. (you can get this by digging in dark garden with Knuckles or Rouge) Then give the chao the skull to put on its head. It's the Evil bunny-footed chao!

Skeleton chao: Get a white chao and make it dark. Then give it a skull for its head. Perfect for Halloween!

Pumpkin chao: Get an orange chao. Then give it a pumpkin to put on its head. (you can get this by digging in hero or chao garden with Knuckles or Rouge)
