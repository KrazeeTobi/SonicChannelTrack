I've picked up these recipies off different sites and want to share them. Enjoy!

Strawberries and cream chao: get a white or regular chao. Give it all the skunk parts and make it hero. When it evolves the skunk parts are reddish-pink. It looks like it's made of strawberries and cream!

Metal sonic chao: Get a shiny blue chao and make it a sonic chao. If you want it to look more like Metal Sonic, make it win a yellow ribbon in the chao race.

Boulder chao: Get a gray chao and make it a sumo chao. (swimming/power I think) Then give it a bat so it loses it's feet. It looks like a boulder!

Simba chao: I know, the Lion King sux a##, but this chao is pretty cute. Get either an orange or a yellow chao and give it all the cheetah parts. (or if you have SADX, all the lion parts) it looks like Simba as a kid.

I hope you enjoy making these chao!
