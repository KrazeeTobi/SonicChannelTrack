the other day I wrote a short Sonic dictionary, describing a few things and people in the sonic inuverse. Here's part 2!

Amy: A pink hedgehog who was eight in sonic CD but was magically aged to 12 or so in the later games.

game boy advance: a special device created just so you can take your hero flying chao and make it look like a plain white chao.

purple chao egg: just like a regular chao egg, only purple.

knuckles chao: a very cool-looking chao designed so people can beg other people for one.

Sonic chao: a chao that is just like sonic in every way, except that it's a chao and you can teach it to swim.

Gamma: a big tough robot that is really a pink bird in disguise.

station square: a town that not only is NOT shaped like a square, but is so small that you can look at everything in just a matter of minutes.

master emerald: a big, green jewel-thing

chaos emeralds: just like the master emerald, only there's seven of them and only one of them is green.

Casino: a place where Tails can go dispite the fact that he's not 18.

Well, that's it for this part of the sonic dictionary. If this dictionary was alot of help to you then you are obviously very new to the world of Sonic. If it didn't help you at all, well... I'm not suprised.
