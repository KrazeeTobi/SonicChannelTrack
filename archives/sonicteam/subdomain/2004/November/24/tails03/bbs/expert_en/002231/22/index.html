Sonic's mystic melody is located in his last level, Final Rush. Pay attention to the yellow rails near the middle of his level... One rail should pass by a gray platform that floats by itself. Jump onto that platform, take the rocket to another platform, and so on.

Shadow's mystic melody is also found in his last level, Final Chase. After the fourth checkpoint, you'll encounter a rail that goes straight into the air. Tip for this, cuz it's hard as heck: Hold down B, then jump and do a homing attack when you begin to slide backwards. Should be a spring, then you'll reach the melody... ^_^

Good luck!
