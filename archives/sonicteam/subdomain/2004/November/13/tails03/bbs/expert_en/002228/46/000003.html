> where can i find the los chao in the first level with shadow i dont remember the name of the mission so can anyone of tell me where can i found the lost chao?????'

The level is called Radical Highway.

After checkpoint 2, you will come across two loops then you run down to a
ramp. Make sure to press A while on it to jump on a spring and finally on
another road. Once there, destroy the robot enemy and jump on the pole. Press
A while up to land on the moving lifts. Then wait for the beetle enemy to
drop its electric field and homing attack ( A+A ) it and then the two others.
Destroy the robot enemy in front of you and take up the springs. You are now
on the upper part of the bridge. There should be a golden beetle here. Go to
the left side where Omochao is (you could also go on the right side). From now
on read carefully. People always have trouble understanding what to do. Right
to Omochao is a bridge ramp with rings on it. Do NOT go on this one. You have
to go backwards. So, left to Omochao is another bridge ramp (to the opposite
direction of the one with rings). There is no rings on this one. This one
takes you to the opposite side. Go on this one and crouch to gain speed. You
will land on another upper part of the bridge and in the middle is an ancient
shrine. Play mystic melody and floating platforms will appear so jump on these
and then to the lost chao.
