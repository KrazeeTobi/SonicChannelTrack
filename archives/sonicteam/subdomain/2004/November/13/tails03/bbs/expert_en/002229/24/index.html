Knuckles Upgrades:

Item: Mystic Melody
Found: Wild Canyon
Ability: Plays mysterious melodies to activate ancient shrines
Requirement: Shovel Claw
How to get: Knuckles' Mystic Melody is hidden in a room behind the painting above the lonely statue.You need the Shovel Claw to dig through.

Item: Shovel Claw
Found: Pumpkin Hill
Ability: Enables digging
Requirement: None
How to get: Straight ahead when you first start, can't miss it

Item: Air Necklace
Found: Aquatic Mine
Ability: Allows breathing underwater
Requirement: None
How to get: Lower the water level down all the water to the bottom. Then look for a cage with a spring in it then go to it to find a tunnel, quickly swim through the tunnel before you run out of air and you'll end up in a small room with the upgrade in it.

Item: Hammer Gloves
Found: Death Chamber
Ability: Increases punch attack power, breaks iron containers
Requirement: None
How to get: Flip the hourglass just ahead of the starting point, and walk through the door that opens. Break the wooden crates to the right in the adjacent hallway, and you’ll find the Hammer Gloves.

Item: Sunglasses
Found: Meteor Herd
Ability: Allows Knuckles to see invisible items
Requirement: Hammer Gloves
How to get: Just to the north of the large central structure, there’s a stationary meteor on the top of a platform. Punch this meteor across the gap to smash the door on the north wall. Flip the revealed switch to open a trap door on the floor of the large structure above you. Use the nearby spring to get up there, and then fall down the hole where Knuckles’ final upgrade waits.

Go to Gamefaqs. They have well decent guides 4 ppls who R stuck.
