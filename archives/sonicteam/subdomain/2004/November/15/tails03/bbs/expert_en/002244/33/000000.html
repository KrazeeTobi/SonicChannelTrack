sunglasses for knuckles:
they are found on meteor hurd, you have to punch a medeor in to a door, you will see a switch, hit it and it will open up the floor in the middle of the level, you will be able to find the sunglasses.

Tresure scope for Rouge:
it is found in security hall, find the emerald temple on the lower floor, play the mistic melody then folow the platforms to steel crates, break them open to find a secret room with the tresure scope.

oxygen mask for knuckles:
it is found in aquatic mine, find a vertical steal shaft that starts at water level 2 (note water must be at level 3) follow that down to a pool of water, follow the water to the mask, must be quick or you will drown.

fire roll for shadow:
it is in Radical Highway, near the begining of the level not long after the first chao box look left and there should be a big rolling pillar roll under that and light dash the rings, keep going after that and you will find it.

light dash for sonic:
its on metal harbour, where you find the first line of rings that you have to follow, you will see a tower on the left take the pully to the top and get the shoes.

Get the magic gloves for sonic:
its in the city escape, after the big drop off there will be a grid of 4 steel crates in line with the ground look for them and bounce through.

get mystic melody for knuckles:
its in wild canyon, go to the lonly statue there will be a painting above it which you can dig in to if you go through it you will find mystic melody(if it isnt there then its in some other painting in that level)
