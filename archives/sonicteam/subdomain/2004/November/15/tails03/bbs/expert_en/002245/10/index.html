I think he meant to ask how to attack it. At first, wait for him to stop chasing you. When he stops, grind up the pipe on the side of his neck and homing-attack the red life support system. After you hit him once, he adds a lightning attack to his arsenal. Jump over the low lightning balls and somersault under the high lightning balls, then grind the pipe to his back again when he stops firing. After two more hits, he no longer pauses after the lightning attack, and instead makes a bunch of pink bubbles appear. Dodge the bubbles' homing attacks and homing attack the bubbles until you can get close enough to hit the life support system. After two more attacks, he stops chasing you and using the lghtning attack, but turns off the gravity when he uses the bubble attack. Float between the bubbles and land on his back, then somersault into the life support system to bring him down.
