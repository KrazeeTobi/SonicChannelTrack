Why don't you guys go to IRC to do your chatting.

Just go to http://www.irchelp.org for a list of (almost all free downloads) software and servers. There is even a server for video game players (including off topic "hang out" rooms) and for Anime fans.

BTW, Netscape 7.1 has a built in IRC client. Just type "irc://server#channel" in the URL bar or hit the little "cZ" in the bottom left corner (that stands for the client name "chatZilla" which is part of the Mozilla project Netscape used for the 6.0+ browsers.)
