PART 2

As they entered the extremley large stadium they saw two path ways and a sign that said
"Chao Races - Right"
"Chao Karate - Left"
so they went right. As they entered they saw the 8 chao that were going to race. They went down and sat on the bleachers. Then an announcer came on the mic and said "Welcome to the chao races! Tonight the racers are going to have to go through a track of very hard things. They have to use their skills in swimming, running, flying, and power.....now please wait and the races will be beginning shortly...." Then about one minute later the announcer came on and said "Now....let the races BEGIN!" And then there was a gunshot and the chao were off.....

10 minutes after watching......"And the winner is.......KALAKARI!" Then the audience clapped as he was about to recieve the trophy and the race was over....so Flash and Jewel set off back to the chao garden.
