Spyke the Silver Echidna:part eight

"I... I.... I......" said Knuckles. "Are you okay?" asked Spyke. "I.. I.... I......" "Hey, are you guys ready to help us build the hut?" called Tails. "Yeah, we're ready! I found some good berries!" replied Spyke.

later:

"Mmmm... Those are good berries, Spyke." said Sonic. They had just finished their meal. "Thanks. Well, it's getting late. Let's go to bed." said Spyke. They went into the hut. Inside were four beds. "Okay, everybody pick one." said Knuckles.

11:38, that night:

"Huh? What was that?" said Knuckles quietly. He thought he heard a noise outside. "Hmmm. I must be imagining things." THUD. "Okay, I know I heard something this time." CREAK. CREAK. This woke Spyke up. "Knuckles, are you making noise?" she asked. "Wasn't me." replied Knuckles. "Well, then, who's, or WHAT'S, out there?".....
