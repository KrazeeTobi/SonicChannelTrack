Claws the Wolverine: part three

Claws didn't know where, and WHEN, he was, but it looked like a big, green forest. When he looked around, Claws found a small clearing. "This looks like a good place to rest." he said. Suddenly, he heard something. It sounded like a robot. Of coarse, Claws had never seen, or even heard of robots before. So he ignored it. In the center of the clearing was a tree with apples growing on it. This reminded Claws that he was hungry. So he walked over and picked an apple. Then he sat down and ate it. When he finished, a bunch of Eggman's robots came crashing through the forest. Eggman himself was not there, but many of his robots were. "What kind of magic is this?" thought Claws. The robots started shooting lasers at him! He tried to run away, but there were robots everywhere he turned! The robots formed a circle around Claws, and they kept shooting lasers. "Uuuuu..." said Claws, then everything faded to black...
