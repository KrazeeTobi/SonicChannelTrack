Kerchak, Zimbarii, and Vixanie stopped walking. Kerchak whispered to Zimbarii. "Zimbarii, there are a lot of first timers here. Make sure they understand." Zimbarii nodded, ten said, for everyone to hear, "If this is your first crossing, listen up! There is no water until we get to the other side of that hill." He pointed a to a large hill, many miles from us. "And you'd better keep up, because if a predator catches you, you're on your own. Move out!" We all started walking again. "Oh..." Said Dante. "It looks like a very long walk." "Hot too!" Said Atri. "If Dante, Gonu, Zephyr, aniyu and Kaya smell something burning, it might be Atri!"

a few days later...

We were much closer to the hill. Kerchak and Zimbarii started roaring happily, and advanced toward the hill. "The lake!" Cried Aniyu happily. "We made it?" I asked. "It's just over that hill!" Replied Aniyu. "Thank goodness." Said Kaya. Since Dante, Gonu, Atri and I were faster, we went over the hill first. But instead of a lake, we saw a dried-up wasteland. There were some dragon bones in there. Kaya walked over the hill too. "Wh-Where's the lake?" she asked. Aniyu walked over the hill, and also saw the wasteland. "B-But..." She stammered. "But we always had water before..."

Kerchak looked at the dried up lake, then turned and said, "The pastures are only a few days away. Keep moving!" Vixanie ran after him and said, "But Kerchak! We've never gone this far without water! If we keep going like this we'll lose half the herd!" Kerchak turned and said, "Then we save the half that deserves to live."

Aniyu hadn't moved a bit. She was still sitting in the middle of the dried lake. "We always had water..." she whimpered. "We always had water here before... We always had water here, always... And now, there isn't even mud..." Kaya walked over to Aniyu, her large feet crushing the hard ground. "There is no water here, Aniyu." I had ran over next to Aniyu, when I ntoiced how Kaya's foot was crushing the dry ground. "Kaya!" I said. "Don't move!" "What is it?" Asked Kaya. "What's wrong?" "Guys!" I shouted. "Dante, Gonu, Atri! Come over here!" They all came flying over. "Guys. listen." I could hear something gurgling under the ground. "I hear it!" Said Dante. "So do I!" Said Gonu. "Atri hears too!" "Lift your foot, Kaya!" I said. Kaya did so. We all started digging in the ground, which was very soft and moist. "Now press down." I said. As soon as Kaya pressed her foot into the ground, water appeared! We all roared loudly. "Water! Over here!" I shouted.

We all took long drinks, until Kerchak showed up. He pushed us all away and took the water hole for himself. We decided to stay a while, and get a few drinks.
