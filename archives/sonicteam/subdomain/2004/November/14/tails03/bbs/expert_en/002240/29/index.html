I know, a lot of you are starters at Sonic Advance 3, but, I've completed the story with no Chaos Emeralds. Or maybe I had the red Emerald only when I beat it, I don't remember. But, I completed the game in 1 day! Here's the first boss's strategy.


Recommended team: Tails (leader) & Sonic (partner)

Strategy: Collect the rings in front of you, then fight G-mel. Don't worry, the first G-mel is easy to beat. Just attack it 4 times (normal) or 2 times (easy). Once it's defeated, advance. Pick up the 3 rings in front of you, then face the boss. It looks like Eggman has another hammer machine with G-mel as the panel (the hammer part is the target). When Eggman tilts slightly, make sure you are against G-mel's face, then jump on the panel. Eggman will hammer down. Now's your time to use the Tail Attack. This can be done up to 2 times each round. Repeat until you defeat him. Now, advance, and pick up the Goal Ring and you've cleared Route 99!

I don't know. Must be for general Sonic stuff. Anyways, here's the second boss!


Recommended team: Any

Strategy: Eggman will come in a giant wheel contraption. There's a blue switch in the middle that you can press to avoid Eggman. It's best to do this, because you can get in lots of aerial attacks this way. Hit the cockpit (or G-mel, it doesn't matter) 8 times (normal) or 6 times (easy). Go into the tunnel in the right when he's defeated and touch the goal ring.
