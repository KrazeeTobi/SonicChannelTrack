Dimitri-
Dimitri is the DEFINITIVE Knuckles villain, serving as the "Doctor Robotnik" of the Knuckles universe! While he is by no means anywhere near as nasty as Robotnik he can certainly run a close second! Serving as the current leader of the Dark Legion, he will do ANYTHING to enforce his will of Technology above all upon the populace of the Floating Isle. However - from his perspective, he sees the Brotherhood Of Guardians as the natural enemies and hypocrites. Dimitri and his brother Edmund created the Chaos Syphon which was supposed to return the Floating Island onto the Mobius Mainland. Instead it backfired and infused Dimitri with the emerald energy instead! Dimitri went positively insane with the power of 11 Chaos Emeralds howling inside him! He remained buried in an avalanche under Mount Fate for centuries until he was revived. There he rebuilt his "master city" of Nekronopolis - Echidnaopolis remade - until Knuckles and The Chaotix shot him into deep space (with unknown help from Locke). Thanks to Mammoth Mogul, Enerjak returned where he resumed leadership of the Legion from Kragok, his own grandson several generations removed. Enerjak didn't realise that he was a pawn in Mogul's scheme and after all those centuries of Chaos energy he could do nothing as every drop of it was drained out of him, reducing him to his true age - that of a shriveled old echidna that could barely breathe. Seeking to keep him alive, the DL somehow managed to infuse his aged body with cybernetic parts almost completely roboticizing him to some effect, and leaving VERY FEW bare spots visible! Hence came the cyborg we know today....Dimitri has an infatuation with Knuckles. Knux is different from the other guardians in mind, body, and spirit - and Dimitri wants to know why...
