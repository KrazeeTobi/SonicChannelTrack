play the level normally( don't take the shortcut by using the ancient ruin) until you get the third spinning platform(don't turn left, but instead go straight ahead and jump and hover using the jet engine to a small little platform with a spring on it. don't walk into the spring)and wait for the moving platform to come to you( it doesn't come all the way and it moves pretty fast, so you'll have to wait a little and time your jumps) when and if you do land on the fast-moving platform, turn, but don't jump, to the left and you will see two small platforms, the one closer to you will have a fence surrounding it, and the other one will have four metal boxes at the edge, 2 on the bottom and 2 on the top, and then one big walkway with a spring on the left side(on your right). shoot the four metal boxes on the farther platform and then jump to it using the jet engine(if you really suck at using the controls, then blow up the 2 metal boxes on the platform with the wooden fence in front. your bullet thingys will go throughit, and jump to that one and then jump to the other platform) and jump to the walkway. once on the walkway just walk to the upper level of the walkway and you will see four torches and two small and big rectangles of gravel or whatever. the lost chao will be on the bigger rectangle to the left( your left).

there you go.
