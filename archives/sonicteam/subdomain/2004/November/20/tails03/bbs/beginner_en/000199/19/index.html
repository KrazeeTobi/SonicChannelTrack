It's easy. You just take the first letter (or letters) from a word, and put it at the end of the word, followed by Ay. Here's and example:

I ate-hay ou-yay all.

translation:

I hate you all.

You don't do this with words that begin with a vowel, though. So when I said you were upid-stay, I was calling you stupid. I just think upid-stay sounds cooler.
