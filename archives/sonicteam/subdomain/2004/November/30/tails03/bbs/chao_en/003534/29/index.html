They're no different from other chaos chao, and they have very low stats. Even flash only averages at about 2000. To raise chaos chao you have to get a chao to like you enough so that it will reincarnate as an egg after it dies. Pet it a lot and don't hit it, and you should be good. If it comes when you whistle that's a good sign. By the way, it takes a chao about 15 hours to complete a life cycle, and therefore reincarnate. Do this TWICE. when it is reborn the second time give it ONE AND ONLY ONE of each animal. If you followed the steps it should evolve into a chaos chao within 3 hours. Getting emblems is a bit more tricky. I recomend buying a guide or printing one from a walkthrough site if you're looking to do it with minimal fuss. Practice the levels alot, and remember, if you spindash and press b before you stop, you will run faster than normal. If you're still having trouble I have some other tips, but I don't feel like adding them now, so you'll have to ask. nicely.
