"I can help you." repeated the little dragon. "I will tell you how your soul sister came into being. Sit down." I did, and the dragon began.

"I will answer questions later." he replied. "Dark Snowfrost was born from your inner hatred. She is still growing, though. But you know how whenever you get angry or scared, she appears? I had many questions. "How did you know my name?! How did you know I keep my anger inside of me?! How did you know I was the one with a soul sister?!..... Who ARE you?!" The small dragon smiled. "I shall answer each of your questions..." he said...
