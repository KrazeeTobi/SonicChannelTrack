Without so much as glancing at the motley band of would-be revolutionaries, he started the ignition. It was as though it were his entire life's purpose to take Swazi's taken car.
"HEY!! What're ya doing!?!?" Swazi shouted at the enigmatic old coot, "That's my jeep!! I worked my liver off trying to get that thing, and like any honest, hardworking man, that means I stole it!!"
