Spyke the Silver Echidna: part three

Last time: Eggman captured Spyke's family and friends! Knuckles got an idea. He ran after the robots...

Knuckles had been running for what seemed like hours. He collapsed on the ground. Looking up, Knuckles realized where he was. "Robotropolis! I made it!" He said. "Now, how will I get inside?" The huge steel door was being guarded by some very tough looking robots. "I have to get in! Spyke needs my help!" he thought. Suddenly, Knuckles noticed a loose piece of metal on the gate. "Maybe I could get in through there." he thought. So, carefully avoiding the robots, Knuckles squeezed through the gate. "Yes! I'm inside!" Said Knuckles. Then he saw something that made his jaw drop. There were robots everywhere! "This might be a bit harder than I thought." Said Knuckles. Then, he reached into his pocket (?) and took out a cell phone. Quietly, he punched in some numbers. "Hello..?" He said. "Yeah, it's me, Knuckles... Come quick, I need some help... Robotropolis... You'll help?... Great!... Okay, see you then... Bye." Knuckles put the cell phone back in his pocket (?). Suddenly, a blue blur zoomed into Robotropolis. It was... Well, I think we all know who it was. Then, Tails flew over the gate in his plane. "Hey, you made it!" said Knuckles. "Okay, here's why I needed your help"...
