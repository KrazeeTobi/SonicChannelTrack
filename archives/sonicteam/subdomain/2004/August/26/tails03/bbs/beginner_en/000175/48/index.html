Spyke the Silver Echidna

By Spyke

One peacefull day, Knuckles was out looking for the master emerald. Someone had stolen it while he was sleeping, and this did not make him very happy. In front of him was a cliff. "Maybe the emerald's up there." Knuckles thought to himself. He climbed up. When he reached the top, he saw the master emerald! Unfortunatly, some of Eggman's robots wre guarding it! When the robots saw Knuckles, they fired a bunch of missils at him! "AAAAAA!!!!" Screamed Knuckles, tumbling off the cliff. He hit the ground with a loud THUD. Then everything went black...

later:

Knuckles woke up inside a hut. He tried to get up, but then noticed he was wraped in a cast. He could only move his head and arms. Suddenly, a young female echidna walked in. She wore an outfit like Amy's, but it was purple and green. Around her neck, she wore a dragon pendant made of crystal, with emerald eyes. She was holding the master emerald! She had emerald-green eyes. And she was SILVER. "Oh good. You're awake. I was getting worried." Her voice sounded like Rouge's, but deeper. "Who are you? Where am I? What happend? What are you doing with the master emerald?" Asked Knuckles exitedly. "My name is Spyke. You are in my village. I found you passed out on the ground. I thought that this emerald might be yours, so I kept it." Ansered the silver echidna. Suddenly, an alarm-like sound rang in Knuckles's ears. "I'll be right back." Said Spyke. "And don't try to get up. You fractured three ribs." Then she rushed out of the hut.
