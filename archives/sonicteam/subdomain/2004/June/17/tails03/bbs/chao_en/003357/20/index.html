Metal Overlord

This is the final battle and Gold Sonic is ready to go with his super powers. The goal is to get the Team Blast Gauge up as fast as possible. To do this, Counter the Overlords attacks. When he uses his Crystal Attack, use Sonic's speed to crash through them (1). When he turns around and uses the Spine Attack, use Tails to shoot them down. He will dive down and lift a ship from the egg fleet as the final attack (2). Destroy it using Knuckles. Once the Gauge is filled, use Team Blast. After five of them Metal Overlord is defeated (3).
