The New World: part 2

Emily looked around. It worked! She was in the new garden! But it wasn't like any garden she had ever seen before. The entire garden was made of clouds! The ground, the trees, everything was made of white, fluffy cloud-stuff. (yeah, I know, you can't really walk on clouds.)

Emily decided to fly up so she could get a better look at everything. She flapped her wings, but nothing happened! She flapped harder. Still nothing. She couldn't fly! "What's going on?" She thought. She turned around to look at her wings.

"What the..." She whispered. Her big, purple condor wings, of which she'd always been proud, were gone. They had been replaced with regular, tiny, pink chao wings. also, she wasn't a hero flying chao anymore. She was a baby chao!

Tails came into the chao garden. But he looked different. He looked older then before.

Emily ran over to Tails. "Tails?" She asked. "You look so... different!"

Tails looked down at Emily. "Oh, hi. Uhh... Have we met before?"
"It's me, Emily!"

Tails looked confused. "Emily...?" He mumbled. "Oh, yeah! I remember now! Emily! Good to see you again!" He petted Emily.

Emily smiled. "How long's it been?" She asked.
Tails thought for a moment. "...About... four years, I'd say."
Emily was shocked. 'FOUR years?!' She thought. 'Where was I for four years?!'

Tails gave Emily a fruit, which (suprise!) looked like a cloud. "Well, time for me to go." He said. "Have to go save the world. See you later Emily." He petted Emily, then left the garden. Emily smiled. She loved being petted, not because it felt good, but because it gave her the feeling that she'd done something right.

End of part two
