The New World: part 1

Tails stepped into the chao garden, with two chao fruits. He walked up to his two favorite chao, Emily the hero flying chao, and Mark the neutral power chao. He handed each of them a chao fruit. "Here you go." He said. "Enjoy your fruits." Emily and Mark smiled, then started munching on their fruits. Tails went to check on the other chao.

Later on, Tails picked up Emily and took her to the chao lobby. "Emily," He said. "I need to talk with you." Emily looked up at Tails. "What is it?" She asked. "Well," Said Tails. "I needed to ask you, how would you feel about going to a different garden?" Emily looked suprised. "A different garden? but why?" Tails sighed. "Well, all the gardens are getting kind of crowded, so I wanted to see if I could move you to a garden in a different world. It won't hurt or anything, the scenery will shift a bit, and then you'll be there. But I've been told there are still a few bugs in the system, so the new garden might not work properly."

Emily thought for a moment, then said, "Okay, sounds intresting!" Tails smiled. "Good." He took out a small remote and pushed some buttons. Emily gasped. Everything was changing! Everything seemed to be spinning. She was getting dizzy. Then she appeared in a different world...

end of part one.
