Spyke the Silver Echidna: part nine

"Knuckles, you stay here. I'm gonna go see what's going on." said Spyke, quietly leaving the hut. "Be careful." said Knuckles. A few seconds later, there was the sound of pieces of metal hitting each other. Sonic and Tails woke up. "What's going on?" asked Tails. "There were some noises outside. Spyke went to check it out." replied Knuckles. "Well, I'm going out there to." said Sonic. "Me to!" said Tails. "Well, your not leaving me behind." said Knuckles. So, they snuck out of the hut. Outside, they saw Spyke beating up a bunch of Eggman's robots! He was back! "Guys, help!" shouted Spyke. So, the three heros joined in the fight. At first, it was easy. But, when they had defeated the last robot, they were all egsausted(<spelling mistake) Well, at least, they THOUGHT it was the last robot. Suddenly, dozens more robots appeared. "We can't beat them." said Spyke. "We have to surrender." Suddenly, someone, or someTHING, ran past the four friends. "What was that?" asked Sonic. A purple wolverine rushed out of the forest. He looked something like knuckles, but with long, elf-like ears, and twice as many spikes on his hands. He went and destroyed all the robots! "Okay, okay, I'll let you go!" Eggman came out of the forest in his robot. "I'll stop trying to catch you." he said. The wolverine didn't seem to hear him. He jumped onto Eggman's robot and knocked him out of it! Eggman fell to the ground, then ran away, screaming. "Don't listen to that guy." said the wolverine in a deep voice. "He's trouble." then he rushed back into the forest. The friends didn't know who that wolverine was, or why he saved them, but they did know one thing: Eggman wasn't going to be bugging them for a while.

THE END

After:

Spyke rebuilt her village, with the help of Sonic, Knuckles and Tails, and it is open to anyone who needs a home. Knuckles told Spyke how he feels about her, and they sometimes go on dates.
