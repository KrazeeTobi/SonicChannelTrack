In Black & White, you play as a god. You can be good, evil, or in between. It's your choice. You can play any way you want, and nobody can tell you what to do. In this game, your spiritual advisers guid you through, telling you how to do different things, what's going on, and more! One adviser is an angel, the other a devil, and, even though you can play however you like, both will try to comvince you to do it their way. Also, you can get a Creature in the game. Creatures look like ordinary animals, exept they always walk on two legs, and, when fully grown, are around 30 meters tall! You can teach your creature to be nice to people, helping them water their crops and herd the cows and sheep, or you can teach him to be destructive, drowning people in the sea and eating their animals. For more information, visit the LionHead studios website.
