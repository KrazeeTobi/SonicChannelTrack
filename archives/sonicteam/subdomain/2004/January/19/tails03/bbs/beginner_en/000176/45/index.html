Spyke the Silver Echidna: part seven

Sonic, Knuckles and Tails had been waiting all morning. Spyke was still inside the hut. "I'm hungry." said Tails. The guys didn't know what kind of food was around here, so they pretty much depended on Spyke. A few minutes later, she came out of the hut, a frightend look on her face. "He's after us." She said. "Who?" asked Sonic. "That ugly guy. The one at Fancy Furs Co. I saw him come over that hill." Spyke pointed to a hill behind the hut. Nobody moved. "Well, what are you waiting for?!? RUN!!!" Cried Spyke. She rushed into the forest, followed by Sonic, Knuckles and Tails.

later:

By now the four friends were very deep in the forest. "Okay, I think we're safe now." said Spyke. "Sonic, Tails! Go into the forest and find some wood and big leaves. As much as you can carry!" She added. "Knuckles and I will look for food." She and Knuckles walked into the forest. "Uh, Spyke," said Knuckles. "There's kinda something I need to tell you...."
"Well, what is it?" asked Spyke. "Well, um... I..." stammerd Knuckles. "What?" asked Spyke. "I... I..... I......."...
