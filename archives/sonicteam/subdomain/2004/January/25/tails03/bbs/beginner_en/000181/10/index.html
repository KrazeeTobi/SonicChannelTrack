My long ears helped guide me through the lab without making a sound. It was as if I had supernatural powers. I darted through the sky and away from the lab. I didn't know anything other than when I was on that platform in the lab. Those scoundrels had kept me there like an animal! The scientists were of different species. Foxes, lions, etc., not the common house pet but they stood upright unusually and they wore glasses. Anyway, what to do now was unclear. Whether to hide out on this unknown planet, fight against the scientists, releasing all the subjects, find out more about that test facility and Dr. Osto or to search for a way to get off this planet!? I lived off the planet's resources, living in caves, eating strange fruits that I saw a giant ape, at least that's what it seemed to be, eat and it cooked it by shooting fire out of its mouth, killing native beasts for meat to eat with a pair of dagger-like weapons I like to call "The Sun and the Moon". Then I stumbled upon a shuttle that was going to be launched to return to Earth. I believe I saw a banner with "Pioneer 1" on it. Apparently, they must not have liked the planet. I became a stowaway on the ship and rode it to Earth and disembarked. I felt as if I was home, but I didn't remember an Earth feeling. I darted around in the sky above a forest and then I saw a clearing. I landed in the clearing. A hidden cavern was to the left of me while a pile of fruit was right in front of me. It was tropical paradise compared to what was on the other planet. I feel lighter here, though. It feels odd. I soon heard a rumble, it sounded like thunder. I looked around frantically for the source. I then saw a black figure in the trees. I ran into the cave, fearing for my safety. The black figure was a strange shape and I couldn't put my finger on what it might be. Anyway, I saw yellow eyes looking at me so I didn't look. The figure took the fruit elsewhere. I wandered out of the cave, hungry and depressed. I heard city-like noises a couple miles away (my ears help that out alot.). I traveled towards those noises. The city was strange. Strange metal things with wheels (cars) were moving fast, near me, who knows what they were. I knew nothing about this world. All I knew was fighting. I saw people hand green paper (money) over to other people. The people traded this money for items. I was confused. I think you get things by giving people green papers.
