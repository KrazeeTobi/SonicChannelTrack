> i heard that your chao is ready to mate when flowers grow around it. this happened to one of my chao, but i didn't know what to do. can someone tell me how to mate chao?

When one of your chao is about to mate.
Put another chao next to it and they both will start mating with each other.
Note: Most chao may not want to mate when you put them together.
But if you want them to mate whenever the time you want, give both of them heart fruits, and they should start automatically going back into their mating season.
