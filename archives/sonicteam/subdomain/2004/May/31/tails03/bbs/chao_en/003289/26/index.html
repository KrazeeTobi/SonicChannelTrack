I always do this:

1: Reincarnate a Chao twice (it must have Neutral, Hero or Dark if you want to evolve it into the Chaos Chao you want it to be in both lives)

2: Give it all 21 animals once

3: Give it a fruit from one of the two (or three) trees that start in the garden.

4: Give it one of these fruits:

Chao Fruit (if you're evolving it into a Neutral type)
Hero Fruit (if you're evolving it into a Hero type)
Dark Fruit (if you're evolving it into a Dark type)

5: Wait for about 3 hours

6: Enjoy your new Chaos Chao!
