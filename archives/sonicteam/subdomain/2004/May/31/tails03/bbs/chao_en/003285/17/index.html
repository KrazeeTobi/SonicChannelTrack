Well, first of all, the chaos garden... It doesn't exist... I would've loved it to exist, though... And, if your chao reaches 6 years, it will die. If you treat your chao like a GOD (really nice), it will reincarnate, instead of die. When a chao reincarnates, its level goes back to 01, and it's stats move up once (before- 2021, after- 202_), and it will appear as an egg. When you hatch your chao, it will have 1 animal piece missing. If your chao was a Dark chao, and it reincarnated, it will be frowning all the time (new trait).
