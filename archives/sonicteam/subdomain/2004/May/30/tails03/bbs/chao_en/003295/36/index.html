The New World: part 4

"So," Said Emily. "Suppose these bad chao find us. What do we do?"

"Come with me," Said Zimbarii. "I'll show you." He walked deeper into the cave, followed by Emily and Illusen.

After walking for a while, Zimbarii stopped. "These are our weapons." He said, pointing at thw wall. Emily looked up. Mounted on the wall were many swords, spears, and even guns, all the perfect size for chao. Illusen flew up, took a sword, and handed it to Zimbarii. But it wasn't like the other swords. There were jewels in the handle, and the blade was so shiny, it seemed to glow.

"This is my sword." Said Zimbarii. "If those... other chao attack us, we'll be ready for a fight."

"But I don't understand." Said Emily. "Why would there be a small animal that would make chao aggressive?"

"I think it's some kind of glitch." said Illusen. As she said that, Emily remembered what Tails had told her. After a moment of silence, she replied. "It probably is..."

Suddenly, Zimbarii said, "Ssshh! Hide!" He hid behind a rock. "What?" Asked Illusen. "What's the problem?" Instead of answering, Zimbarii grabbed Illusen's hand and pulled her behind the rock. "Come on Emily!" He said. Emily dove behind the rock with the other chao. "Why are we hiding?" She asked. "It's the bad chao..." Replied Zimbarii. "They're here..."

end of part 4
