1: Abuse your Chao with a Hero character (the Chao that you want to be a Dark type may have different colors than a regular Dark Chao if you abuse it with Heroes) or love the Chao with a Dark character, give it animals and/or Chaos Drives, give it fruits and/or Dark Fruits

2: Wait until the Chao evolves

3: A bit of music will play

4: The longer version of that music plays, and when the cocoon disappears, your Chao will be a Dark Chao!

5: Enjoy the Dark Garden! Descend down the steps in the lobby or simply jump down to the portal and step into it and it takes you to the Dark Garden
