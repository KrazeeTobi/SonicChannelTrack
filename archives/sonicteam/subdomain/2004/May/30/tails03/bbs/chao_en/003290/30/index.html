Once there was a chao named Apples. Apples was a green chao. He had been abandoned by his previous owner. So he decided to find a new one. But he got lost in a forest. He couldn't find his way out, so he started crying.

meanwhile...

Everwood the fox was taking a walk in the forest. But he heard someone crying. He followed the sound of the crying, and found a little green chao. "Hey, what are you doing here?" He asked. "I'm lost..." Whimpered Apples. "Well, I'll take you home with me." Said Everwood. Apples smiled. "Really?!" "Sure." Replied Everwood. "I've been wanting a chao." so the two went home.

the end

(Apples is a real chao who lives in my chao garden on SA2B!)
