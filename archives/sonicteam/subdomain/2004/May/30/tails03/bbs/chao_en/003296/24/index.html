The New World: part 5

"You mean... those chao that you were telling me about?" Asked Emily. "They're here in this cave?" Zimbarii nodded. "Yes, they are." He turned to his sister. "Illusen! You're the only one who can get those weapons down from the wall. Get some weapons for yourself and Emily."

"Okay." Said Illusen. She flew up and grabbed a spear. She handed it to Emily, then grabbed a mace for herself. (for those who don't know, a mace is a spiky ball on a chain) "Okay," She said. "I'm ready." Zimbarii picked up his sword. "Great." he said. "Let the battle begin."

Zimbarii, Illusen, and Emily stepped out of the cave. The bad chao were waiting. Emily gasped. They were so ugly! And yet... they were facinating. They all had long horns, torn wings, long, scaly tails, huge fangs, and to top it all off, blood red eyes. but that wasn't what really scared her. There were hundreds of them!

The leader of the evil chao stepped out of the crowd. He was a devil chaos chao. His wings, horns, and fangs were much bigger than the wings horns and fangs on the other chao. He was also much taller. he looked down at the three chao and laughed.

"We come here for a fight, and this is what we get?!" He looked down at Zimbarii. "A pathetic zebra chao..." He looked down at Illusen. "A weak pixie chao..." then he looked at Emily. "And a baby chao!" He laughed again. "Is that the best you can do?!"

"We can't do this alone..." Whimpered Emily. Illusen nodded. "You're right..." She said. She whispered to Zimbarii, "What do we do?" Zimbarii just smiled. "Don't you worry." He said. "I've got it all figured out."

Zimbarii whistled loudly, so loudly that everyone covered their ears. No sooner had he stopped whistling, hundreds of chao of all shapes and colors came out of the cave, each with weapons.

"Alright," Said Zimbarii. "Now the fight wil officially begin!"

end of part 5
