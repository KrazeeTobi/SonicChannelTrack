Don't lose your way
Each passing day
You've come so far,
Don't throw it away
Live believing,
Dreams are for weaving,
Wonders are waiting to start!
Live your story,
Faith hope and glory,
Hold to the truth in your heart!

If we hold on together,
I know our dreams
Will never die!
Dreams see us threw
To forever,
For you, and I!

Souls in the wind,
Learn how to bend,
Seek out a star,
Hold on to the end!
Firey Mountain,
There is a fountain
That washes our tears all away!
Worlds are swaying,
Someone is praying,
Please, let it stay for today!

If we hold on together,
I know our dreams
Will never die!
Dreams see us through
To forever,
For you, and I!

And we, are out there,
in the dark!
We'll dream about
The stars!
In the dark we'll,
Feel the light!
Won't our hearts
Feel reward?

If we hold on together,
I know our dreams
Will never die!
Dreams see us through,
To forever,
As high, as souls
Can fly,
As the clouds
Roll by,
For you, and I!
