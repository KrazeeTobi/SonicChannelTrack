In order to achieve the Chaos Chao, you must follow these task.

Note: It may be hard for beginner Chao raiser.

1. Reincarnate a Chao 2 times
2. Give it one of every animal when it's a baby
3. Give it...

Chao Fruit for a Light Chaos Chao
Hero Fruit for a Angel Chaos Chao
Dark Fruit for a Devil Chaos Chao

When it turns into an adult, it will turn into the Chao you desire.

Hope you can achieve this goal!
