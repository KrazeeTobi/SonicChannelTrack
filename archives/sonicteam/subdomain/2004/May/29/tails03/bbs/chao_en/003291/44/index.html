In Aquatic mine, set the water level to 3 (it's in the 2nd level of the mines) and go in the small square hole. Then fall into the water and dive down. You must hurry or you will lose air. Go into the under water tunnel and go through there and use the dash panels along the way, then swim down the stairs and into another underwater tunnel. When you're in the middle of the circular room you enter, return to the surface. Then just climb up the shaft to enter a room with a Dragon and the Air Necklace.
