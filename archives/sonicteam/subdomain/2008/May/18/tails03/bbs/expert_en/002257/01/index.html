QUICK SCREW KICK- Rotate the Analog thumb pad clockwise, then push the B button when you see the action window to make Rouge do an upward drill move.
This move has other names;
Spiral Upper for Knuckles,
Two other names (forgot), one for Tikal and one for Chaos 0.

The move comes in handy for Rouge mostly:
1) Defeat flying Dog with ease with this move
2) The capsule in Dry Lagoon; high up in the next oasis.
3) Getting Emerald number 3 in Mad Space: Hard Mode
4) A floating key in the Egg quarters on a ceiling (this only happens sometimes)

The expanded DC radius (which can be visualized by going to Meteor Herd and imagining a circle that comes up just short of the rings in all directions) only applies to the first frame of the attack. However, it's notable that the safes in Security Hall don't have their "immune to Screw Kick" bits set, so even though the safe is well within range for both versions, only DC can instantly open safes with the move, regardless of whether they're locked or unlocked. This leads to much easier grabbing of many emeralds, especially #3 in hard mode.

But the extra reach does provide access to things like Death Chamber hard key 1 through the floor (though you never even have to go into that room), all 8 rings quickly at the start of Wild Canyon without having to run around the circle, and Meteor Herd's ring/1up boxes behind the breakable walls of the central tower (an occasional normal mode piece 3, or hard mode's piece 1, can also be found there). Sadly, most diggable pieces as well as all three Egg Quarters hard keys are immune to Screw Kick/Spiral Upper.
