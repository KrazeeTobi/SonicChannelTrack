Light Dash the trail of rings, Ignore the Phoenix, grab the handle!

Remember to watch that timer in time attack missions!
There is really no time to fool around in Crazy Gadget. When you are in the room which is dark green and really deep. (It has a black ceiling which you stand upside down on) Go for the down arrow switch and stay close to the wall. You will fall down onto a ledge that you can barely tiptoe on. Hold the Directional pad firmly. There's a rail leading down that you'll need to grind on.

If you miss a ring or an enemy do not go back for it just for points!

Forget the ledges, you can fall straight to the opening.
