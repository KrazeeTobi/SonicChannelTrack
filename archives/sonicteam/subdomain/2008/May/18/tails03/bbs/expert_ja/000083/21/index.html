ハーバーの欠けているリングは最後の赤い滑車ハンドルの上に集められます。 194リングを示す映画では、位置は1分の54秒に示されます。

There is a page at http://www.soniccenter.org/videos/sonic_adventure_2_b with movie walkthroughs for many levels, including FR and SR 1st mission TA.

The ring you are looking for in MH is found on top of the last red pulley cage, after you jump off the handle. In the movie listed as 194 rings, the ring is collected at 01:54.

The ring you are looking for in WC cannot be obtained, unfortunately. It is in the same spot where Big the Cat appears in DC version, and is too high to reach because of an invisible ceiling.
