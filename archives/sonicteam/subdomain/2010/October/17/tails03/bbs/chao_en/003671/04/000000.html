GLITCH (Sonic Adventure 2 / Battle or Sonic Adventure DX Only): Reuse Animals / Chaos Drives. Place the item in front of Chao’s face without giving it to him. It will pose as if it took the item, but you can actually reuse item over and over again with this trick. Works in Sonic adventure DX too. It might be easier if you use Sonic and buy Chao heart fruit so it stays still.

The cheat below is only for Sonic adventure 2 Battle
Tip on how to get rings by cheating-
1) Buy the most expensive item, drop it off in the garden then Save
2) RETURN to the garden, take item to Black market +sell it.
As soon as it’s sold DO NOT SAVE. Hold X+B+Start in the Black market to reset game.
3) Look at your total ring count (It has increased). And if you go back to the garden the item was in, it's still there!
You can sell and reset until you have the desired amount of money!

This test will prove that feeding Chao helps them reach primary evolution faster.
Preparation:
Use 2 normal coloured Chao eggs.
Throw them at the wall so they hatch roughly at the same time, and make sure you keep both Chao in the same garden for this test+ do not let the Chao you are feeding go to sleep, because you need to feed it.
Feed one of the Chao non-stop, but leave the other + do not pet it because both Chao have to stay blue (it is best to recognise the change). You may swap sides if the one you are feeding is beginning to change alignment (i.e. baby devil / angel).
After 30 minutes or so, the Chao that you are feeding lots is becoming a darker blue and the one that you are not feeding is a lighter blue. The one that you fed lots will evolve before the one that you have not fed!
Conclusion: Feeding Chao helps them evolve faster.

This test will prove that feeding Chao helps them reach Secondary evolution faster.

You must have two freshly evolved adult Chao of the same ability. Feed one of them constantly but leave the other. You will notice that the one you are feeding is changing shape / colour faster than the one that you're not feeding.

Conclusion: Secondary evolution can speed up.
