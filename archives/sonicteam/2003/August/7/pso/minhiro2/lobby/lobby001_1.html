2003.05.08

"Minhiro," the Communication Website of PSO, is now open!
For those of you viewing this page for the first time, enjoy your experience with the PSO world. It's nice to meet you!
For the PSO veterans, thanks for waiting!

"Minna no Hiroba (Open Space for All)", which has previously delivered PSO content for the Japanese GC version of PSO -- has now been reincarnated to allow all players who enjoy PSO to have fun here. Its name is "Minhiro" !

"Minhiro's" purpose is to create a more enjoyable community for PSO players.
So not only will there be the latest information on PSO from Sonicteam, but we hope to get lots of contributions and information from you.

Okay, here are a few things we have planned.

Leading role of this week
From the various screenshots posted to the Visual BBS or via mail, we will pick up and feature picture(s) of one person per week !
Infomation
We will feature information, mails and info on player's events we have received from you. We are await your contributions!
Illustration
We will have PSO-related illustrations here ! Send in a bunch of your illustrations!
