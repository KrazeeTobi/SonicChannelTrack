FOREWORD



Welcome to the AMS.


My name is “G.” As an AMS agent,
I’ve been on countless missions,
including the Curien Mansion case.


As an AMS veteran, I thought I’d better
write up this file for all of you rookies
out there, to help you get through
some of the many challenges
I’m sure you’re going to face.


In this file, I’m going to concentrate on
the bosses, the enemies you newbies are
bound to have the most trouble with,
so pay close attention.


But keep in mind that the boss information
I’m going to be giving you here is classified,
so you’d better make certain you don’t
let any of it leak.


Some of you might be the stubborn type,
the kind that likes to prove you can do things
on your own without any help. If that’s you,
then I advise you not to look at this file.


Remember, it’s up to you
whether you open this file or not.


But if you do decide to look at this information,
make sure you learn it inside and out, backwards
and forward, because I guarantee you you’re going
to need it against what you’re about to face.
Hope it helps.
