TOWER

HOD2


Tower is a giant dragon with five heads. In order to defeat it,
you first have to wipe out the four brown heads on the left
and right.


When one of these brown heads approaches to make a
biting attack, its mouth will glow. That’s the moment
you should fire.



Once you’ve wiped out the brown heads on the left and right,
it’s time for one-on-one combat with the final blue head in
the room beyond. This blue head will move around wildly and
try to bite you. Just as with the brown heads, however, its
mouth will glow just before it strikes,

so if you aim for its mouth and fire repeatedly, you should
be able to defeat it without too much difficulty.



Depending on the route you took, the battleground will
either be filled with water or sand. But despite some small
differences in the timing of when the blue head’s mouth
glows, the basic strategy will be the same.
